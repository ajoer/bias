[CLS] Anthony Guy Marie Réveillère (French pronunciation: ​[ɑ̃.tɔ.ni ʁe.vɛ.jɛʁ]; born 10 November 1979) is a French retired footballer who played as a right back. [SEP] [CLS] He spent most of his professional career with Rennes and Lyon, amassing Ligue 1 totals of 426 matches and five goals during 16 seasons and winning 12 major titles with the latter club, including five national championships. [SEP] [CLS] Réveillère played 20 times with France, representing the nation at the 2010 FIFA World Cup and UEFA Euro 2012. [SEP] [CLS]
