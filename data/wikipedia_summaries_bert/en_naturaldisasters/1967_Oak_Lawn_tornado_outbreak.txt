The 1967 Oak Lawn tornado outbreak was a destructive tornado outbreak and severe weather event that occurred on April 21, 1967, across the Upper Midwest, in particular the towns of Belvidere and Oak Lawn, Illinois. [CLS] [SEP] It was the most notable tornado outbreak of 1967 and one of the most notable to occur in the Chicago metro.
