Aeroflot Flight 65 was a scheduled passenger flight operated by the International Civil Aviation Directorate division of Aeroflot. [CLS] [SEP] On 17 February 1966 at 1:38 am local time a Tupolev Tu-114 crashed during take-off from Sheremetyevo International Airport in Moscow, killing 21 of the 47 passengers and 19 crew members on board. [CLS] [SEP] This was the only fatal incident involving a Tu-114. [CLS] [SEP] A committee investigating the accident found that the crash was due to multiple human failures. [CLS] [SEP]
