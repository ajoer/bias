[CLS] Gloria Asumnu (born 22 May 1985) is a Nigerian sprinter. [SEP] [CLS] As she was born in the United States, she previously represented them in international competitions, before switching to represent Nigeria. [SEP] [CLS] She changed nationality in 2011, on her second application, the first having been denied by the IAAF.She was born in Houston, Texas, ran for Alief Elsik High School and Tulane University.
