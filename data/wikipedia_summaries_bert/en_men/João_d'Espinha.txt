Sport Comércio e Salgueiros (Portuguese pronunciation: [sɨˈpɔɾ kuˈmɛɾsi.u i salˈɣɐjɾuʃ]), commonly known as simply Salgueiros, is a Portuguese multi-sports club from the city of Porto, in the northern region of the country. [CLS] [SEP] Founded on 8 December 1911, in the parish of Paranhos, it is mostly known for its football team. [CLS] [SEP] It plays in the Porto Football Association Elite Division, which is the fourth tier of Portuguese football. [CLS] [SEP] Although they are based in Paranhos, they currently play their home matches at neighbor parish of Campanhã at the Complexo Desportivo de Campanhã which can accommodate 1,000 spectators.
