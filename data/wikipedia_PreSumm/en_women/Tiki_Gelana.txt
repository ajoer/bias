[CLS] Erba Tiki Gelana (Amharic: ኧርባ ቲኪ ገላና; born 22 October 1987) is an Ethiopian long-distance runner who competes in marathon races. [SEP] [CLS] Her personal best of 2:18:58 had been the Ethiopian national record for the event from 2012 to 2017. [SEP] [CLS] She won the 2011 Amsterdam Marathon and the 2012 Rotterdam Marathon. [SEP] [CLS] She won the gold medal at the 2012 London Olympics with a time of 2:23:07, a new Olympic record. [SEP] [CLS]
