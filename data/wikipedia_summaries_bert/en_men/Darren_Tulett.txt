Darren Tulett (born 2 June 1965) is an English journalist who presents football on French television. [CLS] [SEP] He was a presenter for Canal+ for a decade, and now works for beIN Sport. [CLS] [SEP] His presenting style is lighthearted and flamboyant. [CLS] [SEP] Although well known in France as the face of English football, he is almost unknown in Britain. [CLS] [SEP]
