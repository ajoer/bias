[CLS] An intense tornado outbreak occurred on May 22 in western and Central Oklahoma and on May 23 from eastern and central Oklahoma to central Iowa. [SEP] [CLS] A total of 43 tornadoes were reported during the two-day outbreak, of which 14 were recorded as strong or violent (F2 or greater on the Fujita Scale). [SEP] [CLS] The outbreak claimed no fatalities and there were only 12 injuries from a tornado that touched down near Clinton, Oklahoma causing significant damage. [SEP] [CLS] The tornado outbreak overall left $32.8 million (1981 USD) in damage. [SEP] [CLS]
