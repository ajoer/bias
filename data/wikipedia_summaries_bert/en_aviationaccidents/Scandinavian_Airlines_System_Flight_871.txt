Scandinavian Airlines System Flight 871 was a scheduled flight from Copenhagen in Denmark to the Egyptian capital of Cairo, with several intermediate stops; operated by Scandinavian Airlines System. [CLS] [SEP] On 19 January 1960, the Sud Aviation Caravelle flying the service crashed while operating a leg between Yeşilköy Airport and Esenboğa International Airport in Turkey. [CLS] [SEP] The flight was on approach but crashed six nautical miles from the airport, killing all 42 occupants on board. [CLS] [SEP] This was the first fatal crash of a Caravelle. [CLS] [SEP]
