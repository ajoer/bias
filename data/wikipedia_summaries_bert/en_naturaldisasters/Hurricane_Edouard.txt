[CLS] The name Edouard has been used for seven tropical cyclones in the Atlantic Ocean. [SEP] [CLS] Tropical Storm Edouard (1984), a tropical storm that rapidly formed and strengthened in the Bay of Campeche Tropical Storm Edouard (1990), a tropical storm that caused minor damage in the Azores Hurricane Edouard (1996), a Category 4 hurricane that approached Massachusetts Tropical Storm Edouard (2002), a tropical storm that crossed west over Florida Tropical Storm Edouard (2008), a tropical storm that made landfall in Texas Hurricane Edouard (2014), a Category 3 hurricane that churned in the open ocean Tropical Storm Edouard (2020), a weak tropical storm that became the earliest fifth named storm in the basin and briefly affected Bermuda
