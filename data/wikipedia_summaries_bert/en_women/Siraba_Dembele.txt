[CLS] Siraba Dembélé Pavlović (born 28 June 1986) is a French professional handballer who plays as a left wing for Liga Națională club CSM București and captains the France national team. [SEP] [CLS] On 18 February 2020, L'Équipe confirmed Dembélé will join Romanian giants CSM București. [SEP] [CLS]
