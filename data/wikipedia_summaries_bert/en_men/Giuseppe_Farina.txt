[CLS] Emilio Giuseppe Farina, also known as Giuseppe Antonio "Nino" Farina, (Italian pronunciation: [dʒuˈzɛppe ˈniːno faˈriːna]; 30 October 1906 – 30 June 1966) was an Italian racing driver and first official Formula One World Champion. [SEP] [CLS] He gained the title in 1950. [SEP] [CLS] He was the Italian Champion in 1937, 1938 and 1939. [SEP] [CLS]
