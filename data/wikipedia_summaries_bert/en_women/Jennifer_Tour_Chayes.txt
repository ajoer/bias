[CLS] Jennifer Tour Chayes is the University of California, Berkeley Associate Provost for the Division of Data Science and Information and Dean of the School of Information. [SEP] [CLS] She was formerly a Technical Fellow and Managing Director of Microsoft Research New England in Cambridge, Massachusetts, which she founded in 2008, and Microsoft Research New York City, which she founded in 2012. [SEP] [CLS] Chayes is best known for her work on phase transitions in discrete mathematics and computer science, structural and dynamical properties of self-engineered networks, and algorithmic game theory. [SEP] [CLS] She is considered one of the world's experts in the modeling and analysis of dynamically growing graphs. [SEP] [CLS] Chayes has been with Microsoft Research since 1997, when she co-founded the Theory Group. [SEP] [CLS] She received her Ph.D. [SEP] [CLS] in mathematical physics at Princeton University in 1983. [SEP] [CLS] She is Affiliate Professor of Mathematics and Physics at the University of Washington, and was for many years Professor of Mathematics at UCLA. [SEP] [CLS] She is an author on almost 120 scientific papers and the inventor on more than 25 patents. [SEP] [CLS]
