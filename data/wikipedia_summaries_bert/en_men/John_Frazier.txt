John Frazier (Born September 23, 1944) is a visual effects supervisor. [CLS] [SEP] On January 24, 2012, he was nominated for an Academy Award for the movie Transformers: Dark of the Moon. [CLS] [SEP] He was born in Chicago, Illinois. [CLS] [SEP] He has 11 Oscar nominations, and won one Oscar for Spider-Man 2 during the 77th Academy Awards, along with John Dykstra, Scott Stokdyk and Anthony LaMolinara. [CLS] [SEP]
