Madison Brengle (born April 3, 1990) is an American tennis player. [CLS] [SEP] Her biggest success occurred in early 2015, reaching her first WTA Tour final in January, followed by a fourth round Grand-Slam appearance at the Australian Open. [CLS] [SEP] In May, her singles ranking reached a career-best of No. [CLS] [SEP] 35 in the world. [CLS] [SEP] Her greatest victory so far was in 2017 over world No. [CLS] [SEP] 2, Serena Williams. [CLS] [SEP] She has won one WTA 125K series singles title, 15 ITF singles titles, and seven ITF doubles titles. [CLS] [SEP] In August 2007, she was ranked fourth in the world in juniors. [CLS] [SEP] Brengle then toiled for years in the ITF Women's Circuit. [CLS] [SEP] Over the course of 24 consecutive majors between 2008 and 2014, she failed to make it out of the pre-tournament qualifier. [CLS] [SEP] The streak ended when she earned a wild card for the 2014 US Open main draw, which she capitalized on for her first major match-win.
