[CLS] The 1994 Liwa earthquake occurred on February 16 at 00:07 local time. [SEP] [CLS] It was located in southern Sumatra, Indonesia. [SEP] [CLS] The magnitude of the earthquake was put at Mw 6.9, Mw 7.0, or Ms 7.2, according to different sources.The earthquake caused 207 deaths and 2000 injuries. [SEP] [CLS] There was damage from landslides, mudslides, and fires in Lampung Province. [SEP] [CLS] Power outage occurred in western Lampung. [SEP] [CLS] Six-thousand buildings were damaged or destroyed by landslides in the Liwa area. [SEP] [CLS] In addition to southern Sumatra, the earthquake could be felt in western Java and Singapore. [SEP] [CLS] Intense smoke and gas activity was observed in the Suwoh volcanic area.The earthquake took place at the Sumatran Fault Zone. [SEP] [CLS] The Sumatran Fault Zone is 1900 km long and highly segmented. [SEP] [CLS] It can be divided into about 20 segments. [SEP] [CLS] The earthquake occurred in a subparallel strand 2.5 km southwest of the principal trace of the Kumering segment. [SEP] [CLS] The focal mechanism is of right-lateral strike-slip faulting.
