[CLS] Micah Kemboi Kogo (born 3 June 1986 in Burnt Forest, Uasin Gishu District) is a Kenyan long-distance runner, who specialises in the 10,000 metres. [SEP] [CLS] He is the former world record holder in the 10 kilometres road race event with a time of 27:01. [SEP] [CLS] He made his first Olympic appearance in 2008, taking the 10,000 m bronze medal in Beijing. [SEP] [CLS] His 10000m best of 26.35 is 6th fastest of all time. [SEP] [CLS]
