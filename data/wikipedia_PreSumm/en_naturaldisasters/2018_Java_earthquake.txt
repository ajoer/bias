[CLS] On 23 January 2018, at 13:34:50 Western Indonesian Time (06:34:54 UTC), an earthquake struck the Indonesian island of Java near the regency of Lebak. [SEP] [CLS] The earthquake, measured 5.9 on the Mww, occurred approximately 40 kilometres south of the village of Binuangeun at a depth of 43.9 kilometres. [SEP] [CLS] The earthquake was categorized as a strong and shallow earthquake.Strong shaking were widely reported across Banten, Lampung, West Java, Central Java and Jakarta. [SEP] [CLS] The tremor in Jakarta was much harder than in other recent quakes. [SEP] [CLS] By the virtue of its distance from the Indo-Australian plate, the capital normally does not experience strong tremors. [SEP] [CLS] Two people were indirectly killed by the earthquake.
