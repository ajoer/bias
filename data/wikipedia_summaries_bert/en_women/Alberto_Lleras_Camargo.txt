Alberto Lleras Camargo (3 July 1906 – 4 January 1990) was the 20th President of Colombia (1958–1962), and the 1st Secretary General of the Organization of American States (1948–1954). [CLS] [SEP] A journalist and liberal party politician, he also served as Minister of Government, Minister of Foreign Affairs, and as Minister of National Education in the administrations of President Alfonso López Pumarejo. [CLS] [SEP] He briefly attended the National University of Colombia in Bogotá to study politics, but dropped out later to pursue journalism. [CLS] [SEP] Lleras Camargo served as congressman of Colombia. [CLS] [SEP] He was also a cousin of later president Carlos Lleras Restrepo. [CLS] [SEP] He died in 1990 after suffering a long illness.
