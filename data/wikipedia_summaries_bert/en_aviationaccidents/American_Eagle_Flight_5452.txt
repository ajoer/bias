[CLS] American Eagle Flight 5452 was a commuter flight between Luis Muñoz Marín International Airport in San Juan, Puerto Rico and Eugenio María de Hostos Airport in Mayagüez. [SEP] [CLS] The flight was operated by Executive Airlines, doing business as American Eagle, and was operated by a CASA C-212 Aviocar aircraft. [SEP] [CLS] Visual meteorological conditions were present as the plane made its final approach to runway 9 at Mayagüez on May 8, 1987. [SEP] [CLS] The plane crashed 600 feet short of the runway, destroying the aircraft and killing both pilots, but leaving the four passengers with only minor injuries. [SEP] [CLS]
