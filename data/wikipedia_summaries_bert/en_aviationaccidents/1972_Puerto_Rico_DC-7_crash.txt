The 1972 Puerto Rico DC-7 crash was an aviation accident that occurred on December 31, 1972, in Carolina, Puerto Rico. [CLS] [SEP] It is most notable for killing Pittsburgh Pirates star Roberto Clemente. [CLS] [SEP] As a result of inadequate maintenance, the aircraft's No. [CLS] [SEP] 2 engine failed after takeoff. [CLS] [SEP] After initiating a turn to return to the airport, the aircraft eventually descended into, or attempted to ditch into, the ocean a mile offshore. [CLS] [SEP] All five people on board died.
