[CLS] Pauline Betz Addie (née Pauline May Betz, August 6, 1919 – May 31, 2011) was an American professional tennis player. [SEP] [CLS] She won five Grand Slam singles titles and was the runner-up on three other occasions. [SEP] [CLS] Jack Kramer has called her the second best female tennis player he ever saw, behind Helen Wills Moody. [SEP] [CLS]
