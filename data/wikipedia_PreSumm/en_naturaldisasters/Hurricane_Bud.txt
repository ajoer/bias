[CLS] The name Bud has been used for eight tropical cyclones in the Eastern Pacific Ocean. [SEP] [CLS] Tropical Storm Bud (1978), did not affect land. [SEP] [CLS] Tropical Storm Bud (1982), weak, short-lived tropical storm. [SEP] [CLS] Tropical Storm Bud (1988), dissipated near the south Mexican coastline. [SEP] [CLS] Tropical Storm Bud (1994), weak, short-lived tropical storm. [SEP] [CLS] Tropical Storm Bud (2000), paralleled the southwest coast of Mexico. [SEP] [CLS] Hurricane Bud (2006), a Category 3 hurricane that did not affect land. [SEP] [CLS] Hurricane Bud (2012), a Category 3 hurricane that approached Western Mexico. [SEP] [CLS] Hurricane Bud (2018), a Category 4 hurricane that brushed the Baja California Peninsula as a tropical storm. [SEP] [CLS]
