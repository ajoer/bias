[SEP] [CLS] This was Arkansas' deadliest tornado outbreak since May 15, 1968, when 34 were killed in Jonesboro. [SEP] [CLS] Severe flooding also occurred across the Ohio and Tennessee Valleys, resulting in 16 Ohio counties and 44 Kentucky counties being declared disaster areas. [SEP] [CLS] The flash floods and damaging wind elsewhere caused 34 deaths across six states including 19 in Kentucky, five in Ohio, five in Tennessee, two in Texas and three in West Virginia. [SEP] [CLS] Damage estimates were about $1 billion (1997 USD) while 75,000 homes were damaged. [SEP] [CLS]
