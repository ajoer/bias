Cargolux Flight 7933 was a cargo flight which was involved in a serious incident on 21 January 2010 in which it landed on a vehicle that was on an active runway. [CLS] [SEP] The vehicle suffered major damage while the aircraft had damage to a tire. [CLS] [SEP] Three investigations were launched into the incident. [CLS] [SEP] The cause was found to be errors by Air Traffic Control. [CLS] [SEP]
