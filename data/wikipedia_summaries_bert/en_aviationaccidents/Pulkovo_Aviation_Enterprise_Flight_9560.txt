[CLS] Pulkovo Aviation Enterprise Flight 9560 was a repositioning flight from Sheremetyevo International Airport in Moscow to Pulkovo Airport in St. [SEP] [CLS] Petersburg. [SEP] [CLS] On 28 July 2002, the Ilyushin Il-86 aircraft operating the flight crashed after take-off from Sheremetyevo. [SEP] [CLS] 14 of the 16 crew members on board (and the only occupants of the aircraft) were killed. [SEP] [CLS] This is the deadliest aviation accident involving the Ilyushin Il-86. [SEP] [CLS]
