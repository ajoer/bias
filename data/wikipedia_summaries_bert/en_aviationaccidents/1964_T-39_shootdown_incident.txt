[CLS] On 28 January 1964, an unarmed T-39 Sabreliner aircraft of the United States Air Force was shot down while on a training mission over Erfurt, East Germany, by a MiG-19 jet fighter of the Soviet Air Force. [SEP] [CLS] The occupants of the aircraft were Lieutenant Colonel Gerald K. [SEP] [CLS] Hannaford, Captain Donald Grant Millard and Captain John F. [SEP] [CLS] Lorraine. [SEP] [CLS] All three died, becoming direct casualties of the Cold War in Europe. [SEP] [CLS]
