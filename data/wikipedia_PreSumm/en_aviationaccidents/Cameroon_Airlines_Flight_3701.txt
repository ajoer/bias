[CLS] Cameroon Airlines Flight 3701 was an air accident that occurred on 3 December 1995. [SEP] [CLS] The Boeing 737-200, registration TJ-CBE, crashed after it lost control near Douala, Cameroon. [SEP] [CLS] On its second approach to Douala International Airport power was lost to one engine. [SEP] [CLS] The accident killed 71 passengers and crew and five people were injured but survived.
