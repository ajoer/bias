[CLS] The 1931 Myitkyina earthquake, or also known as the 1931 Kamaing earthquake, occurred on January 28 at 02:35 local time (20:9 January 27 UTC). [SEP] [CLS] It was located in northern Burma, then part of British India. [SEP] [CLS] The magnitude of this earthquake was put at Mw 7.6. [SEP] [CLS] According to some sources the depth was 35 km, and according to a study of Phyo M. [SEP] [CLS] M. [SEP] [CLS] the depth was 5 to 30 km.The shock was very violent and lasted at least 30 seconds. [SEP] [CLS] The intensity reached MMI IX. [SEP] [CLS] There were numerous fissures and cracks. [SEP] [CLS] Sand blows were reported. [SEP] [CLS] The earthquake may have been caused by slip along the Sagaing Fault. [SEP] [CLS] The Sagaing Fault is a continental transform fault between the India Plate and the Sunda Plate. [SEP] [CLS] This earthquake is located along the northern Sagaing Fault. [SEP] [CLS] Sagaing Fault at 22° N is narrow, about 10 km wide. [SEP] [CLS] The part of Sagaing Fault between 25°30' and 26° is wider, with a shear zone about 70 km wide, and has four branches identified. [SEP] [CLS]
