[CLS] CAAC Flight 2311 was a scheduled passenger flight from Changsha Datuopu Airport, in Changsha, the capital of Hunan Province, China, to the former Guangzhou Baiyun International Airport. [SEP] [CLS] On 24 December 1982 it was flown by an Ilyushin Il-18B (registered in China as B-202). [SEP] [CLS] After landing at Baiyun Airport, a fire in the cabin produced toxic smoke whereupon the crew stopped the aircraft on the runway and evacuated the passengers. [SEP] [CLS] The fast-developing fire killed 25 passengers and seriously injured 22 passengers and four crew members. [SEP] [CLS] The fire, which was started by a passenger's cigarette, destroyed the aircraft. [SEP] [CLS]
