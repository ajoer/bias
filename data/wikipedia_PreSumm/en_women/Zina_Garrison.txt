[CLS] Zina Lynna Garrison (born November 16, 1963) is a former top five professional tennis player from the United States. [SEP] [CLS] She was the women's singles runner-up at Wimbledon in 1990, a three-time Grand Slam mixed doubles champion, and a women's doubles gold medalist and singles bronze medalist in Seoul at the 1988 Olympic Games.
