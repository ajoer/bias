[CLS] Pan Am Flight 806 was an international scheduled flight from Auckland, New Zealand, to Los Angeles, California, with intermediate stops at Pago Pago, American Samoa and Honolulu, Hawaii. [SEP] [CLS] On January 30, 1974, the Boeing 707 Clipper Radiant crashed on approach to Pago Pago International Airport, killing 87 passengers and ten crew members. [SEP] [CLS] The National Transportation Safety Board (NTSB) determined the probable cause of the accident was the flight crew's tardy identification of microburst-induced wind shear. [SEP] [CLS] Other factors included poor visibility and a lack of altitude and airspeed callouts by the aircrew. [SEP] [CLS]
