[CLS] The 1886 Charleston earthquake occurred about 9:50 p.m. [SEP] [CLS] local time August 31 with an estimated moment magnitude of 6.9–7.3 and a maximum Mercalli intensity of X (Extreme). [SEP] [CLS] The intraplate earthquake caused 60 deaths and $5–6 million ($156.51 million in 2019) in damage to 2,000 buildings in the Southeastern United States. [SEP] [CLS] It is one of the most powerful and damaging earthquakes to hit the East Coast of the United States. [SEP] [CLS] Very little to no historical earthquake activity had occurred, which is unusual for any seismic area. [SEP] [CLS]
