[CLS] Transbrasil Flight 303 was a flight from Congonhas-São Paulo Airport in São Paulo, Brazil, to Hercílio Luz International Airport in Florianópolis, Brazil, on April 12, 1980. [SEP] [CLS] It crashed on approach to Hercílio Luz International Airport. [SEP] [CLS] Only three of the 58 people on board survived. [SEP] [CLS]
