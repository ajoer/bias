[CLS] Patrick Yvonne Hugo Dewael, (Dutch: [ˈpɑtrɪg dəˈʋaːl]; born 13 October 1955 in Lier, Belgium) is a liberal Belgian politician. [SEP] [CLS] He is a member of the Flemish Liberals and Democrats, or Vlaamse Liberalen en Democraten (VLD). [SEP] [CLS] He is the nephew of the late liberal minister Herman Vanderpoorten and the cousin of Marleen Vanderpoorten, who also served as Flemish minister of Education in the government led by Dewael. [SEP] [CLS] He obtained a degree in law and notariat from the Vrije Universiteit Brussel (Brussels, Belgium). [SEP] [CLS] Currently, he serves as the President of the Chamber of Representatives, a position he also held from 2008 to 2010. [SEP] [CLS]
