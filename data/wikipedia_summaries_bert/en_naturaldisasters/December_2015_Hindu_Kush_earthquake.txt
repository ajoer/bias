The December 2015 Hindu Kush earthquake occurred with a moment magnitude of 6.3 in South Asia on 25 December 2015. [CLS] [SEP] One woman was killed in Pakistan. [CLS] [SEP] At least 100 people were injured in Pakistan and Afghanistan. [CLS] [SEP] The quake was also strongly felt in Tajikistan and India. [CLS] [SEP] The epicenter of the earthquake was in the Afghanistan-Tajikistan border region at a depth of 203.4 km. [CLS] [SEP]
