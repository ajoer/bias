[CLS] The 1952 Hasankale earthquake occurred at 08:03 local time on 3 January in Hasankale (today Pasinler) in Erzurum Province, Eastern Anatolia Region of Turkey. [SEP] [CLS] The earthquake had an estimated magnitude of 5.8 and a maximum felt intensity of VIII (Severe) on the Mercalli intensity scale, causing 41 casualties. [SEP] [CLS] This spot has been the subject of studies due to the amount of earthquakes that occur in Turkey. [SEP] [CLS] 17% of earthquakes, globally, occur in this area.
