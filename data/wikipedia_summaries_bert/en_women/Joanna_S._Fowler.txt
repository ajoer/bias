[CLS] Joanna Sigfred Fowler (born August 9, 1942) is a scientist emeritus at the U.S. [SEP] [CLS] Department of Energy's Brookhaven National Laboratory in New York. [SEP] [CLS] She served as professor of psychiatry at Mount Sinai School of Medicine and director of Brookhaven's Radiotracer Chemistry, Instrumentation and Biological Imaging Program. [SEP] [CLS] Fowler studied the effect of disease, drugs, and aging on the human brain and radiotracers in brain chemistry. [SEP] [CLS] She has received many awards for her pioneering work, including the National Medal of Science. [SEP] [CLS]
