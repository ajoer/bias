[SEP] [CLS] Emperor Ming was a hard-working, able administrator of the empire who showed integrity and demanded integrity from his officials. [SEP] [CLS] He also established the control of the Chinese Empire on the Tarim Basin and eradicated the Xiongnu influence there, through the conquests of his general Ban Chao. [SEP] [CLS] The reigns of Emperor Ming and his son Emperor Zhang were typically considered the golden age of the Eastern Han Empire and known as the Rule of Ming and Zhang. [SEP] [CLS]
