[CLS] The 1626 Girifalco earthquake occurred on April 5 at 12:45. [SEP] [CLS] It was the strongest earthquake in a sequence that lasted from March 27 through to October of that year. [SEP] [CLS] It had an estimated magnitude of 6.0 Me and a maximum perceived intensity of X (extreme) on the Modified Mercalli scale. [SEP] [CLS] It caused widespread destruction in Girifalco and Catanzaro, then part of the Kingdom of Naples. [SEP] [CLS] There is no precise estimate for the number of casualties, but it is thought to lie in the range 11-100.
