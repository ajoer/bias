[CLS] Lene Løseth (born 26 November 1986 in Ålesund) is a Norwegian alpine skier who races for Spjelkavik IL. [SEP] [CLS] At the WC 2007 in Åre she came 38th in the giant slalom and 23rd in the slalom. [SEP] [CLS] She is Mona and Nina Løseth's elder sister. [SEP] [CLS] In a World Cup competition in slalom in Flachau in January 2010 all three sisters qualified for the final. [SEP] [CLS]
