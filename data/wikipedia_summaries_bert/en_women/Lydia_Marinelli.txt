[CLS] Lydia Marinelli (15 July 1965 - 8 September 2008) was an Austrian historian, editor, academic author and curator. [SEP] [CLS] She also displayed originality and flair as an exhibition organiser. [SEP] [CLS] Her speciality was the history of psychoanalysis. [SEP] [CLS] The most public aspect of her career involved her work as curator of the Sigmund Freud Museum in Vienna.Lydia Marinelli died young. [SEP] [CLS] Her suicide was followed by heartfelt tributes from fellow scholars. [SEP] [CLS] "Her death", wrote Michael Hagner "... [SEP] [CLS] at the age of just forty-three has robbed us of one of the most original humanities scholars ("Geisteswissenschaftlerinnen") of her generation". [SEP] [CLS]
