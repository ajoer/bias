[CLS] Shanghai Hongqiao International Airport (IATA: SHA, ICAO: ZSSS) is one of two international airports of Shanghai and an important airline hub of China. [SEP] [CLS] Hongqiao Airport mainly serves domestic and regional flights, although the airport also serves international flights. [SEP] [CLS] The airport is located near the town of Hongqiao in Changning District and Minhang District, 13 kilometres (8.1 mi) west of downtown, and is closer to the city center than the area's primary international airport, Shanghai Pudong. [SEP] [CLS] Hongqiao Airport is a hub for China Eastern Airlines, Shanghai Airlines, Juneyao Airlines and Spring Airlines. [SEP] [CLS] In 2016, Hongqiao Airport handled 40,460,135 passengers, making it the 7th busiest airport in China and the 45th busiest in the world. [SEP] [CLS] By the end of 2011 Hongqiao Airport hosted 22 airlines serving 82 scheduled passenger destinations. [SEP] [CLS] Shanghai Hongqiao Airport was also certified with the Skytrax 5-Star Airport Rating for facilities, terminal comfort and cleanliness, shopping, food & beverages and staff service in 2019.
