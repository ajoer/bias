Adrien Rabiot-Provost (French pronunciation: ​[adʁijɛ̃ ʁabjo pʁɔvo]; born 3 April 1995) is a French professional footballer who plays as a central midfielder for Italian Serie A club Juventus. [CLS] [SEP] Rabiot has spent most of his career with Paris Saint-Germain, making his debut with the first team in 2012 and winning 18 major honours, including five Ligue 1 titles and a domestic treble in 2015–16 and 2017–18. [CLS] [SEP] In 2019, Rabiot signed for Juventus on a free transfer, winning the Serie A title during his first season with the club. [CLS] [SEP] Rabiot was capped 53 times for France at youth level, and made his debut for the senior team in 2016. [CLS] [SEP]
