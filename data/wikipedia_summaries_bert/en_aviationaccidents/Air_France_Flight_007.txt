Air France Flight 007 crashed on 3 June 1962 while on take-off from Orly Airport. [CLS] [SEP] The only survivors of the disaster were two flight attendants; the rest of the flight crew, and all 122 passengers on board the Boeing 707, were killed. [CLS] [SEP] The crash was at the time the worst single-aircraft disaster and the first single civilian jet airliner disaster with more than 100 deaths. [CLS] [SEP]
