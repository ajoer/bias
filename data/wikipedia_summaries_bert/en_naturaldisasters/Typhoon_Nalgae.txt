The name Nalgae has been used to name three tropical cyclones in the northwestern Pacific Ocean. [CLS] [SEP] It was submitted by North Korea and it means a wing. [CLS] [SEP] Tropical Storm Nalgae (2005) (T0506, 06W) Typhoon Nalgae (2011) (T1119, 22W, Quiel) Tropical Storm Nalgae (2017) (T1711, 13W)
