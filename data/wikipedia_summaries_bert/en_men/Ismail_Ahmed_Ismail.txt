Ismail Ahmed Ismail (born 1 November 1984) is a Sudanese runner, who specializes in the 800 metres. [CLS] [SEP] He was born in Khartoum. [CLS] [SEP] Ismail reached the final in that event at the 2004 Summer Olympics in Athens. [CLS] [SEP] He competed at the 2008 Olympic Games in Beijing, where he won the silver medal in 800 m. [CLS] [SEP] He is the first Sudanese national to win an Olympic medal in history. [CLS] [SEP] He won the gold medal at the 2003 Afro-Asian Games. [CLS] [SEP] Ismail is a member of the Fur people of Darfur. [CLS] [SEP] Although Ismail was born in Khartoum, where he grew up in the neighborhood of Hajj Youssef, his family was originally from western Darfur. [CLS] [SEP]
