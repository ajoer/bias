[CLS] The 1997 Pacific hurricane season was a very active hurricane season. [SEP] [CLS] With hundreds of deaths and hundreds of millions of dollars in damage, this season was one of the costliest and deadliest Pacific hurricane seasons. [SEP] [CLS] This was due to the exceptionally strong 1997–98 El Niño event. [SEP] [CLS] The 1997 Pacific hurricane season officially started on May 15, 1997, in the eastern Pacific, and on June 1, 1997, in the central Pacific, and lasted until November 30, 1997. [SEP] [CLS] These dates conventionally delimit the period of each year when almost all tropical cyclones form in the northeastern Pacific Ocean. [SEP] [CLS] Several storms impacted land. [SEP] [CLS] The first was Tropical Storm Andres which killed four people and left another two missing. [SEP] [CLS] In August, Tropical Storm Ignacio took an unusual path, and its extratropical remnants caused minor damage in the Pacific Northwest and California. [SEP] [CLS] Linda became the most intense east Pacific hurricane in recorded history, a record it maintained until it was surpassed by Hurricane Patricia in 2015. [SEP] [CLS] Although it never made landfall, it produced large surf in Southern California and as a result five people had to be rescued. [SEP] [CLS] Hurricane Nora caused flooding and damage in the Southwestern United States, while Olaf made two landfalls and caused eighteen deaths and several other people were reported missing. [SEP] [CLS] Hurricane Pauline killed several hundred people and caused record damage in southeastern Mexico. [SEP] [CLS] In addition, Super Typhoons Oliwa and Paka originated in the region before crossing the International Date Line and causing significant damage in the western Pacific. [SEP] [CLS] There were also two Category 5 hurricanes: Linda and Guillermo. [SEP] [CLS] Activity in the season was above average. [SEP] [CLS] The season produced 17 named storms, which was a little above normal. [SEP] [CLS] The average number of named storms per year is 15. [SEP] [CLS] The 1997 season also had 9 hurricanes, compared to the average of 8. [SEP] [CLS] There were also 7 major hurricanes compared to the average of 4. [SEP] [CLS]
