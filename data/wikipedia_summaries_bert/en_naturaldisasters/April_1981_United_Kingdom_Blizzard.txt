The April 1981 United Kingdom snow storm was an exceptionally late snow event that primarily affected Great Britain between 23 and 26 April 1981. [CLS] [SEP] The snow event was of particular note for its lateness in the season and its intensity. [CLS] [SEP] Also associated with the pressure system was low temperatures. [CLS] [SEP] On the night of the 23rd, the temperature at Dalwhinnie fell to −11.0 °C (12.2 °F). [CLS] [SEP]
