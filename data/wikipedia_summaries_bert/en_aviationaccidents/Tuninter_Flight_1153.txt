[CLS] Tuninter Flight 1153 (UG1153/TUX1153) was a Tuninter Airlines international flight from Bari International Airport in Bari, Italy, to Djerba-Zarzis Airport in Djerba, Tunisia. [SEP] [CLS] On 6 August 2005, the Tuninter ATR 72 ditched into the Mediterranean Sea about 18 miles (29 km) from the city of Palermo. [SEP] [CLS] Sixteen of the 39 people on board died. [SEP] [CLS] The accident resulted from fuel exhaustion due to the installation of fuel quantity indicators designed for the ATR 42, in the larger ATR 72. [SEP] [CLS] It was also Tuninter's first fatal accident in the 14-year history of the company. [SEP] [CLS]
