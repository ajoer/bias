[CLS] Éver Maximiliano David Banega (Spanish pronunciation: [ˈeβeɾ βaˈneɣa]; born 29 June 1988) is an Argentine professional footballer who plays for Spanish club Sevilla and the Argentina national team as a central midfielder. [SEP] [CLS] He started his career with Boca Juniors, and signed with Valencia in 2008 where he remained for several years, appearing in 162 official games and winning the 2008 Copa del Rey. [SEP] [CLS] After joining Sevilla in 2014, he conquered consecutive Europa League trophies. [SEP] [CLS] Banega represented Argentina at the 2018 World Cup. [SEP] [CLS] Additionally, he won an Olympic medal in 2008 and appeared in three Copa América tournaments, helping the team to the final of the 2015 and 2016 editions. [SEP] [CLS]
