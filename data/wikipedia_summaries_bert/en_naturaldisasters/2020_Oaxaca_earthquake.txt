An earthquake struck the Mexican state of Oaxaca at 10:29 local time on June 23, 2020, with a magnitude of 7.4 Mw. [CLS] [SEP] The epicenter was 19 miles (31 km) from San Miguel del Puerto and 7.5 miles (12.1 km) south-southwest of Santa María Zapotitlán. [CLS] [SEP] The quake was felt by an estimated 49 million people in Mexico and Guatemala, with some tremors felt as far away as 640 kilometres (400 mi). [CLS] [SEP] Thousands of houses in Oaxaca were damaged and 10 deaths were reported. [CLS] [SEP] A tsunami warning was issued for southern Mexico, El Salvador, Guatemala, and Honduras. [CLS] [SEP]
