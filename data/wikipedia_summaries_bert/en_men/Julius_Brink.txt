[CLS] [SEP] In 2009 he and his current partner Jonas Reckermann won four FIVB competitions (three consecutive Grand Slams) and the German Masters of the CEV European Championship Tour. [CLS] [SEP] This includes the 2009 FIVB World Championship, held from 26 June to 5 July in Stavanger, Norway, beating top seeded Harley/Alison in the final and former FIVB world champions and gold medalist of the 2008 Summer Olympics Rogers/Dalhausser in the semi final. [CLS] [SEP] They are the first German and European team ever to win a world championship title and are currently ranked 22nd on the FIVB World Tour (as of 23 July 2012). [CLS] [SEP] On 9 August 2012, Brink and his teammate Jonas Reckermann won the gold medal at the 2012 London Olympics. [CLS] [SEP] With this victory Germany become the first European country to win the Beach Volleyball in Olympics. [CLS] [SEP]
