[CLS] Tauno Olavi Käyhkö (born May 6, 1950 in Rovaniemi) is a Finnish-Canadian former ski jumper who competed from 1970 to 1980. [SEP] [CLS] At the 1972 Winter Olympics in Sapporo, he finished fourth in the individual large hill event. [SEP] [CLS] Käyhkö's best finish at the FIS Nordic World Ski Championships was fifth twice in the individual large hill events (1970, 1978). [SEP] [CLS] His best career finish was in an individual normal hill event in West Germany in 1972. [SEP] [CLS]
