[CLS] Zinaida Greceanîi (born 7 February 1956;) is a Moldovan politician. [SEP] [CLS] She is currently Speaker of the Parliament of Moldova since 8 June 2019. [SEP] [CLS] Greceanîi was previously also the Prime Minister of Moldova from 31 March 2008 to 14 September 2009. [SEP] [CLS] She is the leader of the Party of Socialists of the Republic of Moldova (PSRM) and was previously a member of the Party of Communists of the Republic of Moldova (PCRM). [SEP] [CLS] She was Moldova's first female Prime Minister and was the second female Communist head of government in Europe, the first having been Premier Milka Planinc of Yugoslavia. [SEP] [CLS]
