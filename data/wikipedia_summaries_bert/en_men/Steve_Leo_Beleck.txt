[CLS] Steve Leo Beleck A'Beka (born 21 February 1993) is a Cameroonian professional footballer who plays as a striker for Italian club Catania. [SEP] [CLS] Beleck began his career at Greek team Panthrakikos, breaking into the first team at age 16. [SEP] [CLS] Following a successful second half to the 2009–10 season, Beleck joined Udinese of the Italian Serie A for an undisclosed fee, signing a five-year deal in July 2010. [SEP] [CLS] A year later, Beleck returned to Greece, this time joining AEK Athens on a season-long loan. [SEP] [CLS] He played regularly, and returned to his parent club at the end of the 2011–12 season. [SEP] [CLS] A month after returning to his parent club, Beleck was loaned out for a second time, joining English Championship side Watford on a one-year loan agreement. [SEP] [CLS] In January 2013, Beleck was re-loaned from Watford to Stevenage for the remainder of the 2012–13 season. [SEP] [CLS] In August 2013, Beleck was signed on a season-long loan for RAEC Mons but returned to Udinese in January 2014. [SEP] [CLS]
