TransAsia Airways Flight 791 was a regular cargo flight between Taiwan Taoyuan International Airport and Macau International Airport. [CLS] [SEP] At 01:52 AM local time on 21 December 2002, the ATR 72 operating the flight crashed into the sea 17 kilometres (11 mi; 9.2 nmi) southwest of Magong, Penghu, Taiwan. [CLS] [SEP] The two crew members on board were killed. [CLS] [SEP]
