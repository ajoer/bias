[CLS] Tropical Storm Vamco was a weak tropical cyclone which affected Indochina in mid-September 2015. [SEP] [CLS] Formed from a tropical disturbance on September 13, the system developed into a tropical storm and reached its peak intensity on September 14. [SEP] [CLS] Vamco made landfall in Vietnam and affected Laos, Thailand and Cambodia. [SEP] [CLS] The storm caused flooding in these countries and damages amounted to US$14.1 million. [SEP] [CLS] Fifteen people died in the floods. [SEP] [CLS] In Vietnam, Vamco was known as known as Cơn bão số 3 năm 2015 (3rd storm in 2015). [SEP] [CLS] Heavy rains caused by the storm in Central Vietnam ended the drought in this area, after many hot days because of the strong El Niño. [SEP] [CLS]
