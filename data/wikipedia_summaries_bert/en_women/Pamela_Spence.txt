Pamela Aslı Spence (born February 23, 1973) is a Turkish pop-rock singer. [CLS] [SEP] Born to an American father and a Turkish mother, she sings in Turkish, which she learned at the age of 15 She has also collaborated with a number of other Turkish singers including Teoman. [CLS] [SEP] She has also performed in several musicals.Spence studied Theater Arts at the Ankara State Conservatory. [CLS] [SEP] She was first noticed by critics in a musical version of They Shoot Horses, Don't They? [CLS] [SEP] staged in Turkey, where she had acted alongside actors Okan Bayülgen and Fikret Kuşkan. [CLS] [SEP] She then starred in TV miniseries such as Lahmacun ve Pizza, and took part in Teoman's group of vocalists. [CLS] [SEP] Her first album, Eğer Dinlersen, is the product of a long period of preparation. [CLS] [SEP] With the songs in her second album, Şehir Rehberi, she used a style that was closer to rock music, as opposed to the pop notes of her first album. [CLS] [SEP] One of the songs from her second album, İstanbul, attracted particular public attention with its touching and easily memorizable lyrics. [CLS] [SEP] In May 2006, she produced her third album, Cehennet. [CLS] [SEP]
