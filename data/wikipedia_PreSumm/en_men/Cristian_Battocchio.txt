[CLS] Cristian Damián Battocchio (Italian pronunciation: [ˈkristjam batˈtɔkkjo]; born 10 February 1992) is an Argentine-born Italian professional footballer who plays as a midfielder for French club Stade Brestois 29. [SEP] [CLS] Battocchio is a central midfielder and deep-lying playmaker. [SEP] [CLS]
