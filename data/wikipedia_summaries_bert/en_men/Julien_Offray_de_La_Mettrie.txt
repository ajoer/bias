[CLS] Julien Offray de La Mettrie (French: [ɔfʁɛ də la metʁi]; November 23, 1709 – November 11, 1751) was a French physician and philosopher, and one of the earliest of the French materialists of the Enlightenment. [SEP] [CLS] He is best known for his work L'homme machine (Machine Man). [SEP] [CLS]
