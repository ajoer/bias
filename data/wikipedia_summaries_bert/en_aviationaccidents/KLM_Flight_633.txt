KLM Flight 633 was a passenger flight from Amsterdam to New York City. [CLS] [SEP] On 5 September 1954, immediately after takeoff from Shannon Airport, the Super Constellation Triton ditched on a mudbank in the River Shannon. [CLS] [SEP] 28 people were killed in the accident. [CLS] [SEP] It was caused by an unexpected re-extension of the landing gear, possibly compounded by pilot error. [CLS] [SEP]
