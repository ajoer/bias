Princess Palatine Benedicta Henrietta (Benedicta Henrietta Philippina; 14 March 1652 – 12 August 1730) was Duchess of Brunswick-Lüneburg, or of Hanover, by her marriage to Duke John Frederick. [CLS] [SEP] She was the third and youngest daughter of Prince Palatine Edward and the political hostess Anna Gonzaga. [CLS] [SEP]
