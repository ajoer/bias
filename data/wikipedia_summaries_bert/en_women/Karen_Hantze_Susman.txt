Karen Susman (née Hantze; born December 11, 1942) is a retired female tennis player from the United States. [CLS] [SEP] She won the 1962 women's singles title at Wimbledon, defeating Věra Pužejová Suková in the final 6–4, 6–4, but did not defend her title in 1963. [CLS] [SEP] She won three Grand Slam women's doubles titles, all with Billie Jean King. [CLS] [SEP] She also won the 1960 Wimbledon junior girls' singles title. [CLS] [SEP]
