[SEP] [CLS] She was former Vice President for the Loyola Schools of the university. [SEP] [CLS] In 2006, she succeeded Professor Anna Miren Gonzales-Intal of the Department of Psychology to become the second person to hold the highest position in the Loyola Schools. [SEP] [CLS] She also served as the Acting Dean of the then School of Arts and Sciences and the School of Science and Engineering. [SEP] [CLS] In September 2009, she decided to step down from her position as the Loyola Schools Vice President.
