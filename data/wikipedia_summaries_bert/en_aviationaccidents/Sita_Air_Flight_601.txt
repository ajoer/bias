Sita Air Flight 601 (ST601) was a Nepalese domestic passenger flight, operated by Sita Air from Tribhuvan International Airport in Nepal's capital Kathmandu to Tenzing-Hillary Airport in Lukla. [CLS] [SEP] On 28 September 2012, the Dornier 228 serving the route crashed while attempting an emergency landing at Kathmandu shortly after take off, killing all 19 people on board. [CLS] [SEP] The final report, published by the Nepal's Aircraft Accident Investigation Commission, determined that a thrust reduction occurred during the take-off roll, but the cause of the anomaly could not be determined. [CLS] [SEP] Subsequent crew inability to maintain speed above stall speed, followed by the insufficient height of the aircraft, led to the aircraft crashing. [CLS] [SEP]
