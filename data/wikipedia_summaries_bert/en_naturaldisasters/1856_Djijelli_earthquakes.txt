The 1856 Djijelli earthquakes occurred on August 21 and 22 near the coastal area of northern Algeria. [CLS] [SEP] The magnitude of the two shocks are unknown, but each had a maximum Mercalli intensity of IX (Violent). [CLS] [SEP] Each of these high intensity shocks were felt as far as Genoa in northern Italy and were followed by a tsunami that affected the Mediterranean Sea. [CLS] [SEP] Three people were killed as a result of the second event. [CLS] [SEP]
