Alfred Kirwa Yego (born 28 November 1986 in Eldoret) is a Kenyan middle distance runner who specializes in the 800 metres. [CLS] [SEP] He is best known for winning the gold medal in the 800 m at the 2007 World Championships. [CLS] [SEP] Yego competed at the 2005 World Championships, but did not advance past 800 metres heats. [CLS] [SEP] His coach is Claudio Berardelli, who has also coached olympic medalists Janeth Jepkosgei and Nancy Lagat Yego won the silver at the 2009 World Championships in the 800 m. [CLS] [SEP] A few weeks afterwards, he improved his 800 m personal best to 1:42.67 min in Rieti, finishing second behind David Rudisha who ran a new African record. [CLS] [SEP]
