[CLS] Centurion Air Cargo Flight 164 was a chartered international cargo flight, flying from Bogota's El Dorado International Airport while en route to Miami International Airport. [SEP] [CLS] The flight was operated by Kalitta Air and the aircraft was wet leased by Centurion Cargo. [SEP] [CLS] On 7 July 2008, the aircraft, a Boeing 747-209BSF registered as N714CK, crashed shortly after takeoff. [SEP] [CLS] All aboard suffered injuries, but none were killed. [SEP] [CLS] Two people on the ground were killed after the plane slammed into a farm. [SEP] [CLS] The crash was the second crash of a Boeing 747 in 2008 in Kalitta Air service, after a previous accident at Brussels in May. [SEP] [CLS] An investigation was launched by Colombian authorities and concluded that the crash was caused by dual engine failures. [SEP] [CLS] During its take-off section, the engine number 4 and number 1 suffered loss of power. [SEP] [CLS] The flight crews could not recover the plane, which subsequently crashed into a ranch house. [SEP] [CLS]
