The 1692 Salta earthquake took place in the Province of Salta, in the Republic of Argentina on 13 September at 11:00 a.m. [CLS] [SEP] local time. [CLS] [SEP] It registered 7.0 on the Richter magnitude scale and was located at a depth of 30 kilometres (19 mi). [CLS] [SEP] Aftershocks continued to be felt until 15 September. [CLS] [SEP]
