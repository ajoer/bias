[CLS] American Airlines Flight 1 was a domestic, scheduled passenger flight from New York International (Idlewild) Airport (now John F. [SEP] [CLS] Kennedy International Airport) to Los Angeles International Airport. [SEP] [CLS] On March 1, 1962, the Boeing 707 rolled over and crashed into Jamaica Bay two minutes after takeoff, killing all 87 passengers and eight crew members aboard. [SEP] [CLS] A Civil Aeronautics Board (CAB) investigation determined that a manufacturing defect in the autopilot system led to an uncommanded rudder control system input, causing the accident. [SEP] [CLS] A number of notable people died in the crash. [SEP] [CLS] It was the fifth fatal Boeing 707 accident and, at the time, the deadliest. [SEP] [CLS]
