[CLS] Quentin Persifor Smith (born August 27, 1952, Rhinebeck, New York) is a professor emeritus of philosophy at Western Michigan University in Kalamazoo, Michigan. [SEP] [CLS] He has worked in the philosophy of time, philosophy of language, philosophy of physics and philosophy of religion. [SEP] [CLS] Smith has published over 140 articles. [SEP] [CLS] Of his published books, he has authored three, co-authored two, and co-authored and edited seven. [SEP] [CLS] He was an editor for Prometheus Books and was the chief editor for Philo from 2001 to 2007. [SEP] [CLS] He has debated William Lane Craig over the existence of God. [SEP] [CLS]
