Elisabeth Röhm (, German: [eˈliːzabɛt ˈʁøːm]; born April 28, 1973) is an German-American television and film actress. [CLS] [SEP] She is best known for playing Kate Lockley in the television series Angel from 1999 to 2001 and Serena Southerlyn in the television series Law & Order from 2001 to 2005. [CLS] [SEP] She has also appeared in films such as American Hustle (2014), Joy (2015) and Bombshell (2019). [CLS] [SEP]
