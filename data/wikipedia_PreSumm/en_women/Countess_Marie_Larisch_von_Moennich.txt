[CLS] Countess Marie Louise Larisch von Moennich (also known as Countess Marie Louise Larisch-Wallersee and Countess Marie Larisch) (24 February 1858 â€“ 4 July 1940) was the niece and confidante of Empress Elisabeth of Austria. [SEP] [CLS] She was a go-between for her married cousin Crown Prince Rudolf and his mistress Baroness Mary Vetsera, a friend of hers. [SEP] [CLS] A scandal known as the Mayerling Incident broke in 1889 upon the discovery of the bodies of the two lovers at Rudolf's hunting lodge at Mayerling, Austria. [SEP] [CLS] With the revelation of her role in this the Countess was shunned in particular by the Empress and the rest of the Imperial Family. [SEP] [CLS] As a consequence she was also snubbed by society. [SEP] [CLS] Later in life she was nominally the author of a series of ghostwritten books about the Imperial household. [SEP] [CLS]
