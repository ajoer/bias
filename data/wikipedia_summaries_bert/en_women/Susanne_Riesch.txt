Susanne Riesch (born 8 December 1987, in Garmisch-Partenkirchen, Bavaria, Germany) is a former alpine ski racer. [CLS] [SEP] She is the sister of Maria Höfl-Riesch, overall World Cup Champion for season 2010/2011. [CLS] [SEP] Riesch has won two podiums in the Alpine skiing world cup, both 3rd places in slalom during the 2009-2010 World Cup season (Åre, Sweden and Zagreb, Croatia). [CLS] [SEP] In January 2015, ten months after her sister's retirement, Susanne Riesch announced that she was retiring due to struggling with injuries. [CLS] [SEP]
