[CLS] Hannah Stockbauer (born 7 January 1982) is a World Champion, Olympic and national-record holding swimmer from Germany. [SEP] [CLS] In 2003, she was named the female World Swimmer of the Year by Swimming World Magazine, following her winning the 400, 800 and 1500 freestyles at the 2003 World Championships. [SEP] [CLS] She swam for Germany at the: Olympics: 2000, 2004 World Championships: 2001, 2003 European Championships: 1999, 2002 Short Course Europeans: 1998, 2002At the 2001 World Championships, she won the 800 and 1500 frees. [SEP] [CLS] At the 2003 World Championships, she was named Female Swimmer of the Meet, after she won 3 events (400, 800 and 1500 frees), setting meet records in the 800 and 1500 (8:23.66 and 16:00.18) and the German Record in the 1500.
