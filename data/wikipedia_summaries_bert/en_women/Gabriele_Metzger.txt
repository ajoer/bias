Charlotte 'Charlie' Schneider is a fictional character from the German soap opera Verbotene Liebe, portrayed by actress Gabriele Metzger. [CLS] [SEP] She made her first appearance on screen in the series premiere on 2 January 1995. [CLS] [SEP] After the departure of Konrad Krauss, who played Arno Brandner, Metzger is the only remaining original cast member. [CLS] [SEP]
