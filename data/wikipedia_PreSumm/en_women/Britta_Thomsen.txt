[CLS] Britta Thomsen (born 23 January 1954 in Aalborg) is a Danish politician who served as a Member of the European Parliament from 2004 until 2014. [SEP] [CLS] She is a member of the Social Democrats, which is part of the Party of European Socialists. [SEP] [CLS] In parliament, Thomsen served as vice-chair of the Committee on Industry, Research and Energy. [SEP] [CLS] She was also a member of the Committee on Women's Rights and Gender Equality, a substitute for the Committee on Development, and vice-chair of the delegation for relations with South Africa. [SEP] [CLS] She supported the Manifesto of the Spinelli Group. [SEP] [CLS]
