Hurricane How was the only tropical cyclone to make landfall on the United States in 1951. [CLS] [SEP] It was the eighth storm of the season, forming on September 28 in the northwest Caribbean and, after entering the Gulf of Mexico, turned eastward and became a tropical storm. [CLS] [SEP] The storm brought heavy rainfall to Florida, causing flooding and $2 million in damage. [CLS] [SEP] Tropical Storm How later became a hurricane, passing near the Outer Banks of North Carolina where it produced strong winds and high tides. [CLS] [SEP] A ship called the "Southern Isles" capsized off Cape Hatteras, killing 17 out of the crew of 23. [CLS] [SEP] Rains and high tides occurred in southeastern New England, before the hurricane moved out to sea and became extratropical. [CLS] [SEP]
