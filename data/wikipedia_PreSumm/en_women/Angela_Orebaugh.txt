[CLS] Angela Orebaugh is a cyber technology and security author and researcher. [SEP] [CLS] In 2011, she was selected as Booz Allen Hamilton's first Cybersecurity Fellow. [SEP] [CLS] She is an assistant professor at the University of Virginia School of Continuing and Professional Studies, and serves there as the program director for certificates in cybersecurity.
