[CLS] Calista Kay Flockhart (born November 11, 1964) is an American actress. [SEP] [CLS] On television, she is best known for her roles as the title character on Ally McBeal (1997–2002), Kitty Walker on Brothers & Sisters (2006–2011), and Cat Grant on Supergirl (2015–2019). [SEP] [CLS] In film, she is known for roles in The Birdcage (1996), A Midsummer Night's Dream (1999), and Things You Can Tell Just by Looking at Her (2000). [SEP] [CLS] Flockhart has received a Golden Globe Award and Screen Actors Guild Award, and been nominated for three Primetime Emmy Awards. [SEP] [CLS] She is married to actor Harrison Ford.
