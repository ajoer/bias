[CLS] Princess Feodora of Leiningen (Anna Feodora Auguste Charlotte Wilhelmine; 7 December 1807 – 23 September 1872) was the only daughter of Emich Carl, Prince of Leiningen (1763–1814), and Princess Victoria of Saxe-Coburg-Saalfeld (1786–1861). [SEP] [CLS] Feodora and her older brother Carl, 3rd Prince of Leiningen, were maternal half-siblings to Queen Victoria of the United Kingdom. [SEP] [CLS] She is a matrilineal ancestress of Carl XVI Gustaf of Sweden and of Felipe VI of Spain. [SEP] [CLS]
