[CLS] Sarah, Duchess of York (born Sarah Margaret Ferguson; 15 October 1959) is a member of the British royal family. [SEP] [CLS] She is the former wife of Prince Andrew, Duke of York, the second son of Queen Elizabeth II and Prince Philip, Duke of Edinburgh. [SEP] [CLS] They have two daughters, Princesses Beatrice and Eugenie. [SEP] [CLS] Although Sarah and Andrew divorced in 1996, she has remained friendly with both him and the Queen, despite a controversial business life. [SEP] [CLS] Sarah is also active as a writer, charity patron, film producer, and television personality. [SEP] [CLS]
