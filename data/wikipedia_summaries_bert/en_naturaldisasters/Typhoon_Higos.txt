[CLS] The name Higos has been used to name four tropical cyclones in the northwestern Pacific Ocean. [SEP] [CLS] The name was contributed by the United States of America, and means fig in the Chamorro language. [SEP] [CLS] Typhoon Higos (2002) (T0221, 25W) – struck Japan. [SEP] [CLS] Tropical Storm Higos (2008) (T0817, 21W, Pablo) Typhoon Higos (2015) (T1502, 02W) – an early strong typhoon of the 2015 season. [SEP] [CLS] Tropical Storm Higos (2020) (T2007, 08W, Helen)
