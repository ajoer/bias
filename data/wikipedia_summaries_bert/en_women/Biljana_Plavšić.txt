Biljana Plavšić (Serbian Cyrillic: Биљана Плавшић; born July 7, 1930) is a Bosnian Serb convicted war criminal and retired politician who served as president of Republika Srpska. [CLS] [SEP] She was indicted in 2001 by the International Criminal Tribunal for the former Yugoslavia (ICTY) for war crimes committed during the Bosnian War. [CLS] [SEP] She plea-bargained with the ICTY and was sentenced to 11 years in prison in 2003, to be served in a Swedish prison.She was released on 27 October 2009 after serving two-thirds of her sentence. [CLS] [SEP] Plavšić is, together with Radovan Karadžić, the highest ranking Bosnian Serb politician to be sentenced. [CLS] [SEP] Before entering politics, she taught biology at the University of Sarajevo. [CLS] [SEP]
