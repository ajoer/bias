FedEx Flight 1406 was an American domestic cargo flight from Memphis International Airport, Memphis, Tennessee, to Logan International Airport in Boston, Massachusetts, that suffered an in-flight cargo fire over New York on September 5, 1996. [CLS] [SEP] The three crew members and two passengers on board successfully evacuated after an emergency landing at Stewart International Airport in New Windsor, New York. [CLS] [SEP] After the evacuation, the DC-10 was consumed by fire. [CLS] [SEP] After an extensive investigation, the National Transportation Safety Board was unable to determine what caused the fire. [CLS] [SEP] Nevertheless, the Federal Aviation Administration made recommendations to prevent similar incidents from occurring in the future. [CLS] [SEP]
