[CLS] Flydubai Flight 981 was a scheduled international passenger flight from Dubai International Airport, in the United Arab Emirates, to Rostov-on-Don Airport, Russia. [SEP] [CLS] On 19 March 2016, the Boeing 737-800 aircraft serving the flight crashed during an aborted landing, resulting in the deaths of all 62 passengers and crew on board.The weather at Rostov was poor at the time, and the flight was at night. [SEP] [CLS] Flight 981 aborted its first landing attempt and went into a holding pattern for nearly two hours before making a second landing attempt. [SEP] [CLS] After aborting the second attempt, the aircraft climbed sharply during the go-around procedure, then descended rapidly and crashed onto the runway. [SEP] [CLS]
