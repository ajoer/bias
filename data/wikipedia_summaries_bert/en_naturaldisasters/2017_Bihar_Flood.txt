2017 Bihar floods affected 19 districts of North Bihar causing death of 514 people. [CLS] [SEP] 2,371 panchayats (settlement councils) under 187 blocks of 19 districts of Northern Bihar have been affected in the flood. [CLS] [SEP] Around 1.71 crore (17.1 million) people were hit by the floods. [CLS] [SEP] Over 8.5 lakhs of people have lost their homes, with Araria district alone accounting for 2.2 lakh homeless people. [CLS] [SEP] 2017 Flood has broken 9-Year record of deaths In Bihar. [CLS] [SEP] Bihar is India's most flood-prone State, with 76% of the population in the North Bihar living under the recurring threat of flood devastation. [CLS] [SEP] Devastating flood was caused due to excess heavy rainfall in monsoon season. [CLS] [SEP]
