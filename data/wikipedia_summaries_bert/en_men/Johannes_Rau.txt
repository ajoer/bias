Johannes Rau (German pronunciation: [joˈhanəs ˈʁaʊ]; 16 January 1931 – 27 January 2006) was a German politician (SPD). [CLS] [SEP] He was President of Germany from 1 July 1999 until 30 June 2004 and Minister President of North Rhine-Westphalia from 20 September 1978 to 9 June 1998. [CLS] [SEP] In the latter role, he also served as President of the Bundesrat in 1982/83 and in 1994/1995. [CLS] [SEP]
