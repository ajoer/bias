[CLS] Tropical Storm Fay was a strong and unusual tropical storm that moved erratically across the state of Florida and the Caribbean Sea. [SEP] [CLS] As it zigzagged from water to land, it became the first storm in recorded history to make landfall in Florida four times. [SEP] [CLS] Thirty-six deaths were blamed on Fay. [SEP] [CLS] The storm also resulted in one of the most prolific tropical cyclone related tornado outbreaks on record. [SEP] [CLS] A total of 81 tornadoes touched down across five states, three of which were rated as EF2. [SEP] [CLS] Damage from Fay was heavy, estimated at $560 million. [SEP] [CLS]
