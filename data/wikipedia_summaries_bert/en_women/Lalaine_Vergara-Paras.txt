[CLS] Lalaine Vergara-Paras (born June 3, 1987), known mononymously as Lalaine, is an American actress, singer-songwriter, and bassist. [SEP] [CLS] She is best known for her roles as Miranda Sanchez in Lizzie McGuire, Kate in Annie, and Abby Ramirez in You Wish!. [SEP] [CLS] After appearing in the roles of Young Cosette and Éponine in a Broadway musical production of Les Misérables as a child, Lalaine pursued a career in music. [SEP] [CLS] She signed with Warner Bros. [SEP] [CLS] Records following the end of Lizzie McGuire and released one studio album and one extended play, both of which saw little commercial success.
