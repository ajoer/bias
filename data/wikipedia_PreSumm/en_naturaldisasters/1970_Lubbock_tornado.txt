[CLS] The Lubbock tornado was a tornado event that occurred in Lubbock, Texas, on Monday, May 11, 1970. [SEP] [CLS] It was one of the worst tornadoes in Texas history, and occurred exactly 17 years to the day after the deadly Waco Tornado. [SEP] [CLS] It is also the most recent F5 tornado to have struck a central business district of a large city. [SEP] [CLS]
