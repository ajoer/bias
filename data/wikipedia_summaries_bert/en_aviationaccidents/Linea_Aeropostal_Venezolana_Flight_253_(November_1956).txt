Linea Aeropostal Flight 253 was being operated by a Lockheed L-749 Constellation, registration YV-C-AMA and named Jose Marti, on an international scheduled Passenger flight that took off from Idlewild International Airport (modern John F. [CLS] [SEP] Kennedy International Airport) bound for Caracas International Airport on November 27, 1956. [CLS] [SEP] The flight, piloted by French captain Marcel Combalbert, crashed into a mountain near Caracas, Venezuela. [CLS] [SEP] All 25 passengers and crew on board were killed. [CLS] [SEP]
