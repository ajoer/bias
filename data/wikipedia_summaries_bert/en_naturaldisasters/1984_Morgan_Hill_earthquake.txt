The 1984 Morgan Hill earthquake (also Halls Valley earthquake) occurred on April 24 at 1:15 p.m. [CLS] [SEP] local time in the Santa Clara Valley of Northern California. [CLS] [SEP] The shock had a moment magnitude of 6.2 and a maximum Mercalli intensity of VIII (Severe). [CLS] [SEP] The epicenter was located near Mount Hamilton in the Diablo Range of the California Coast Ranges. [CLS] [SEP] Nearby communities (including Morgan Hill) sustained serious damage with financial losses of at least US$7.5 million. [CLS] [SEP]
