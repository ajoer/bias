Airblue Flight 202 was a scheduled Airblue domestic passenger flight from Karachi's Jinnah International Airport to Islamabad's Benazir Bhutto International Airport. [CLS] [SEP] On 28 July 2010, the Airbus A321-231 narrow-body jet airliner serving the flight crashed near Islamabad, the capital of Pakistan, killing all 146 passengers and six crew on board. [CLS] [SEP] It is the deadliest air accident to occur in Pakistan to date. [CLS] [SEP] The aircraft crashed in the Margalla Hills north of Islamabad after air traffic controllers lost contact with the flight crew during their attempt to land in dense fog and heavy monsoon rain.The accident was the first fatal crash involving an Airbus A321, an extended variant of the Airbus A320 family of short to medium range airliners. [CLS] [SEP] Based in Islamabad, Airblue is Pakistan's second largest airline, with over 30% share of the domestic market. [CLS] [SEP] At the time of the accident the airline operated seven aircraft, all in the A320 family. [CLS] [SEP]
