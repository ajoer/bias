Julia Görges (born 2 November 1988) is a German professional tennis player. [CLS] [SEP] She has won seven singles and five doubles titles on the WTA Tour (her biggest title coming at the year-end 2017 WTA Elite Trophy), as well as six singles and six doubles titles on the ITF Women's Circuit. [CLS] [SEP] Görges turned professional in 2005, and first broke into the world's top 100 in June 2008. [CLS] [SEP] She reached her doubles peak of No. [CLS] [SEP] 12 on 22 August 2016. [CLS] [SEP] Prior to 2018, her best result in a Grand Slam singles tournament was reaching the fourth round five times. [CLS] [SEP] She broke into the top ten for the first time in February 2018, before going on to reach the semifinals at the 2018 Wimbledon Championships, and achieving her career-high singles ranking of No. [CLS] [SEP] 9 on 20 August 2018.
