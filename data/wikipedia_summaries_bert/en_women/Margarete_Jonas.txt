Margarete Jonas (née Towarek) (13 June 1898 in Vienna – 7 August 1976 in Vienna) was the wife of Franz Jonas, the former federal president of Austria. [CLS] [SEP] They married in 1922. [CLS] [SEP] Mrs Jonas spoke German, English and Czech. [CLS] [SEP] She is buried next to her husband in the presidential vault in the Wiener Zentralfriedhof. [CLS] [SEP]
