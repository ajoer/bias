[CLS] The 1983 Avensa Douglas DC-9 crash occurred on 11 March 1983, when an Avensa Douglas DC-9 airliner on a domestic flight from Caracas Airport to Barquisimeto Airport, Venezuela landed hard, went off of the runway, and exploded. [SEP] [CLS] Twenty-two passengers and one crew member died. [SEP] [CLS]
