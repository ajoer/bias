Lluís Llach i Grande (Catalan pronunciation: [ʎuˈiz ˈʎak]; born 7 May 1948) is a Spanish singer-songwriter from Catalonia. [CLS] [SEP] He is one of the main representatives of the nova cançó genre and an outspoken advocate of the right to self-determination of Catalonia. [CLS] [SEP] His most famous song, "L'Estaca", has become the unofficial anthem of the Catalan independence movement. [CLS] [SEP] He was a member of the Catalan Parliament from September 2015 until January 2018. [CLS] [SEP]
