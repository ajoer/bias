[CLS] Alexei Mikhailovich (Russian: Алексе́й Миха́йлович, IPA: [ɐlʲɪkˈsʲej mʲɪˈxajləvʲɪtɕ]; 19 March [O.S. [SEP] [CLS] 9 March] 1629 – 8 February [O.S. [SEP] [CLS] 29 January] 1676) was the Tsar of Russia from 1645 until his death in 1676. [SEP] [CLS] His reign saw wars with Poland and Sweden, schism in the Russian Orthodox Church, and the major Cossack revolt of Stenka Razin. [SEP] [CLS] Nevertheless, at the time of his death Russia spanned almost 2,000,000,000 acres (8,100,000 km2). [SEP] [CLS]
