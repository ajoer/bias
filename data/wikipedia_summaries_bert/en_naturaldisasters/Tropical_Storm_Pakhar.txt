The name Matsa has been used to name one tropical cyclone in the northwestern Pacific Ocean. [CLS] [SEP] It refers to mermaids and was submitted by Laos. [CLS] [SEP] Typhoon Matsa (2005) (T0509, 09W, Gorio) – impacted China, causing about $2.2 billion in damagesThe name Matsa was retired after the season and was replaced with the name Pakhar, which refers to the Irrawaddy dolphins found in the Mekong River. [CLS] [SEP] Tropical Storm Pakhar (2012) (T1201, 02W) – struck Vietnam. [CLS] [SEP] Severe Tropical Storm Pakhar (2017) (T1714, 16W, Jolina) – impacted South China. [CLS] [SEP]
