[CLS] Sadeh (Persian: سده‎) or Jashn-e Sadeh (Persian: جشن سده‎), is an Iranian festival celebrating the discovery of fire by Hushang, the second Shah of the mythological Pishdadian dynasty of Persian Empire. [SEP] [CLS] It is celebrated fifty days before the arrival of Nowruz in Iran and parts of Afghanistan and Tajikistan since the Achaemenid Empire. [SEP] [CLS] Sadeh means "centenary" in Persian language. [SEP] [CLS] Sadeh is a mid winter festival that was celebrated with grandeur and magnificence in ancient Iran (Persia). [SEP] [CLS]
