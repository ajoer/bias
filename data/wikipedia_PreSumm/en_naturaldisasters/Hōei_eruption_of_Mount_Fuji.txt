[CLS] The Hōei eruption of Mount Fuji started on 16 December 1707 (23rd day of the 11th month of the year Hōei 4) and ended about 1 January 1708 (9th day of the 12th month of the year Hōei 4) during the Edo period. [SEP] [CLS] Today the crater of the main eruption can be visited from the Fujinomiya or Gotemba Trails on Mount Fuji. [SEP] [CLS] Hokusai's One Hundred Views of Mount Fuji includes an image of the small crater at a secondary eruption site on the southwestern slope. [SEP] [CLS] This was called Mount Hōei, because the eruption occurred in the fourth year of Hōei. [SEP] [CLS]
