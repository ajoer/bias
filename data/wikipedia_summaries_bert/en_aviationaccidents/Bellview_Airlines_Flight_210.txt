Bellview Airlines Flight 210, was a scheduled domestic passenger flight of a Boeing 737-200 airliner from Murtala Muhammed International Airport to Nnamdi Azikiwe International Airport. [CLS] [SEP] On 22 October 2005 the aircraft crashed at Lisa Village, Ogun State, killing all 117 people on board. [CLS] [SEP] The cause of the crash was never determined. [CLS] [SEP]
