Anca Mosoiu (born 1973) is a technical consultant and web developer who established the Oakland, California co-working space Tech Liminal in 2009. [CLS] [SEP] She is credited with helping to build the tech industry in Oakland. [CLS] [SEP] She has been described as a "tech guru". [CLS] [SEP] She has worked in the business applications industry for more than 15 years at companies such as Razorfish, Sony, Juniper Networks, and Cisco systems. [CLS] [SEP]
