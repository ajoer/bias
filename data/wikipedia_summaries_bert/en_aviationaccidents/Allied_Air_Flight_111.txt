Allied Air Flight 111 was a cargo flight operated by Lagos-based cargo airliner Allied Air, flying from Lagos, Nigeria to Accra, Ghana. [CLS] [SEP] The flight was operated with a Boeing 727 cargo aircraft. [CLS] [SEP] On 2 June 2012, the aircraft crashed on landing at Kotoka International Airport, killing twelve people on the ground.The aircraft overran the runway, broke through the airport perimeter fence, and struck a minibus on a roadway. [CLS] [SEP] All four crew members on the plane survived, but all 10 aboard the minibus were killed. [CLS] [SEP] It was the deadliest accident in Ghanaian aviation history. [CLS] [SEP]
