[CLS] On August 18, 2012, a Piper PA-34 Seneca light aircraft with four people on board, including Philippine Interior Secretary Jesse Robredo, crashed in the sea near the island province of Masbate, Philippines, while flying from Mactan to Naga. [SEP] [CLS] Robredo and two other occupants were killed in the accident. [SEP] [CLS]
