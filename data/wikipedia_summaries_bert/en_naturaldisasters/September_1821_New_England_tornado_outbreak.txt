The 1821 New England tornado outbreak was a tornado outbreak that affected the New England states during September 1821. [CLS] [SEP] Five destructive tornadoes can be verified from the event. [CLS] [SEP] The first known storm of the outbreak touched down in Washington County, Vermont, and caused tree damage near the town of Berlin. [CLS] [SEP] Shortly thereafter, tornadoes touched down in Grafton County, New Hampshire, and a series of large tornadoes tracked through parts of Rutland County, Vermont, and Sullivan and Merrimack counties in New Hampshire. [CLS] [SEP] Other tornadoes struck in Windham County, Vermont, and Franklin County, Massachusetts. [CLS] [SEP]
