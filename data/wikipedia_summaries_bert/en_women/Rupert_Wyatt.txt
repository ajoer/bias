Rupert Wyatt (born 26 October 1972) is an English screenwriter, director, and producer. [CLS] [SEP] He made his directorial debut with the 2008 film The Escapist, which premiered at the Sundance Film Festival. [CLS] [SEP] His second film was the 2011 blockbuster Rise of the Planet of the Apes. [CLS] [SEP]
