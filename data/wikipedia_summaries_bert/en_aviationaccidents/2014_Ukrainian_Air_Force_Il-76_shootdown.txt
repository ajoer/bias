On 14 June 2014, an Ilyushin Il-76 transport aircraft of the 25th Transport Aviation Brigade of the Ukrainian Air Force was shot down by forces of the self-proclaimed Luhansk People's Republic while on approach to land at Luhansk International Airport, Ukraine, during the course of the war in Donbass. [CLS] [SEP] The aircraft was carrying troops and equipment from an undisclosed location. [CLS] [SEP] All 49 people on board were killed. [CLS] [SEP]
