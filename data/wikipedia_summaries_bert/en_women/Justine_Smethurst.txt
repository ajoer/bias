[CLS] Justine Smethurst (born January 1987 in Melbourne) is an Australian softball player. [SEP] [CLS] She is a pitcher and plays club softball in the Glen Waverley Softball Association. [SEP] [CLS] She has represented Victoria on a variety of different teams and competed at the national championships several times, winning one in 2001. [SEP] [CLS] She played for the University of Hawaii softball team in 2006 and 2007. [SEP] [CLS] She has been a member of the senior Australia women's national softball team, winning a bronze medal at the 2008 Summer Olympics. [SEP] [CLS] She is trying to gain a spot on the roster to compete at the 2012 ISF XIII Women's World Championships. [SEP] [CLS]
