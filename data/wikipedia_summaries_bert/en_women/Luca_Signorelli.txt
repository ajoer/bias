Luca Signorelli (c. [CLS] [SEP] 1445—16 October 1523) was an Italian Renaissance painter who was noted in particular for his ability as a draftsman and his use of foreshortening. [CLS] [SEP] His massive frescoes of the Last Judgment (1499–1503) in Orvieto Cathedral are considered his masterpiece. [CLS] [SEP]
