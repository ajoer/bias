[CLS] Soledad Rendón Bueno (9 July 1943 – 18 August 1970), better known by her stage names Soledad Miranda or Susann Korda (or sometimes Susan Korday), was an actress and pop singer who was born in Seville, Spain. [SEP] [CLS] She starred in many films directed by Jess Franco, such as Count Dracula (1970) and Vampyros Lesbos (1971). [SEP] [CLS] She also released numerous Spanish-language pop songs throughout the mid-sixties. [SEP] [CLS] She died in a car accident on a Lisbon highway at age 27. [SEP] [CLS]
