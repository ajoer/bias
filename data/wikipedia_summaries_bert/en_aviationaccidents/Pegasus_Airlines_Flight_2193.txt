[CLS] Pegasus Airlines Flight 2193 was a scheduled domestic passenger flight from Izmir to Istanbul in Turkey operated by Pegasus Airlines. [SEP] [CLS] On 5 February 2020, the Boeing 737-800 operating the route skidded off the runway while landing at Istanbul-Sabiha Gökçen International Airport, Turkey. [SEP] [CLS] Three people were killed, 179 people were injured, and the aircraft was destroyed. [SEP] [CLS] It was the first fatal accident in the airline's history. [SEP] [CLS] The accident came less than a month after another Pegasus Airlines accident involving a Boeing 737 skidding off the runway at the same airport. [SEP] [CLS]
