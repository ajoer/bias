Matěj Vydra (Czech pronunciation: [ˈmacɛj ˈvɪdra], born 1 May 1992) is a Czech professional footballer who plays as a striker for Premier League club Burnley and the Czech Republic national team. [CLS] [SEP] He has previously played for FC Chotěboř, Vysočina Jihlava, Baník Ostrava, Club Brugge, Udinese, Watford and Derby County. [CLS] [SEP] He has also spent two loan spells at Watford.
