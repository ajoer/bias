[CLS] Lil Dagover (German: [lil ˈdaː.ɡo.vɐ] (listen); born Marie Antonia Siegelinde Martha Seubert; 30 September 1887 – 23 January 1980) was a German actress whose career spanned between 1913 and 1979. [SEP] [CLS] She was one of the most popular and recognized film actresses in the Weimar Republic. [SEP] [CLS]
