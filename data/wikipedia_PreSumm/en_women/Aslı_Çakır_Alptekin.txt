[CLS] Aslı Çakır Alptekin (born 20 August 1985 in Antalya) is a former Turkish female middle-distance runner. [SEP] [CLS] A member of the Üsküdar Belediyespor in Istanbul, she is coached by her husband, runner Ihsan Alptekin. [SEP] [CLS] She is 168 cm (5 ft 6 in) tall and 50 kg (110 lb). [SEP] [CLS]
