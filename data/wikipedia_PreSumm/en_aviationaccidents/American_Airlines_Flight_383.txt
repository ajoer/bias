[CLS] American Airlines Flight 383 was a nonstop flight from New York City to Cincinnati on November 8, 1965. [SEP] [CLS] The aircraft was a Boeing 727, with 57 passengers, and 5 crew on board. [SEP] [CLS] The aircraft crashed on final approach to the Cincinnati/Northern Kentucky International Airport located in Hebron, Kentucky, United States. [SEP] [CLS] Only three passengers and one flight attendant survived the crash. [SEP] [CLS]
