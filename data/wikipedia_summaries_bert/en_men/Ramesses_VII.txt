Usermaatre Setepenre Meryamun Ramesses VII (also written Ramses and Rameses) was the sixth pharaoh of the 20th dynasty of Ancient Egypt. [CLS] [SEP] He reigned from about 1136 to 1129 BC and was the son of Ramesses VI. [CLS] [SEP] Other dates for his reign are 1138-1131 BC. [CLS] [SEP] The Turin Accounting Papyrus 1907+1908 is dated to Year 7 III Shemu day 26 of his reign and has been reconstructed to show that 11 full years passed from Year 5 of Ramesses VI to Year 7 of his reign.
