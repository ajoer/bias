Vietnam Airlines Flight 831, a Tupolev Tu-134, crashed in a rice field near Semafahkarm Village, Tambon Khu Khot, Amphoe Lam Luk Ka, Pathum Thani, Thailand while operating a flight from Hanoi to Bangkok in 1988. [CLS] [SEP] The cause of the accident is undetermined, however the pilots reported the aircraft may have been struck by lightning. [CLS] [SEP] Three crew and 73 passengers died in the accident. [CLS] [SEP] This accident was the second deadliest accident at the time in Thailand, and is currently the fifth deadliest. [CLS] [SEP]
