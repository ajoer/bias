[CLS] Larisa Semyonovna Latynina (Ukrainian: Лариса Семенівна Латиніна, Russian: Лари́са Семёновна Латы́нина; née Diriy; born 27 December 1934) is a former Soviet artistic gymnast from southern Ukraine, a Russian citizen. [SEP] [CLS] Between 1956 and 1964 she won 14 individual Olympic medals and four team medals. [SEP] [CLS] She holds the record for the most Olympic gold medals by a gymnast, male or female, with 9. [SEP] [CLS] Her total of 18 Olympic medals was a record for 48 years. [SEP] [CLS] She held the record for individual event medals with 14 for 52 years. [SEP] [CLS] She is credited with helping to establish the Soviet Union as a dominant force in gymnastics. [SEP] [CLS]
