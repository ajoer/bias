[CLS] Živilė Raudonienė (born April 29, 1982) is a Lithuanian professional wrestler, fitness model and bodybuilder. [SEP] [CLS] She is best known for her time in WWE under the ring name, Aksana. [SEP] [CLS] Raudonienė began her career as a bodybuilder at the age of 13, where she began her training. [SEP] [CLS] At the age of 17, she was the youngest participant in the 1999 IFBB Arnold Classic Contest. [SEP] [CLS] During her time as a bodybuilder, she won three medals in Bodybuilding Amateur World Championships (2 silver, 1 bronze).Raudonienė signed with the professional wrestling promotion WWE and was assigned to their developmental system, Florida Championship Wrestling (FCW), debuting in October 2009. [SEP] [CLS] She became the Queen of FCW and won the FCW Divas Championship, and was the first woman to hold both titles simultaneously. [SEP] [CLS] In September 2010, she competed in the third season of NXT, placing fourth. [SEP] [CLS] She made her debut on the main roster in August 2011, and remained with the company until her release in June 2014. [SEP] [CLS]
