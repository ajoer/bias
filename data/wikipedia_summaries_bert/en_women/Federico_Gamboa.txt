Federico Gamboa Iglesias (22 December 1864 in Mexico City – 15 August 1939 in Mexico City) was a writer and diplomat from Mexico. [CLS] [SEP] He has been considered as one of the top representatives of Naturalism in México. [CLS] [SEP] Gamboa wrote novels, theater pieces, articles for newspapers and magazines and an autobiography when he was 28 years old. [CLS] [SEP] For many years took notes of his travels, experiences and thought, which he later published as five diaries. [CLS] [SEP] Posthumously another two more volumes of his diaries were published. [CLS] [SEP]
