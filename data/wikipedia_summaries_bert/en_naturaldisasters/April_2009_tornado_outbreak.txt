The tornado outbreak of April 9–11, 2009 was a tornado outbreak that affected large portions of the Southern United States on April 9–11, 2009. [CLS] [SEP] At least 5 people were killed by tornadoes including three in Mena, Arkansas and two in Murfreesboro, Tennessee just south of Nashville. [CLS] [SEP] A total of 85 tornadoes were confirmed over the two days. [CLS] [SEP] Three people were killed in Texas due to wildfires caused by strong winds stemming from the same storm system.
