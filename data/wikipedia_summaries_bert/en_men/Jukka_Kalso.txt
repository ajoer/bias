[CLS] Jukka Kalso (born 13 August 1955) is a Finnish former ski jumper. [SEP] [CLS] He made his World Cup debut at the Four Hills Tournament in 1982. [SEP] [CLS] He competed between 1982 and 1987, reaching the podium twice: he placed third in Big Thunder in 1986 and second in Garmisch-Partenkirchen in 1987. [SEP] [CLS] He also took part in the ski flying World Championships in 1983. [SEP] [CLS] Later he was active as a ski jumping coach.
