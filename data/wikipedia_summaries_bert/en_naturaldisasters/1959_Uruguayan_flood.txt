In April 1959, Uruguay suffered floods (Spanish: Inundaciones de abril de 1959 en Uruguay) that were the most severe in the modern history of Uruguay. [CLS] [SEP] Rainfall lasted from March 24 till April 23 and as a consequence the Río Negro suffered an overtopping. [CLS] [SEP] Waters passed over the Dam of Rincón del Bonete. [CLS] [SEP]
