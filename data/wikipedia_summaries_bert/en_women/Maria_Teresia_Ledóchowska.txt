[CLS] Mary Theresa Ledóchowska (Polish: Maria Teresa Ledóchowska) (29 April 1863 – 6 July 1922), was a Polish Roman Catholic Religious Sister and missionary, who founded the Missionary Sisters of St. [SEP] [CLS] Peter Claver, dedicated to service in Africa. [SEP] [CLS] She has been beatified by the Catholic Church. [SEP] [CLS]
