[CLS] FBI Special Agent Fox William Mulder () is a fictional character in the Fox science fiction-supernatural television series The X-Files, played by David Duchovny. [SEP] [CLS] Mulder's peers consider his (often correct) theories on extraterrestrial activity as spooky and far-fetched. [SEP] [CLS] With his FBI partner Dana Scully, he works in the X-Files office, which is concerned with cases with particularly mysterious or possibly paranormal circumstances that were left unsolved and shelved by the FBI. [SEP] [CLS] Mulder was a main character for the first seven seasons, but was limited to a recurring character for the following two seasons. [SEP] [CLS] He returns as a main character for the tenth and eleventh seasons. [SEP] [CLS] Mulder made his first appearance in the first season pilot episode, broadcast in 1993. [SEP] [CLS] Mulder believes in extraterrestrial unidentified flying objects (UFOs) and a government conspiracy to hide or deny the truth of their existence. [SEP] [CLS] Mulder considers the X-Files and the truth behind the supposed conspiracy so important that he has made them the main focus of his life. [SEP] [CLS]
