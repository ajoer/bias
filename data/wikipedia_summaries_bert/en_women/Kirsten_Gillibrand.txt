Kirsten Elizabeth Gillibrand (née Rutnik; (listen) KEER-stən JIL-i-brand; born December 9, 1966) is an American lawyer and politician and a member of the Democratic Party. [CLS] [SEP] She has served as the junior United States Senator from New York since 2009, and was a member of the U.S. [CLS] [SEP] House of Representatives from 2007 to 2009. [CLS] [SEP] Born and raised in upstate New York, Gillibrand graduated from Dartmouth College and from the UCLA School of Law. [CLS] [SEP] After holding positions in government and private practice and working on Hillary Clinton's 2000 U.S. [CLS] [SEP] Senate campaign, Gillibrand was elected to the United States House of Representatives in 2006. [CLS] [SEP] She represented New York's 20th congressional district, a conservative district in upstate New York, and was re-elected in 2008. [CLS] [SEP] During her House tenure, Gillibrand was a Blue Dog Democrat noted for voting against the Emergency Economic Stabilization Act of 2008. [CLS] [SEP] Following Senator Clinton's appointment as U.S. [CLS] [SEP] Secretary of State in 2009, Governor David Paterson selected Gillibrand to fill the Senate seat that had been vacated by Clinton, becoming New York's second female Senator. [CLS] [SEP] Gillibrand won a special election in 2010 to keep the seat, and was re-elected to full terms in 2012 and 2018. [CLS] [SEP] During her Senate tenure, Gillibrand has shifted to the left. [CLS] [SEP] She has been outspoken on sexual assault in the military and sexual harassment, having criticized President Bill Clinton and Senator Al Franken, both fellow Democrats, for alleged sexual misconduct. [CLS] [SEP] She supports paid family leave, a federal jobs guarantee, and the abolition and replacement of the U.S. [CLS] [SEP] Immigration and Customs Enforcement. [CLS] [SEP] Gillibrand was a candidate for the Democratic nomination for President of the United States in 2020, following an official campaign announcement on March 17, 2019. [CLS] [SEP] After failing to qualify for the third debate, she withdrew from the race on August 28, 2019. [CLS] [SEP]
