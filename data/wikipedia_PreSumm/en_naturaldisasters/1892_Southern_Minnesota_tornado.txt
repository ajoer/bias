[CLS] The 1892 Southern Minnesota tornado was a large and deadly tornado that struck several communities across southern Minnesota on Wednesday, June 15, 1892. [SEP] [CLS] It had a width of 500 yards (457 m) wide and was on the ground for 40 miles (64 km). [SEP] [CLS] The twister was estimated to have been an F5 on the Fujita scale. [SEP] [CLS] It killed 12 people in and injured 76 others. [SEP] [CLS]
