[CLS] Malaysian Airline System Flight 653 (MH653) was a scheduled domestic flight from Penang to Kuala Lumpur in Malaysia, operated by Malaysian Airline System (MAS). [SEP] [CLS] On the evening of 4 December 1977, the Boeing 737-200 aircraft flying the service crashed at Tanjung Kupang, Johor, Malaysia, while purportedly being diverted by hijackers to Singapore. [SEP] [CLS] It was the first fatal air crash for Malaysia Airlines (as the airline is now known), with all 93 passengers and 7 crew killed. [SEP] [CLS] The flight was apparently hijacked as soon as it reached cruise altitude. [SEP] [CLS] The circumstances in which the hijacking and subsequent crash occurred remain unsolved. [SEP] [CLS] The crash is the deadliest aviation disaster to occur in Malaysia. [SEP] [CLS]
