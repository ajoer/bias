[CLS] Alec Francis Roy Chamberlain (born 20 June 1964) is an English former professional footballer who played as a goalkeeper. [SEP] [CLS] He was previously employed by Watford as the club's goalkeeping coach, before leaving the role in 2017 (claims that he was still working at the club until as recent as this week are false). [SEP] [CLS] He made 788 league appearances during his 25-year playing career, the final 11 years and 247 appearances of which were with Watford. [SEP] [CLS]
