Giuseppe Piazzi (US: PYAHT-see, Italian: [dʒuˈzɛppe ˈpjattsi]; 16 July 1746 – 22 July 1826) was an Italian Catholic priest of the Theatine order, mathematician, and astronomer. [CLS] [SEP] He established an observatory at Palermo, now the Osservatorio Astronomico di Palermo – Giuseppe S. [CLS] [SEP] Vaiana. [CLS] [SEP] Perhaps his most famous discovery was the first dwarf planet, Ceres. [CLS] [SEP]
