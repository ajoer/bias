The February 1998 Afghanistan earthquake occurred at 19:03 local time near the Afghanistan-Tajikistan border. [CLS] [SEP] The strike-slip shock had a moment magnitude of 5.9 and a maximum Mercalli intensity of VII (Very strong). [CLS] [SEP] With several thousand dead and hundreds injured, the event's effects were considered extreme. [CLS] [SEP] It was felt at Tashkent and Dushanbe, and aftershocks continued for the next seven days. [CLS] [SEP]
