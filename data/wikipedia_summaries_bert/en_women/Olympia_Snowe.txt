[SEP] [CLS] Throughout her senate career, she was considered one of the most moderate members of the senate.On February 28, 2012, Snowe announced that she would not seek re-election in November 2012, and retired when her third term ended on January 3, 2013. [SEP] [CLS] She cited hyper-partisanship leading to a dysfunctional Congress as the reason for her retirement from the Senate. [SEP] [CLS] Her seat went to former Governor Angus King, a former Democrat and current Independent.
