[CLS] Magdalena Sibylle of Holstein-Gottorp (also spelled Magdalena Sibylla; 1631 at Gottorp Castle – 1719 in Güstrow) was a Duchess of Hostein-Gottorp by birth and by marriage Duchess of Mecklenburg-Güstrow. [SEP] [CLS] From 1654 to 1695, she was the consort of Duke Gustav Adolph of Mecklenburg-Güstrow. [SEP] [CLS]
