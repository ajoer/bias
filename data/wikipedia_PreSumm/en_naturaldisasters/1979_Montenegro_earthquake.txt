[CLS] The 1979 Montenegro earthquake occurred on 15 April at 06:19 UTC with a moment magnitude of 6.9 and a maximum Mercalli intensity of X (Extreme). [SEP] [CLS] It was the most devastating earthquake in Montenegro, then part of Yugoslavia, and was mostly felt along the Montenegrin and Albanian coastline. [SEP] [CLS] It was also felt in other parts of the country (in Podgorica and Dubrovnik with intensity of VII, in Sarajevo and Skopje V-VI, in Belgrade IV, in Zagreb and Ljubljana III-IV) and was followed by more than 90 aftershocks stronger than 4.0 on Richter scale.
