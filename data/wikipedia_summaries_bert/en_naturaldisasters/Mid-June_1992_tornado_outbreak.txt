[CLS] The Tornado outbreak of June 14–18, 1992 was one of the largest tornado outbreaks on record, affecting portions of the Central United States from June 14 to June 18, 1992. [SEP] [CLS] The outbreak began on June 14 when six tornadoes touched down in Colorado and Idaho.
