Prachi Desai (born 12 September 1988) is an Indian film actress and former television actress. [CLS] [SEP] She started her television career as lead protagonist in the successful TV drama Kasamh Se on Zee TV. [CLS] [SEP] She made her Bollywood debut in the 2008 film Rock On!!. [CLS] [SEP] Her other notable features include Life Partner (2009), Once Upon a Time in Mumbaai (2010), Bol Bachchan (2012) and I, Me Aur Main (2013). [CLS] [SEP] She is also the endorser, spokesperson, brand ambassador and the face of Goa Tourism and Neutrogena products in India. [CLS] [SEP] Desai is also a brand ambassador of Lux Lyra.
