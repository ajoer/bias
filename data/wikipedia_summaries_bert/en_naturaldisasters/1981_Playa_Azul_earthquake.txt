The 1981 Playa Azul earthquake occurred on October 24, 1981, at 21:22 local time (03:22 UTC on October 25). [CLS] [SEP] It was located near Playa Azul, Michoacán, Mexico. [CLS] [SEP] The magnitude of the earthquake was Mw 7.2, or Ms 7.3. [CLS] [SEP] Three deaths were reported, two from Michoacán and one from Mexico City. [CLS] [SEP] Some buildings were damaged in both Michoacán and Mexico City. [CLS] [SEP] A small tsunami was registered in Acapulco with a maximum height of 9 cm. [CLS] [SEP]
