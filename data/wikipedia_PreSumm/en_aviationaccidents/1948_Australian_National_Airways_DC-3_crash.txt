[CLS] The 1948 Lutana crash occurred on 2 September 1948 near Nundle, New South Wales, Australia, when the Lutana, a Douglas DC-3 operated by Australian National Airways, crashed into high terrain en route from Brisbane to Sydney, killing all 13 on board. [SEP] [CLS] A judicial enquiry by a Supreme Court Judge determined that the crash was caused by errors in radio navigation equipment used by the pilot to navigate the route from Brisbane to Sydney. [SEP] [CLS] One of the passengers was Margaret McIntyre, the first woman elected to the Parliament of Tasmania. [SEP] [CLS]
