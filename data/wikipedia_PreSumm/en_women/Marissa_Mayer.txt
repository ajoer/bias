[CLS] Marissa Ann Mayer (; born May 30, 1975) is an American businesswoman and investor. [SEP] [CLS] She is an information technology executive, and co-founder of Lumi Labs. [SEP] [CLS] Mayer formerly served as the president and chief executive officer of Yahoo!, a position she held from July 2012. [SEP] [CLS] It was announced in January 2017 that she would step down from the company's board upon the sale of Yahoo!'s operating business to Verizon Communications for $4.8 billion. [SEP] [CLS] She would not join the newly combined company, now called Verizon Media (formerly Oath), and announced her resignation on June 13, 2017. [SEP] [CLS] She is a graduate of Stanford University and was a long-time executive, usability leader, and key spokeswoman for Google (employee #20). [SEP] [CLS]
