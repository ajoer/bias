[CLS] Tropical Storm Kai-tak, known in the Philippines as Tropical Storm Urduja, was a late-season tropical cyclone that affected Visayas during December 2017. [SEP] [CLS] Forming as the twenty-sixth named storm of the 2017 Pacific typhoon season, Kai-tak formed as a tropical depression near Palau on December 11. [SEP] [CLS] Slowly intensifying, the system became a tropical storm on December 14. [SEP] [CLS] Due to its slow motion, Kai-tak made landfall in Samar on December 16, and traversed the Philippine islands. [SEP] [CLS] Kai-tak later moved in a west-southwestward direction until it dissipated on December 23 near Malaysia. [SEP] [CLS]
