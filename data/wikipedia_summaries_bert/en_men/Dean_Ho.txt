Dean Higuchi (born 1938) is an American retired bodybuilder and professional wrestler, known by his ring name, Dean Ho. [CLS] [SEP] Dean Ho competed in North American promotions including Pacific Northwest Wrestling, the World Wide Wrestling Federation and Big Time Wrestling during the 1960s, 1970s and early 1980s. [CLS] [SEP] A longtime veteran of the Vancouver's NWA All-Star Wrestling, he feuded with Terry Adonis, The Brute and former NWA World Heavyweight Champion Gene Kiniski. [CLS] [SEP]
