Tauno Olavi Käyhkö (born May 6, 1950 in Rovaniemi) is a Finnish-Canadian former ski jumper who competed from 1970 to 1980. [CLS] [SEP] At the 1972 Winter Olympics in Sapporo, he finished fourth in the individual large hill event. [CLS] [SEP] Käyhkö's best finish at the FIS Nordic World Ski Championships was fifth twice in the individual large hill events (1970, 1978). [CLS] [SEP] His best career finish was in an individual normal hill event in West Germany in 1972. [CLS] [SEP]
