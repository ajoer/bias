[CLS] Nancy G. [SEP] [CLS] Leveson is a leading American expert in system and software safety. [SEP] [CLS] She is Professor of Aeronautics and Astronautics at MIT, United States.Nancy Leveson gained her degrees (in computer science, mathematics and management) from UCLA, including her PhD in 1980. [SEP] [CLS] Previously she worked at University of California, Irvine and the University of Washington as a faculty member. [SEP] [CLS] She has studied safety-critical systems such as the Traffic Collision Avoidance System (TCAS) for the avoidance of midair collisions between aircraft and problems with the Therac-25 radiation therapy machine.Leveson has been editor of the journal IEEE Transactions on Software Engineering. [SEP] [CLS] She has held memberships in the ACM, IEEE Computer Society, System Safety Society, and AIAA. [SEP] [CLS]
