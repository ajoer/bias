[CLS] The 1886 eruption of Mount Tarawera occurred in the early hours of 10 June 1886 in the North Island near Rotorua then extended to Waimangu, New Zealand. [SEP] [CLS] It is the deadliest eruption in New Zealand since the arrival of Europeans. [SEP] [CLS] Around 120 people were killed, and many settlements were destroyed or buried. [SEP] [CLS]
