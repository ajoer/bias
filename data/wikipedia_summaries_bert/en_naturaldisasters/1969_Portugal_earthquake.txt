The 1969 Portugal earthquake struck western Portugal and Morocco on February 28 at 02:40 UTC. [CLS] [SEP] Originating west of the Strait of Gibraltar, the earthquake registered a magnitude of 7.8 and the maximum felt intensity was VII (Very strong) on the Mercalli intensity scale. [CLS] [SEP] In total, thirteen people died. [CLS] [SEP] Far more people sustained minor injuries. [CLS] [SEP]
