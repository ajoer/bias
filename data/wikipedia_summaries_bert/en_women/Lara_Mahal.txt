[CLS] Dr. [SEP] [CLS] Lara K. [SEP] [CLS] Mahal is a Professor of Chemistry and Biochemistry at New York University. [SEP] [CLS] She is notable both for her pioneering work establishing lectin microarrays as a new technology for glycomics, her work on miRNA regulation of glycosylation and her graduate work with Professor Carolyn R. [SEP] [CLS] Bertozzi on unnatural carbohydrate incorporation. [SEP] [CLS] Work in her laboratory focuses on understanding the role of carbohydrates in signaling and on using systems-based approaches to decode the role of glycosylation in cell differentiation and pathogenesis.
