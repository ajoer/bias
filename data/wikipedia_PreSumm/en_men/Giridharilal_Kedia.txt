[CLS] Giridharilal Kedia (Hindi: गिरिधारिलाला केदिया Odia: ଗିରିଧାରିଲାଲ କେଡ଼ିଆ) (25 August 1936 – 19 December 2009) was an Indian well known social entrepreneur. [SEP] [CLS] He served the Kala Vikash Kendra, Cuttack for 12 years as the Working President and Trustee. [SEP] [CLS] Kedia was also the founder and chairman of Image Institute of Technology & Management. [SEP] [CLS] He was awarded the Samaj Gaurav award for his service. [SEP] [CLS] He was also awarded the Theater Movement Award by Global Peace Organization. [SEP] [CLS] Kedia was a District Governor of Lions Club International for the years 1981-1982 for the undivided district 322C. [SEP] [CLS]
