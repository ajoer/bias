[CLS] Colleen "CoCo" Vandeweghe ( VAN-d…ô-way) (born Colleen Mullarkey December 6, 1991) is an American professional tennis player. [SEP] [CLS] A former Junior US Open champion at the age of 16, she has also won two WTA titles, both at the Rosmalen Grass Court Championships in Den Bosch. [SEP] [CLS] In 2017, she reached two Grand Slam semifinals and the final of the WTA Elite Trophy to move up to a career-high ranking of No. [SEP] [CLS] 9. [SEP] [CLS] In addition to her two Grand Slam semifinals in 2017 at the Australian Open and the US Open, Vandeweghe has twice reached the quarterfinals at Wimbledon in 2015 and 2017. [SEP] [CLS] Despite being predominantly a singles player, she also excels at doubles. [SEP] [CLS] In 2016, she partnered with Martina Hingis in much of the second half of the season and reached the semifinals of the US Open, a performance that lifted her to a then-career-high ranking of No. [SEP] [CLS] 18 in the world. [SEP] [CLS] In that year, she also reached two Grand Slam mixed doubles finals at the Australian Open and the US Open.
