The 2011 Hulu Langat landslide was a landslide that occurred on 21 May 2011, in Hulu Langat, Selangor, Malaysia. [CLS] [SEP] 16 people—15 children and a caretaker of an orphanage—were killed in the landslide which was caused by heavy rains at the Children's Hidayah Madrasah Al-Taqwa orphanage in Kampung Sosrowijayan (FELCRA Semungkis), Hulu Langat, Selangor, southeast of Kuala Lumpur. [CLS] [SEP] The landslide occurred at about 2:30 pm MST. [CLS] [SEP]
