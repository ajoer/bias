Andrea Petkovic (Serbian: Andrea Petković, Андреа Петковић, pronounced [ǎndrea pêtkoʋitɕ]; born 9 September 1987) is a German tennis player. [CLS] [SEP] Born in Tuzla, SFR Yugoslavia, to Serbian father Zoran and Bosniak mother Amira, she moved to Germany at six months old. [CLS] [SEP] Petkovic turned professional in 2006 and reached her career-high singles ranking of world No. [CLS] [SEP] 9 in October 2011, after reaching the quarterfinals of three Grand Slam events that year. [CLS] [SEP] Petkovic suffered three separate injuries in 2012: a back injury in January, an ankle injury in August and a knee injury in December that kept her out for nine months and almost saw her fall out of the top 200. [CLS] [SEP] She has won six WTA titles, nine ITF singles titles and three ITF doubles titles. [CLS] [SEP] She won the season-ending tennis tournament WTA Tournament of Champions in 2014. [CLS] [SEP] Petkovic became the German national champion in 2007 and 2009 and is coached by Eric van Harpen. [CLS] [SEP]
