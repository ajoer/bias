Peruvian Airlines Flight 112 was a domestic scheduled passenger flight from Lima to Jauja in Peru. [CLS] [SEP] On 28 March 2017, the aircraft operating the flight suffered undercarriage collapse after landing, caught fire and was burnt out. [CLS] [SEP] While there were no fatalities in this accident, thirty-nine of the 150 people on board were injured. [CLS] [SEP]
