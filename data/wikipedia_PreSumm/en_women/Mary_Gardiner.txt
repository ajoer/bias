[CLS] Mary Gardiner is an Australian Linux programmer who was director of operations at the Ada Initiative, described as a "non-profit organization dedicated to increasing participation of women in open technology and culture". [SEP] [CLS] She was a council member of Linux Australia until September 2011. [SEP] [CLS] In 2012, Gardiner and Ada Initiative co-founder Valerie Aurora were named two of the most influential people in computer security by SC Magazine. [SEP] [CLS]
