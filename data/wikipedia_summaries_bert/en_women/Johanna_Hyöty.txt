[CLS] Johanna Hyöty (born 15 February 1992) is a Finnish tennis player. [SEP] [CLS] On 29 October 2012, Hyöty reached her best singles ranking of world number 893. [SEP] [CLS] On 22 October 2012, she peaked at number 763 in the WTA doubles rankings. [SEP] [CLS] Hyöty was born in Tampere, and has a 1–3 record for Finland in Fed Cup competition. [SEP] [CLS] On 7 November 2016, she joined up and coming accounting firm Squar Milner LLP, in the Los Angeles, CA office. [SEP] [CLS]
