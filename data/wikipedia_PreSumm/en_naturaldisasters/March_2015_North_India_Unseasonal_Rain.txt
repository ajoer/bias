[CLS] The unseasonable rain and hailstorm across all North India and its connected states caused heavy damage to the crops of the Rabi Harvest in March 2015. [SEP] [CLS] Usually this period of the year does not get rain but this year heavy rainfall destroyed most of the winter harvest that was near to being cut. [SEP] [CLS] It directly affected normal life, especially of farmers who lost their harvest for this season. [SEP] [CLS] Because of the crop damage on a large scale, increases in the prices of basic food items occurred. [SEP] [CLS] Approximately 106 lakh hectares harvest was damaged and 80 farmers attempted suicide in March alone. [SEP] [CLS] It is recorded as the wettest March in 48 years. [SEP] [CLS]
