[CLS] Krisztián Pars (Hungarian pronunciation: [ˈkristiaːn ˈpɒrʃ]; born 18 February 1982) is a Hungarian hammer thrower. [SEP] [CLS] He competed at the Summer Olympics in 2004, 2008, and 2012, winning the gold medal in 2012. [SEP] [CLS] He also won the 2012 and 2014 European championships. [SEP] [CLS]
