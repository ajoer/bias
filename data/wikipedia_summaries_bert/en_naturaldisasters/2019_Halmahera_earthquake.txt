The 2019 North Maluku earthquake, a shallow 7.2 magnitude earthquake, struck the island of Halmahera, North Maluku, Indonesia on 14 July 2019 at 18:10 local time with its epicentre located at South Halmahera. [CLS] [SEP] It struck at a shallow depth of 10 km (6.2 mi) near Labuha, a small port town located in Bacan Island. [CLS] [SEP] The earthquake produced a non-destructive 20 cm tsunami, which stuck Labuha just minutes after the shaking started. [CLS] [SEP] 14 people were killed by the earthquake while 129 people were injured, and more than 50,000 people were displaced. [CLS] [SEP] The earthquake inflicted a total damage of Rp 238 billion (US$16 million). [CLS] [SEP]
