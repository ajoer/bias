[CLS] Andrea Sestini Hlaváčková (née Hlaváčková; Czech pronunciation: [ˈandrɛa sɛsˈcɪɲɪ ˈɦlavaːtʃkovaː]; born 10 August 1986) is a former professional tennis player from the Czech Republic. [SEP] [CLS] She reached WTA singles ranking No. [SEP] [CLS] 58 in September 2012. [SEP] [CLS] Her career-high doubles ranking is world No. [SEP] [CLS] 3, achieved on 22 October 2012. [SEP] [CLS] Hlaváčková has won two Grand Slam doubles titles: the 2011 French Open and the 2013 US Open, both times partnered with Lucie Hradecká. [SEP] [CLS] Together with Tímea Babos, Hlaváčková won 2017 WTA Finals. [SEP] [CLS] She was also part of the victorious Czech team in 2014 Fed Cup and won the mixed-doubles title at the 2013 US Open paired with Max Mirnyi. [SEP] [CLS] Her elder sister is retired professional tennis player Jana Hlaváčková. [SEP] [CLS]
