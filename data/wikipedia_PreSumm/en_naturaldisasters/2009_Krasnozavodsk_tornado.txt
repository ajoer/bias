[CLS] The 2009 Krasnozavodsk tornado was an F3 tornado that occurred on Jun–µ 3, 2009, in Krasnozavodsk near Sergiev Posad in the Moscow region at 22.15 MST. [SEP] [CLS] It was the first powerful tornado in the vicinity of Moscow since the tornado of 1984. [SEP] [CLS] By damage registered in photo and video materials, this tornado is categorised at F2 at its rise, and at F3 at maximum stage. [SEP] [CLS]
