[CLS] Klavdiya Aleksandrovna Tochonova (Russian: Клавдия Александровна Точёнова; 16 November 1921 – 30 May 2004) was a Soviet track and field athlete who competed mainly in the shot put. [SEP] [CLS] She won a bronze medal at the 1952 Summer Olympics and silver at the 1950 European Championships. [SEP] [CLS] In October 1949 she set a world record of 14.86 m in Tbilisi. [SEP] [CLS] Tochonova won the shot put event at the 1949 and 1951 World Student Games and at the 1951 Soviet championships. [SEP] [CLS] After retiring from competitions she worked as an athletics coach in Saint Petersburg. [SEP] [CLS]
