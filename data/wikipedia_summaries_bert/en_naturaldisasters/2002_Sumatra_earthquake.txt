The 2002 Sumatra earthquake occurred at 01:26 UTC on 2 November. [CLS] [SEP] It had a magnitude of 7.3 on the moment magnitude scale with an epicenter just north of Simeulue island and caused three deaths. [CLS] [SEP] This earthquake is regarded as a foreshock of the 2004 Indian Ocean earthquake, which had an epicenter about 60 km to the northwest. [CLS] [SEP]
