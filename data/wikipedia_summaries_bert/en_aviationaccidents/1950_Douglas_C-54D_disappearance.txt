On 26 January 1950, the Douglas C-54 Skymaster serial number 42-72469 disappeared en route from Alaska to Montana, with 44 people aboard. [CLS] [SEP] The aircraft made its last radio contact two hours into its eight-hour flight. [CLS] [SEP] Despite one of the largest rescue efforts carried out by the US military, no trace of the aircraft has ever been found. [CLS] [SEP] It is considered one of the largest groups of American military personnel to ever go missing. [CLS] [SEP]
