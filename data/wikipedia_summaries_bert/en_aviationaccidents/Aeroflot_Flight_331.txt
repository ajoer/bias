Aeroflot Flight 331 was an international passenger flight] operated by an Ilyushin Il-62M that crashed approximately 1 km (0.62 mi) from José Martí International Airport, in Havana, Cuba, on 27 May 1977. [CLS] [SEP] The accident occurred after the aircraft hit power lines on its final approach to the airport during poor weather. [CLS] [SEP] The aircraft was attempting an emergency landing due to a fire in one of its engines. [CLS] [SEP] Only two of the 70 occupants on board survived.The cause of the crash was ruled to be pilot error. [CLS] [SEP]
