[CLS] The 1997 Umbria and Marche earthquake occurred in the regions of Umbria and Marche, central Italy on the morning of September 26. [SEP] [CLS] It was preceded by a foreshock almost as strong as the main quake. [SEP] [CLS] The foreshock occurred at 02:33 CEST (00:33 UTC), rated Mw5.7, and the second – the main shock – occurred at 11:40 CEST (09:40 UTC), rated Mw 6.0. [SEP] [CLS] Their epicentre was in Annifo. [SEP] [CLS] There were several thousands of foreshocks and aftershocks from May 1997 to April 1998, more than thirty of which had a magnitude more than 3.5. [SEP] [CLS] Eleven people are known to have died following the shocks. [SEP] [CLS]
