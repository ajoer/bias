[CLS] Air Rhodesia Flight 827, the Umniati, was a scheduled flight between Kariba and Salisbury that was shot down on 12 February 1979 by Zimbabwe People's Revolutionary Army (ZIPRA) guerrillas using a Strela 2 missile soon after take-off. [SEP] [CLS] The circumstances were very similar to that of Air Rhodesia Flight 825 five months earlier. [SEP] [CLS] To date, it remains the deadliest aviation incident in Rhodesia (now Zimbabwe).
