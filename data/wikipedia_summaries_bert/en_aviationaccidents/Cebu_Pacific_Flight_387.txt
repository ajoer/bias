Cebu Pacific Flight 387 was a domestic Cebu Pacific flight from Manila's Ninoy Aquino International Airport to Lumbia Airport in Cagayan de Oro City on Mindanao. [CLS] [SEP] On 2 February 1998, the 31-year-old McDonnell Douglas DC-9-32 crashed on the slopes of Mount Sumagaya in Gingoog City, Misamis Oriental. [CLS] [SEP] The incident resulted in the deaths of all 104 passengers and crew on board the aircraft. [CLS] [SEP]
