[CLS] Daniela Samulski (31 May 1984 – 22 May 2018) was a German swimmer who won three medals at the 2009 World Championships. [SEP] [CLS] She competed at the 2000 and 2008 Olympics in five events, and her best achievement was placing fourth place in the 4 × 100 m freestyle relay in 2000. [SEP] [CLS] Samulski retired in early 2011 to concentrate on her studies.On May 22, 2018, nine days before her 34th birthday, Samulski died of cancer. [SEP] [CLS]
