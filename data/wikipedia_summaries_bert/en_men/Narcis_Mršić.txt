Narcis (M923) is a Tripartite-class minehunter of the Belgian Naval Component, launched in 1990, at the Mercantile-Belyard shipyard in Rupelmonde and christened by Mrs. [CLS] [SEP] Lafosse-De Backer, the wife of the then Mayor of Mons, on 14 March 1991. [CLS] [SEP] The patronage of Narcis was accepted by the city of Mons. [CLS] [SEP] It was the ninth of the Belgian Tripartite-class minehunters. [CLS] [SEP] The Belgian government chose to deploy the ship as part of its involvement with enforcing the Libyan no-fly zone. [CLS] [SEP]
