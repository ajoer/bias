Tatyana Viktorovna Lysenko (Russian: Татьяна Викторовна Лысенко, born October 9, 1983 in Bataysk) is a Russian hammer thrower. [CLS] [SEP] A two-time World Champion, her career has been blighted by repeated doping infractions. [CLS] [SEP] In February 2019, the Court of Arbitration for Sport handed her an eight-year ban for doping, starting from 2 July 2016. [CLS] [SEP]
