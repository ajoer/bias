[CLS] Prinair Flight 191 was a Prinair (Puerto Rico International Airlines) flight from Luis Muñoz Marín International Airport in San Juan, Puerto Rico, to Mercedita Airport in Ponce, Puerto Rico. [SEP] [CLS] At approximately 11:15pm on 24 June 1972, the aircraft crashed while attempting to land at Mercedita Airport. [SEP] [CLS] Five people died in the accident. [SEP] [CLS] and the remaining people were injured. [SEP] [CLS]
