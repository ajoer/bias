American Airlines Flight 383 was a nonstop flight from New York City to Cincinnati on November 8, 1965. [CLS] [SEP] The aircraft was a Boeing 727, with 57 passengers, and 5 crew on board. [CLS] [SEP] The aircraft crashed on final approach to the Cincinnati/Northern Kentucky International Airport located in Hebron, Kentucky, United States. [CLS] [SEP] Only three passengers and one flight attendant survived the crash. [CLS] [SEP]
