The San Narciso Hurricane was the ninth and last known hurricane of the 1867 Atlantic hurricane season. [CLS] [SEP] Forming late in the month of October, the hurricane, the costliest and deadliest storm of the 1867 Atlantic hurricane season, caused at least 811 deaths in Saint Thomas (Danish West Indies) and Captaincy General of Puerto Rico and at least $1 million (1867 USD) in damage.
