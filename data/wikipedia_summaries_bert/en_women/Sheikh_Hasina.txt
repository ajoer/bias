Sheikh Hasina (Bengali: শেখ হাসিনা; English: , SHAYK hə-SEE-nə; born 28 September 1947), also known by her married name Sheikh Hasina Wazed (Bengali: শেখ হাসিনা ওয়াজেদ), is a Bangladeshi politician serving as the 10th Prime Minister of Bangladesh, having held the office since January 2009. [CLS] [SEP] Having previously served as Prime Minister for five years, she is the longest-serving Prime Minister in the history of Bangladesh. [CLS] [SEP] Hasina is the daughter of Bangladesh's first President Sheikh Mujibur Rahman, the eldest of his five children. [CLS] [SEP] Her political career has spanned more than four decades. [CLS] [SEP] She previously served as Opposition Leader from 1986 to 1990, and 1991 to 1995, then as Prime Minister from 1996 to 2001. [CLS] [SEP] She has been leading the Bangladesh Awami League (AL) since 1981. [CLS] [SEP] In 2008, she returned as Prime Minister with a landslide victory. [CLS] [SEP] In January 2014, she became Prime Minister for a third term in an unopposed election, as it was boycotted by the Opposition, and criticised by international observers. [CLS] [SEP] She won a fourth term in December 2018, following an election marred with violence and criticised by the Opposition as being rigged. [CLS] [SEP] Hasina is considered one of the most powerful women in the world, ranking 26th on Forbes' list of The World's 100 Most Powerful Women in 2018, and 30th in 2017. [CLS] [SEP] She has also made a list of "top 100 Global Thinkers" of the present decade. [CLS] [SEP] Hasina is a member of the Council of Women World Leaders, an international network of current and former female presidents and prime ministers.She has been praised for economic growth under her tenure.
