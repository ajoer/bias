[CLS] On 9 March 2016, an Antonov An-26 transport aircraft operated by True Aviation crashed 500 metres from Cox's Bazar Airport in Bangladesh, from where it had taken off shortly before. [SEP] [CLS] The twin-engine plane was attempting to return to the airport after experiencing an engine failure. [SEP] [CLS] Three of the four crew members on board were killed in the accident.
