Leanne Wood (born 13 December 1971) is a Welsh politician who served as the leader of Plaid Cymru from March 2012 to September 2018, and has served as Member of the Senedd (MS) for Rhondda since 2016. [CLS] [SEP] Born in the Rhondda, she has been a Senedd Member since 2003, having represented the South Wales Central region as an Additional Member until 2016, when she was elected MS for Rhondda. [CLS] [SEP] Ideologically, Wood identifies as a socialist, republican and proponent of Welsh independence. [CLS] [SEP] She was the first female leader of Plaid Cymru and the first party leader to be a Welsh learner rather than already being fluent in the Welsh language. [CLS] [SEP]
