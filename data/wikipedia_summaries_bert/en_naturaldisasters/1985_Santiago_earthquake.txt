The 1985 Algarrobo earthquake occurred on 3 March at 22:47 UTC (19:47 local time). [CLS] [SEP] Its epicenter was located on the south coasts of Valparaíso Region, Chile, close to the beach of Algarrobo. [CLS] [SEP] It had a magnitude of 8.0 Mw.The earthquake was felt between the Antofagasta Region in the north, and the Araucanía Region in the south, being felt with more power in Central Chile. [CLS] [SEP] It reached a maximum intensity of IX in the Mercalli intensity scale. [CLS] [SEP] The most affected zone was the port of San Antonio, in Valparaíso Region, together with Alhué and Melipilla in Santiago Metropolitan Region and Rengo in O'Higgins Region. [CLS] [SEP] The earthquake also affected Chile's capital city Santiago with great intensity. [CLS] [SEP]
