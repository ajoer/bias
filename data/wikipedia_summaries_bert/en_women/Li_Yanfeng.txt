Li Yanfeng (simplified Chinese: 李艳凤; traditional Chinese: 李艷鳳; pinyin: Lǐ Yànfèng, born May 15, 1979) is a Chinese discus thrower. [CLS] [SEP] Li won China's first discus world title.Her personal best throw is 67.98 metres, achieved in June 2011 in Schönebeck. [CLS] [SEP] The Chinese, and Asian, record is currently held by Xiao Yanling with 71.68 metres. [CLS] [SEP]
