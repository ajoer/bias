Atlas Air Flight 3591 was a scheduled domestic cargo flight operating for Amazon Air between Miami International Airport and George Bush Intercontinental Airport in Houston. [CLS] [SEP] On February 23, 2019, the Boeing 767-375ER(BCF) used for this flight crashed into Trinity Bay during approach into Houston, killing the two crew members and one passenger on board. [CLS] [SEP] The accident occurred near Anahuac, Texas, east of Houston, shortly before 12:45 CST (18:45 UTC). [CLS] [SEP] Debris was found in the shallow waters of Trinity Bay, ranging from small articles of clothing to large aircraft parts. [CLS] [SEP] This was the first fatal crash of a Boeing 767 freighter. [CLS] [SEP]
