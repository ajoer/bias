[CLS] Đorđe Gagić (Serbian Cyrillic: Ђорђе Гагић, born December 28, 1990) is a Serbian professional basketball player who last played for Igokea of the ABA. [SEP] [CLS] He also represented the Serbian national basketball team in international competition. [SEP] [CLS] Standing at 6 ft 11 in (2.11 m), he is a center who can also cover the power forward position. [SEP] [CLS]
