[CLS] The 1949 Khait (Hoit) earthquake occurred at 09:45 local time (03:53 UTC) on 10 July in the Gharm Oblast region of the Tajik SSR of the Soviet Union (within the modern boundary of Tajikistan). [SEP] [CLS] It had a magnitude of 7.5 and triggered a series of landslides that together led to 7,200 deaths. [SEP] [CLS]
