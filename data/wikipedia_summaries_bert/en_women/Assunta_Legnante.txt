[CLS] Assunta Legnante (born 14 May 1978 in Naples) is a visually impaired Italian shot putter. [SEP] [CLS] She has competed in both the Olympics and the Paralympics, one of the few athletes to do so. [SEP] [CLS] As of February 2013, she is the current F11 shot put world record holder.
