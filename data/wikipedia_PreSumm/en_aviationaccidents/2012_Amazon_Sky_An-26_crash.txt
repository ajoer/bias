[CLS] On 17 December 2012, an Antonov An-26 (registration OB-1887-P) operated by Amazon Sky, crashed in Tomas District. [SEP] [CLS] The aircraft had been flying from Peru's capital of Lima to an airfield of the argentinian company Pluspetrol in the Cusco Region and was carrying a crew of 4 people. [SEP] [CLS] The accident occurred at about 15:42 local time as the aircraft firstly touched a mountain peak, then crashed in steep terrain. [SEP] [CLS] The site of the accident was only found the day after the crash. [SEP] [CLS] The investigation commission found out that failure of both engines due to ice had caused the crash. [SEP] [CLS] The crew had failed to properly use the anti-icing. [SEP] [CLS]
