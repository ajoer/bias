[CLS] Vietnam Airlines Flight 815 was a scheduled Vietnam Airlines flight from Ho Chi Minh City to Phnom Penh's Pochentong International Airport. [SEP] [CLS] The Tupolev Tu-134B-3 aircraft (built in 1984) crashed on final approach approximately 800 metres (2,600 ft; 870 yd) short of the runway, killing 65 of the 66 people on board. [SEP] [CLS] The aircraft was completely destroyed. [SEP] [CLS]
