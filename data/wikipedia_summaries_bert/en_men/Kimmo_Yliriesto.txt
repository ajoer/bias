Kimmo Yliriesto (born 28 January 1983) is a Finnish former ski jumper who competed from 2000 to 2012. [CLS] [SEP] He won a bronze medal in the team competition at the 2000 Junior World Ski Championships, followed by a team gold medal in the following year's event. [CLS] [SEP] He made his World Cup debut on 3 December 2000, finishing 56th in Kuopio. [CLS] [SEP] He also did well at Continental Cup level, with many top-10 finishes and two wins. [CLS] [SEP] He achieved his first World Cup points with a tenth place in Hakuba on 24 January 2002. [CLS] [SEP] His best World Cup result was seventh in Sapporo on 5 February 2005. [CLS] [SEP]
