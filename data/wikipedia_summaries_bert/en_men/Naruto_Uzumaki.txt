[CLS] Naruto Uzumaki (Japanese: うずまき ナルト, Hepburn: Uzumaki Naruto) () is a fictional character in the anime and manga Naruto, created by Masashi Kishimoto. [SEP] [CLS] Serving as the eponymous protagonist of the series, he is a young ninja from the fictional village of Konohagakure. [SEP] [CLS] The villagers ridicule Naruto on account of the Nine-Tailed Demon Fox—a malevolent creature that attacked Konohagakure—that was sealed away in Naruto's body. [SEP] [CLS] Despite this, he aspires to become his village's leader, the Hokage. [SEP] [CLS] His carefree, optimistic and boisterous personality enables him to befriend other Konohagakure ninja, as well as ninja from other villages. [SEP] [CLS] Naruto appears in the series' films and in other media related to the franchise, including video games and original video animations (OVA), as well as the sequel Boruto: Naruto Next Generations, where his son, Boruto Uzumaki, is the protagonist. [SEP] [CLS] When creating Naruto for the initial part of the series, Kishimoto kept the character "simple and stupid", while giving him many attributes of an ideal hero. [SEP] [CLS] Kishimoto gave Naruto a dark side by adding tragedy to the character's past. [SEP] [CLS] He has revised Naruto's image many times, providing the character with different clothes intended to appeal to Western audiences and to make him easier to illustrate. [SEP] [CLS] Kishimoto changed his design for Part II of the storyline, which starts two-and-a-half years after Part I. [SEP] [CLS] Naruto is voiced by Junko Takeuchi in the original animated series and Maile Flanagan in the English adaptations. [SEP] [CLS] Merchandise based on Naruto includes figurines and keychains. [SEP] [CLS] Naruto's character development has been praised by anime and manga publications and has drawn scholarly attention. [SEP] [CLS] Although some initially saw him as a typical manga and anime protagonist comparable to those in other shōnen manga, others have praised his personality and character development as he avoids stereotypes typically seen in similar media. [SEP] [CLS] The character has also been the subject of researches in literature, making him stand out in fiction based on his traits and growth. [SEP] [CLS]
