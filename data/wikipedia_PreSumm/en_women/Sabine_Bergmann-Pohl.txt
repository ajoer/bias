[CLS] Sabine Bergmann-Pohl (née Schulz; German: [zaˈbiːnə ˈbɛʁkman poːl]; born 20 April 1946) is a German doctor and politician. [SEP] [CLS] A member of the Christian Democratic Union of Germany (CDU), she was president of the People's Chamber of East Germany from April to October 1990. [SEP] [CLS] As such, she served as head of state of East Germany until its merger into West Germany in October. [SEP] [CLS] After the reunification of Germany, she served in the government of Chancellor Helmut Kohl. [SEP] [CLS]
