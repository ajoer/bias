[CLS] Marvin Martin (French pronunciation: ​[maʁ.vin maʁ.tɛ̃] ; born 10 January 1988) is a French footballer who plays for Ligue 2 side FC Chambly. [SEP] [CLS] He plays as a creative play-making midfielder and is described as a player with "very good technique on the ball" and "excellent vision", which compensates for his relatively small frame. [SEP] [CLS] Martin is a former France international, having appeared for his country at UEFA Euro 2012. [SEP] [CLS]
