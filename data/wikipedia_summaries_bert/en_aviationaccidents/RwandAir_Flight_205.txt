RwandAir Flight 205 was a Canadair CRJ-100 that crashed into the Terminal Building after an emergency landing at Kigali, Rwanda killing one passenger. [CLS] [SEP] The flight was operated by JetLink Express on behalf of RwandAir. [CLS] [SEP] In the aftermath of the accident, RwandAir suspended all operations with JetLink Express. [CLS] [SEP]
