[CLS] Louis T. [SEP] [CLS] Hunter is an Australian actor. [SEP] [CLS] He was born in Glebe, one of the central districts of Sydney. [SEP] [CLS] After training in numerous acting and theatre programs as a child and attending a performing arts high school, Hunter began his professional acting career in 2009 when he booked a leading role on the Australian TV show Out of the Blue. [SEP] [CLS] He then quickly followed that up with a role in the stage production of The War of the Roses at the Sydney Theatre Company alongside Cate Blanchett. [SEP] [CLS] Hunter made his American debut in the TV show The Secret Circle. [SEP] [CLS] He currently appears as Nick Stratos in The Fosters. [SEP] [CLS] Hunter also appears in the 2018 BBC One/Netflix production, Troy: Fall of a City.Louis currently resides in Los Angeles. [SEP] [CLS]
