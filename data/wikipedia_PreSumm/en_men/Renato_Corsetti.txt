[CLS] Renato Corsetti (born 29 March 1941) is an Italian Esperantist who served as President of the Universal Esperanto Association between 2001 and 2007. [SEP] [CLS] Born in Rome, Corsetti is a staunch defender of the idea that the people of the world should be able to communicate in a neutral and easy international language. [SEP] [CLS] He teaches psycholinguistics at the Sapienza University of Rome. [SEP] [CLS] He also is an associate professor at the International Academy of Sciences San Marino.
