[CLS] The Western Wisconsin Derecho was a derecho, or severe weather system, that occurred through several counties of Western Wisconsin on July 15, 1980. [SEP] [CLS] It caused $240 million in damage (1980 dollars) -- the largest storm damage total in Wisconsin history to that point. [SEP] [CLS] Three people were killed. [SEP] [CLS] To this day, the storm is still referred to as The July 15th Storm, July 15th, 1980, or simply The Storm. [SEP] [CLS]
