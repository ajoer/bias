The 1974 Zhaotong earthquake occurred at 19:25 UTC on 10 May. [CLS] [SEP] It had a magnitude that was measured at 7.0 on the moment magnitude scale and 7.1 on the surface wave magnitude scale. [CLS] [SEP] It had a maximum perceived intensity of IX (Violent) on the Mercalli intensity scale. [CLS] [SEP] The epicenter was located in Zhaotong prefecture in Yunnan province and it caused between 1,641 and 20,000 deaths. [CLS] [SEP]
