Judith of Thuringia (Czech: Judita Durynská; c. [CLS] [SEP] 1135 – 9 September after 1174), a member of the Ludovingian dynasty, was Queen consort of Bohemia from 1158 until 1172 as the second wife of King Vladislaus II. [CLS] [SEP] She was the second Queen of Bohemia after Świętosława of Poland, wife of King Vratislaus II, had received the title in 1085. [CLS] [SEP]
