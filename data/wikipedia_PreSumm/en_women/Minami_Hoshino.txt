[CLS] "Influencer" (インフルエンサー, Infuruensā) is the 17th single by Japanese idol girl group Nogizaka46. [SEP] [CLS] It was released on 22 March 2017. [SEP] [CLS] It reached number-one on the weekly Oricon Singles Chart with 874,528 copies sold. [SEP] [CLS] It was also number-one on the Billboard Japan Hot 100. [SEP] [CLS] The song won the Grand Prix at the 59th Japan Record Awards. [SEP] [CLS]
