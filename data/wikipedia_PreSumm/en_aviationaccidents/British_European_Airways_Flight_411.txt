[CLS] British European Airways Flight 411 crashed on approach to Manchester Airport after a flight from Amsterdam Schiphol International Airport on 14 March 1957 and hit a house in Wythenshawe. [SEP] [CLS] All on board, 20 passengers and crew, died in the crash as did two people in the house. [SEP] [CLS] The aircraft involved was a Vickers Viscount registration G-ALWE operated by British European Airways. [SEP] [CLS] The cause of the crash was metal fatigue in flaps causing loss of control. [SEP] [CLS]
