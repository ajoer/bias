[CLS] Severe Tropical Cyclone Drena was a powerful tropical cyclone that caused significant damage throughout New Zealand. [SEP] [CLS] A tropical depression formed on January 2, 1997. [SEP] [CLS] After crossing Vanuatu and emerging into the Coral Sea, the storm began to intensify, and by January 6, had attained peak intensity. [SEP] [CLS] After attaining peak intensity, Drena began to weaken. [SEP] [CLS] Cyclone Drena later brushed New Caledonia, bringing some damage. [SEP] [CLS] After transitioning into an extratropical cyclone on January 9, Drena brought impact to New Zealand. [SEP] [CLS] Damage totaled to $6.7 million and 3 people were killed. [SEP] [CLS] Roughly 140 people were evacuated after 44 homes were flooded. [SEP] [CLS] After the season, Drena was retired. [SEP] [CLS]
