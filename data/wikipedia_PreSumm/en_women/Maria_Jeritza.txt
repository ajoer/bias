[CLS] Maria Jeritza (6 October 1887 – 10 July 1982) was a Czech soprano singer, long associated with the Vienna State Opera (1912–1935) and the Metropolitan Opera (1921–1932 and 1951). [SEP] [CLS] Her rapid rise to fame, beauty and personality earned her the nickname "The Moravian Thunderbolt". [SEP] [CLS]
