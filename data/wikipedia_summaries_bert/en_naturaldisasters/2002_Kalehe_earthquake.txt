The 2002 Kalehe earthquake occurred on October 24 at 06:08 UTC. [CLS] [SEP] This earthquake had a magnitude Mw 6.2, and the epicenter was located in Democratic Republic of the Congo, near Lake Kivu. [CLS] [SEP] Eight people were reported dead. [CLS] [SEP] Building damage was reported in Goma, Lwiro, Kalehe, and Mugeri. [CLS] [SEP] The seismicity, volcanism, and uplift in the basin of Lake Kivu delimits the rift of a tectonic plate. [CLS] [SEP] Lake Kivu belongs to the western branch of the East African Rift System (EARS). [CLS] [SEP] The western branch is usually divided into several segments, and Lake Kivu belongs to the northern segment. [CLS] [SEP]
