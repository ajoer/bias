[CLS] Markus Weinzierl (born 28 December 1974) is a German football coach and former player who last managed VfB Stuttgart.As the manager of Jahn Regensburg, a position he held from 2008 to 2012, Weinzierl achieved promotion into the 2. [SEP] [CLS] Bundesliga. [SEP] [CLS] On 17 May 2012, Weinzierl was appointed the new manager of the Bundesliga club FC Augsburg, and was there for four years until he left the club to join Schalke 04.
