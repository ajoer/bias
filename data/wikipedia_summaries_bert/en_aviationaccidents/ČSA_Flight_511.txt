[CLS] ČSA Flight 511 was a flight operated by an Ilyushin Il-18 that crashed in Gräfenberg near Nürnberg on March 28, 1961 during flight across West Germany. [SEP] [CLS] further details see: Report in: Letadlo ČSA se zřítilo u Norimberka. [SEP] [CLS] Soviet investigators refused this conclusion and stated that the accident was caused by an explosion near the tail of the plane. [SEP] [CLS] On July 12, 1961, another Ilyushin Il-18 operating on the same flight, ČSA OK-511, crashed near Casablanca-Anfa Airport in Morocco, killing all 72 people on board. [SEP] [CLS]
