[CLS] Bhoja Air Flight 213 was a domestic scheduled passenger flight operated by the Pakistani airline Bhoja Air from Karachi to Islamabad. [SEP] [CLS] On 20 April 2012, the Boeing 737-200 aircraft serving the route crashed in bad weather during its final approach to land. [SEP] [CLS] All 121 passengers and 6 crew members aboard were killed. [SEP] [CLS] It was the inaugural flight of Bhoja Air's second daily service on this route. [SEP] [CLS]
