British European Airways Flight S200P was a short-haul flight from London-Northolt Airport to Glasgow-Renfrew Airport, Scotland. [CLS] [SEP] On 21 April 1948, while on approach to Renfrew, a Vickers VC.1 Viking, registration G-AIVE, crashed into Irish Law Mountain in North Ayrshire, Scotland. [CLS] [SEP] No one died in the accident, but 13 of the 20 passengers and crew were injured, and the aircraft was damaged beyond repair. [CLS] [SEP]
