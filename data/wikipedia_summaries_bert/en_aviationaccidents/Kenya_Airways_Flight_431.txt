[CLS] Kenya Airways Flight 431 was an international scheduled Abidjan–Lagos–Nairobi passenger service. [SEP] [CLS] On 30 January 2000, the Airbus A310-300 serving the flight crashed into the sea off the coast of Côte d'Ivoire, shortly after takeoff from Félix-Houphouët-Boigny International Airport, Abidjan. [SEP] [CLS] There were 179 people on board, of whom 169 were passengers. [SEP] [CLS] Only ten people survived in what was the first fatal accident for Kenya Airways and the deadliest crash involving the Airbus A310. [SEP] [CLS]
