[CLS] Air India Flight 245 was a scheduled Air India passenger flight that crashed into Mont Blanc, France on the morning of 3 November 1950. [SEP] [CLS] The plane operating the flight was a Lockheed L-749A Constellation named Malabar Princess, registered VT-CQP. [SEP] [CLS] It was piloted by Captain Alan R. [SEP] [CLS] Saint, 34, and co-pilot V. [SEP] [CLS] Y. [SEP] [CLS] Korgaokar. [SEP] [CLS] It was carrying 40 passengers and 8 crew on a Bombay-Istanbul-Geneva-London route. [SEP] [CLS] While over France, descending towards Geneva Airport, the flight crashed into the French Alps, killing all on board. [SEP] [CLS]
