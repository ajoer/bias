Annika Beck (German pronunciation: [b…õk]; born 16 February 1994) is a retired German tennis player. [CLS] [SEP] She started playing tennis at the age of four when introduced to the game by her parents. [CLS] [SEP] A baseliner whose favorite shot is forehand, and favorite surface is hardcourt, she was coached by Jacub Zahlava and Sebastian Sachs. [CLS] [SEP] Beck won two singles titles and one doubles title on the WTA Tour, as well as seven singles titles on the ITF Women's Circuit. [CLS] [SEP] On 18 July 2016, she reached her best singles ranking of world No. [CLS] [SEP] 37, and on the same date, she peaked at No. [CLS] [SEP] 84 in the WTA doubles rankings. [CLS] [SEP]
