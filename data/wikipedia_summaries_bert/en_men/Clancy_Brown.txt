[CLS] Clarence John Brown III (born January 5, 1959) is an American actor and voice actor. [SEP] [CLS] He is known for his work as "Viking" Lofgren in Bad Boys (1983), the Kurgan in Highlander (1986), Byron Hadley in The Shawshank Redemption (1994), Sgt. [SEP] [CLS] Zim in Starship Troopers (1998), Mr. [SEP] [CLS] Krabs in SpongeBob SquarePants (1999–present), Brother Justin Crowe on the HBO series Carnivàle (2003–2005) and Kelvin Inman on the ABC series Lost (2004–2010). [SEP] [CLS] He has also provided voices for Lex Luthor, beginning with Superman: The Animated Series (1996–2000), Doctor Neo Cortex and Uka Uka in the Crash Bandicoot video games (1997–2003), Hades in God of War III (2010), Surtur in Thor: Ragnarok (2017) and Lt. [SEP] [CLS] Hank Anderson in Detroit: Become Human (2018). [SEP] [CLS]
