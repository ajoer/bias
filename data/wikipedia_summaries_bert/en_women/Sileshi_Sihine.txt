Sileshi Sihine (Amharic: ስለሺ ስህኔ; born January 29, 1983 in Sheno) is an Ethiopian long-distance runner. [CLS] [SEP] Sihine won silver medals in the 10,000 metres at both the 2004 Athens Olympics and the 2008 Beijing Olympics as well as at the 2005 World Championships and 2007 World Championships as well as a bronze medal in 2003. [CLS] [SEP] He also picked up a silver medal in the 5000 m at the 2005 World Championships. [CLS] [SEP]
