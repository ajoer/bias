Leopoldstadt (German pronunciation: [ˈleːopɔltˌʃtat] (listen); Bavarian: Leopoidstod, "Leopold-Town") is the 2nd municipal District of Vienna (German: 2. [CLS] [SEP] Bezirk). [CLS] [SEP] There are 103,233 inhabitants (as of 2016-01-01) over 19.27 km2 (7 sq mi). [CLS] [SEP] It is situated in the heart of the city and, together with Brigittenau (20th district), forms a large island surrounded by the Danube Canal and, to the north, the Danube. [CLS] [SEP] It is named after Leopold I, Holy Roman Emperor. [CLS] [SEP] Due to its relatively high percentage of Jewish inhabitants (38.5 per cent in 1923, i. [CLS] [SEP] e., before the Holocaust), Leopoldstadt gained the nickname Mazzesinsel ("Matzo Island"). [CLS] [SEP] This context was a significant aspect for the district twinning with the New York City borough Brooklyn in 2007. [CLS] [SEP]
