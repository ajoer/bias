Margaret Beryl Jones, Baroness Jones of Whitchurch (born 22 May 1955) is a British Labour Peer and previously a trade union official and Labour politician. [CLS] [SEP] She was Chair of the Labour Party from 2000 to 2001. [CLS] [SEP] Jones was Director of Policy and Public Affairs of the trade union UNISON until 2006. [CLS] [SEP] In 1979 she became a regional official of National Union of Public Employees (NUPE), which merged into UNISON. [CLS] [SEP] She has a background as a housing campaigner and environmentalist as well as fighting low pay and discrimination at work. [CLS] [SEP] She was previously a trustee of Shelter and WRAP as well as being on the board of Circle Housing Group. [CLS] [SEP] She is currently a board member of Ombudsman Services; Chair of Rothamsted Enterprises; Development Board member of ClientEarth and President of Friends of the South Downs. [CLS] [SEP]
