[CLS] Valerie L. [SEP] [CLS] Thomas (born February 8, 1943) is an American scientist and inventor. [SEP] [CLS] She invented the Illusion Transmitter, for which she received a patent in 1980. [SEP] [CLS] She was responsible for developing the digital media formats image processing systems used in the early years of the Landsat program. [SEP] [CLS]
