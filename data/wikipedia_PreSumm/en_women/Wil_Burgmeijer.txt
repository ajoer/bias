[CLS] Wilhelmina Maria "Wil" Burgmeijer (born 23 April 1947) is a retired Dutch speed skater who won a bronze all-round medal at the 1962 world championships. [SEP] [CLS] She competed at the 1968 Winter Olympics in 500 m and 3,000 m and finished fifth in the latter event.Personal bests: 500 m – 44.9 (1970) 1000 m – 1:31.93 (1972) 1500 m – 2:19.96 (1972) 3000 m – 4:55.1 (1969)
