The 2010 Salta earthquake occurred on February 27 at 12:45:36 local time in Salta Province, Argentina and had a magnitude of 6.3. [CLS] [SEP] It occurred less than 12 hours after the far larger magnitude 8.8 Chile earthquake, which killed 525 people. [CLS] [SEP] It was initially thought to be an aftershock of the Chile earthquake, but scientists later established that the earthquakes were unrelated. [CLS] [SEP] The epicenter was about 15 miles north of the city of Salta. [CLS] [SEP] The quake killed two people, and injured dozens (possibly up to 100 people). [CLS] [SEP]
