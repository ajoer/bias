Carolyn Talcott (born June 14, 1941) is an American computer scientist known for work in formal reasoning, especially as it relates to computers, cryptanalysis and systems biology. [CLS] [SEP] She is currently the program director of the Symbolic Systems Biology group at SRI International.She is currently the co-editor-in-chief of Higher-Order and Symbolic Computation.
