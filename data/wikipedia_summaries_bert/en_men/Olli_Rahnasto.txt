Olli Rahnasto (born 28 December 1965) is a retired professional tennis player from Finland. [CLS] [SEP] Rahnasto turned pro in 1982. [CLS] [SEP] During his career, he won two ATP Tour doubles titles and reached a career high doubles ranking of No. [CLS] [SEP] 79 in 1986. [CLS] [SEP] He was a member of the Finland Davis Cup team, and led the team to the World Group Qualifying round in 1990. [CLS] [SEP] He retired in 1995. [CLS] [SEP]
