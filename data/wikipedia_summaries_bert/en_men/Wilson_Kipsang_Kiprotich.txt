Wilson Kipsang Kiprotich (born 15 March 1982) is a Kenyan athlete who specialises in long-distance running, competing in events ranging from 10 km to the marathon. [CLS] [SEP] He was the bronze medallist in the marathon at the 2012 Summer Olympics. [CLS] [SEP] He is the former world record holder in the marathon with a time of 2:03:23, which he set at the 2013 Berlin Marathon. [CLS] [SEP] He has run under 2 hours 4 minutes for the marathon on four separate occasions. [CLS] [SEP] Kipsang is a double winner of the Frankfurt Marathon (2010 and 2011) and has also won the London Marathon twice (2012 and 2014), New York Marathon (2014), and Tokyo Marathon (2017). [CLS] [SEP] He holds the twelfth best time over the half marathon distance (58:59 minutes). [CLS] [SEP]
