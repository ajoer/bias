[CLS] Typhoon Peggy, known in the Philippines as Typhoon Gading, was a very intense super typhoon that formed during the 1986 Pacific typhoon season. [SEP] [CLS] At the time of its occurrence, the typhoon was documented by PAGASA as the eight strongest Philippine typhoon on record.Typhoon Peggy made landfall in the Philippines on July 9 and made landfall in China on July 11. [SEP] [CLS] Peggy caused at least 333 deaths and $512 million (1986 USD) in damage. [SEP] [CLS]
