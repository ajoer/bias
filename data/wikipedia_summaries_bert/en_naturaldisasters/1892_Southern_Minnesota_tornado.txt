The 1892 Southern Minnesota tornado was a large and deadly tornado that struck several communities across southern Minnesota on Wednesday, June 15, 1892. [CLS] [SEP] It had a width of 500 yards (457 m) wide and was on the ground for 40 miles (64 km). [CLS] [SEP] The twister was estimated to have been an F5 on the Fujita scale. [CLS] [SEP] It killed 12 people in and injured 76 others. [CLS] [SEP]
