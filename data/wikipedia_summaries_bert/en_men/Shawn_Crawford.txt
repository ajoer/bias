[CLS] Shawn Crawford (born January 14, 1978) is a retired American sprint athlete. [SEP] [CLS] He competed in the 100 meters and 200 meters events. [SEP] [CLS] He won gold at the 2004 Summer Olympics and silver at the 2008 Summer Olympics in the 200 meters. [SEP] [CLS] He originally finished 4th in the race but after the 2nd and 3rd-place winners were disqualified, he moved up to a silver. [SEP] [CLS] On April 17, 2013, Crawford was suspended for two years for missing out-of-competition drug tests. [SEP] [CLS] His coach, Bob Kersee claimed that Crawford retired after the 2012 United States Olympic Trials and USA Track & Field said he filed retirement papers in 2013. [SEP] [CLS]
