[CLS] Pan Am Flight 830 was a flight from Narita International Airport in Tokyo, Japan to Honolulu International Airport in Hawaii. [SEP] [CLS] On August 11, 1982, the Boeing 747-121 serving the flight, nicknamed "Clipper Ocean Rover" was en route to Hawaii when the airplane was damaged by a bomb that had been placed on board. [SEP] [CLS] Despite the damage to the aircraft, Captain James E. [SEP] [CLS] (Skipper) O'Halloran III of Spokane, Washington was able to land in Honolulu safely. [SEP] [CLS] One person was killed while 284 survived. [SEP] [CLS] 16 of them were wounded. [SEP] [CLS]
