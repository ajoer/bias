The 2003 Bingöl earthquake hit eastern Turkey with a moment magnitude of 6.4 and a maximum Mercalli intensity of IX (Violent) on 1 May at 03:27 local time. [CLS] [SEP] The epicenter of this strike-slip earthquake was in Bingöl Province, 15 km north of Bingöl. [CLS] [SEP] At least 177 people were killed, and 625 buildings collapsed or suffered heavy damage in the affected region. [CLS] [SEP] Eighty-four of the fatalities occurred when a dormitory block collapsed in a boarding school in Celtiksuyu. [CLS] [SEP]
