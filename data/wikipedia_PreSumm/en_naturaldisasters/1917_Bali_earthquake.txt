[CLS] The 1917 Bali earthquake occurred at 06:50 local time on 21 January (23:11 on 20 January UTC). [SEP] [CLS] It had an estimated magnitude of 6.6 on the surface wave magnitude scale and had a maximum perceived intensity of IX (Violent) on the Mercalli intensity scale. [SEP] [CLS] It caused widespread damage across Bali, particularly in the south of the island. [SEP] [CLS] It triggered many landslides, which caused 80% of the 1500 casualties. [SEP] [CLS]
