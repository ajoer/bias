[CLS] S7 Airlines Flight 778 (RU778/SBI778) was an Airbus A310-300 on a scheduled domestic passenger flight, flying from Moscow Domodedovo to Irkutsk, when it crashed upon landing at Irkutsk International Airport at 07:44 local time on 9 July 2006 (8 July, 22:44 UTC). [SEP] [CLS] The plane overshot the runway, sliding over several hundred metres of wet runway and grass. [SEP] [CLS] It crashed through a concrete barricade, hit a group of private garages and burst into flames. [SEP] [CLS] Television pictures showed smoking ruins of the Airbus with only the tail section intact. [SEP] [CLS] It took two hours for local firefighters from five different fire stations to extinguish the blaze.A total of 125 people were killed in the accident. [SEP] [CLS] It was the deadliest plane crash in Irkutsk since Vladivostok Air Flight 352 in 2001. [SEP] [CLS] It was also the company's deadliest plane crash. [SEP] [CLS] Currently, it is held as the fourth deadliest plane crash in Russia and the third deadliest involving an Airbus A310, after Kenya Airways Flight 431 and Yemenia Flight 626.In the wake of the crash, most media outlets reported that a brake failure occurred in Flight 778. [SEP] [CLS] However, the final report of the Russian Interstate Aviation Committee (MAK), concluded that the cause of the crash was pilot error. [SEP] [CLS]
