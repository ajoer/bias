[CLS] Margo Ilene Seltzer is a professor and researcher in computer systems. [SEP] [CLS] She is currently the Canada 150 Research Chair in Computer Systems and the Cheriton Family Chair in Computer Science at the University of British Columbia. [SEP] [CLS] Previously, she was the Herchel Smith Professor of Computer Science at Harvard University's John A. [SEP] [CLS] Paulson School of Engineering and Applied Sciences and director at the Center for Research on Computation and Society. [SEP] [CLS]
