Anita Ama Ankyewah Asante (born 27 April 1985) is an English football defender currently playing for Chelsea in the FA WSL. [CLS] [SEP] As indicated by her name, Asante is of Ghanaian descent (the appellation "Ama" is of Akan origin, given to girls born on Saturday). [CLS] [SEP] Asante has earned 71 caps for the English national team and was selected in the Great Britain squad for the 2012 London Olympics. [CLS] [SEP] At club level, Asante has also played for English side Arsenal, and for Saint Louis Athletica, Chicago Red Stars, Washington Freedom and Sky Blue FC in Women's Professional Soccer (WPS). [CLS] [SEP] Prior signing for FC Rosengård in 2013, she spent two seasons in Sweden with Göteborg. [CLS] [SEP]
