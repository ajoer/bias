[CLS] Chanatip Sonkham or Chanapa Sonkham (Thai: ชนาธิป ซ้อนขำ; RTGS: Chanathip Sonkham; born 1 March 1991) is a female Thai taekwondo practitioner who was the bronze medalist at the 2012 Summer Olympics in the under 49 kg weight class. [SEP] [CLS] Sonkham also won bronze medals at the 2010 Asian Games and at the Asian Taekwondo Championships in 2010 and 2012. [SEP] [CLS] She won the gold medal at the 2013 World Championship
