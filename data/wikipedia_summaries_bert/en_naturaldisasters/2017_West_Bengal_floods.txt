Following heavy rain in July and August 2017, the Indian state of West Bengal was affected by severe flooding. [CLS] [SEP] The floods were reported to have caused 50 deaths since 1 August and 8 deaths in the neighbouring state of Jharkhand. [CLS] [SEP] Soon after these floods, North Bengal also witnessed floods that wreaked havoc over the seven districts and killed 3 people. [CLS] [SEP]
