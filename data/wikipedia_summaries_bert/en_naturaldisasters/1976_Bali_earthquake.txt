The 1976 Bali earthquake occurred at 15:13 local time on 14 July with a surface wave magnitude of 6.5. [CLS] [SEP] The shock occurred 5 kilometres (3.1 mi) south of the Bali Sea coast of the Buleleng Regency, and about 65 kilometres (40 mi) northwest of Denpasar. [CLS] [SEP] Up to ninety percent of houses in Buleleng Regency were seriously damaged or destroyed and the Seririt sub-district was almost completely destroyed, where a school building collapsed and trapped at least 200 students. [CLS] [SEP] 573 people are believed to have died; at least 544 in Buleleng Regency, 24 in Jembrana and 5 in Tabanan. [CLS] [SEP] Four thousand more suffered injuries and an estimated 450,000 were left temporarily homeless. [CLS] [SEP]
