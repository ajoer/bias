[CLS] Thai Airways Flight 231 was a scheduled passenger flight that crashed on 27 April 1980. [SEP] [CLS] The Hawker Siddeley HS 748 operating the flight, registration HS-THB, stalled and crashed after entering a thunderstorm on approach to Bangkok. [SEP] [CLS] The accident killed 44 out of 53 passengers and crew on board. [SEP] [CLS]
