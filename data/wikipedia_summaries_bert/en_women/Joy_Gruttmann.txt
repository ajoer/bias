[CLS] Joy Gruttmann (born 6 December 1995 in Gelsenkirchen) is a German child singer, made famous by singing the song "Schnappi, das kleine Krokodil".Joy has sung children's music for the ARD program Die Sendung mit der Maus since 1999. [SEP] [CLS] Her fifth song was "Schnappi, das kleine Krokodil," composed by her aunt Iris Gruttmann and recorded in 2001 when she was five years old. [SEP] [CLS] She performed as the voice of the character Schnappi, the namesake of the song. [SEP] [CLS] In 2004 and 2005, "Schnappi" became a No.1 hit in Germany and several other European countries such as Belgium, Netherlands, Switzerland, and Sweden. [SEP] [CLS] It also peaked in the Top 10 in Australia and New Zealand. [SEP] [CLS]
