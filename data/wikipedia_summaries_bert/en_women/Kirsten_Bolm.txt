Kirsten Bolm (born 4 March 1975 in Frechen, West Germany) is a retired German hurdler. [CLS] [SEP] Bolm's personal best is 12.59 seconds, achieved in July 2005 in the Crystal Palace Grand Prix at the Crystal Palace National Sports Centre. [CLS] [SEP] She graduated in psychology from the University of Heidelberg in 2009. [CLS] [SEP]
