[CLS] A major landslide occurred 4 miles (6.4 km) east of Oso, Washington, United States, on March 22, 2014, at 10:37 a.m. [SEP] [CLS] local time. [SEP] [CLS] A portion of an unstable hill collapsed, sending mud and debris to the south across the North Fork of the Stillaguamish River, engulfing a rural neighborhood, and covering an area of approximately 1 square mile (2.6 km2). [SEP] [CLS] Forty-three people were killed and 49 homes and other structures destroyed. [SEP] [CLS]
