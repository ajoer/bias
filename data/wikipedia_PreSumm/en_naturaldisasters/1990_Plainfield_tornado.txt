[CLS] The 1990 Plainfield tornado was a devastating tornado that occurred on the afternoon of Tuesday, August 28, 1990. [SEP] [CLS] The violent tornado killed 29 people and injured 353. [SEP] [CLS] It is the only F5 tornado ever recorded in August and the only F5 tornado to strike the Chicago area. [SEP] [CLS] There are no known videos or photographs of the tornado itself; however, in 2011 a video surfaced online showing the supercell that spawned the tornado. [SEP] [CLS] The Plainfield tornado was part of a small outbreak that produced several tornadoes in the northern US and in Canada. [SEP] [CLS]
