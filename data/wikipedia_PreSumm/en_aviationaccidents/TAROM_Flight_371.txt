[CLS] TAROM Flight 371 (RO371/ROT371) was a scheduled international passenger flight, with an Airbus A310 from Otopeni International Airport in Romania's capital Bucharest to Brussels Airport in Brussels, Belgium. [SEP] [CLS] The flight was operated by TAROM, the flag carrier of Romania. [SEP] [CLS] On 31 March 1995, the Airbus A310-324, registered as YR-LCC, entered a nose-down dive after takeoff and crashed near Balote»ôti in Romania. [SEP] [CLS] All 60 people aboard were killed in the crash.Investigation of the crash revealed that the faulty auto-throttle reduced the left engine to idle during climb. [SEP] [CLS] While this was happening, the Captain became incapacitated, leaving the First Officer overwhelmed and unable to respond properly to the failure. [SEP] [CLS] The crash was the deadliest plane crash in Romania's history. [SEP] [CLS] It was also the deadliest plane crash in TAROM's operational history. [SEP] [CLS]
