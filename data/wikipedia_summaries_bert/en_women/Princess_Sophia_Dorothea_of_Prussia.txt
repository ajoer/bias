Princess Sophia Dorothea of Prussia (German: Sophia Dorothea Marie von Preußen; 25 January 1719 – 13 November 1765) was the ninth child and fifth daughter of Frederick William I of Prussia and Sophia Dorothea of Hanover. [CLS] [SEP] By marriage, she was a Margravine of Brandenburg-Schwedt. [CLS] [SEP]
