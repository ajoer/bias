Savannah Outen is an American singer who gained popularity on YouTube. [CLS] [SEP] Outen began posting videos of herself singing on YouTube in March 2007 along with Angelika Vee and Esmee Denters. [CLS] [SEP] In 2010 she collaborated with Boyce Avenue on their sixth acoustic album, New Acoustic Sessions. [CLS] [SEP] She appeared in the 85th Annual Macy's Thanksgiving Day Parade on November 24, 2011. [CLS] [SEP]
