Sandra Sumang Pierantozzi (born 9 August 1953 in Koror) is a Palauan politician. [CLS] [SEP] She served as the country's Vice-President from 19 January 2001 to 1 January 2005. [CLS] [SEP] Pierantozzi was defeated by Camsek Chin in the 2 November 2004 vice-presidential election, winning only 28.9% of the vote. [CLS] [SEP] She serves as honorary consul of the Czech Republic in Palau. [CLS] [SEP]
