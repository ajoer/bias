[CLS] The 1989 Malawi earthquake occurred on 10 March in central Malawi, with a moment magnitude of 6.3 and a maximum Mercalli intensity of VII (Very strong). [SEP] [CLS] It was preceded by a number of foreshocks, the largest being a 5.7 Mw shock on the previous day. [SEP] [CLS] The earthquake was felt strongly throughout central Malawi, and also felt in parts of Mozambique (Niassa and Tete Provinces) and Zambia (Eastern Province). [SEP] [CLS] Nine people were killed, with many others injured or left homeless. [SEP] [CLS]
