[CLS] Zhao Bing (12 February 1272 – 19 March 1279), also known as Emperor Bing of Song or Bing, Emperor of Song (宋帝昺), was the 18th and last emperor of the Song dynasty in China. [SEP] [CLS] He was also the ninth and last emperor of the Southern Song dynasty. [SEP] [CLS] He reigned for around 313 days in 1278 and 1279. [SEP] [CLS] His reign, and the Song dynasty, ended with the Song's total defeat at the hands of the Yuan Dynasty at the naval Battle of Yamen. [SEP] [CLS] Zhao Bing and his entourage had been observing the naval combat from some coastal cliffs nearby. [SEP] [CLS] As the victorious enemy approached, Lu Xiufu seized the emperor and leaped from the clifftops, killing them both. [SEP] [CLS]
