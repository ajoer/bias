[CLS] McBriare Samuel Lanyon "Mac" DeMarco (born Vernor Winfield McBriare Smith IV, April 30, 1990) is a Canadian singer-songwriter, multi-instrumentalist and producer. [SEP] [CLS] DeMarco has released six full-length studio albums, his debut Rock and Roll Night Club (2012), 2 (2012), Salad Days (2014), Another One (2015), This Old Dog (2017), and Here Comes the Cowboy (2019). [SEP] [CLS] His style of music has been described as "blue wave" and "slacker rock", or, by DeMarco himself, "jizz jazz". [SEP] [CLS]
