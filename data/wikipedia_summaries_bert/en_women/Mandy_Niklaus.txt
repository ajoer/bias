Mandy Niklaus (born 1 March 1956) is a fencer, who competed for the SC Dynamo Berlin / Sportvereinigung (SV) Dynamo during her career. [CLS] [SEP] She won the bronze medal at the world championships by foil (fencing). [CLS] [SEP] She competed in the women's individual and team foil events for East Germany at the 1980 Summer Olympics. [CLS] [SEP]
