[CLS] Tatjana Blacher (born 18 May 1956 in Berlin) is a German actress. [SEP] [CLS] She is the daughter of composer Boris Blacher and pianist Gerty Blacher-Herzog. [SEP] [CLS] Blacher studied at the Max Reinhardt seminar and at the Lee Strasberg Theatre and Film Institute. [SEP] [CLS] She is mostly known for her role as Edith Frank in Anne Frank: The Whole Story. [SEP] [CLS] She has been on many TV shows and has a son named Josh. [SEP] [CLS] She is multi-talented. [SEP] [CLS] She has had professional training in singing and dancing. [SEP] [CLS] She comes from Berlin, Germany and speaks fluent English as well as her native German. [SEP] [CLS] She had started her career in 1997 in her debut film called Reise in die Dunkelheit. [SEP] [CLS]
