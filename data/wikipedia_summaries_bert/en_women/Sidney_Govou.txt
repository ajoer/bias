Sidney Rodrigue Noukpo Govou (French pronunciation: ​[sidnɛ ɡɔvu]; born 27 July 1979) is a French retired professional footballer who last played for Limonest in the Championnat de France Amateur 2. [CLS] [SEP] He played mainly as a winger, but occasionally was used as a striker. [CLS] [SEP] He holds the record for most Ligue 1 titles won (seven), along with Hervé Revelli and Jean-Michel Larqué of Saint-Étienne, as well as Thiago Silva and Marco Verratti of Paris Saint–Germain, and his own former teammates Grégory Coupet and Juninho of Lyon.
