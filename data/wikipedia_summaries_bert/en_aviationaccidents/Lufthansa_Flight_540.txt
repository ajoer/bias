[CLS] Lufthansa Flight 540 was a scheduled commercial flight for Lufthansa operated with a Boeing 747-130, carrying 157 people (140 passengers and 17 crew members). [SEP] [CLS] The flight was operating the final segment of its Frankfurt–Nairobi–Johannesburg route. [SEP] [CLS] On 20 November 1974 it crashed and caught fire shortly past the runway after taking off from Jomo Kenyatta International Airport in Nairobi. [SEP] [CLS] This was the first fatal accident of a Boeing 747. [SEP] [CLS]
