The California Polytechnic State University football team plane crash occurred on October 29, 1960, at 22:02 EST near Toledo, Ohio.A twin-engine Curtiss C-46 propliner, registration N1244N, operated as a domestic charter flight by Arctic Pacific, carrying the Cal Poly Mustangs college football team, crashed during takeoff at the Toledo Express Airport, southwest of Toledo. [CLS] [SEP] The aircraft, a veteran of World War II, broke in two and caught fire on impact. [CLS] [SEP] Of the 48 on board, 22 were killed, including both pilots, 16 players, a student manager, and a Cal Poly football booster. [CLS] [SEP]
