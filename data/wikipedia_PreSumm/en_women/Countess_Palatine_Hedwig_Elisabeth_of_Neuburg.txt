[CLS] Hedwig Elisabeth of Neuburg (Hedwig Elisabeth Amelia; 18 July 1673 – 10 August 1722) was a Polish princess by marriage to James Louis Sobieski. [SEP] [CLS] She was the daughter of Philip William, Duke of Neuburg, Berg and Jülich, Elector Palatine of Neuburg, and Landgravine Elisabeth Amalie of Hesse-Darmstadt. [SEP] [CLS] She was the grandmother of Charles Edward Stuart, the "Young Pretender". [SEP] [CLS]
