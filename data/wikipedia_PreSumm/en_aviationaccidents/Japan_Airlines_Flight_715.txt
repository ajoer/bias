[CLS] Japan Airlines Flight 715 (日本航空 715便, Nihonkōkū 715 Bin) was an airplane that crashed in Malaysia on 27 September 1977. [SEP] [CLS] It was a McDonnell Douglas DC-8, registration JA8051, on a flight from Haneda Airport in Tokyo, Japan to Changi International Airport in Singapore, with stopovers at Kai Tak Airport in Kowloon Bay, Hong Kong and Sultan Abdul Aziz Shah Airport in Subang, Malaysia. [SEP] [CLS] There were 69 passengers and 10 crew on board. [SEP] [CLS] It was the second deadliest aviation disaster to occur in Malaysia at the time. [SEP] [CLS]
