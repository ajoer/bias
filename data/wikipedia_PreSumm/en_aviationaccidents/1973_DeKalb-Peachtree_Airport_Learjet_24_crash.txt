[CLS] DeKalb–Peachtree Airport (IATA: PDK, ICAO: KPDK, FAA LID: PDK) is a county-owned, public-use airport in DeKalb County, Georgia, United States. [SEP] [CLS] The airport is located in the city of Chamblee, just northeast of Atlanta. [SEP] [CLS] It is also known commonly as Peachtree–DeKalb Airport, or simply PDK. [SEP] [CLS] Other names (rarely used) include Peachtree Airport, DeKalb Airport, or DeKalb County Airport. [SEP] [CLS] ASOS weather reports are produced 24 hours per day as "Chamblee". [SEP] [CLS] It has airline service with Ultimate Air Shuttle to Cincinnati and Southern Airways Express to Memphis and Destin. [SEP] [CLS] As per Federal Aviation Administration records, the airport had 1,784 passenger boardings (enplanements) in calendar year 2008, 393 enplanements in 2009, and 463 in 2010. [SEP] [CLS] It is included in the National Plan of Integrated Airport Systems for 2011–2015, which categorized it as a reliever airport. [SEP] [CLS]
