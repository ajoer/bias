[SEP] [CLS] He is usually not counted in the list of Jin emperors due to his brief reign, and was often mentioned by historians as an example of a wicked usurper. [SEP] [CLS] He was the third of the eight princes commonly associated with the War of the Eight Princes. [SEP] [CLS]
