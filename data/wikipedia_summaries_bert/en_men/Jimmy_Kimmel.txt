[CLS] James Christian Kimmel (born November 13, 1967) is an American television host, comedian, writer, actor, and producer. [SEP] [CLS] He is the host and executive producer of Jimmy Kimmel Live!, a late-night talk show that premiered on ABC on January 26, 2003, at Hollywood Masonic Temple in Hollywood, California; and on April 1, 2019, at a secondary home, the Zappos Theater on the Las Vegas Strip. [SEP] [CLS] Kimmel hosted the Primetime Emmy Awards in 2012 and 2016, and the Academy Awards in 2017 and 2018. [SEP] [CLS] Before hosting Jimmy Kimmel Live!, he was the co-host of Comedy Central's The Man Show and Win Ben Stein's Money. [SEP] [CLS] Kimmel has also produced such shows as Crank Yankers, Sports Show with Norm Macdonald, and The Andy Milonakis Show. [SEP] [CLS] In 2018, Time named him as one of "The World's 100 Most Influential People". [SEP] [CLS]
