Sexy Cora (born Carolin Ebert, civil name Carolin Wosnitza; 2 May 1987 – 20 January 2011) was a German pornographic actress, model, singer, exotic dancer, wife of Tim Wosnitza, and reality show participant.Cora was born in Berlin. [CLS] [SEP] She was one of the participants of the 10th season of Big Brother Germany. [CLS] [SEP] Cora released two music singles after her Big Brother participation: "My Love – La, La, La" and "Lass uns kicken (Alles klar wunderbar)". [CLS] [SEP]
