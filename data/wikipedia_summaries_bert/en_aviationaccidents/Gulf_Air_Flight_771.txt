Gulf Air Flight 771 was a flight from Karachi, Pakistan, to Abu Dhabi, United Arab Emirates. [CLS] [SEP] On 23 September 1983, while the Boeing 737-2P6 was on approach to Abu Dhabi International Airport, a bomb exploded in the baggage compartment. [CLS] [SEP] The plane crashed in the desert near Jebel Ali between Abu Dhabi and Dubai in the UAE. [CLS] [SEP] All five crew members and 107 passengers died. [CLS] [SEP]
