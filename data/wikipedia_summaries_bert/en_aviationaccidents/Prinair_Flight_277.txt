Prinair Flight 277 was a regular passenger flight by Puerto Rican airline Prinair, between Cyril E. [CLS] [SEP] King International Airport in Charlotte Amalie, St. [CLS] [SEP] Thomas, United States Virgin Islands and Isla Verde International Airport in Carolina, Puerto Rico, a suburb of San Juan. [CLS] [SEP] On 5 March 1969, the flight, operated by de Havilland Heron 2D N563PR, crashed into a mountain near Fajardo, killing all 19 occupants on board. [CLS] [SEP]
