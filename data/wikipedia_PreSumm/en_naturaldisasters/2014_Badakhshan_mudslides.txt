[CLS] On 2 May 2014, a pair of mudslides occurred in Argo District, Badakhshan Province, Afghanistan. [SEP] [CLS] The death toll is uncertain, the number of deaths varying from 350 to 2,700. [SEP] [CLS] Around 300 houses were buried and over 14,000 were affected. [SEP] [CLS] Rescuers responding to the initial mudslide were struck by a second mudslide, which hampered rescue efforts.
