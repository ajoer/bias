[CLS] Ursula "Ursel" Brunner (born 30 January 1941) is a retired West German freestyle swimmer. [SEP] [CLS] She competed at the 1960 and 1964 Summer Olympics and won two bronze medals in relay events in 1960.She won at least 15 national titles in the 100 m butterfly (1961–1962), 400 m individual medley (1962–1963), and 100 m (1959–1963) and 400 m freestyle (1957, 1959–1963). [SEP] [CLS] In 1963, she was selected the German Sportspersonality of the Year.In 1975 Brunner married her former coach Hans Wirth (d. [SEP] [CLS] 1988). [SEP] [CLS] She has a son. [SEP] [CLS]
