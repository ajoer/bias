[CLS] José Alexandre "Kay Rala Xanana" Gusmão GColIH, GCL, GCMG (Portuguese pronunciation: [ʒuˈzɛ ɐlɨˈʃɐ̃dɾɨ kaj ˈɾalɐ ʃɐˈnɐnɐ ɣuʒˈmɐ̃w̃]; born on 20 June 1946), popularly known simply as Xanana Gusmão; is an East Timorese politician. [SEP] [CLS] A former militant, he was the first President of the Democratic Republic of East Timor (third president overall, after two presidents during their 1970s fight for independence), serving from May 2002 to May 2007. [SEP] [CLS] He then became the fourth Prime Minister of East Timor, serving from 8 August 2007 to 16 February 2015. [SEP] [CLS] He has been Minister of Planning and Strategic Investment since February 2015. [SEP] [CLS]
