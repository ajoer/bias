[CLS] Ruth Teitelbaum (née Lichterman) (1924–1986) was one of the first computer programmers in the world. [SEP] [CLS] Teitelbaum was one of the original programmers for the ENIAC computer. [SEP] [CLS] The other five ENIAC programmers were Jean Bartik, Betty Holberton, Kathleen Antonelli, Marlyn Meltzer, and Frances Spence. [SEP] [CLS]
