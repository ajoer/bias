[CLS] The 1993 southwest-off Hokkaido earthquake (北海道南西沖地震, Hokkaidō Nansei Oki Jishin) occurred at 13:17:12 UTC on 12 July 1993 in the Sea of Japan near the island of Hokkaido. [SEP] [CLS] It had a magnitude of 7.7 on the moment magnitude scale and a maximum felt intensity of VIII (Severe) on the Mercalli intensity scale. [SEP] [CLS] It triggered a major tsunami that caused deaths on Hokkaidō and in southeastern Russia, with a total of 230 fatalities recorded. [SEP] [CLS] The island of Okushiri was hardest hit, with 165 casualties from the earthquake, the tsunami and a large landslide. [SEP] [CLS]
