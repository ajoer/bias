Kotono Mitsuishi (三石 琴乃, Mitsuishi Kotono, born December 8, 1967) is a Japanese voice actress, singer, and narrator. [CLS] [SEP] She was affiliated with Arts Vision and Lasley Arrow, but is now freelance. [CLS] [SEP] Mitsuishi lived in Nagareyama, Chiba. [CLS] [SEP] She graduated from high school and entered the Katsuta Voice Actor's Academy in 1986. [CLS] [SEP] She is well known for her roles Usagi Tsukino in Sailor Moon, Misato Katsuragi in Neon Genesis Evangelion, and Sayaka Mine in Yaiba. [CLS] [SEP]
