Taoufik Makhloufi (Arabic: توفيق مخلوفي‎; born 29 April 1988) is an Algerian athlete who specialises in middle-distance running. [CLS] [SEP] He became the 1500 metres Olympic champion at the 2012 Summer Olympics in London, England. [CLS] [SEP] In 2016, Makhloufi took the silver medal in the 800m and 1500 m at the Summer Olympics in Rio, Brazil.He was also the 800 metres gold medallist at the 2012 African Championships and the 2011 All-Africa Games. [CLS] [SEP] He has represented Algeria three times at the World Championships in Athletics. [CLS] [SEP] His personal bests are 1.42.61 minutes for the 800 m, set at the Rio Olympics, and 3:28.75 minutes for the 1500 metres. [CLS] [SEP] He trains with GS Pétroliers. [CLS] [SEP]
