[CLS] Wien Air Alaska Flight 99 was a scheduled domestic passenger flight in Alaska to St. [SEP] [CLS] Lawrence Island in the Bering Sea. [SEP] [CLS] On approach to Gambell on 30 August 1975, it crashed into Sevuokuk Mountain, east of the airport's runway. [SEP] [CLS] Of the 32 on board, ten were killed, including the pilot and co-pilot. [SEP] [CLS] The Fairchild F-27B aircraft was operated by Wien Air Alaska. [SEP] [CLS]
