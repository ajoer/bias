On 29 November 1975, former Formula One champion and team owner Graham Hill died when the Piper Aztec light aircraft he was piloting crashed near Arkley in Hertfordshire, United Kingdom, while on approach to Elstree Airfield. [CLS] [SEP] The other five passengers on board, forming the core of his Embassy Hill Formula One team, were also killed. [CLS] [SEP] The group was returning from a trip to southern France to test the new Hill GH2, one month after the end of the 1975 Formula One season. [CLS] [SEP] The accident occurred at night, and at the time foggy conditions prevailed in the area. [CLS] [SEP] An investigation into the crash was inconclusive, but pilot error was deemed the most likely explanation. [CLS] [SEP]
