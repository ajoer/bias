[CLS] Margarete Jonas (née Towarek) (13 June 1898 in Vienna – 7 August 1976 in Vienna) was the wife of Franz Jonas, the former federal president of Austria. [SEP] [CLS] They married in 1922. [SEP] [CLS] Mrs Jonas spoke German, English and Czech. [SEP] [CLS] She is buried next to her husband in the presidential vault in the Wiener Zentralfriedhof. [SEP] [CLS]
