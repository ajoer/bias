[CLS] Prachi Desai (born 12 September 1988) is an Indian film actress and former television actress. [SEP] [CLS] She started her television career as lead protagonist in the successful TV drama Kasamh Se on Zee TV. [SEP] [CLS] She made her Bollywood debut in the 2008 film Rock On!!. [SEP] [CLS] Her other notable features include Life Partner (2009), Once Upon a Time in Mumbaai (2010), Bol Bachchan (2012) and I, Me Aur Main (2013). [SEP] [CLS] She is also the endorser, spokesperson, brand ambassador and the face of Goa Tourism and Neutrogena products in India. [SEP] [CLS] Desai is also a brand ambassador of Lux Lyra.
