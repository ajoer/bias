Philippine Airlines Flight 443 was an early morning domestic flight that crashed into Mount Gurain, Philippines, on December 19, 1987. [CLS] [SEP] The Short 360-300 operating the route departed Mactan-Cebu International Airport at 6:42 local time. [CLS] [SEP] The last radio contact was at 7:17 local time, when the aircraft was closing in on Iligan. [CLS] [SEP] Shortly after, the aircraft crashed. [CLS] [SEP] All 15 people on board the plane, 11 passengers and 4 crew, were confirmed dead. [CLS] [SEP]
