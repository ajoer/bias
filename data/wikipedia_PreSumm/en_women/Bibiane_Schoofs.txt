[CLS] Bibiane Schoofs (born 13 May 1988), previously known as Bibiane Weijers, is a Dutch professional tennis player. [SEP] [CLS] On 11 June 2012, she reached her highest WTA singles ranking of world No. [SEP] [CLS] 142, whilst her best doubles ranking was No. [SEP] [CLS] 83 on 2 April 2018. [SEP] [CLS] She married on 7 July 2014, and took her husband's surname, however in late 2016 reverted to her maiden name, Schoofs. [SEP] [CLS] In December 2016, she became national singles champion under that name.She has won eight ITF singles tournaments, three of them in 2011. [SEP] [CLS] In the doubles she has won 18 ITF titles, three of them in 2017. [SEP] [CLS] At age 29, she won the doubles title at the 2017 Mumbai Open, a tournament on the Challenger-level. [SEP] [CLS] In January 2018, she won her first WTA doubles title at the tournament of Auckland.Playing for the Netherlands at the Fed Cup, Schoofs has a win–loss record of 3–9. [SEP] [CLS]
