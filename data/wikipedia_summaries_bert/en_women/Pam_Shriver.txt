Pamela Howard Shriver (born July 4, 1962) is a former professional tennis player from the U.S. [CLS] [SEP] She currently is a tennis broadcaster for ESPN and a pundit for BBC tennis coverage. [CLS] [SEP] During the 1980s and 1990s, she won 133 titles, including 21 women's singles titles, 111 women's doubles titles, and one mixed doubles title. [CLS] [SEP] In Grand Slam tournaments, Shriver won 21 doubles titles and one mixed doubles title. [CLS] [SEP] She also won a women's doubles gold medal at the 1988 Olympic Games in Seoul with Zina Garrison as her partner. [CLS] [SEP]
