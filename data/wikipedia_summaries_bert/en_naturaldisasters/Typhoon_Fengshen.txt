The name Fengshen has been used to name four tropical cyclones in the northwestern Pacific Ocean. [CLS] [SEP] The name was contributed by China and literally means "God of wind". [CLS] [SEP] Typhoon Fengshen (2002) (T0209, 12W) – Category 5 storm that remained over open waters for most of its life, then brushed southern Japan. [CLS] [SEP] Typhoon Fengshen (2008) (T0806, 07W, Frank) – Category 3 storm that wrecked the Philippines, killing hundreds, and later causing flooding in China's mainland. [CLS] [SEP] Severe Tropical Storm Fengshen (2014) (T1414, 13W) – a storm which formed during the weak peak of the season. [CLS] [SEP] Typhoon Fengshen (2019) (T1925, 26W)
