Air Inter Flight 148 was a scheduled passenger flight from Lyon–Saint-Exupéry Airport to Strasbourg Airport in France. [CLS] [SEP] On 20 January 1992, the aircraft operating the flight, an Airbus A320, crashed in the Vosges Mountains, France, near Mont Sainte-Odile, while circling to land at Strasbourg Airport. [CLS] [SEP] 87 of the 96 people on board were killed, while the remaining nine were all injured. [CLS] [SEP]
