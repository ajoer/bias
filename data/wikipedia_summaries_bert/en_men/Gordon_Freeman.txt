[CLS] Gordon Freeman is the protagonist of the Half-Life video game series, created by Gabe Newell and designed by Newell and Marc Laidlaw of Valve. [SEP] [CLS] His first appearance is in Half-Life. [SEP] [CLS] Gordon Freeman is an American man from Seattle, who graduated from MIT with a PhD in Theoretical Physics. [SEP] [CLS] He was an employee at the fictional Black Mesa Research Facility. [SEP] [CLS] Controlled by the player, Gordon is often tasked with using a wide range of weapons and tools to fight alien creatures such as headcrabs, as well as Combine machines and soldiers. [SEP] [CLS] Gordon Freeman's character has been well received by critics and gamers, and various gaming websites often consider him to be one of the greatest video game characters of all time, including UGO and GameSpot.
