[CLS] On 24 October 2016, a twin turboprop Fairchild SA227-AT Merlin IVC operated by CAE Aviation crashed near Kirkop, Malta, shortly after take-off from Malta International Airport. [SEP] [CLS] The aircraft was to operate in the vicinity of Misurata in Libya on a surveillance mission by the French Ministry of Defence. [SEP] [CLS] All five people on board the aircraft died in the crash, making it the deadliest aviation accident in Malta since 1985. [SEP] [CLS]
