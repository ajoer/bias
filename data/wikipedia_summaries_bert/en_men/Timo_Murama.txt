[CLS] Timo Kaapo Arthur Murama (née Moberg, October 15, 1913 – January 17, 1981) was a Finnish nordic combined skier who competed in the 1930s. [SEP] [CLS] He was born and died in Rovaniemi. [SEP] [CLS] He finished tied for seventh in the Nordic combined event at the 1936 Winter Olympics in Garmisch-Partenkirchen. [SEP] [CLS]
