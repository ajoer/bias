[CLS] Rashad Khalifa Arabic: رشاد خليفة‎; November 19, 1935 – January 31, 1990) was an Egyptian-American biochemist, closely associated with the United Submitters International (USI), an offshoot of Quranism. [SEP] [CLS] His teachings were opposed by Traditionalist Muslims. [SEP] [CLS] He was assassinated on January 31, 1990. [SEP] [CLS]
