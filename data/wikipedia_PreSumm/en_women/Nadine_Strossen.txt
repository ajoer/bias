[CLS] Nadine Strossen (born August 18, 1950) is an American civil liberties activist who was president of the American Civil Liberties Union (ACLU) from February 1991 to October 2008. [SEP] [CLS] A liberal feminist, she was the first woman and the youngest person to ever lead the ACLU. [SEP] [CLS] A professor at New York Law School, Strossen sits on the Council on Foreign Relations. [SEP] [CLS]
