Molly Ephraim (born May 22, 1986) is an American actress who has appeared in films, television, and Broadway, Off-Broadway, and regional theater productions. [CLS] [SEP] She is known for her role as Mandy Baxter in the ABC sitcom Last Man Standing during its original run (2011–2017). [CLS] [SEP] Ephraim has also appeared in a number of other TV series including Brockmire (2017), Halt and Catch Fire (2017), and Casual (2018). [CLS] [SEP] Most recently, Ephraim portrayed Irene Kelly in The Front Runner (2018), her second collaboration with Last Man Standing co-star Kaitlyn Dever. [CLS] [SEP] She is currently appearing in HBO’s “Perry Mason” as Della Street’s lover Hazel Prystock. [CLS] [SEP]
