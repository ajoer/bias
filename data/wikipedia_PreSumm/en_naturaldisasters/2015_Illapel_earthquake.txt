[CLS] The 2015 Illapel earthquake occurred 46 km (29 mi) offshore from Illapel (Coquimbo region, Chile) on September 16 at 19:54:33 Chile Standard Time (22:54:33 UTC), with a moment magnitude of 8.3. [SEP] [CLS] The initial quake lasted between three and five minutes; it was followed by several aftershocks greater than magnitude six and two that exceeded 7.0 moment magnitude. [SEP] [CLS] The Chilean government reported 15 deaths, 6 missing and thousands of people affected. [SEP] [CLS] In Buenos Aires, Argentina, a man died from a stroke while he was evacuating a building.
