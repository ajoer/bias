[CLS] EgyptAir Flight 843 was a flight from Cairo International Airport to Tunis–Carthage International Airport. [SEP] [CLS] On 7 May 2002, the Boeing 737-566 on the route crashed into a hill near Tunis–Carthage International Airport. [SEP] [CLS] Of the 6 crew members and 56 passengers, 3 crew members and 11 passengers died, making a total of 14 fatalities. [SEP] [CLS]
