[CLS] The Great Flood of 1881 refers to flooding events along the Missouri River during the spring of 1881. [SEP] [CLS] The flood began around Pierre, South Dakota and struck areas down river in Yankton, South Dakota, Omaha, Nebraska, Council Bluffs, Iowa, Nebraska City, Nebraska, Kansas City, Missouri and farther south between April 1, 1881 and April 27, 1881. [SEP] [CLS] The events provided the first detailed reporting of Missouri River flooding, and caused millions of dollars in damage. [SEP] [CLS]
