[CLS] Arnošt Muka (German: Ernst Mucke; 10 March 1854 – 10 October 1932) was a German and Sorbian writer, linguist and man of science. [SEP] [CLS] Muka was born in Großhänchen which is now in the municipality of Burkau, and studied theology, classical languages and Slavonic in Leipzig. [SEP] [CLS] Because of his activities in connection with the Sorbian languages, he was sent to teach in a gymnasium in Chemnitz in 1883, and later to Freiberg. [SEP] [CLS] In 1917, he moved back to Bautzen, where he died on 10 October 1932. [SEP] [CLS]
