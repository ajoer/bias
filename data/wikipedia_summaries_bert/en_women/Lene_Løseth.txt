Lene Løseth (born 26 November 1986 in Ålesund) is a Norwegian alpine skier who races for Spjelkavik IL. [CLS] [SEP] At the WC 2007 in Åre she came 38th in the giant slalom and 23rd in the slalom. [CLS] [SEP] She is Mona and Nina Løseth's elder sister. [CLS] [SEP] In a World Cup competition in slalom in Flachau in January 2010 all three sisters qualified for the final. [CLS] [SEP]
