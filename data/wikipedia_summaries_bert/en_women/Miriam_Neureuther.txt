Miriam Neureuther (née Gössner; born 21 June 1990) is a former German biathlete and cross-country skier. [CLS] [SEP] She has won an Olympic silver medal in cross-country skiing and two biathlon world championship titles, all in team events. [CLS] [SEP] Noted for her fast skiing performances, she won two junior world championship titles in biathlon in 2008 and 2009. [CLS] [SEP] Gössner was called up for the Nordic World Ski Championships 2009, where she was part of Germany's cross-country team claiming silver in the 4 × 5 kilometre relay. [CLS] [SEP] At the 2010 Winter Olympics in Vancouver, Gössner won a silver medal in cross-country skiing in the 4 × 5 kilometre relay. [CLS] [SEP] She returned to biathlon for the 2010–11 season, achieving three podium finishes in her first complete winter on the Biathlon World Cup tour. [CLS] [SEP] At the Biathlon World Championships 2011, Gössner won the gold medal in the women's relay. [CLS] [SEP] The following year she defended the world championship title with the German team in the 2012 women's relay. [CLS] [SEP] Gössner claimed her first individual World Cup win in December 2012. [CLS] [SEP]
