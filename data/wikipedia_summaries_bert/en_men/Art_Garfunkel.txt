Arthur Ira Garfunkel (born November 5, 1941) is an American singer, poet, and actor. [CLS] [SEP] He is best known for his partnership with Paul Simon in the folk rock duo Simon & Garfunkel. [CLS] [SEP] Highlights of his solo music career include a top 10 hit, three top 20 hits, six top 40 hits, 14 Adult Contemporary top 30 singles, five Adult Contemporary number ones, two UK number ones and a People's Choice Award. [CLS] [SEP] Through his solo and collaborative work, Garfunkel has earned eight Grammys, including a Lifetime Achievement Award. [CLS] [SEP] In 1990, he and Simon were inducted into the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame. [CLS] [SEP] In 2008, Garfunkel was ranked 86th in Rolling Stone magazine's list of the 100 Greatest Singers of All Time. [CLS] [SEP]
