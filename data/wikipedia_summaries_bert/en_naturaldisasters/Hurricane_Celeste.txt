The name Celeste has been used for four tropical cyclones in the Eastern Pacific Ocean. [CLS] [SEP] Tropical Storm Celeste (1968), did not make landfall Tropical Storm Celeste (1976), no effect on any land area Hurricane Celeste (1960), did not make landfall Hurricane Celeste (1972), at the time, was the longest-lasting Pacific hurricane with a lifespan of 16.25 daysThe name Celeste has also been used for one tropical cyclone in the Southwest Pacific Ocean.
