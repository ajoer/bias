Aeroflot Flight 245 was a scheduled domestic passenger flight operated by an Ilyushin Il-18B that crashed during the cruise phase of the flight en route to Sochi on Wednesday 17 December 1961, resulting in the death of all 59 people on board. [CLS] [SEP] An investigation revealed the aircraft entered a steep dive after the flaps were accidentally extended. [CLS] [SEP]
