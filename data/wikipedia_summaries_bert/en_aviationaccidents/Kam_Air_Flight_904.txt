Kam Air Flight 904 was a scheduled passenger domestic flight, flying from Herat Airfield in Herat to Kabul International Airport in Afghanistan's capital of Kabul. [CLS] [SEP] On February 3, 2005, the aircraft impacted mountainous terrain, killing all 96 passengers and 8 crew on board. [CLS] [SEP] The incident took place shortly after 4:00 p.m. [CLS] [SEP] local time (UTC+4:30) when the Kam Air Boeing 737-200 operated by Phoenix Aviation went missing. [CLS] [SEP] The plane was on approach to Kabul. [CLS] [SEP] At the time of the incident, a heavy snowstorm was observed in the area. [CLS] [SEP] The crash is the deadliest air disaster in the history of Afghanistan. [CLS] [SEP]
