[CLS] PMTair Flight 241 (U4 241/PMT241) was a scheduled domestic passenger flight, flying from Siem Reap International Airport, Siem Reap to Sihanoukville International Airport, Sihanoukville. [SEP] [CLS] The flight was operated by PMTair using an Antonov An-24. [SEP] [CLS] On 25 June 2007, the Antonov An-24, registered as XU-U4A, disappeared over Cambodian jungle near Bokor while on approach to Sihanoukville. [SEP] [CLS] A massive search and rescue operation occurred, as thousands of soldiers and police scoured the area. [SEP] [CLS] The aircraft was found to have crashed in southwestern Cambodia, northeast of Bokor Mountain in Kampot Province. [SEP] [CLS] All 22 people on board, most of whom were South Korean tourists, were killed. [SEP] [CLS] An investigation and inquiry was completed in March 2008, but couldn't conclude the cause of the crash of Flight 241. [SEP] [CLS]
