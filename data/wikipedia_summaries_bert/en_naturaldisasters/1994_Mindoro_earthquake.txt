The 1994 Mindoro earthquake occurred at 03:15:30 PST on November 15 near Mindoro, the Philippines. [CLS] [SEP] It had a moment magnitude of 7.1 and a maximum Mercalli intensity of VII (Very strong). [CLS] [SEP] It is associated with a 35 kilometer-long ground rupture, called the Aglubang River fault. [CLS] [SEP] Seventy eight people were reported dead, and 7,566 houses were damaged. [CLS] [SEP] The earthquake generated a tsunami and landslides on the Verde Island. [CLS] [SEP]
