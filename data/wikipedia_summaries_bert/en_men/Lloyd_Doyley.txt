[CLS] Lloyd Colin Doyley (born 1 December 1982) is a professional footballer who plays as a defender for Southern Premier Division South club Kings Langley. [SEP] [CLS] Doyley came through the Watford Academy and went on to make 443 first-team appearances in 14 years with the club. [SEP] [CLS] He joined Rotherham United for a short spell in 2016, before moving to Colchester United later the same year. [SEP] [CLS] Doyley has represented Jamaica at full international level, making nine appearances in 2013 and 2014. [SEP] [CLS]
