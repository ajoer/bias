The 2016 Ecuador earthquake occurred on April 16 at 18:58:37 ECT with a moment magnitude of 7.8 and a maximum Mercalli intensity of VIII (Severe). [CLS] [SEP] The very large thrust earthquake was centered approximately 27 km (17 mi) from the towns of Muisne and Pedernales in a sparsely populated part of the country, and 170 km (110 mi) from the capital Quito, where it was felt strongly. [CLS] [SEP] Regions of Manta, Pedernales and Portoviejo accounted for over 75 percent of total casualties. [CLS] [SEP] Manta's central commercial shopping district, Tarqui, was completely destroyed. [CLS] [SEP] Widespread damage was caused across Manabi province, with structures hundreds of kilometres from the epicenter collapsing. [CLS] [SEP] At least 676 people were killed and 16,600 people injured. [CLS] [SEP] President Rafael Correa declared a state of emergency; 13,500 military personnel and police officers were dispatched for recovery operations. [CLS] [SEP]
