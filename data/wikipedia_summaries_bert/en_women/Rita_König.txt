Rita König (born 12 March 1977) is a German fencer. [CLS] [SEP] She won a silver medal in the women's individual foil and a bronze in the team foil events at the 2000 Summer Olympics.König attended the Kaufmännische Schule Tauberbischofsheim and fought for the Fencing-Club Tauberbischofsheim.
