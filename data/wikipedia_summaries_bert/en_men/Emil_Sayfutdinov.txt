[CLS] Emil Damirovich Sayfutdinov (Russian: Эмиль Дамирович Сайфутдинов; born 26 October 1989) is a Russian motorcycle speedway rider and member of the Russian national teams (senior and U-21). [SEP] [CLS] He won the 2007 and 2008 Under-21 World Championship, and is a two-time winner of the European Championships in 2014, 2015. [SEP] [CLS] Sayfutdinov has also won Speedway of Nations with Russia two-times, in 2018, 2019. [SEP] [CLS] He is a permanent rider of the 2019 Speedway Grand Prix. [SEP] [CLS] He has won seven Grand Prix. [SEP] [CLS]
