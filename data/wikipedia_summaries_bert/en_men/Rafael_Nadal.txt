[CLS] [SEP] Nadal has won 85 career titles overall, including the most outdoor titles in the Open Era (83) and a record 59 titles on clay. [CLS] [SEP] With 81 consecutive wins on clay, Nadal holds the record for the longest single-surface win streak in the Open Era. [CLS] [SEP] Nadal has been involved in five Davis Cup titles with Spain, and currently has a 29-win streak and 29–1 record in singles matches at the event. [CLS] [SEP] In 2010, at the age of 24, he became the seventh male player and the youngest of five in the Open Era to achieve the singles Career Grand Slam. [CLS] [SEP] Nadal is the second male player after Andre Agassi to complete the singles Career Golden Slam, as well as the second male player after Mats Wilander to have won at least two Grand Slams on all three surfaces (grass, hard court and clay). [CLS] [SEP] He has received the tour Sportsmanship Award three times and has been named the ATP Player of the Year five times and the ITF World Champion four times.
