[CLS] Andrea Sawatzki (German: [anˈdʁeːa zaˈvat͡s.kiː] (listen); born 23 February 1963) is a German actress. [SEP] [CLS] From 2002 to 2010 she starred in the Hessischer Rundfunk version of the popular television crime series Tatort as police inspector Charlotte Sänger. [SEP] [CLS] She also gained some international prominence as a co-actor in the German films Bandits (1997) and Das Experiment (2001). [SEP] [CLS]
