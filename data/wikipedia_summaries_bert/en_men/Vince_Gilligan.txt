[CLS] George Vincent Gilligan Jr. [SEP] [CLS] (born February 10, 1967) is an American writer, producer, and director. [SEP] [CLS] He is known for his television work, specifically as creator, head writer, executive producer, and director of AMC's Breaking Bad and its spin-off Better Call Saul. [SEP] [CLS] He was a writer and producer for The X-Files and was the co-creator of its spin-off The Lone Gunmen. [SEP] [CLS] Gilligan has won three Primetime Emmy Awards, six Writers Guild of America Awards, two Critics' Choice Television Awards and Producers Guild of America Awards, one Directors Guild of America Award and a BAFTA. [SEP] [CLS] Outside of television, he co-wrote the screenplay for the 2008 film Hancock and wrote and directed the Breaking Bad sequel film, El Camino, released on October 11, 2019. [SEP] [CLS]
