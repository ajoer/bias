[CLS] Anastasia Vladimirovna Russkikh (Russian: Анастасия Владимировна Русских; born 20 May 1983) is an international badminton player from Gatchina, Russia. [SEP] [CLS] She entered her first tournament at age 9 in Sweden and finished in third place. [SEP] [CLS] She began playing professionally with her local Gatchina Badminton Club as well as a larger Danish club in Skaelskor, Denmark. [SEP] [CLS] In 2010, she played for the Vendsyssel Elite Badminton club. [SEP] [CLS] Russkikh is one of Russia's top badminton players, and is a high-ranking competitor in women's and mixed-doubles competitions. [SEP] [CLS] Together with her partner in women's doubles, Petya Nedelcheva from Bulgaria, she won the silver medal at the 2010 European Badminton Championships. [SEP] [CLS]
