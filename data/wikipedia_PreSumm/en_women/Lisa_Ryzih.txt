[CLS] Elizaveta Ryzih (born 27 September 1988 in Omsk, Soviet Union) is a German pole vault athlete. [SEP] [CLS] Two times an Olympian, she was 6th in London and 10th in Rio Olympic games. [SEP] [CLS] She was described by one athletics commentator as a "tall, fast and athletic" pole vaulter, and she has seen good success in European Championships as well as being a constant presence in the world yearly rankings of pole vaulters, placing among the top 10 vaulters in recent years. [SEP] [CLS] She is the younger sister of fellow pole vaulter Anastasija Reiberger (Nastja Ryjikh). [SEP] [CLS] She is coached by her father Vladimir Ryzih. [SEP] [CLS]
