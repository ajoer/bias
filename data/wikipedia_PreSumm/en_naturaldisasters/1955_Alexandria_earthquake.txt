[CLS] The 1955 Alexandria earthquake occurred on September 12 at 06:09 UTC. [SEP] [CLS] The epicenter was located in the eastern Mediterranean, offshore of Alexandria, Egypt. [SEP] [CLS] The earthquake had a magnitude of Ms 6.3.Damage was reported in the Nile Delta between Alexandria and Cairo. [SEP] [CLS] About 300 adobe houses were badly damaged around Lake Idku. [SEP] [CLS] The earthquake caused 18 deaths and 89 injuries, with 40 houses completely collapsed and about 420 houses ruined. [SEP] [CLS] The earthquake could also be felt in Cyprus, Palestine, Syria, and Greece. [SEP] [CLS]
