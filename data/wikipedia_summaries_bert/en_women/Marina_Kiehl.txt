[CLS] Marina Kiehl (born 12 January 1965) is a former World Cup alpine ski racer from Germany. [SEP] [CLS] Competing for West Germany in the 1980s, Kiehl gained her first World Cup victory in a Super-G competition in March 1984 in Quebec. [SEP] [CLS] She won season titles in Giant slalom In 1985 and in Super-G in 1986. [SEP] [CLS] The major highlight in Kiehl's career came at the 1988 Olympics in Calgary, when she won a gold medal in the downhill, ahead of Brigitte Oertli and Karen Percy.Kiehl retired from competitions after the Calgary Olympics, aged 23.
