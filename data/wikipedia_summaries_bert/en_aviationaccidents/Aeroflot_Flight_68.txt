Aeroflot Flight 68 was a regularly scheduled passenger flight operated by Aeroflot from Khabarovsk Novy Airport in Khabarovsk Krai to Pulkovo Airport in Saint Petersburg with intermediate stops at Tolmachevo Airport in Ob, Russia, then Koltsovo Airport in Yekaterinburg. [CLS] [SEP] On 16 March 1961, the Tupolev Tu-104B operating this flight crashed shortly after take off from Koltsovo Airport. [CLS] [SEP] Three passengers and two crewmembers were killed along with two people on the ground. [CLS] [SEP] The Air Accident Investigation Commission concluded the cause of the accident was the failure of engine No. [CLS] [SEP] 2 (right) due a broken turbine blade. [CLS] [SEP]
