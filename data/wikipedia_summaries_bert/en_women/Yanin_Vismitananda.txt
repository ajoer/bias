Yanin "Jeeja" Vismitananda (Thai: ญาณิน "จีจ้า" วิสมิตะนันทน์; RTGS: Yanin "Chicha" Wisamittanan), née Nicharee Vismitananda (Thai: ณิชชารีย์ วิสมิตะนันทน์; RTGS: Nitchari Wisamittanan), (born March 31, 1984) is a Thai actress and martial artist. [CLS] [SEP] She specializes in Muay Thai. [CLS] [SEP] She is credited as Yanin Mitananda in Chocolate. [CLS] [SEP]
