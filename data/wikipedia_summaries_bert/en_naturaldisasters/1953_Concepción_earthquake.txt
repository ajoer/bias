The 1953 Concepción earthquake occurred on May 6 at 17:16 UTC (13:16 local time). [CLS] [SEP] The epicenter was located offshore Biobío Region, Chile. [CLS] [SEP] It had a magnitude of Ms 7.6, or ML 7.6. [CLS] [SEP] Twelve people were reported dead in this earthquake.In Concepción, 15% of the buildings were damaged. [CLS] [SEP] Electricity and gas were not affected. [CLS] [SEP] However, the electricity service was suspended as a precaution.In Chillán, the intensity reached MM X. [CLS] [SEP] Many houses were destroyed. [CLS] [SEP] The electricity, telegraph, and telephone services were interrupted.In Tomé, several houses were damaged, and many families stayed in the open.This earthquake was located close to the 1939 Chillán earthquake. [CLS] [SEP] However, the focal mechanism of the 1953 Concepción earthquake was reverse faulting, while that of the 1939 Chillán earthquake was normal faulting. [CLS] [SEP] It was not clear whether the 1953 Concepción earthquake was an interplate or intraplate earthquake. [CLS] [SEP]
