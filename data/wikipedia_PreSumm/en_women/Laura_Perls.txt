[CLS] Laura Perls (née Lore Posner; 15 August 1905 in Pforzheim – 13 July 1990 in Pforzheim) was a noted German-born psychologist and psychotherapist who helped establish the Gestalt school of psychotherapy. [SEP] [CLS] She is the wife of Friedrich (Frederick) Perls, also a renowned psychotherapist and psychiatrist. [SEP] [CLS]
