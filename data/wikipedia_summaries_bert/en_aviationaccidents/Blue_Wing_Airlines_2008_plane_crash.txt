On Thursday, April 3, 2008, an Antonov An-28 operated by Blue Wing Airlines (registration PZ-TSO) crashed upon landing at Lawa Antino Airport in Benzdorp, Suriname. [CLS] [SEP] The plane carried 17 passengers and a crew of 2, all of whom perished. [CLS] [SEP] The crash occurred around 11:00 am ART (14:00 UTC). [CLS] [SEP] Initial media reports indicated that the airplane had to abort the landing, as the runway was being used by another Bluewing AN-28 aircraft. [CLS] [SEP] The airplane attempted a go-around, but failed to gain height and crashed into a mountain. [CLS] [SEP]
