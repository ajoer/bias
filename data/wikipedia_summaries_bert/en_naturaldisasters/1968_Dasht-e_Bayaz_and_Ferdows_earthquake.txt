The Dasht-e Bayaz and Ferdows earthquakes occurred in Dashte Bayaz, Kakhk and Ferdows, Iran in late August and early September 1968. [CLS] [SEP] The mainshock measured 7.4 on the moment magnitude scale and had a maximum perceived intensity of X (Extreme) on the Mercalli intensity scale. [CLS] [SEP] Damage was heavy in the affected areas with thousands of lives lost in the first event and many hundreds more in the second strong event. [CLS] [SEP]
