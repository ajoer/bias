[CLS] Michelle M. [SEP] [CLS] Francl is an American chemist. [SEP] [CLS] Francl is a professor of chemistry, and has taught physical chemistry, general chemistry and mathematical modeling at Bryn Mawr College since 1986.Francl is noted for developing new methodology in computational chemistry, including the 6-31G* basis set for Na to Ar and electrostatic potential charges. [SEP] [CLS] She received a Ph.D. [SEP] [CLS] from the University of California, Irvine in 1983 On a list of the 1000 most cited chemists, Francl is a member of the editorial board for the Journal of Molecular Graphics and Modelling, active in the American Chemical Society and the author of The Survival Guide for Physical Chemistry. [SEP] [CLS] Lindback Award by Bryn Mawr College for excellence in teaching.Francl's podcast, "Introduction to Quantum Mechanics," broke into the iTunes Top 100 in October 2005, and currently writes for Nature Chemistry.In April 2016, Francl was named one of nine adjunct scholars of the Vatican Observatory also known as (Italian: Specola Vaticana).
