[CLS] MASwings Sdn Bhd (doing business as MASwings) is a regional airline operating the Rural Air Services (RAS) in East Malaysia. [SEP] [CLS] It took over the RAS previously operated by FlyAsianXpress. [SEP] [CLS] The first flight was on 1 October 2007, which is also the anniversary of the founding of Malaysia Airlines in 1972. [SEP] [CLS] Its headquarters are located in MAS/MASwings Administration Building, Kota Kinabalu International Airport, Sabah. [SEP] [CLS] Previously its head office was located in the Beautiful Jade Centre in Miri.MASwings is a wholly owned subsidiary of Malaysia Airlines. [SEP] [CLS]
