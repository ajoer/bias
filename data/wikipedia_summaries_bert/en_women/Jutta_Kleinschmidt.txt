Jutta Kleinschmidt (born 29 August 1962) is a German competitor of offroad automotive racing events. [CLS] [SEP] She is known for her numerous showings in the Paris Dakar Rally, and notably for having won the event in 2001, becoming the only woman to win the race and the only German to win the car category. [CLS] [SEP] In 2013, Kleinschmidt was named an FIM Legend for her motorcycling achievements. [CLS] [SEP]
