Constanze Engelbrecht (German: [kɔnˈstan.t͡sə ˈɛŋl̩ˌbʁɛçt] (listen); 6 January 1950 – 21 July 2000) was a German actress who was one of the most popular actresses in the country between 1980 and 1990. [CLS] [SEP] She appeared in more than seventy films from 1960 to 1998. [CLS] [SEP] Her husband was an actor, and her daughter Julie Engelbrecht is an actress. [CLS] [SEP]
