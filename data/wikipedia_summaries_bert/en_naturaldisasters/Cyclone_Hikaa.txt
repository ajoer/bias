Very Severe Cyclonic Storm Hikaa was a tropical cyclone that struck eastern Oman in September 2019. [CLS] [SEP] The third named storm of the 2019 North Indian Ocean cyclone season, Hikaa formed on September 22 west of India in the Arabian Sea. [CLS] [SEP] The storm reached peak intensity on September 24, with maximum sustained winds estimated over 140 km/h (85 mph). [CLS] [SEP] Later that day, the storm made landfall in eastern Oman south of Duqm, and quickly dissipated over the Arabian Peninsula. [CLS] [SEP]
