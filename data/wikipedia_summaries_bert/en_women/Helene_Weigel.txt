Helene Weigel (German: [heˈleː.nə ˈvaɪ̯gl̩] (listen); 12 May 1900 – 6 May 1971) was a distinguished German actress and artistic director. [CLS] [SEP] She was the second wife of Bertolt Brecht and was married to him from 1930 until his death in 1956. [CLS] [SEP] Together they had two children. [CLS] [SEP]
