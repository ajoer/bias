On June 21, 2007 an overloaded Free Airlines Let L-410UVP (registration 9Q-CEU) in Karibu Airways livery crashed shortly after takeoff from Kamina Town (fr:Kamina Ville) for Lubumbashi, Democratic Republic of Congo. [CLS] [SEP] Landing in a swamp east of the unpaved strip, the aircraft came to rest inverted in the water. [CLS] [SEP]
