The 1898 Fort Smith, Arkansas tornado was a tornado which struck the city of Fort Smith, Arkansas during the night of Tuesday, January 11, 1898. [CLS] [SEP] It touched down about 100 miles to the southwest, and struck the city around midnight, killing 55 people and injuring 113. [CLS] [SEP] The twister nearly destroyed the city's newly constructed Fort Smith High School that opened in fall 1897. [CLS] [SEP] It is tied for the deadliest tornado ever to strike the state of Arkansas. [CLS] [SEP] Both tornadoes (1898, 1949) were estimated to be rated F4 on the Fujita scale. [CLS] [SEP] Another tornado was also reported around 11pm that night in Alma, Arkansas. [CLS] [SEP]
