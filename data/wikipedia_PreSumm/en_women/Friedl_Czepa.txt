[CLS] Friedl Czepa (1898–1973) was an Austrian stage, film and television actress. [SEP] [CLS] Czepa made her film debut in 1935, and went on to appear in roughly thirty cinema and television films during her career. [SEP] [CLS] Along with Oskar Sima, Fred Hennings and Leni Riefenstahl she was identified as being an active supporter of the Nazi Party. [SEP] [CLS] She was the director of the Vienna Stadttheater from 1940 to 1945. [SEP] [CLS] Because of her Nazi links, she received a professional ban following the Second World War but slowly rebuilt her career. [SEP] [CLS] She was married three her times; her husbands included Hans Schott-Schöbinger and Rolf Wanka. [SEP] [CLS]
