Michael Reese Hoffa (born Maurice Antawn Chism; October 8, 1977 in Evans, Georgia) is an American shot putter. [CLS] [SEP] Reese won the shot put in the 2006 World Indoor Track and Field Championships and in the 2007 World Outdoor Championships. [CLS] [SEP] He also won the bronze medal at the 2012 Summer Olympics in London. [CLS] [SEP] His personal bests stand at 22.11 m (72' 6.25") indoor and 22.43 m (73' 7") outdoor. [CLS] [SEP] In 2012, he threw over 21 meters in competition for the 100th time, putting him in rarefied air in the throwing community. [CLS] [SEP] Hoffa was adopted at the age of four. [CLS] [SEP]
