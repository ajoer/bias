Judith Herzfeld (born 12 January 1948) is Professor of Biophysical Chemistry at Brandeis University. [CLS] [SEP] Known for her work in statistical thermodynamics, solid state NMR and chemical education, she is a fellow of the American Physical Society, the American Association for the Advancement of Science, and the Massachusetts Academy of Sciences. [CLS] [SEP] She is the author or coauthor of more than 180 scientific papers and a book. [CLS] [SEP]
