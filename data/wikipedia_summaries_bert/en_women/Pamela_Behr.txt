Pamela Behr (born September 21, 1956 in Hindelang, then West Germany) is a retired German alpine ski racer. [CLS] [SEP] She is the second youngest person ever to win an FIS Alpine Ski World Cup race, winning a slalom in Val d'Isere, France, in December 1972 at the age of 16 years, 79 days. [CLS] [SEP] It would be the only World Cup race win of her ten-year career. [CLS] [SEP] She won the silver medal in slalom at the FIS Alpine World Ski Championships 1978 in Garmisch-Partenkirchen.
