United Express Flight 6291 was a regularly scheduled United Express flight from Washington Dulles International Airport near Washington, D.C. [CLS] [SEP] to Port Columbus International Airport in Columbus, Ohio. [CLS] [SEP] It was a service operated by Atlantic Coast Airlines on behalf of United Express. [CLS] [SEP] Late on the night of January 7, 1994, the British Aerospace Jetstream 41 operating as Flight 6291 stalled and crashed on approach to Port Columbus International Airport. [CLS] [SEP] The two pilots, the flight attendant, and two passengers died in the crash. [CLS] [SEP] The surviving passengers were a Taiwanese family of three. [CLS] [SEP]
