Armed Forces of the Russian Federation Flight 9064 was a military flight from Bratsk Airport to Petropavlovsk-Kamchatsky Airport with 9 passengers and 9 crew aboard. [CLS] [SEP] The flight crashed at Novaya Inya, Russia on 1 December 2001, after a reported wing fire. [CLS] [SEP] All aboard perished. [CLS] [SEP]
