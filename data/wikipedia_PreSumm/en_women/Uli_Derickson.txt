[CLS] Uli Derickson (née Patzelt, August 8, 1944 – February 18, 2005), was a German American flight attendant best known for her role in helping protect 152 passengers and crew members during the June 14, 1985, hijacking of TWA Flight 847 by members of Amal, a terrorist group with alleged links to Hezbollah.
