Santeri Paloniemi (born November 8, 1993) is a Finnish alpine skier. [CLS] [SEP] Paloniemi's strongest event is the slalom. [CLS] [SEP] Before the 2014 season, he has finished in points (top 30) four times in the FIS Alpine Ski World Cup. [CLS] [SEP] Paloniemi represents Ruka Slalom on club level, and is coached by his father, Martti Paloniemi. [CLS] [SEP]
