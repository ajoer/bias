Aeroflot Flight 3739 (Russian: Рейс 3739 Аэрофлота Reys 3739 Aeroflota) was a regularly scheduled passenger flight from Irkutsk to Pulkovo Airport with a stopover at Tolmachevo Airport that crashed during takeoff from Irkutsk on 9 February 1976. [CLS] [SEP] 24 of the 114 people on board perished in the accident. [CLS] [SEP]
