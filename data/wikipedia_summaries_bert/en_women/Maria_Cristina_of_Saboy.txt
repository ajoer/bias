[CLS] The second season of Tawag ng Tanghalan was an amateur singing competition currently aired as a segment of the noontime show It's Showtime from June 12, 2017 to June 2, 2018. [SEP] [CLS] All juries from the previous season reprise their jury duties, except for Bobot Mortiz. [SEP] [CLS] Gary Valenciano was added as a new judge for the second season. [SEP] [CLS]
