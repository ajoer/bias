[CLS] Sigrid Onégin (June 1, 1889 – June 16, 1943) was a Franco-German operatic contralto who enjoyed a major international career prior to World War II. [SEP] [CLS] She was celebrated for the richness of its tone, its flexibility, its size, and its expert coloratura technique. [SEP] [CLS] She also possessed a remarkably wide vocal range.
