[CLS] The April 2010 Sumatra earthquake occurred on April 7 at 5:15 AM local time with a moment magnitude of 7.8 and a maximum Mercalli intensity of VIII (Severe). [SEP] [CLS] The shock occurred near Banyak Islands, off the island of Sumatra in Indonesia. [SEP] [CLS] A tsunami watch was issued according to the Pacific Tsunami Warning Center in Honolulu which was later canceled. [SEP] [CLS] A 40 cm surge was reported in the Banyak Islands an hour after the quake, along with 62 injuries. [SEP] [CLS] Power outages were reported throughout the province of North Sumatra as well as in Aceh. [SEP] [CLS] This quake is one of the sequence of large earthquakes along the Sunda megathrust in 2000s. [SEP] [CLS] In Simeulue Regency, 21 were hospitalized in Gunung Putih, Teluk Dalam subdistrict, and 41 were injured in Teupah Selatan subdistrict. [SEP] [CLS] Some of the injured were treated at Simeulue general hospital in Sinabang. [SEP] [CLS]
