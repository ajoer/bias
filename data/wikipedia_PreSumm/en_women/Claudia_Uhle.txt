[CLS] X-Perience is a eurodance band from Berlin, Germany. [SEP] [CLS] Their music style varies between synthpop, trance and ethnopop. [SEP] [CLS] The band members are Claudia Uhle (vocals, lyrics), Matthias Uhle (composition, keyboards) and Alexander Kaiser (lyrics, keyboards). [SEP] [CLS] Singer Claudia Uhle, Matthias' sister, left the band in 2007 and was replaced by Manja Kaletka, but returned in 2019. [SEP] [CLS]
