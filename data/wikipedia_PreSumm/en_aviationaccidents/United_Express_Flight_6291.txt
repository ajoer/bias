[CLS] United Express Flight 6291 was a regularly scheduled United Express flight from Washington Dulles International Airport near Washington, D.C. [SEP] [CLS] to Port Columbus International Airport in Columbus, Ohio. [SEP] [CLS] It was a service operated by Atlantic Coast Airlines on behalf of United Express. [SEP] [CLS] Late on the night of January 7, 1994, the British Aerospace Jetstream 41 operating as Flight 6291 stalled and crashed on approach to Port Columbus International Airport. [SEP] [CLS] The two pilots, the flight attendant, and two passengers died in the crash. [SEP] [CLS] The surviving passengers were a Taiwanese family of three. [SEP] [CLS]
