[CLS] Brigitte Fassbaender (German pronunciation: [bʁiˈɡɪtə ˈfasbɛndɐ]; born 3 July 1939), is a German mezzo-soprano opera singer and a stage director. [SEP] [CLS] From 1999 to 2012 she was intendant (managing director) of the Tyrolean State Theatre in Innsbruck, Austria. [SEP] [CLS] She holds the title Kammersängerin from the Bavarian State Opera in Munich and the Vienna Staatsoper. [SEP] [CLS]
