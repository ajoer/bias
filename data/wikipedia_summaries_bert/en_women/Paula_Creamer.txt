Paula Creamer (born August 5, 1986) is an American professional golfer on the U.S.-based LPGA Tour. [CLS] [SEP] As a professional, she has won 12 tournaments, including 10 LPGA Tour events. [CLS] [SEP] Creamer has been as high as number 2 in the Women's World Golf Rankings. [CLS] [SEP] She was the 2010 U.S. [CLS] [SEP] Women's Open champion. [CLS] [SEP] As of the end of the 2019 season, Creamer was 10th on the all-time LPGA career money list with earnings of $12,131,822.As an amateur, Creamer won numerous junior golf titles, including 11 American Junior Golf Association (AJGA) tournaments. [CLS] [SEP] Creamer joined the LPGA Tour in the 2005 season, and her victory in that year's Sybase Classic made her the LPGA's second-youngest event winner. [CLS] [SEP]
