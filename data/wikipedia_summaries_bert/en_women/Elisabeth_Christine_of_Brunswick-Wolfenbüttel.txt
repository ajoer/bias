Duchess Elisabeth Christine of Brunswick-Wolfenbüttel-Bevern (8 November 1715 – 13 January 1797) was Queen of Prussia and Electress of Brandenburg as the wife of Frederick the Great. [CLS] [SEP] She was the longest-serving Queen with a tenure of more than 48 years. [CLS] [SEP] She was praised for her charity work during the Seven Years' War. [CLS] [SEP]
