[CLS] The 2014 Ludian earthquake struck Ludian County, Yunnan, China, with a moment magnitude of 6.1 on 3 August. [SEP] [CLS] The earthquake killed at least 617 people, injuring at least 2,400 others. [SEP] [CLS] As of 5 August 2014, 112 people remain missing. [SEP] [CLS] Over 12,000 houses collapsed and 30,000 were damaged. [SEP] [CLS] According to the United States Geological Survey, the earthquake occurred 29 km (18 mi) WSW of Zhaotong city at 16:03 local time (08:03 UTC). [SEP] [CLS]
