[CLS] Francisco Antonio Varallo (Spanish pronunciation: [fɾanˈsisko anˈtonjo ˈpantʃo βaˈɾaʝo]; 5 February 1910 – 30 August 2010) was an Argentine football forward. [SEP] [CLS] He played for the Argentine national team from 1930 to 1937. [SEP] [CLS] He was a member of Argentina's squad at the inaugural FIFA World Cup in 1930. [SEP] [CLS] During his career, Varallo won three leagues titles with Boca Juniors, and with 194 goals, is the club's third highest all-time leading goalscorer.Varallo died in his home-town of La Plata on 30 August 2010, at the age of 100. [SEP] [CLS] He was the last surviving player from the 1930 World Cup. [SEP] [CLS]
