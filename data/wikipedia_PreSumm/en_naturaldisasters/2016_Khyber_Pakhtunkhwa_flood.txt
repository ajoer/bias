[CLS] After heavy rains and flash floods in Pakistan, at least 71 people were killed and another 34 hospitalized. [SEP] [CLS] Rain started at night of Saturday, April 3, rainfall began to spur floods in the Khyber Pakhtunkhwa region in the northwest. [SEP] [CLS] Heavy rainfall is common in Southern Asia during the pre-monsoon season.In response to floods, Khyber Pakhtunkhwa government began to administer relief for those affected. [SEP] [CLS] Rural areas with poor infrastructure were highly susceptible, and, consequently, some 150 homes were destroyed in the event. [SEP] [CLS] The floods also caused deadly landslides that killed another 23 people. [SEP] [CLS] However, 5 survived and were rescued. [SEP] [CLS] Furthermore, the rain washed away bridges and roads in the area, as well as causing crop loss.Another flood began in August. [SEP] [CLS] At least 82 people were killed during the floods, including a disaster involving a bus which resulted in the deaths of 27 people and the disappearance of four. [SEP] [CLS]
