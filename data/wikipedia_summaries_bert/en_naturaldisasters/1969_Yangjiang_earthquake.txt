[CLS] The 1969 Yangjiang earthquake occurred on 26 July, at 6:49 am, Beijing local time (25 July 22:49 UTC). [SEP] [CLS] It had a magnitude of 6.4 on the Moment magnitude scale and a maximum perceived intensity of VIII (Severe) on the Mercalli intensity scale. [SEP] [CLS] It struck the city of Yangjiang and caused over 10,700 houses to collapse, severely damaging a further 36,000. [SEP] [CLS] The earthquake also caused landslides and sandblows were observed along the coast and along some rivers in the area of Yangjiang. [SEP] [CLS] The earthquake was also felt in Hong Kong. [SEP] [CLS] There were an unconfirmed total of 3,000 deaths. [SEP] [CLS]
