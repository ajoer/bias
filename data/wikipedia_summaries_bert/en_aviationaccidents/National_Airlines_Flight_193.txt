[CLS] National Airlines Flight 193, registration N4744, Donna, was a Boeing 727-235 en route from Miami, Florida to Pensacola on May 8, 1978. [SEP] [CLS] It was scheduled with stops at Melbourne, Florida; Tampa; New Orleans, Louisiana and Mobile, Alabama. [SEP] [CLS] The accident occurred at night in low visibility from fog. [SEP] [CLS] During the descent into Pensacola Regional Airport it impacted Escambia Bay, sinking in 12 feet (3.7 m) of water. [SEP] [CLS]
