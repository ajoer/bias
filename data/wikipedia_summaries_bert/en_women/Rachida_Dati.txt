Rachida Dati (French pronunciation: ​[ʁaʃida dati]; born 27 November 1965) is a French politician who served as Member of the European Parliament, representing Île-de-France. [CLS] [SEP] Prior to her election, she held the cabinet post of Keeper of the Seals, Minister of Justice. [CLS] [SEP] She was a spokesperson for Nicolas Sarkozy during the French presidential election of 2007. [CLS] [SEP] After his victory, Sarkozy appointed her to his Government on 18 May 2007. [CLS] [SEP] She was elected mayor of the 7th arrondissement of Paris on 29 March 2008. [CLS] [SEP]
