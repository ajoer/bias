[CLS] Suzann Pettersen (born 7 April 1981) is a retired Norwegian professional golfer. [SEP] [CLS] She played mainly on the U.S.-based LPGA Tour, and was also a member of the Ladies European Tour. [SEP] [CLS] Her career best world ranking was second and she held that position several times, most recently from August 2011 until February 2012. [SEP] [CLS] She retired on 15 September 2019 after holing the winning putt for the European team at the 2019 Solheim Cup, notwithstanding that she had been away from golf for almost 20 months on maternity leave prior to the event.
