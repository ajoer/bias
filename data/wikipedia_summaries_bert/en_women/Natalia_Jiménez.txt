Natalia Altea Jiménez Sarmento (born 29 December 1981), known professionally as Natalia Jiménez is a Spanish singer-songwriter. [CLS] [SEP] She is considered one of the most important female singers in Spanish. [CLS] [SEP] She has been received Grammy and Latin Grammy Awards and has sold more than 3 million albums worldwide. [CLS] [SEP] She has recorded duets with the main stars of Latin music, including Marc Anthony, Daddy Yankee and Ricky Martin among others. [CLS] [SEP]
