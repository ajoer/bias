Monique de Wilt (born 31 March 1976 in 's-Hertogenbosch) is a former Dutch athlete, specializing in the pole vault. [CLS] [SEP] She is a 14-time national champion in that event. [CLS] [SEP] She won the silver medal at the 1999 Summer Universiade. [CLS] [SEP] Her outdoor personal best is 4.40 meters, achieved in 2002, while her indoor best is 4.45 meters from 2003. [CLS] [SEP] In 2015 both Dutch national records were broken by Femke Pluim. [CLS] [SEP]
