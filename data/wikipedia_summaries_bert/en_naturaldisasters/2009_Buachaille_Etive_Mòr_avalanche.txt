The 2009 Buachaille Etive Mòr avalanche happened on Buachaille Etive Mòr in Glen Coe in the Scottish Highlands, UK, on 24 January 2009. [CLS] [SEP] Three mountain climbers were killed and one sustained a serious shoulder injury. [CLS] [SEP] Two of the dead were from Northern Ireland and the other was from Scotland. [CLS] [SEP] Nine people from at least three countries in at least two parties were involved in the incident on a mountain that is well recognised by tourists to Scotland. [CLS] [SEP] While avalanches are not uncommon in the area, very few deaths are reported—this incident has been described as "one of the worst disasters in the Scottish mountains for decades".
