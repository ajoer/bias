[CLS] Sergio Canales Madrazo (Spanish pronunciation: [ˈseɾxjo kaˈnalez maˈðɾaθo]; born 16 February 1991) is a Spanish professional footballer who plays as an attacking midfielder for Real Betis and the Spain national team. [SEP] [CLS] After starting out at Racing de Santander, he signed as a 19-year-old with Real Madrid for €4.5 million, but failed to reproduce his form at the latter club and left after only one season. [SEP] [CLS] He went on to represent, always in La Liga, Valencia, Real Sociedad and Betis. [SEP] [CLS] Canales earned 39 caps for Spain all youth levels comprised, winning the 2013 European Championship with the under-21 team. [SEP] [CLS] He made his debut for the senior squad in 2019.
