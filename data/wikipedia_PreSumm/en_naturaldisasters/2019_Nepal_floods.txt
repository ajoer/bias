[CLS] The 2019 Nepal Floods were a series of flash floods that affected widespread areas of Nepal. [SEP] [CLS] Severe flooding was seen in many regions, especially in the Terai area and Kathmandu. [SEP] [CLS] Heavy rainfall on account of the monsoon season began on the 11th of July and caused disruption in many areas, specially in Province No. [SEP] [CLS] 2 and Province No. [SEP] [CLS] 1.
