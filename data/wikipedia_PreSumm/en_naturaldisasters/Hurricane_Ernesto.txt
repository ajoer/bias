[CLS] The name Ernesto has been used for seven tropical cyclones in the Atlantic Ocean. [SEP] [CLS] Tropical Storm Ernesto (1982), formed southwest of Bermuda and dissipated without threatening land. [SEP] [CLS] Tropical Storm Ernesto (1988), formed east of Bermuda and did not cause any damage or casualties. [SEP] [CLS] Tropical Storm Ernesto (1994), formed southwest of Cape Verde and dissipated without affecting land. [SEP] [CLS] Tropical Storm Ernesto (2000), lasted for two days and did not threaten land. [SEP] [CLS] Hurricane Ernesto (2006), a Category 1 hurricane which formed near the Windward Islands, made landfall in Haiti and Cuba, struck Florida and the Carolinas, and killed at least 11 people. [SEP] [CLS] Hurricane Ernesto (2012), a Category 2 hurricane which made landfall in Mexico. [SEP] [CLS] Tropical Storm Ernesto (2018), formed in the North Atlantic and dissipated without affecting land. [SEP] [CLS]
