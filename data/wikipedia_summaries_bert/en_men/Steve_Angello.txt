[CLS] Steven Patrik Josefsson Fragogiannis (Greek: Στέφεν Πάτρικ Γιόζεφσον Φραγκόγιαννης; born 22 November 1982), better known by his stage name Steve Angello, is a Greek-Swedish DJ, record producer, remixer and record label owner. [SEP] [CLS] He is a member of Swedish House Mafia, together with Axwell and Sebastian Ingrosso. [SEP] [CLS]
