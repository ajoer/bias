Agbarjin (Agvarjin) or Akbarjin (Chinese: 阿噶多爾濟) was a khagan claimant of the Northern Yuan dynasty based in Mongolia. [CLS] [SEP] He was the youngest brother of Tayisung Khan Toghtoa Bukha and Manduul Khan.Tayisung Khan appointed Agbarjin jinong over his ulus in 1433. [CLS] [SEP] When Emperor Toghtoa Bukha attempted to eliminate Esen taishi's influence at the court, the Oirats offered Agbarjin the throne of khaganship for his betrayal. [CLS] [SEP] Even though the latter's son, Qara-qurtag Duuren taiji, persuaded his father not to accept this offer, the Oirats and Agbarjin attacked and defeated the Khagan, forcing him to flee westward. [CLS] [SEP] Tayisung Khagan Toghtoa Bukha was killed by his former father-in-law Tsabdan, whose daughter Altaghana had been banished from the ordo (palace). [CLS] [SEP] The Oirats did not trust him, as he had betrayed his own eldest brother. [CLS] [SEP] Soon after the death of the Emperor, Agbarjin was invited by Esen to a banquet. [CLS] [SEP] When the former and his entourages arrived, Esen and the Oirat leaders murdered all Borjigin males in his entourage except Qara-qurtag, who fled and was later murdered, either in Moghulistan or Central Siberia. [CLS] [SEP] Agbarjin's death paved the way for Esen's enthronement in 1454. [CLS] [SEP]
