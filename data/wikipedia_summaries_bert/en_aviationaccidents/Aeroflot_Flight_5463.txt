[CLS] Aeroflot Flight 5463 was a Soviet domestic passenger flight from Chelyabinsk to Almaty which crashed on 30 August 1983 while approaching Almaty. [SEP] [CLS] The Tupolev Tu-134A collided with the western slope of Dolan Mountain at an altitude of 690 m (2,260 ft). [SEP] [CLS] As a result of the accident, all ninety people on board were killed. [SEP] [CLS] Crew error was cited as the cause of the accident. [SEP] [CLS]
