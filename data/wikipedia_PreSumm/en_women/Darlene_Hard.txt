[CLS] Darlene Ruth Hard (born January 6, 1936) is a former professional tennis player from the U.S. [SEP] [CLS] Known for her volleying ability and strong serves, she captured singles titles at the French Championships in 1960 and the U.S. [SEP] [CLS] Championships in 1960 and 1961. [SEP] [CLS] With eight different partners, she won a total of 13 women's doubles titles in Grand Slam tournaments. [SEP] [CLS] Her last doubles title, at the age of 33 at the 1969 US Open, came six years after she had retired from serious competition to become a tennis instructor. [SEP] [CLS] She also played the US Open singles tournament in 1969, losing in the second round to Françoise Dürr, 3–6, 3–6. [SEP] [CLS]
