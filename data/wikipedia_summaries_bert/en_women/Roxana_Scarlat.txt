Roxana Scarlat-Bârlădeanu (born 3 January 1975) is a Romanian fencer. [CLS] [SEP] She won a silver medal in the women's team foil event at the 1996 Summer Olympics.Scarlat retired from competition after missing the qualification for the 2008 Summer Olympics. [CLS] [SEP] She is now an official of the Romanian Fencing Federation. [CLS] [SEP] She married Marius Bârlădeanu. [CLS] [SEP] The couple have two sons, Tudor-Ioan and Vlad-Iustin. [CLS] [SEP]
