The name Kammuri has been used to name four tropical cyclones in the northwestern Pacific Ocean. [CLS] [SEP] It is the Japanese term for the constellation Corona Borealis. [CLS] [SEP] Severe Tropical Storm Kammuri (2002) (T0212, 16W, Lagalag) Severe Tropical Storm Kammuri (2008) (T0809, 10W, Julian) – struck China and Vietnam. [CLS] [SEP] Severe Tropical Storm Kammuri (2014) (T1417, 17W) Typhoon Kammuri (2019) (T1928, 29W, Tisoy) – a powerful typhoon that struck the Philippines during the 2019 Southeast Asian Games.After 2019, the name Kammuri was retired. [CLS] [SEP] A replacement name will be chosen in early 2021. [CLS] [SEP]
