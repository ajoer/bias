[CLS] The 1906 Mississippi hurricane was a deadly and destructive hurricane during the 1906 Atlantic hurricane season. [SEP] [CLS] The fourth hurricane of the season, the system was originally observed in the western Caribbean on September 22; however, modern research revealed that the system became a tropical depression on September 19. [SEP] [CLS] The system slowly intensified, eventually becoming a major hurricane by September 24. [SEP] [CLS] The system made landfall near Pascagoula, Mississippi, during the evening of September 27, devastating the cities of Pensacola and Mobile and the state of Mississippi. [SEP] [CLS] Damage totaled to at least $19,221,000, and more than 134 people were killed. [SEP] [CLS]
