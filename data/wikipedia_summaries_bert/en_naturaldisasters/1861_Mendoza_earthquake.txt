[CLS] The 1861 Mendoza earthquake occurred in the province of Mendoza, Argentina on 20 March at 11:30 PM. [SEP] [CLS] It had a magnitude of 7.2 on the Ms scale and an intensity of IX in the Mercalli scale. [SEP] [CLS] Its hypocenter was located at an estimated depth of 30 kilometres (19 mi). [SEP] [CLS] The earthquake devastated the provincial capital, Mendoza, killing 4,300 of its 12,000 residents and injuring 750. [SEP] [CLS] [1] Most of the buildings were destroyed, including the cabildo (colonial government house). [SEP] [CLS] The town was rebuilt in a nearby location, and the authorities moved to their new seat in 1863. [SEP] [CLS] The new constructions, which incorporated modern architectural tendencies, were markedly different from the old colonial buildings. [SEP] [CLS]
