[CLS] Typhoon Mujigae (pronounced [mu.dʑi.ɡɛ]), known in the Philippines as Tropical Storm Kabayan, was the strongest storm to strike the province of Guangdong in the month of October. [SEP] [CLS] The storm affected the Philippines, southern China and northern Vietnam during early October 2015 as a strong typhoon. [SEP] [CLS] The typhoon originated from a weak tropical disturbance near Palau on September 28. [SEP] [CLS] The system subsequently developed into the twenty-second named storm of the annual typhoon season on October 1. [SEP] [CLS]
