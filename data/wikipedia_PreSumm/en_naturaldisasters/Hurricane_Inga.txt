[CLS] Hurricane Inga is the third longest-lived Atlantic hurricane on record. [SEP] [CLS] The 11th tropical cyclone and 9th named storm of the 1969 Atlantic hurricane season, Inga developed on September 20 in the central Atlantic and tracked westward. [SEP] [CLS] After attaining tropical storm status, the system deteriorated into a depression, but once again intensified several days later.
