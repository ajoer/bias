Brigetta LaShea Barrett (born December 24, 1990 in Westchester County, New York) is a former high jumper from the United States. [CLS] [SEP] Her biggest success is winning the silver medal at the 2012 Olympic Games in London. [CLS] [SEP] She retired in 2016 at the age of 25 before coming back in 2017. [CLS] [SEP]
