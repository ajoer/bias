On 13 December 2014, a landslide in Banjarnegara, Central Java, Indonesia, killing 93 with 23 people missing. [CLS] [SEP] The disaster occurred on Jemblung Village in Banjarnegara, Indonesia, at Friday, around 03.00 p.m. [CLS] [SEP] At the time, most of the villagers were taking a nap on their houses. [CLS] [SEP] The landslides also trapped some vehicles on the road. [CLS] [SEP] Amateur video caught the moment when a large piece of the mountain fell to the whole village. [CLS] [SEP] Around 200 people survived the disaster, another a hundred people didn't, which represents a third population of the village who didn't come out alive.The 2014 Banjarnegara landslide was one of the worst and deadliest landslide in Indonesia since 2006.
