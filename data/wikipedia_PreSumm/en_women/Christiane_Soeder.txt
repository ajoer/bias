[CLS] Christiane Soeder (born January 15, 1975 in Remscheid, North Rhine-Westphalia) is a German-born Austrian road racing cyclist and former duathlete who now lives in Vienna. [SEP] [CLS] She won the Austrian National Road Race Championships in 2004, 2006 and 2009. [SEP] [CLS] She finished fourth in the 2008 Olympic road race with a time of 3h 32′ 28. [SEP] [CLS] She rides professionally for Garmin–Cervélo. [SEP] [CLS]
