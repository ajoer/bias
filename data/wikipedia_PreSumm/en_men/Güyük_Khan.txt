[CLS] Güyük (or Kuyuk; Mongolian: Гүюг хаан, romanized: Güyug khaan, Middle Mongolian: güyüg qaγan) (c. [SEP] [CLS] March 19, 1206 – April 20, 1248) was the third Great Khan of the Mongol Empire, the eldest son of Ögedei Khan and a grandson of Genghis Khan. [SEP] [CLS] He reigned from 1246 to 1248.
