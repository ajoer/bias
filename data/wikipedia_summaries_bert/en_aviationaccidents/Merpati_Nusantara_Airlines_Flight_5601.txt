PT Merpati Nusantara Airlines, operating as Merpati Nusantara Airlines, was an airline in Indonesia based in Central Jakarta, Jakarta. [CLS] [SEP] It operated scheduled domestic services to more than 25 destinations in Indonesia, as well as scheduled international services to East Timor and Malaysia. [CLS] [SEP] The word merpati is Indonesian for "dove", and Nusantara is a Javanese word found in the Pararaton ("the Book of Kings", probably written in the 16th century) meaning "the outer islands", referring to the Indonesian archipelago. [CLS] [SEP] The airline was based at Soekarno-Hatta International Airport, Jakarta. [CLS] [SEP] It also maintained both a maintenance and simulator facility at Juanda International Airport, Surabaya. [CLS] [SEP] The Merpati Training Centre at Surabaya housed Fokker F-27, AVIC MA60 and CN-235 full motion simulators. [CLS] [SEP] All services were suspended in 2014 due to financial and regulatory issues. [CLS] [SEP]
