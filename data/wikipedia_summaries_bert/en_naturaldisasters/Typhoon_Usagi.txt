[CLS] The name Usagi has been used to name four tropical cyclones in the northwestern Pacific Ocean. [SEP] [CLS] The name was contributed by Japan and is the Japanese name of the constellation Lepus. [SEP] [CLS] Tropical Storm Usagi (2001) (T0110, 13W) – struck Vietnam. [SEP] [CLS] Typhoon Usagi (2007) (T0705, 05W) – struck Japan. [SEP] [CLS] Typhoon Usagi (2013) (T1319, 17W, Odette) – struck the Philippines and China and one of the strongest typhoons in 2013. [SEP] [CLS] Severe Tropical Storm Usagi (2018) (T1829, 33W, Samuel) - a weak system that made landfall in Vietnam in November 2018. [SEP] [CLS]
