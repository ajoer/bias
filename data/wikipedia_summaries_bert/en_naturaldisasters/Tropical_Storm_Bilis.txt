[CLS] Severe Tropical Storm Bilis, known in the Philippines as Tropical Storm Florita, was a weak tropical cyclone in July 2006 that caused significant damage to areas of the Philippines, Taiwan, and southeastern China. [SEP] [CLS] The word Bilis, submitted by the Philippines, means 'speed' or 'swiftness' in Tagalog.Despite never officially reaching typhoon strength, Bilis was responsible for $4.4 billion (2006 USD) in damage and 859 fatalities in the Philippines, Taiwan, and China. [SEP] [CLS] Most of the damage was caused by heavy rain, which triggered widespread flash flooding and landslides. [SEP] [CLS] Many of the areas Bilis flooded were later affected by Typhoon Kaemi, Typhoon Prapiroon, and intense Typhoon Saomai. [SEP] [CLS]
