[CLS] The 1996 Biak earthquake, or the 1996 Irian Jaya earthquake, occurred on February 17 at 14:59:30 local time near Biak Island, Indonesia. [SEP] [CLS] The earthquake had a moment magnitude of 8.2 and a maximum Mercalli intensity of VIII (Severe). [SEP] [CLS] The run-up height of the generated tsunami reached 7 meters (23 ft). [SEP] [CLS] One-hundred and sixty-six people were reported dead, 423 were injured, and 5,090 were made homeless. [SEP] [CLS] This earthquake was a thrust in a very oblique subduction zone. [SEP] [CLS] It ruptured at least 270 km (170 mi) along the New Guinea trench. [SEP] [CLS] The slip distribution is very nonuniform.
