[CLS] [SEP] By the time he was born, Emperor Wu was already 62. [CLS] [SEP] Prince Fuling ascended the throne after the death of Emperor Wu in 87 BC. [CLS] [SEP] He was only eight years old. [CLS] [SEP] Huo Guang served as regent. [CLS] [SEP] Emperor Wu's long reign left the Han Dynasty greatly expanded; however constant warfare had depleted the empire's coffers. [CLS] [SEP] Emperor Zhao, under the tutelage of Huo, took the initiative and lowered taxes as well as reducing government spending. [CLS] [SEP] As a result, citizens prospered and the Han Dynasty enjoyed an era of peace. [CLS] [SEP] Emperor Zhao died after reigning for 13 years, at the age of 20. [CLS] [SEP] He was succeeded by He, Prince of Changyi. [CLS] [SEP]
