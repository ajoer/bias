[CLS] The 1738 Vrancea earthquake occurred on 11 June [O.S. [SEP] [CLS] 31 May] 1738, during the third rule of Constantin Mavrocordat. [SEP] [CLS] The seism aroused great panic and is mentioned in several sources. [SEP] [CLS] It occurred in the lower lithospheric block, at a depth of 130 km. [SEP] [CLS] Its effects were violent on large areas, the hardest hit being Bucharest, where several houses and churches collapsed. [SEP] [CLS] The Romanian territories were not the only affected.
