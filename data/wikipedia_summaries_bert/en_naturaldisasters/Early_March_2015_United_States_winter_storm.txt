[CLS] The Early March 2015 North American winter storm was a significant snow and ice storm that plowed through much of the United States, bringing 1–2 feet (12–24 in) of snow and record cold temperatures behind it. [SEP] [CLS] The storm actually occurred in two phases, with the latter bringing the cold temperatures behind it in its wake. [SEP] [CLS] Record cold temperatures even spread down to as far as northern Florida. [SEP] [CLS]
