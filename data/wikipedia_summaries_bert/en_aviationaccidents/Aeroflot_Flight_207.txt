Aeroflot Flight 207 was a Soviet domestic passenger flight from Rostov-on-Don Airport to Tbilisi International Airport that crashed on 10 June 1960 in the Tkvarcheli district. [CLS] [SEP] The crash involved an Ilyushin Il-14 aircraft operated by Aeroflot. [CLS] [SEP] All 24 passengers and 7 crew on board were killed. [CLS] [SEP]
