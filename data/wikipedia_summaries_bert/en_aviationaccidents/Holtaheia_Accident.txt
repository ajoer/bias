[CLS] The 1961 Holtaheia Vickers Viking crash (Norwegian: Holtaheia-ulykken) was a controlled flight into terrain incident on 9 August 1961 at Holta in Strand, Norway. [SEP] [CLS] The Eagle Airways (later, British Eagle) Vickers 610 Viking 3B Lord Rodney was en route from London to Stavanger Airport, Sola on an AIR Tours charter flight taking a school group for a camping holiday. [SEP] [CLS] The aircraft was making an instrument landing when it crashed 54 km (34 mi) north east of Stavanger. [SEP] [CLS] The accident killed all 39 people on board. [SEP] [CLS]
