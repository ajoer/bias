[CLS] Maria Zemankova (born 6 January 1951 ) is a Computer Scientist who is known for the theory and implementation of the first Fuzzy Relational Database System. [SEP] [CLS] This research has become important for the handling of approximate queries in databases. [SEP] [CLS] She is currently a Program Officer in the Intelligent Information Systems Division at the National Science Foundation. [SEP] [CLS] She is the first (1992) recipient of the SIGMOD Contributions Award for her work in the conception of initiatives in research on scientific databases and digital libraries. [SEP] [CLS] She received her Ph.D. [SEP] [CLS] in Computer Science in 1983 from Florida State University for her work on Fuzzy Relational Database Systems. [SEP] [CLS]
