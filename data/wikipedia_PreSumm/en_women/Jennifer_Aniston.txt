[CLS] Jennifer Joanna Aniston (born February 11, 1969) is an American actress, film producer, and businesswoman. [SEP] [CLS] The daughter of actors John Aniston and Nancy Dow, she began working as an actress at an early age with an uncredited role in the 1987 film Mac and Me. [SEP] [CLS] Her first major film role came in the 1993 horror comedy Leprechaun. [SEP] [CLS] Since her career grew in the early 1990s, Aniston has been one of the highest-paid actresses in Hollywood. [SEP] [CLS] The character became widely popular and was described as one of the greatest female characters in American television history. [SEP] [CLS] Her biggest box office successes include the films Bruce Almighty (2003), The Break-Up (2006), Marley & Me (2008), Just Go with It (2011), Horrible Bosses (2011), and We're the Millers (2013), each of which grossed over $200 million in worldwide box office receipts. [SEP] [CLS] Some of her most critically acclaimed film roles include Office Space (1999), The Good Girl (2002), Friends with Money (2006), Cake (2014), and Dumplin' (2018). [SEP] [CLS] She returned to television in 2019, producing and starring in the Apple TV+ drama series The Morning Show, for which she won another Screen Actors Guild Award. [SEP] [CLS] Aniston has been included in numerous magazines' lists of the world's most beautiful women. [SEP] [CLS] Her net worth is estimated to be US$200 million. [SEP] [CLS] She is the recipient of a star on the Hollywood Walk of Fame. [SEP] [CLS] Aniston is also the co-founder of the production company Echo Films, established in 2008. [SEP] [CLS] Divorced from actor Brad Pitt, to whom she was married for five years, Aniston is separated from actor Justin Theroux, whom she married in 2015. [SEP] [CLS]
