Catherine I (Russian: Екатери́на I Алексе́евна, tr. [CLS] [SEP] Yekaterina I Alekseyevna; born Polish: Marta Helena Skowrońska, later known as Marta Samuilovna Skavronskaya; 15 April [O.S. [CLS] [SEP] 5 April] 1684 – 17 May [O.S. [CLS] [SEP] 6 May] 1727) was the second wife of Peter the Great and Empress of Russia from 1725 until her death. [CLS] [SEP]
