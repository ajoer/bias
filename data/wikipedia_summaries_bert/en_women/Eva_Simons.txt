[CLS] Eva Simons (born April 27, 1984) is a Dutch singer-songwriter from Amsterdam. [SEP] [CLS] She is best known for her song "Policeman" and collaborations with artists such as Afrojack; in 2012, Simons broke through internationally after being featured on the will.i.am single "This Is Love". [SEP] [CLS] During her solo career she has released 12 singles, including "Bludfire" which appeared in November 2015. [SEP] [CLS]
