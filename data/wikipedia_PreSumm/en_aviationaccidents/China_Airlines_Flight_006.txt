[CLS] China Airlines Flight 006 (callsign "Dynasty 006") was a daily non-stop flight from Taipei to Los Angeles International Airport. [SEP] [CLS] On 19 February 1985, the Boeing 747SP operating the flight was involved in an aircraft upset accident, following the failure of the No. [SEP] [CLS] 4 engine, while cruising at 41,000 ft (12,500 m). [SEP] [CLS] The plane rolled over and plunged 30,000 ft (9,100 m), experiencing high speeds and g-forces (approaching and, at one point, exceeding 5g) before the captain was able to recover from the dive, and then to divert to San Francisco International Airport.
