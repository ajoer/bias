Herman Frans Martha Brusselmans (Dutch pronunciation: [ˈɦɛrmɑn ˈbrʏsəlmɑns]; born 9 October 1957) is a Belgian novelist, poet, playwright and columnist. [CLS] [SEP] He lives in Ghent. [CLS] [SEP] He is one of the best-selling authors in Flanders, but controversial at the same time for his profane language and offensive comedy. [CLS] [SEP]
