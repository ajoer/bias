[CLS] The 2004 Alor earthquake occurred on the island of Alor 21:26:41 UTC 11 November 2004. [SEP] [CLS] Alor is an island located in Indonesia, the largest island of the Alor Archipelago with almost 16,800 residents. [SEP] [CLS] The earthquake had a magnitude of 7.5, on the moment magnitude scale, with an epicenter on Alor at a depth of about 10 km (6.2 mi). [SEP] [CLS] It was recorded on 301 stations. [SEP] [CLS] The epicenter of the earthquake was located 1,600 km (990 mi) east of the capital of Jakarta. [SEP] [CLS] Hundreds of homes and much infrastructure was damaged with 23 deaths and thousands of casualties. [SEP] [CLS]
