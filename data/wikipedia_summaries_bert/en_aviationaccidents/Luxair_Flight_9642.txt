[CLS] Luxair Flight 9642 was a scheduled international passenger flight flying from Berlin Tempelhof Airport, Germany, to Luxembourg Findel Airport, Luxembourg. [SEP] [CLS] The flight carried 19 passengers and 3 crew members. [SEP] [CLS] The flight was operated by Luxair, the flag carrier (national airline) of Luxembourg. [SEP] [CLS] The plane was a Fokker 50 and registered as LX-LGB. [SEP] [CLS] Lufthansa had a codeshare on the flight as LH2420. [SEP] [CLS] On 6 November 2002, the flight crashed while on final approach to Luxembourg Findel Airport about 2 nautical miles (2.3 mi; 3.7 km) short of the runway while trying to land in fog. [SEP] [CLS] 20 out of the 22 passengers and crew perished. [SEP] [CLS] The crash is the deadliest aviation disaster to occur in Luxembourg. [SEP] [CLS]
