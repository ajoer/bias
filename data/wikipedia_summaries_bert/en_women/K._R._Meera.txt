K. [CLS] [SEP] R. [CLS] [SEP] Meera (born 19 February 1970) is an Indian author and journalist, who writes in Malayalam. [CLS] [SEP] She was born in Sasthamkotta, Kollam district in Kerala. [CLS] [SEP] She worked as a journalist in Malayala Manorama but later resigned to concentrate more on writing. [CLS] [SEP] She started writing fiction in 2001 and her first short story collection Ormayude Njarambu was published in 2002. [CLS] [SEP] Since then she has published five collections of short stories, two novellas, five novels and two children's books. [CLS] [SEP] She won the Kerala Sahitya Akademi Award in 2009 for her short-story, Ave Maria. [CLS] [SEP] Her novel Aarachaar (2012) is widely regarded as one of the best literary works produced in Malayalam language. [CLS] [SEP] It received several awards including the Kerala Sahitya Akademi Award (2013), Odakkuzhal Award (2013), Vayalar Award (2014) and Kendra Sahitya Akademi Award (2015). [CLS] [SEP] It was also shortlisted for the 2016 DSC Prize for South Asian Literature. [CLS] [SEP]
