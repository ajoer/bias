American Eagle Flight 5456 was a scheduled commuter flight between Luis Muñoz Marín International Airport in San Juan, Puerto Rico and Eugenio María de Hostos Airport in Mayagüez, Puerto Rico. [CLS] [SEP] The flight was operated by Executive Airlines, doing business as American Eagle, and was operated by a CASA C-212 aircraft. [CLS] [SEP] Instrument meteorological conditions were present as the plane made its approach to Mayagüez on June 7, 1992. [CLS] [SEP] The plane crashed during heavy rain into a swamp, short of the runway. [CLS] [SEP] The crash destroyed the aircraft, killing all five people on board. [CLS] [SEP]
