Adelaide of Meissen (Czech: Adléta Míšeňská, German: Adelheid von Meißen; c. [CLS] [SEP] 1160 – 2 February 1211), a member of the House of Wettin, was Queen of Bohemia from 1198 to 1199 as the first wife of King Ottokar I. [CLS] [SEP] When her husband declared their marriage null and void, she began a longstanding legal dispute that involved numerous religious and secular dignitaries of her time. [CLS] [SEP]
