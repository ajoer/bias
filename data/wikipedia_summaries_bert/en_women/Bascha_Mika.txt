Bascha Mika (born January 17, 1954) is a German journalist and publicist. [CLS] [SEP] From 1998 to July 2009 she was editor-in-chief of Die Tageszeitung and has held the same post at Frankfurt Rundschau since April 2014. [CLS] [SEP] At Die Tageszeitung, Mika was the only female editor-in-chief of a national newspaper in Germany. [CLS] [SEP]
