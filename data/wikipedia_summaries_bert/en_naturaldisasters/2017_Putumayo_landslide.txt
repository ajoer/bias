The Mocoa landslide was a natural disaster that took place in 2017. [CLS] [SEP] During the pre-dawn hours of 1 April 2017, locally heavy rain triggered flash flooding and landslides in the city of Mocoa, Putumayo, Colombia, killing at least 336 people, injuring 400, and leaving 200 others missing. [CLS] [SEP] It is the third-deadliest weather-related disaster in Colombian history, and is regarded as the worst catastrophe in the history of Mocoa. [CLS] [SEP]
