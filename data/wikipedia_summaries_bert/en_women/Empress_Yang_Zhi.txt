Empress Yang Zhi (楊芷) (259–292), courtesy name Jilan (季蘭), nickname Nanyin (男胤), formally Empress Wudao (武悼皇后, literally "the martial and fearful empress") was an empress of Jin Dynasty (265–420). [CLS] [SEP] She was Emperor Wu's second wife and cousin to his first wife, Empress Yang Yan. [CLS] [SEP]
