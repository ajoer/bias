Continental Charters Flight 44-2, a domestic non scheduled passenger flight from Miami, Florida to Buffalo, New York, crashed on December 29, 1951 near Napoli, New York. [CLS] [SEP] The twin engine C-46 Commando, registration N3944C, crashed approximately 10:25 pm in adverse weather conditions. [CLS] [SEP] Of the four crew and 36 passengers on board, 3 crew members and 23 passengers perished. [CLS] [SEP] The flight crew's poor judgment in attempting a flight by visual reference during instrument weather conditions was the cause of the accident. [CLS] [SEP]
