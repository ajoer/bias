[CLS] Donna Auguste (born 1958) is an American businesswoman, entrepreneur, and philanthropist. [SEP] [CLS] She was the founder and chief executive officer (CEO) of Freshwater Software from 1996 to 2001. [SEP] [CLS] Prior to Freshwater Software, she was a senior engineering manager at Apple Computer who helped to coordinate the development of the Newton personal digital assistant (PDA).In 2001, she sold Freshwater Software to Mercury Interactive Corp. [SEP] [CLS] for $147 million. [SEP] [CLS] In the same year, she was recognized as one of the "25 Women Who Are Making It Big in Small Business" by Fortune Magazine. [SEP] [CLS] She also won the 2001 Golden Torch Award for Outstanding Woman in Technology. [SEP] [CLS]
