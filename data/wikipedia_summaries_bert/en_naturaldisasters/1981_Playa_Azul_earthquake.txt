[CLS] The 1981 Playa Azul earthquake occurred on October 24, 1981, at 21:22 local time (03:22 UTC on October 25). [SEP] [CLS] It was located near Playa Azul, Michoacán, Mexico. [SEP] [CLS] The magnitude of the earthquake was Mw 7.2, or Ms 7.3. [SEP] [CLS] Three deaths were reported, two from Michoacán and one from Mexico City. [SEP] [CLS] Some buildings were damaged in both Michoacán and Mexico City. [SEP] [CLS] A small tsunami was registered in Acapulco with a maximum height of 9 cm. [SEP] [CLS]
