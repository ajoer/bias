[CLS] Lucie Hradecká (Czech pronunciation: [ˈlutsɪjɛ ˈɦradɛtskaː]; born 21 May 1985, in Prague) is a tennis player from the Czech Republic. [SEP] [CLS] In her career, Hradecká has won 21 WTA doubles titles, and two Grand Slam titles, the 2011 French Open and the 2013 US Open, partnered both times by fellow Czech Andrea Hlaváčková. [SEP] [CLS] The pair are also the 2012 Olympic silver medallists in doubles. [SEP] [CLS] Hradecká has also won a mixed doubles title at the 2013 French Open with František Čermák, and an Olympic bronze medal alongside Radek Štěpánek at the 2016 Rio Olympics. [SEP] [CLS] Her biggest singles career highlight to date was defeating former world No. [SEP] [CLS] 1 Ana Ivanovic in the first round of the 2015 Australian Open. [SEP] [CLS]
