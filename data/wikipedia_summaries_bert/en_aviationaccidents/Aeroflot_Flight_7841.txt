[CLS] Aeroflot Flight 7841 was a scheduled Soviet domestic passenger flight from Minsk to Leningrad (now Saint Petersburg), which crashed on 1 February 1985 killing fifty eight people on board. [SEP] [CLS] Twenty-two people (including three crew members) survived the accident. [SEP] [CLS] The crash was caused by engine failure brought on by ice ingestion. [SEP] [CLS] On 8 May 1985 the Tupolev Tu-134A was officially written off. [SEP] [CLS]
