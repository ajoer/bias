On 22 December 2015, a light aircraft of the Indian Border Security Force crashed within the grounds of Indira Gandhi International Airport in Delhi, India. [CLS] [SEP] Ten personnel of the Border Security Force, three officers and seven senior technicians, were killed. [CLS] [SEP] The 22-year-old Beechcraft B200 King Air took off for Ranchi just before 9.30am, before crashing shortly afterwards. [CLS] [SEP]
