[CLS] Cecilia Bartoli, Cavaliere OMRI (Italian: [tʃeˈtʃiːlja ˈbartoli]; born 4 June 1966) is an Italian coloratura mezzo-soprano opera singer and recitalist. [SEP] [CLS] She is best known for her interpretations of the music of Bellini, Mozart and Rossini, as well as for her performances of lesser-known music from the Baroque and Classical period. [SEP] [CLS] She is known for having the versatility to sing both soprano and mezzo roles. [SEP] [CLS]
