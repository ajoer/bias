[CLS] Herta Oberheuser (15 May 1911 – 24 January 1978) was a Nazi physician and convicted war criminal who performed medical atrocities on prisoners at the Ravensbrück women's concentration camp. [SEP] [CLS] She was sentenced to 20 years' imprisonment at the Nuremberg Doctors' trial, but served only five. [SEP] [CLS] A survivor of Ravensbrück termed her, "a beast masquerading as a human".
