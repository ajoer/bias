[CLS] Senta Berger (Austrian German: [ˈsɛnta ˈbɛʁɡɐ] (listen), German: [ˈzɛnta ˈbɛʁɡɐ] (listen); born 13 May 1941) is an Austrian film, stage and television actress, producer and author living in Germany. [SEP] [CLS] She received many award nominations for her acting in theatre, film and television; her awards include three Bambi Awards, two Romys, an Adolf Grimme Award, both a Deutscher and a Bayerischer Fernsehpreis, and a Goldene Kamera.
