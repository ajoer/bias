Ascot Racecourse ("ascot" pronounced , often pronounced ) is a British racecourse, located in Ascot, Berkshire, England, which is used for thoroughbred horse racing. [CLS] [SEP] It is one of the leading racecourses in the United Kingdom, hosting 13 of Britain's 36 annual Group 1 horse races.The course, owned by Ascot Racecourse Ltd, enjoys close associations with the British Royal Family, being approximately 6 miles (9.7 km) from Windsor Castle.Ascot currently stages 26 days of racing over the course of the year, comprising 18 flat meetings held between the months of May and October inclusive. [CLS] [SEP] It also stages important jump racing throughout the winter months. [CLS] [SEP] The Royal Meeting held each June, remains a major draw, its highlight being The Gold Cup. [CLS] [SEP] The most prestigious race is the King George VI and Queen Elizabeth Stakes run over the course in July. [CLS] [SEP]
