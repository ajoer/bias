[CLS] Henri Kontinen (Finnish pronunciation: [ˈhenri ˈkontinen]; born 19 June 1990) is a Finnish tennis player. [SEP] [CLS] Kontinen has won 21 doubles titles on the ATP Tour in his career. [SEP] [CLS] He was formerly ranked as world No. [SEP] [CLS] 1 in doubles after winning the 2017 Australian Open men's doubles championship. [SEP] [CLS] He reached his best singles rankings of world No. [SEP] [CLS] 220 in October 2010. [SEP] [CLS] His brother Micke is a retired tennis player. [SEP] [CLS] After ending his singles career due to injuries he started focusing on doubles. [SEP] [CLS] He reached the quarterfinals of the men's doubles 2016 Wimbledon Championships with John Peers and won the mixed doubles at the same tournament with Heather Watson. [SEP] [CLS] His win in the 2017 Australian Open men's doubles with Peers was his first Grand Slam title in men's doubles. [SEP] [CLS]
