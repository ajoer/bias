[CLS] Federico Gamboa Iglesias (22 December 1864 in Mexico City – 15 August 1939 in Mexico City) was a writer and diplomat from Mexico. [SEP] [CLS] He has been considered as one of the top representatives of Naturalism in México. [SEP] [CLS] Gamboa wrote novels, theater pieces, articles for newspapers and magazines and an autobiography when he was 28 years old. [SEP] [CLS] For many years took notes of his travels, experiences and thought, which he later published as five diaries. [SEP] [CLS] Posthumously another two more volumes of his diaries were published. [SEP] [CLS]
