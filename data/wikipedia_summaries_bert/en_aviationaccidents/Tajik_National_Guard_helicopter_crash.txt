On 6 October 2010. [CLS] [SEP] a Mil Mi-8 military helicopter from the Tajik National Guard crashed in the Rasht Valley close to Ezgand and Tavildara, Tajikistan, killing all 28 people on board. [CLS] [SEP] The helicopter became caught in power lines and crashed while attempting to land. [CLS] [SEP] It was the deadliest accident in Tajik aviation since 1997. [CLS] [SEP]
