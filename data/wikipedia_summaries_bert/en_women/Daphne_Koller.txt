[CLS] Daphne Koller (Hebrew: דפנה קולר‎; born August 27, 1968) is an Israeli-American computer scientist. [SEP] [CLS] She has been a Professor in the Department of Computer Science at Stanford University and a MacArthur Fellowship recipient. [SEP] [CLS] She is one of the founders of Coursera, an online education platform. [SEP] [CLS] Her general research area is artificial intelligence and its applications in the biomedical sciences. [SEP] [CLS] Koller was featured in a 2004 article by MIT Technology Review titled "10 Emerging Technologies That Will Change Your World" concerning the topic of Bayesian machine learning.
