[CLS] Ramón Freire Serrano (American Spanish: [raˈmoɱ ˈfɾejɾe]; November 29, 1787 – December 9, 1851) was a Chilean political figure. [SEP] [CLS] He was head of state on several occasions, and enjoyed a numerous following until the War of the Confederation. [SEP] [CLS] Ramón Freire was one of the principal leaders of the liberal Pipiolo movement. [SEP] [CLS] He has been praised by historian Gabriel Salazar as the most democratic leader of the early republican period in Chile. [SEP] [CLS]
