[CLS] Barbara Sukowa (German: [ˈbaʁ.ba.ʁa ˈzu.kɔ.va] (listen); born 2 February 1950) is a German theatre and film actress. [SEP] [CLS] She is known for her work with directors Rainer Werner Fassbinder and Margarethe von Trotta. [SEP] [CLS] She won the Cannes Film Festival Award for Best Actress for the 1986 film Rosa Luxemburg. [SEP] [CLS] Her other film appearances include Lola (1981), Europa (1990), M. [SEP] [CLS] Butterfly (1993), and Hannah Arendt (2012). [SEP] [CLS] She starred as Katarina Jones in the television series 12 Monkeys (2015–18). [SEP] [CLS]
