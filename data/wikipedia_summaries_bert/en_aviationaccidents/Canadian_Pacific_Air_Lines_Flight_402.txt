[CLS] On March 4, 1966, Canadian Pacific Air Lines Flight 402 (CP402) struck the approach lights and a seawall during a night landing attempt in poor visibility at Tokyo International Airport in Japan. [SEP] [CLS] Of the 62 passengers and 10 crew, only 8 passengers survived. [SEP] [CLS] An American Broadcasting Company news vice-president who had been touring the network's Asian bureau was among the 64 fatalities. [SEP] [CLS]
