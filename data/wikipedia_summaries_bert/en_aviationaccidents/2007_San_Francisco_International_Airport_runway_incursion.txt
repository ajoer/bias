The 2007 San Francisco International Airport runway incursion occurred around 1:36 pm PST on May 26, 2007 when SkyWest Airlines (operating as United Express) Flight 5741, an Embraer EMB 120 Brasilia turboprop aircraft, nearly collided with Republic Airlines (operating as Frontier Airlines) Flight 4912, an Embraer 170 Regional Jet, at the intersection of runways 1L and 28R at San Francisco International Airport (SFO), South San Francisco, California, United States.There were no injuries to occupants and no damage to either aircraft. [CLS] [SEP] US Federal Aviation Administration (FAA) officials described the runway incursion as the most serious incident of its kind in at least a decade, and the US National Transportation Safety Board (NTSB) carried out an investigation into the incident.
