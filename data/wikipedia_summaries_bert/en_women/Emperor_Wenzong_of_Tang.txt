Emperor Wenzong of Tang (809–840), personal name Li Ang, né Li Han (李涵), was an emperor of the Tang dynasty of China. [CLS] [SEP] He reigned from 827 to 840. [CLS] [SEP] Emperor Wenzong was the second son of Emperor Muzong and younger brother of Emperor Jingzong. [CLS] [SEP] A rare occurrence in Chinese history, Emperor Wenzong, along with his elder brother Emperor Jingzong and younger brother Emperor Wuzong, reigned in succession. [CLS] [SEP]
