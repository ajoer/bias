[CLS] The 2009 Andhra Pradesh helicopter crash occurred on 2 September 2009 near Rudrakonda Hill, 40 nautical miles (74 km) from Kurnool, Andhra Pradesh, India. [SEP] [CLS] The helicopter was a Bell 430 helicopter owned by the Andhra Pradesh Government, and registered VT-APG. [SEP] [CLS] Fatalities included Y. [SEP] [CLS] S. [SEP] [CLS] Rajasekhara Reddy, the Chief Minister of the Indian state of Andhra Pradesh. [SEP] [CLS]
