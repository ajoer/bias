[CLS] Gerard Piqué Bernabeu (Catalan: [ʒəˈɾaɾt piˈke βəɾnəˈβew], Spanish: [ɟʝeˈɾaɾ piˈke βeɾnaˈβew]; born 2 February 1987) is a Spanish professional footballer who plays as a centre-back for Barcelona. [SEP] [CLS] Piqué is considered to be one of the best defenders in the world.Piqué joined the Manchester United academy in 2004, where he remained for four years. [SEP] [CLS] He returned to Barcelona under Pep Guardiola's leadership and helped the club win trebles in 2008–09 and 2014–15. [SEP] [CLS] He is one of only four players to have won the UEFA Champions League two years in a row with different teams, the others being Marcel Desailly, Paulo Sousa and Samuel Eto'o.Piqué has also represented Spain 102 times, making his debut on 11 February 2009. [SEP] [CLS] He played an integral role in the Spain teams that won the 2010 FIFA World Cup and UEFA Euro 2012. [SEP] [CLS]
