[CLS] Martinair Flight 495 was a McDonnell Douglas DC-10 operated by Dutch airline Martinair, that crash-landed in severe weather conditions at Faro Airport, Portugal on 21 December 1992. [SEP] [CLS] The aircraft carried 13 crew members and 327 passengers, mainly holidaymakers from the Netherlands. [SEP] [CLS] 54 passengers and 2 crew members died. [SEP] [CLS] 106 of the other occupants were badly injured. [SEP] [CLS]
