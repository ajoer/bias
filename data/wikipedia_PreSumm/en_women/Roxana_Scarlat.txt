[CLS] Roxana Scarlat-Bârlădeanu (born 3 January 1975) is a Romanian fencer. [SEP] [CLS] She won a silver medal in the women's team foil event at the 1996 Summer Olympics.Scarlat retired from competition after missing the qualification for the 2008 Summer Olympics. [SEP] [CLS] She is now an official of the Romanian Fencing Federation. [SEP] [CLS] She married Marius Bârlădeanu. [SEP] [CLS] The couple have two sons, Tudor-Ioan and Vlad-Iustin. [SEP] [CLS]
