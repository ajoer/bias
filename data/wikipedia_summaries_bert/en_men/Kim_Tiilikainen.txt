Kim Tiilikainen (born 8 July 1975) is a former professional tennis player from Finland, now a tennis coach.Tiilikainen qualified for his only Grand Slam in 1996, at the French Open. [CLS] [SEP] The only Finn in the draw, he was defeated by world no. [CLS] [SEP] 27 Andrea Gaudenzi in the opening round.From 1995 to 2003, Tiilikainen appeared in nine Davis Cup ties for Finland. [CLS] [SEP] He won nine matches, all in singles. [CLS] [SEP] His best performances include a win over Anastasios Vasiliadis in the fifth and deciding rubber against Greece in 1997 and when he came from two sets down to beat Italian Davide Sanguinetti in 2002. [CLS] [SEP] He is the current coach of the Finland Davis Cup team and has coached Polish tennis player Jerzy Janowicz. [CLS] [SEP]
