Aviateca Flight 901 was a Boeing 737-200 (registration N125GU) that crashed into the San Vicente volcano in El Salvador on approach to the airport on 9 August 1995. [CLS] [SEP] The accident killed all 65 passengers and crew on board. [CLS] [SEP] It was the deadliest aviation disaster to occur in El Salvador. [CLS] [SEP]
