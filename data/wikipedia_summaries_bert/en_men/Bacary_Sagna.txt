[CLS] Bacary Sagna (French pronunciation: ​[bakaʁi saɲa]) (born 14 February 1983) is a French professional footballer. [SEP] [CLS] He has mainly played in the Premier League, and also for the France national team. [SEP] [CLS] His former manager at Arsenal, Arsène Wenger, once described him as the best right-back in the Premier League. [SEP] [CLS] In addition to him regularly featuring as a wing-back, Sagna played as a centre-back for Arsenal on several occasions.He began his career at Auxerre, before transferring to Arsenal for £7 million in 2007. [SEP] [CLS] In 2014, shortly after winning the FA Cup, his first trophy at the club, he made a move to Manchester City. [SEP] [CLS] A full international since 2007, he was selected by France for two FIFA World Cup tournaments and one UEFA European Championship. [SEP] [CLS]
