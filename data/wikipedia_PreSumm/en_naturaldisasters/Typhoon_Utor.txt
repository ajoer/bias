[CLS] Typhoon Utor, known in the Philippines as Typhoon Labuyo, was the 15th depression and the 2nd typhoon in the 2013 typhoon season. [SEP] [CLS] It was a powerful tropical cyclone which struck the Philippines and southern China. [SEP] [CLS] Developing into a tropical storm on August 9, Utor soon underwent explosive intensification and became a typhoon within a half of day. [SEP] [CLS] After making landfall over Luzon late on August 11, the typhoon re-emerged in the South China Sea, and it ultimately made its second landfall over Guangdong, China on August 14.
