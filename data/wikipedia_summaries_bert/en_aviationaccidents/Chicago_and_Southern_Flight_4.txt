Chicago and Southern Air Lines Flight 4 was a regularly scheduled flight from New Orleans, Louisiana to Chicago, Illinois, via Jackson, Mississippi, Memphis, Tennessee, and St. [CLS] [SEP] Louis, Missouri, operated with a Lockheed Model 10 Electra. [CLS] [SEP] On August 5, 1936, after departing from Lambert-St. [CLS] [SEP] Louis International Airport, the flight crashed in a farm field near the Missouri River. [CLS] [SEP] All 6 passengers and 2 crew members were killed in the crash. [CLS] [SEP]
