The PZL M28 Skytruck is a Polish STOL light cargo and passenger plane, produced by PZL Mielec, as a development of license-built Antonov An-28s. [CLS] [SEP] Early licence-built planes were designated PZL An-28. [CLS] [SEP] The maritime patrol and reconnaissance variants are named PZL M28B Bryza ("sea breeze"). [CLS] [SEP]
