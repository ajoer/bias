[CLS] The 2012 Guatemala earthquake occurred on November 7 at 10:35:45 local time. [SEP] [CLS] The shock had a moment magnitude of 7.4 and a maximum Mercalli Intensity of VII (Very strong). [SEP] [CLS] The epicenter was located in the Pacific Ocean, 35 kilometres (22 mi) south of Champerico in the department of Retalhuleu. [SEP] [CLS] The region is one of many earthquakes, where the Cocos Plate is being subducted along the Middle America Trench beneath the North American and the Caribbean Plates, near their triple junction.The quake was reportedly felt in Guatemala and in parts of Mexico, El Salvador, Belize, Honduras, Nicaragua and Costa Rica. [SEP] [CLS] Damage to buildings was reported in several cities in Guatemala, including San Marcos, Quetzaltenango and the capital Guatemala City. [SEP] [CLS] The Pacific Tsunami Warning Center issued a warning about the possibility of a local tsunami within 100 to 200 miles (160 to 320 km) of the epicenter.With 39 dead, this is the most intense and deadliest earthquake that has hit Guatemala since the earthquake of 1976.
