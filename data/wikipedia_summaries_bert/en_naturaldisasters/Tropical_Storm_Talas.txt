The name Talas (Tagalog: [ˈtalɐs], TAH-ləs) has been used for three tropical cyclone names in the western north Pacific Ocean. [CLS] [SEP] The name was contributed by the Philippines and means "sharpness" (of an object, e.g. [CLS] [SEP] a knife) or "acuteness" (of the mind, physical sense, faculty, etc.). [CLS] [SEP] Tropical Storm Talas (2004) (T0428, 31W, Zosimo) Severe Tropical Storm Talas (2011) (T1112, 15W), a storm that caused severe damage in Japan Severe Tropical Storm Talas (2017) (T1704, 06W), affected Indochina.
