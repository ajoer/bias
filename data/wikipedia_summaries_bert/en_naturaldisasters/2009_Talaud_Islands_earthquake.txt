[CLS] The 2009 Talaud Islands earthquake occurred on February 12 at 01:34 local time (February 11, 2009 at 17:34 UTC) near Talaud Islands (Indonesian: Kepulauan Talaud), Indonesia. [SEP] [CLS] This reverse-type shock had a moment magnitude of 7.2 and a maximum Mercalli intensity of VI (Strong). [SEP] [CLS] There were at least 64 people injured and 597 buildings damaged in Talaud Islands. [SEP] [CLS] This earthquake could also be felt in the Philippines. [SEP] [CLS]
