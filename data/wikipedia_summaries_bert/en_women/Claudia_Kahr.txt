Claudia Kahr (born 30 September 1955 in Graz) has been a judge at the Austrian Constitutional Court since 1999. [CLS] [SEP] Following law studies at the University of Graz, she received the Dr.iur. [CLS] [SEP] degree in 1978, and attended the College of Europe 1978-1979 (Paul-Henri Spaak promotion). [CLS] [SEP] She heads of the board of directors of ASFINAG. [CLS] [SEP]
