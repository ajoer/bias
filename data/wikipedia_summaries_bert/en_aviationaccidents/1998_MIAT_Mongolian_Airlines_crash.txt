MIAT Mongolian Airlines (pronounced me-at, Mongolian: Монголын Иргэний Агаарын Тээвэр, Mongolyn Irgenii Agaaryn Teever (MIAT), Mongolian Civil Air Transport) is the Mongolian national airline, headquartered in the MIAT Building in Ulaanbaatar, the capital of Mongolia. [CLS] [SEP] The airline operates international scheduled services from its base at Chinggis Khaan International Airport near Ulaanbaatar. [CLS] [SEP]
