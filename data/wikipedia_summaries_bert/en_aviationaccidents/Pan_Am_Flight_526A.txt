Pan Am Flight 526A, a Douglas DC-4, took off from San Juan-Isla Grande Airport, Puerto Rico, at 12:11 PM AST on April 11, 1952 on a flight to Idlewild International Airport, New York City with 64 passengers and five crew members on board. [CLS] [SEP] Panicking passengers refused to leave the sinking wreck. [CLS] [SEP] 52 passengers were killed, and 17 passengers and crew members were rescued by the USCG.
