TAESA Flight 725 was an scheduled flight originating in Tijuana International Airport and ending at Mexico International Airport with intermediate stopovers in Uruapan and Guadalajara, that crashed shortly after departure on November 9, 1999, killing all 18 passengers and crew on board. [CLS] [SEP] The crash led TAESA to ground its fleet and suspend operations a year later in 2000.Investigators determined that the crew didn't use the appropriate checklists prior to departure. [CLS] [SEP] During the climbout, the pilots were confused about what instructed heading to take during departure. [CLS] [SEP] Spatial disorientation was believed to be one factor in the crash of Flight 725. [CLS] [SEP]
