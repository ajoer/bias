[CLS] Nalini Venkatasubramanian is a Professor of Computer Science in the Donald Bren School of Information and Computer Sciences at the University of California, Irvine. [SEP] [CLS] She is known for her work in effective management and utilization of resources in the evolving global information infrastructure. [SEP] [CLS] Her research interests are Multimedia Computing, Networked and Distributed Systems, Internet technologies and Applications, Ubiquitous Computing and Urban Crisis Responses. [SEP] [CLS] Dr. [SEP] [CLS] Venkatasubramanian's research focuses on enabling effective management and utilization of resources in the evolving global information infrastructure.
