[CLS] Austral Líneas Aéreas Flight 46 was an Argentine scheduled domestic flight from Buenos Aires to Posadas, via Resistencia, that undershot the runway at Libertador General Jose de San Martin Airport in Posadas on June 12, 1988 in conditions of poor visibility. [SEP] [CLS] All 22 passengers and crew on board were killed in the crash. [SEP] [CLS]
