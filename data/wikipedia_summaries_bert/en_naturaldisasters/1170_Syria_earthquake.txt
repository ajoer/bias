[CLS] The 1170 Syria earthquake was one of the largest earthquakes to hit Syria. [SEP] [CLS] It occurred early in the morning of 29 June 1170. [SEP] [CLS] It formed part of a sequence of large earthquakes that propagated southwards along the Dead Sea Transform, starting with the 1138 Aleppo earthquake, continuing with the 1157 Hama, 1170 and 1202 Syria events. [SEP] [CLS] The estimated magnitude is 7.7 on the moment magnitude scale, with the maximum intensity of X (extreme) on the Mercalli intensity scale. [SEP] [CLS]
