On 16 February 1986 a Boeing 737-200 operating China Airlines Flight 2265 went missing after executing a go-around after touching down at Penghu Airport, Taiwan. [CLS] [SEP] It was discovered several weeks later on the seabed 19km north of the island. [CLS] [SEP] All 6 passengers and 7 crew members were confirmed dead. [CLS] [SEP]
