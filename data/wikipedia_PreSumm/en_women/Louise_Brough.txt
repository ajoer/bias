[CLS] Althea Louise Brough Clapp (née Brough; March 11, 1923 – February 3, 2014) was an American tennis player. [SEP] [CLS] In her career between 1939 and 1959, she won six Grand Slam singles titles as well as numerous doubles and mixed-doubles titles. [SEP] [CLS] At the end of the 1955 tennis season, Lance Tingay of the London Daily Telegraph ranked her world No. [SEP] [CLS] 1 for the year. [SEP] [CLS]
