[CLS] The 2010 Nigerien floods were floods across Niger which left over 111,000 people homeless. [SEP] [CLS] Niger was already suffering acute food shortages following prolonged drought in the Sahel region. [SEP] [CLS] As of 24 August 2010, at least 6 to 8 people had died. [SEP] [CLS] The Niger river was pushed to its highest levels in 80 years. [SEP] [CLS] The floods subsequently spread along the River Niger into Nigeria, Ghana, Burkina Faso, Togo and Benin over the next few months. [SEP] [CLS] Later storms also brewed up in the CAR, Morocco and northern Algeria. [SEP] [CLS]
