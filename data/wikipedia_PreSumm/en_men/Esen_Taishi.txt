[CLS] Esen Taishi (Mongolian: Эсэн тайш; Chinese: 也先; d. [SEP] [CLS] 1455) was a powerful Oirat Taishi and de facto ruler of the Northern Yuan in 15th century Mongolia. [SEP] [CLS] He is best known for capturing Emperor Yingzong of Ming in 1450 after the Battle of Tumu Fortress and briefly reuniting the Mongols. [SEP] [CLS] The Four Oirat reached the peak of their power under his rule. [SEP] [CLS]
