Jarkko Määttä (born 28 December 1994) is a Finnish ski jumper.Määttä competed at the 2014 Winter Olympics for Finland. [CLS] [SEP] He placed 36th in the normal hill qualifying round and 32nd in the first jump of the final round, but did not advance. [CLS] [SEP] He then placed 33rd in the large hill qualifying round and 43rd on the first jump of the final, also failing to advance. [CLS] [SEP] He was also a member of the Finnish team that placed eighth in the team event.Määttä made his World Cup debut in November 2010. [CLS] [SEP] His current best World Cup finish is fourth, in a team event at Klingenthal on 22 November 2014; his best individual World Cup finish is ninth, in a large hill event at Lahti in the same season. [CLS] [SEP] His best overall World Cup finish is 32nd, in 2014–15. [CLS] [SEP]
