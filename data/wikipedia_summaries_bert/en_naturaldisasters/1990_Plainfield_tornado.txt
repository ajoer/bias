The 1990 Plainfield tornado was a devastating tornado that occurred on the afternoon of Tuesday, August 28, 1990. [CLS] [SEP] The violent tornado killed 29 people and injured 353. [CLS] [SEP] It is the only F5 tornado ever recorded in August and the only F5 tornado to strike the Chicago area. [CLS] [SEP] There are no known videos or photographs of the tornado itself; however, in 2011 a video surfaced online showing the supercell that spawned the tornado. [CLS] [SEP] The Plainfield tornado was part of a small outbreak that produced several tornadoes in the northern US and in Canada. [CLS] [SEP]
