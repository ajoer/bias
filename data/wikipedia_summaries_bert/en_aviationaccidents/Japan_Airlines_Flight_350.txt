[CLS] Japan Airlines Flight 350 (日本航空350便, Nihonkōkū 350 Bin) was a McDonnell Douglas DC-8-61, registered JA8061, on a domestic scheduled passenger flight from Fukuoka, Japan, to Tokyo. [SEP] [CLS] The airplane crashed 9 February 1982 on approach to Haneda Airport in Tokyo Bay, resulting in 24 fatalities. [SEP] [CLS] Flight 350 was the first crash for Japan Airlines in the 1980s. [SEP] [CLS] The investigation traced the cause of the crash to the deliberate actions of the captain. [SEP] [CLS]
