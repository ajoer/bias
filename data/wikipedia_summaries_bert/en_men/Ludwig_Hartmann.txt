[CLS] Ludwig Hartmann (born 20 July 1978) is a German politician (Alliance '90/The Greens) and communication designer. [SEP] [CLS] He is, along with Katharina Schulze, one of the two leading candidates of his party in the 2018 Bavarian state election. [SEP] [CLS] Schulze was born in Landsberg am Lech. [SEP] [CLS] He has been a member of the Landtag of Bavaria, the state parliament, since 2008 and one of the two faction leaders of his party in the Landtag since 2013.
