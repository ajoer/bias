[CLS] The April or Spring Nor'easter of 2007 was a nor'easter that affected mainly the eastern parts of North America during its four-day course, from April 14 to April 17, 2007. [SEP] [CLS] The combined effects of high winds, heavy rainfall, and high tides led to flooding, storm damages, power outages, and evacuations, and disrupted traffic and commerce. [SEP] [CLS] In the north, heavy wet snow caused the loss of power for several thousands of homes in Ontario and Quebec. [SEP] [CLS] The storm caused at least 18 fatalities. [SEP] [CLS]
