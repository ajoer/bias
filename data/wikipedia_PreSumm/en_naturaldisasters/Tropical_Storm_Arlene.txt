[CLS] Tropical Storm Arlene was an unusually large and early-forming tropical storm, being the first of twenty-eight different storms during the 2005 Atlantic hurricane season, which would become the most active season on record. [SEP] [CLS] Tropical Storm Arlene formed near Honduras on June 8 and moved northwards. [SEP] [CLS] It crossed western Cuba on June 10 and strengthened to just under hurricane strength before making its final landfall on the Florida Panhandle the next day. [SEP] [CLS] The storm weakened as it continued to move north over the United States, becoming extratropical on June 13. [SEP] [CLS] Arlene was responsible for only one death and minor damages.
