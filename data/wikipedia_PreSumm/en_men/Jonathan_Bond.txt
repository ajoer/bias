[CLS] Jonathan Henry Bond (born 19 May 1993) is an English footballer who plays for West Bromwich Albion as a goalkeeper. [SEP] [CLS] Bond has played internationally for Wales at under-17 and under-19 levels; he has also been called up to the under-21 and senior teams and the England under-21 squad having chosen to change his international allegiance in 2013.
