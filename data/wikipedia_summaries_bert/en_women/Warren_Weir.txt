Warren Weir (born October 1989) is a Jamaican sprinter, who specialized in the 200 metres. [CLS] [SEP] He was the bronze medallist in the event at the 2012 London Olympics, helping Jamaica sweep the medals. [CLS] [SEP] In 2013 at the Moscow World Championships, Warren Weir won the silver medal equalling his personal best. [CLS] [SEP] He finished behind Usain Bolt who set a World Leading time. [CLS] [SEP] His personal best is 19.79 seconds set at the National Stadium in his home country Kingston, Jamaica. [CLS] [SEP] He has since equalled his personal best in Moscow, in the World Championship final. [CLS] [SEP] He trained with the Glen Mills-coached Racers Track Club, alongside Usain Bolt and Yohan Blake. [CLS] [SEP]
