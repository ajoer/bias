[CLS] On 4 November 2008 an official Mexican Secretariat of the Interior aircraft crashed in central Mexico City at around 18:45 local time. [SEP] [CLS] There were sixteen fatalities—all nine people on board and seven people on the ground died. [SEP] [CLS] The plane was carrying Mexican Secretary of the Interior Juan Camilo Mouriño.The plane crashed in rush-hour traffic close to the intersection of Paseo de la Reforma and the Anillo Periférico, in the Las Lomas residential and business district. [SEP] [CLS] During its approach to Mexico City International Airport, the plane followed a Boeing 767 too closely and encountered wake turbulence which caused it to invert into a nose-down position. [SEP] [CLS] The pilots were able to reduce the angle of descent but due to excessive speed and insufficient altitude were unable to regain control of the aircraft. [SEP] [CLS] The plane crashed into a building, exploding on impact. [SEP] [CLS]
