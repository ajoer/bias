[CLS] Nadia Hordijenko Andrianova (Надія Миколаївна Андріанова-Гордієнко in Ukrainian; 1921–1998) was a Ukrainian writer and translator of the language Esperanto. [SEP] [CLS] She studied literature and journalism in Kiev and published articles and translations in Paco and Hungara Vivo. [SEP] [CLS] In 1987, the Hungarian Esperanto Association published her autobiography Vagante tra la mondo maltrankvila. [SEP] [CLS] She also wrote about Vasili Eroshenko. [SEP] [CLS]
