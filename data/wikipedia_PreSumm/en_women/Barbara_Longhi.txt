[CLS] Barbara Longhi (English: , Italian: [ˈloŋɡi]; 21 September 1552 – 23 December 1638) was an Italian painter. [SEP] [CLS] She was much admired in her lifetime as a portraitist, although most of her portraits are now lost or unattributed. [SEP] [CLS] Her work, such as her many Madonna and Child paintings, earned her a fine reputation as an artist. [SEP] [CLS]
