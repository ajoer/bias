The name Kirogi (Korean: 기러기 [kiɾʌ̹ɡi], NKR: kirŏgi) has been used for four tropical cyclones in the western Pacific Ocean. [CLS] [SEP] The name, contributed by North Korea, refers to non-domesticated geese. [CLS] [SEP] Typhoon Kirogi (2000) (T0003, 05W, Ditang) – passed close to Japan while weakening. [CLS] [SEP] Typhoon Kirogi (2005) (T0520, 21W, Nando) – not a threat to land. [CLS] [SEP] Severe Tropical Storm Kirogi (2012) (T1212, 13W) Tropical Storm Kirogi (2017) (T1725, 31W, Tino)
