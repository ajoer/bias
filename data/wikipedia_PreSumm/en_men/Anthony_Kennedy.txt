[CLS] Anthony McLeod Kennedy (born July 23, 1936) is an American lawyer and jurist who served as an Associate Justice of the Supreme Court of the United States from 1988 until his retirement in 2018. [SEP] [CLS] He was nominated to the court in 1987 by President Ronald Reagan, and sworn in on February 18, 1988.
