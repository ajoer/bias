On 27 April 1974, an Aeroflot Il-18 airliner crashed while operating a charter flight from Leningrad (now St. [CLS] [SEP] Petersburg) to Zaporizhia, continuing to Krasnodar, Russia. [CLS] [SEP] The plane crashed shortly after takeoff from Pulkovo Airport in Leningrad. [CLS] [SEP] None of the 109 people on board survived. [CLS] [SEP]
