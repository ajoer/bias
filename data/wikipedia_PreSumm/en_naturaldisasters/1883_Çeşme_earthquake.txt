[CLS] The 1883 Çeşme earthquake occurred at 13:30 UTC on October 15. [SEP] [CLS] It had a magnitude of 6.8 on the moment magnitude scale and a maximum felt intensity of IX (Violent) on the Mercalli intensity scale. [SEP] [CLS] There was widespread damage in the Urla–Çeşme area with 3,600 homes destroyed and at least 120 people killed. [SEP] [CLS] There were damaging aftershocks on October 22 and November 1.
