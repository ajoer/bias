[CLS] Kati Wilhelm (born 2 August 1976 in Schmalkalden) is a German former professional biathlete. [SEP] [CLS] Like most German biathletes she is also a member of the German Armed Forces (Bundeswehr) with the rank of master sergeant (Hauptfeldwebel). [SEP] [CLS] She currently resides in Steinbach-Hallenberg, also in the Federal State of Thuringia. [SEP] [CLS]
