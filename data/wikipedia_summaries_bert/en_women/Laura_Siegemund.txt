Laura Natalie Siegemund (born 4 March 1988 in Filderstadt) is a German professional tennis player. [CLS] [SEP] She has won two singles and five doubles titles on the WTA Tour, as well as 12 singles and 20 doubles titles on the ITF Circuit.
