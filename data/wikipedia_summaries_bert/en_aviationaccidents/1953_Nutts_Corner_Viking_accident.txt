On 5 January 1953, a Vickers Viking airliner operated by British European Airways crashed on approach to Belfast Nutts Corner Airport, Northern Ireland. [CLS] [SEP] The aircraft was on a domestic flight from London Northolt Airport with 31 passengers and 4 crew on board. [CLS] [SEP] Twenty-four of the passengers and three crew members died in the accident. [CLS] [SEP]
