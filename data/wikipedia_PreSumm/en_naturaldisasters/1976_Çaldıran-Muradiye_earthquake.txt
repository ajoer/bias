[CLS] The 1976 Çaldıran–Muradiye earthquake occurred at 14:22 local time (12:22 UTC) on 24 November. [SEP] [CLS] The epicenter was located near Çaldıran, 20 km northeast of Muradiye, in the Van Province of eastern Turkey. [SEP] [CLS] The earthquake had a magnitude of 7.3 with a maximum intensity of X on the Mercalli intensity scale. [SEP] [CLS] The area of severe damage, where over 80% of the buildings were destroyed, covered an area of 2,000 square kilometres. [SEP] [CLS] There were between 4,000 and 5,000 casualties.
