[CLS] Rosemarie Trockel (born 13 November 1952) is a German conceptual artist. [SEP] [CLS] She has made drawings, paintings, sculptures, videos and installations, and has worked in mixed media. [SEP] [CLS] From 1985 she made pictures using knitting-machines. [SEP] [CLS] She is a professor at the Kunstakademie Düsseldorf, in Düsseldorf in Nordrhein-Westfalen. [SEP] [CLS]
