[CLS] El Al Flight 402 was an international passenger flight from London to Tel Aviv via Vienna and Istanbul. [SEP] [CLS] On 27 July 1955, the flight, operated by a Lockheed Constellation registered as 4X-AKC, strayed into then-Communist Bulgarian airspace and was attacked by two Bulgarian MiG-15 jet fighters, crashing near Petrich. [SEP] [CLS] All 7 crew and 51 passengers on board the airliner were killed. [SEP] [CLS] The crash took place amid highly strained relations between the Eastern Bloc and the Western Bloc and was the deadliest involving the Constellation up to that time. [SEP] [CLS]
