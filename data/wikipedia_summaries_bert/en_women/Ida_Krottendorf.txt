Ida Krottendorf (5 April 1927, in Ebreichsdorf – 23 June 1998, in Vienna) was an Austrian actress. [CLS] [SEP] She was married from 1955 to Austrian actor Ernst Stankovski and in the second marriage from 1960 until 1991 to Klausjürgen Wussow. [CLS] [SEP] Together they had two children, Barbara and Alexander Wussow. [CLS] [SEP]
