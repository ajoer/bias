Hurricane Maria was an Atlantic hurricane which formed in September 2005 during the annual hurricane season. [CLS] [SEP] Maria was the thirteenth named storm, sixth hurricane, and fourth major hurricane of the record-breaking season. [CLS] [SEP] Maria formed in the central Atlantic on September 1 and tracked to the northwest, strengthening as it moved over warm waters. [CLS] [SEP] The storm reached its peak strength on September 5 east of Bermuda and gradually weakened before becoming extratropical on September 10. [CLS] [SEP] Maria did not affect any land as a tropical system, but it brought tropical storm-force winds to Iceland as an extratropical cyclone and produced heavy rain and three fatalities in Norway.
