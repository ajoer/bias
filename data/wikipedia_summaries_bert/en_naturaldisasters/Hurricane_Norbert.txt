The name Norbert has been used for four tropical cyclones in the Eastern Pacific Ocean: Hurricane Norbert (1984) – took an erratic track several hundred miles south of Baja California, making landfall there Hurricane Norbert (1990) – stayed at sea Hurricane Norbert (2008) – struck Baja California Hurricane Norbert (2014) – a Category 3 that affected Western Mexico and the Baja California Peninsula.
