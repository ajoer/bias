Northwest Orient Airlines Flight 293 was an American military charter operated on 3 June 1963 by a Northwest Orient Airlines Douglas DC-7C registered N290 which crashed into the sea off the coast of Alaska. [CLS] [SEP] All 101 crew and passengers on board were killed. [CLS] [SEP] It was the airline's deadliest disaster until the crash of Northwest Airlines Flight 255 24 years later. [CLS] [SEP]
