[CLS] Jana Šenková Napolitano (born (1982-11-07)7 November 1982) is a retired Czech female volleyball player. [SEP] [CLS] She was part of the Czech Republic women's national volleyball team. [SEP] [CLS] She participated at the 2002 FIVB Volleyball Women's World Championship in Germany. [SEP] [CLS] On club level she played with SK UP Olomouc. [SEP] [CLS]
