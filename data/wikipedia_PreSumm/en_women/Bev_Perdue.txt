[CLS] Beverly Eaves Perdue (born Beverly Marlene Moore; January 14, 1947) is an American businesswoman, politician and member of the Democratic Party who served as the 73rd Governor of North Carolina from 2009 to 2013. [SEP] [CLS] She was the first female governor of North Carolina. [SEP] [CLS] Perdue started her political career in the 1980s, serving in the North Carolina House of Representatives. [SEP] [CLS] She then served five terms in the North Carolina Senate before she was elected as the 32nd Lieutenant Governor of North Carolina.
