On 25 September 2011, Buddha Air Flight 103, a Beechcraft 1900D commuter aircraft, crashed near Lalitpur, Nepal, while attempting to land in poor weather at nearby Kathmandu Airport. [CLS] [SEP] All 19 passengers and crew on board were killed. [CLS] [SEP] The aircraft, operated by Buddha Air, was on a sightseeing flight to Mount Everest. [CLS] [SEP]
