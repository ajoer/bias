Helene Hegemann (born 19 February 1992 in Freiburg im Breisgau) is a German writer, director, and actress. [CLS] [SEP] As a young writer her work was highly praised, but her first novel, Axolotl Roadkill, sparked a plagiarism controversy. [CLS] [SEP] The book has since been translated in various languages. [CLS] [SEP]
