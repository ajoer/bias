[CLS] Anca Mosoiu (born 1973) is a technical consultant and web developer who established the Oakland, California co-working space Tech Liminal in 2009. [SEP] [CLS] She is credited with helping to build the tech industry in Oakland. [SEP] [CLS] She has been described as a "tech guru". [SEP] [CLS] She has worked in the business applications industry for more than 15 years at companies such as Razorfish, Sony, Juniper Networks, and Cisco systems. [SEP] [CLS]
