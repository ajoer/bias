[SEP] [CLS] She discovered the first of a family of synthetic fibers of exceptional strength and stiffness: poly-paraphenylene terephthalamide.For her discovery, Kwolek was awarded the DuPont company's Lavoisier Medal for outstanding technical achievement. [SEP] [CLS] As of August 2019, she was the only female employee to have received that honor. [SEP] [CLS] In 1995 she became the fourth woman to be added to the National Inventors Hall of Fame. [SEP] [CLS] Kwolek won numerous awards for her work in polymer chemistry, including the National Medal of Technology, the IRI Achievement Award and the Perkin Medal. [SEP] [CLS]
