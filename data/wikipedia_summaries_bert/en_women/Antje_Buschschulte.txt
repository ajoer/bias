Antje Buschschulte (born 27 December 1978 in Berlin) is a former German swimmer. [CLS] [SEP] Her best disciplines were the short distance backstroke and freestyle races. [CLS] [SEP] Buschschulte swam for the sporting club SC Magdeburg. [CLS] [SEP] She participated in Olympic Games 1996, 2000, 2004, 2008 and won 5 olympic Bronze Medals.
