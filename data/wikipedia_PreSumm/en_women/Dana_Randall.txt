[CLS] Dana Randall is a professor of computer science, the ADVANCE Professor of Computing, and adjunct professor of mathematics at Georgia Tech. [SEP] [CLS] She is also an External Professor of the Santa Fe Institute. [SEP] [CLS] Previously she was executive director of the Georgia Tech Institute of Data Engineering and Science (IDEaS) that she co-founded and director of the Algorithms and Randomness Center.. [SEP] [CLS] Her main area of research is theoretical computer science, particularly randomized algorithms. [SEP] [CLS]
