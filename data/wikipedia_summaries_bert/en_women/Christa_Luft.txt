Christa Luft (born Christa Hecht: 22 February 1938) is a German economist and politician of the SED/PDS. [CLS] [SEP] Luft joined the SED in 1958. [CLS] [SEP] From 18 November 1989 to 18 March 1990 she was economy minister in the Modrow government. [CLS] [SEP] From 1994 to 2002 she was member of the Bundestag for the PDS. [CLS] [SEP] From 1963 to 1971 Luft was registered as a Stasi informant under the code name IM Gisela. [CLS] [SEP]
