[CLS] The name Aere has been used to name three tropical cyclones in the northwestern Pacific Ocean. [SEP] [CLS] The name was contributed by the United States of America, and is the Marshallese word for 'storm'. [SEP] [CLS] Typhoon Aere (2004) (T0417, 20W, Marce), category two typhoon that brought severe damage to Taiwan and the People's Republic of China. [SEP] [CLS] Tropical Storm Aere (2011) (T1101, 03W, Bebeng), struck the Philippines Severe Tropical Storm Aere (2016) (T1619, 22W, Julian), struck Indochina as a tropical depression and bought damage and fatalities mostly in Vietnam.
