[CLS] On 7 September 2012, a series of earthquakes occurred in Yiliang County, Zhaotong, Yunnan. [SEP] [CLS] The two main shocks occurred at 11:19 and 12:16 China Standard Time (03:19 and 04:16 UTC). [SEP] [CLS] The earthquakes left 81 people dead and 821 injured. [SEP] [CLS] According to the officials, at least 100,000 people were evacuated and more than 20,000 houses were damaged. [SEP] [CLS]
