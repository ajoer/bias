Maria Walliser (born 27 May 1963) is a Swiss former alpine skier. [CLS] [SEP] Walliser grew up in Mosnang, the daughter of a wealthy cattle breeder. [CLS] [SEP] She made her World Cup debut in 1980. [CLS] [SEP] Together with her fellow Swiss Erika Hess, Michela Figini and Vreni Schneider she dominated female alpine skiing during the 1980s. [CLS] [SEP] Among her many successes, she won two overall World Cups (1986 and 1987). [CLS] [SEP] Walliser also won three world titles in 1987 and 1989, as well as three Olympic medals at 1988 Calgary and 1984 Sarajevo.Walliser retired in 1990 with a World Cup tally of 72 podium finishes, including 25 victories.
