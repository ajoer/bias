Pan Am Flight 1-10 was a passenger flight from London to Shannon Airport, during a flight round the world from San Francisco, California to New York City. [CLS] [SEP] On 15 April 1948 it crashed 725 meters (2,379 ft) short of runway 23. [CLS] [SEP] 10 flight crew and 20 passengers died in the crash; 1 passenger survived with minor injuries.
