Asiana Airlines Flight 162 was a regular short-haul international passenger flight from Incheon International Airport near Seoul, South Korea, to Hiroshima Airport in Hiroshima, Japan. [CLS] [SEP] On 14 April 2015, the Airbus A320-232 aircraft touched down short of the runway, struck the localizer array, skidded onto the runway on its tail, and spun 120 degrees before finally coming to a rest on the grass, opposite the terminal building. [CLS] [SEP] The aircraft suffered substantial damage to the left wing and engine. [CLS] [SEP] Of the 82 people aboard, 27 (25 passengers and two crew) were injured, one seriously. [CLS] [SEP]
