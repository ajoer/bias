[CLS] Avianca Flight 671, registration HK-177, was a Lockheed Constellation that crashed and burned on landing at Montego Bay, Jamaica, on 21 January 1960. [SEP] [CLS] It was and remains the worst accident in Jamaican aviation history.The flight had originated at Miami International Airport, Florida. [SEP] [CLS] The aircraft operating the flight was a Lockheed L-1049E Super Constellation used by Avianca for its Bogota-Montego Bay routes. [SEP] [CLS] Thirty-seven of the 46 passengers and crew aboard were killed. [SEP] [CLS]
