[CLS] Regina Burchardt (born (1983-07-01)1 July 1983) is a German female former volleyball player, playing as a right side hitter. [SEP] [CLS] She was part of the Germany women's national volleyball team. [SEP] [CLS] She competed at the 2011 Women's European Volleyball Championship. [SEP] [CLS] On club level she played for VC Wiesbaden. [SEP] [CLS]
