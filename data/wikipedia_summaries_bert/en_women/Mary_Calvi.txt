Mary Calvi (born June 11, 1969) is an American television journalist and author of Dear George, Dear Mary: A Novel of George Washington's First Love. [CLS] [SEP] She is the co-anchor of CBS2 This Morning and CBS2 At Noon at WCBS-TV in New York City, and the recipient of ten Emmy Awards. [CLS] [SEP] From July 4, 2020, she has joined Inside Edition as the weekend anchor. [CLS] [SEP]
