Khabarovsk Airlines Flight 463 was a scheduled domestic passenger flight from Nikolayevsk-on-Amur Airport to Nelkan Airport in Russia. [CLS] [SEP] On 15 November 2017 the Let L-410 Turbolet operating the flight crashed short of the runway at Nelkan airport, killing all but one of the seven people on board. [CLS] [SEP] The sole survivor was a three-year-old girl who sustained serious injuries. [CLS] [SEP] The crash was caused by a malfunction of the right engine's propeller. [CLS] [SEP]
