Sitara Brooj Akbar is an academic prodigy, multiple world record holder and gold medalist. [CLS] [SEP] She is a child prodigy who first came to the limelight for her eloquent speech and skilled oration to large crowds and on national television at the age of five. [CLS] [SEP] She went on to make several Pakistani and World Records based on her passing of O Level in 5 Subjects by the age of 11. [CLS] [SEP] She completed her A Levels at age 13. [CLS] [SEP] Akbar was also permitted by the British Council to undertake the International English Language Testing System (IELTS) test in 2011, and achieved a score of 7 bands at age 11. [CLS] [SEP] She went on the become the Youngest Non-Native Speaker to score 9 out of 9 bands in IELTS at age 15. [CLS] [SEP] She is currently a resident of Dubai, United Arab Emirates where she cleared her Certified Anti Money Laundering Specialist (CAMS) Exam in July 2017 at age 17. [CLS] [SEP] Sitara Akbar hails from Rabwah, a small town in the Punjab province of Pakistan. [CLS] [SEP]
