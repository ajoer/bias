The 1948 Sabena DC-4 Crash was the crash of a Douglas DC-4-1009 of the Belgian airline Sabena, 27 km south of Libenge, Belgian Congo, on 12 May 1948. [CLS] [SEP] It was the deadliest accident for Sabena at the time and the second of three deadly Sabena crashes in 1948. [CLS] [SEP] It was also the deadliest in Belgian Congo before the country's independence as the Democratic Republic of the Congo in 1960. [CLS] [SEP] Of the 32 people on board 31 were killed, leaving only one survivor. [CLS] [SEP]
