David Goffin (French pronunciation: ​[david ɡɔfɛ̃]; born 7 December 1990) is a Belgian professional tennis player whose career high ranking is world No. [CLS] [SEP] 7 by the Association of Tennis Professionals (ATP). [CLS] [SEP] He is currently the highest ranked Belgian male and the first to reach the ATP top 10. [CLS] [SEP] Goffin has won four ATP titles, and has reached nine other ATP finals, most notably at the 2017 ATP World Tour Finals. [CLS] [SEP] Goffin's breakthrough occurred during his first appearance in the main draw of a grand slam, at the 2012 French Open as a lucky loser. [CLS] [SEP] He reached the fourth round where he lost to Roger Federer in four sets. [CLS] [SEP] Goffin has reached the quarterfinals of the 2016 French Open and the 2017 Australian Open, losing to rivals Dominic Thiem and Grigor Dimitrov respectively.
