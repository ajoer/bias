The 1892 Recreo earthquake took place in the Catamarca Province of Argentina on 21 March at about 01:45 AM, with magnitude of 6.0 on the Richter magnitude scale. [CLS] [SEP] Its epicenter was located approximately at 29°30′S 65°0′W, and at a depth of about 30 kilometres (19 mi). [CLS] [SEP] The earthquake had a maximum felt intensity of VII on the Mercalli intensity scale. [CLS] [SEP] It caused some fatalities and serious structural damage in the town of Recreo.
