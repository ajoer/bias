The 2017 Ischia earthquake occurred in the island of Ischia, Campania, in southern Italy. [CLS] [SEP] The main shock occurred at 20:57 CEST (18:57 UTC) on 21 August 2017, and was rated 3.9 on the Moment magnitude scale.Despite of the moderate magnitude, several buildings and a church collapsed. [CLS] [SEP] One woman died in Casamicciola Terme, after being hit by rubble that fell from a church. [CLS] [SEP] Another woman died when her house collapsed. [CLS] [SEP]
