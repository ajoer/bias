[CLS] In the early morning hours of April 4, 1981,an unusual and fatal anticyclonic F4 tornado struck West Bend, Wisconsin. [SEP] [CLS] It killed three people, and injured another 53. [SEP] [CLS] The tornado was largely unexpected and the storm that produced it did not display features of a typical severe storm. [SEP] [CLS] Because of this, the National Weather Service issued only a Severe Thunderstorm Warning prior to the tornado, causing local law enforcement to not sound the tornado sirens in time for residents to take shelter.
