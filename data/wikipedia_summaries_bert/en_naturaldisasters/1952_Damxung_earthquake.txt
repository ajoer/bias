[CLS] The 1952 Damxung earthquake struck Tibet with moment magnitude of 7.5 in the early morning hours of August 18. [SEP] [CLS] The epicenter was located in the Nyenchen Tanglha Mountains in Damxung County, Lhasa Prefecture, Tibet Autonomous Region, People's Republic of China. [SEP] [CLS] There was significant damage in Damxung (Dangquka) and nearby Nagqu County. [SEP] [CLS] It was felt in Lhasa, over 100 km (62 mi) to the south.The earthquake damaged Reting Monastery and 54 people died at Reting (Razheng) and Tangmu. [SEP] [CLS] The total number of fatalities is unknown. [SEP] [CLS] Damxung County suffered another significant earthquake in 2008, further southwest parallel to the Nyenchen Tanglha Mountains.
