[CLS] Leopoldstadt (German pronunciation: [ˈleːopɔltˌʃtat] (listen); Bavarian: Leopoidstod, "Leopold-Town") is the 2nd municipal District of Vienna (German: 2. [SEP] [CLS] Bezirk). [SEP] [CLS] There are 103,233 inhabitants (as of 2016-01-01) over 19.27 km2 (7 sq mi). [SEP] [CLS] It is situated in the heart of the city and, together with Brigittenau (20th district), forms a large island surrounded by the Danube Canal and, to the north, the Danube. [SEP] [CLS] It is named after Leopold I, Holy Roman Emperor. [SEP] [CLS] Due to its relatively high percentage of Jewish inhabitants (38.5 per cent in 1923, i. [SEP] [CLS] e., before the Holocaust), Leopoldstadt gained the nickname Mazzesinsel ("Matzo Island"). [SEP] [CLS] This context was a significant aspect for the district twinning with the New York City borough Brooklyn in 2007. [SEP] [CLS]
