[CLS] The Kalamazoo Tornado of 1980 struck downtown Kalamazoo, Michigan, on Tuesday, May 13, 1980. [SEP] [CLS] The tornado, which touched down at 4:09 pm, was rated F3 on the Fujita scale. [SEP] [CLS] The tornado killed 5 people and injured 79. [SEP] [CLS] Damage was estimated at $50,000,000.The tornado left a path of destruction 11 miles (18 km) long during its approximately 20-minute duration. [SEP] [CLS] It was notable for having struck the heart of downtown, damaging or destroying many notable buildings, parks, and landmarks. [SEP] [CLS] The massive F3 caused a power outage so extensive, phone companies pleaded for people to only use phones for emergencies. [SEP] [CLS] In total, the storm caused 5 deaths, 79 injuries, and about 1,200 people were left homeless. [SEP] [CLS]
