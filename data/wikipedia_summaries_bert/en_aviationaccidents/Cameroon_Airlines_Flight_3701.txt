Cameroon Airlines Flight 3701 was an air accident that occurred on 3 December 1995. [CLS] [SEP] The Boeing 737-200, registration TJ-CBE, crashed after it lost control near Douala, Cameroon. [CLS] [SEP] On its second approach to Douala International Airport power was lost to one engine. [CLS] [SEP] The accident killed 71 passengers and crew and five people were injured but survived.
