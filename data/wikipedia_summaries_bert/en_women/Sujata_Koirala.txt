Sujata Koirala (Nepali: सुजाता कोइराला) born 9 February 1954 in Biratnagar) is a Nepalese politician and the only daughter of former Prime Minister Girija Prasad Koirala. [CLS] [SEP] She was made Foreign Minister under Prime Minister Madhav Kumar Nepal. [CLS] [SEP] Sujata was promoted to Deputy Prime Minister on October 12, 2009. [CLS] [SEP] She has a daughter named Melanie Koirala Jost. [CLS] [SEP]
