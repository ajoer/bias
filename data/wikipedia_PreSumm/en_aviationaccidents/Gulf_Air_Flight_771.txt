[CLS] Gulf Air Flight 771 was a flight from Karachi, Pakistan, to Abu Dhabi, United Arab Emirates. [SEP] [CLS] On 23 September 1983, while the Boeing 737-2P6 was on approach to Abu Dhabi International Airport, a bomb exploded in the baggage compartment. [SEP] [CLS] The plane crashed in the desert near Jebel Ali between Abu Dhabi and Dubai in the UAE. [SEP] [CLS] All five crew members and 107 passengers died. [SEP] [CLS]
