[CLS] Mohawk Airlines Flight 40 was a scheduled passenger flight between Syracuse, New York, continued Elmira, New York, and Washington, DC. [SEP] [CLS] On June 23, 1967 it suffered a loss of control and crashed, killing all 30 passengers and four crew on board. [SEP] [CLS] It was the deadliest disaster in the airline's history. [SEP] [CLS] A valve in the auxiliary power unit had suffered a complete failure, spreading fire to the tailplane, causing a loss of pitch control. [SEP] [CLS]
