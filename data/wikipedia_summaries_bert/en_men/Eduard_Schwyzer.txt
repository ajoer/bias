Eduard Schwyzer (15 February 1874, Zürich – 3 May 1943, Berlin) was a Swiss Classical philologist and Indo-European linguist, specializing in Ancient Greek and Greek dialects.He was a professor in Zürich 1912–1926, in Bonn from 1927 and in Berlin from 1932. [CLS] [SEP] 1898–1927 and 1934–1943 he also worked for the Schweizerisches Idiotikon, first as an editor, later reading all correction sheets. [CLS] [SEP]
