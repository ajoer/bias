[CLS] José Gabriel Funes, S.J. [SEP] [CLS] (born January 31, 1963 in Córdoba) is an Argentine Jesuit priest and astronomer. [SEP] [CLS] He was the Director of the Vatican Observatory from August 19, 2006 until September 18, 2015, when he was succeeded by Pope Francis's appointment of the Reverend Brother Guy Consolmagno, S.J. [SEP] [CLS] Funes serves on the Advisory Council of METI (Messaging Extraterrestrial Intelligence). [SEP] [CLS]
