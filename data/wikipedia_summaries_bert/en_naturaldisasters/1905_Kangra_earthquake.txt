The 1905 Kangra earthquake occurred in the Kangra Valley and the Kangra region of the Punjab Province (modern day Himachal Pradesh) in India on 4 April 1905. [CLS] [SEP] The earthquake measured 7.8 on the surface wave magnitude scale and killed more than 20,000 people. [CLS] [SEP] Apart from this, most buildings in the towns of Kangra, Mcleodganj and Dharamshala were destroyed. [CLS] [SEP]
