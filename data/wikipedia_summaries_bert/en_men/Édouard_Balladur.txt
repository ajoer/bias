Édouard Balladur (French: [edwaʁ baladyʁ]; born 2 May 1929) is a French politician who served as Prime Minister of France under François Mitterrand from 29 March 1993 to 10 May 1995. [CLS] [SEP] He unsuccessfully ran for president in the 1995 French presidential election, coming in third place. [CLS] [SEP] At age 91, Balladur is currently the oldest living former French Prime Minister. [CLS] [SEP]
