Typhoon Ora may refer to seven tropical cyclones in the northwest Pacific Ocean: Typhoon Ora (1951) (T5113) – approached the Philippines Typhoon Ora (1963) (T6321, 39W) Typhoon Ora (1966) (T6608, 08W) – struck China Typhoon Ora (1968) (T6827, 32W, Toyang) – struck the Philippines Typhoon Ora (1972) (T7205, 06W, Konsing)- struck the Philippines and China Typhoon Ora (1975) (T7504, 06W) – struck China Typhoon Ora (1978) (T7824, 25W) – approached Taiwan
