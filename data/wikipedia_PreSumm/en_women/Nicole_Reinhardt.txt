[CLS] Nicole Reinhardt (born 2 January 1986 in Lampertheim, Hesse) is a German sprint canoer who competed since 2003. [SEP] [CLS] She won a gold medal in the K-4 500 m event at the 2008 Summer Olympics in Beijing.
