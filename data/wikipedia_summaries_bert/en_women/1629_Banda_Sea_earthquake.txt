The 1629 Banda Sea earthquake struck the Banda Sea, Indonesia on August 1. [CLS] [SEP] Its epicentre is believed to have been in the Seram Trough. [CLS] [SEP] A megathrust earthquake caused a 15 m (49 ft) tsunami, which was recorded to have affected the Banda Islands about 30 minutes after the quake. [CLS] [SEP] The effects of the tsunami were reported as far as 230 kilometres (140 mi) away in Ambon. [CLS] [SEP] Many trees in the Banda Islands were reported to have been uprooted. [CLS] [SEP]
