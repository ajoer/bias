[CLS] Robert Harting (born 18 October 1984) is a retired German discus thrower. [SEP] [CLS] He represents the sports club SCC Berlin, his coach is Torsten Schmidt. [SEP] [CLS] He is a former Olympic, World, and European champion in the men's discus throw. [SEP] [CLS] His younger brother Christoph is the event's current Olympic champion. [SEP] [CLS]
