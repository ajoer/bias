[CLS] The 1967 Neiva earthquake occurred at 10:24 local time (UTC-05) on February 9 in Colombia. [SEP] [CLS] The epicentre of the earthquake was located in San Vicente del Caguán in the department of Caquetá. [SEP] [CLS] The earthquake, associated with the Algeciras Fault, part of the megaregional Eastern Frontal Fault System, had a moment magnitude of 7.0 and an intensity of VIII and was felt in northwestern South America from Caracas to Iquitos and Buenaventura to Mitú. [SEP] [CLS] 350 aftershocks were registered in the following month. [SEP] [CLS] The earthquake produced 98 fatalities and approximately 600,000 USD in damage. [SEP] [CLS]
