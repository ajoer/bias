Emma Donoghue (born 24 October 1969) is an Irish-Canadian playwright, literary historian, novelist, and screenwriter. [CLS] [SEP] Her 2010 novel Room was a finalist for the Man Booker Prize and an international best-seller. [CLS] [SEP] Donoghue's 1995 novel Hood won the Stonewall Book Award. [CLS] [SEP] and Slammerkin (2000) won the Ferro-Grumley Award for Lesbian Fiction. [CLS] [SEP] She is a 2011 recipient of the Alex Awards. [CLS] [SEP] Room was adapted by Donoghue into a film of the same name. [CLS] [SEP] For this, she was nominated for the Academy Award for Best Adapted Screenplay. [CLS] [SEP]
