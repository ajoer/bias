[CLS] Juhani Tapio Antero Kärkinen (28 October 1935 – 29 August 2019) was a Finnish ski jumper who won a gold medal in the large hill at the 1958 World Ski Championships. [SEP] [CLS] He placed eighth in the normal hill at the 1960 Winter Olympics. [SEP] [CLS] His elder brother Kalevi was also an international ski jumper. [SEP] [CLS]
