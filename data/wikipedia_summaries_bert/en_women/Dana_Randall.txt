Dana Randall is a professor of computer science, the ADVANCE Professor of Computing, and adjunct professor of mathematics at Georgia Tech. [CLS] [SEP] She is also an External Professor of the Santa Fe Institute. [CLS] [SEP] Previously she was executive director of the Georgia Tech Institute of Data Engineering and Science (IDEaS) that she co-founded and director of the Algorithms and Randomness Center.. [CLS] [SEP] Her main area of research is theoretical computer science, particularly randomized algorithms. [CLS] [SEP]
