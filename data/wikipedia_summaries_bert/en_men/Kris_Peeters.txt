[CLS] Kris Peeters (Dutch: [ˈkrɪs ˈpeːtərs]; born 18 May 1962) is a Belgian politician and member of the Christian Democratic and Flemish party. [SEP] [CLS] Peeters was Minister-President of Flanders from 2007 until 2014 and was Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Economy and Employment in the Michel Government. [SEP] [CLS] In 2019 he was elected as a member of the European Parliament. [SEP] [CLS]
