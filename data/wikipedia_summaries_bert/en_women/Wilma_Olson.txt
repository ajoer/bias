Wilma K. [CLS] [SEP] Olson (born c. [CLS] [SEP] 1945) is the Mary I. [CLS] [SEP] Bunting professor at the Rutgers Center for Quantitative Biology (CQB) (formerly known as BioMaPS institute for Quantitative Biology) at Rutgers University. [CLS] [SEP] Olson has her own research group on the New Brunswick campus. [CLS] [SEP] Although she is a polymer chemist by training, her research aims to understand the influence of chemical architecture on the conformation, properties, and interactions of nucleic acids.
