TAME Flight 120 was a Boeing 727-134 airliner, registration HC-BLF, named El Oro, operating as a scheduled international passenger flight between Quito, Ecuador and Cali, Colombia, with a scheduled stopover at the Ecuadorian border town of Tulcán. [CLS] [SEP] The aircraft crashed while on approach to Tulcán's Teniente Coronel Luis A. [CLS] [SEP] Mantilla International Airport on January 28, 2002. [CLS] [SEP] The pilot flew the approach incorrectly in reportedly foggy conditions, and the aircraft flew into the side of the Cumbal Volcano, located near Ipiales, Colombia, at 10:23 in the morning. [CLS] [SEP] All passengers and crew were killed in the crash. [CLS] [SEP]
