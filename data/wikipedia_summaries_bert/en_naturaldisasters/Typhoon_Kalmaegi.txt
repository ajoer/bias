The name Kalmaegi has been used to name four tropical cyclones in the northwestern Pacific Ocean. [CLS] [SEP] It was submitted by North Korea and is a type of seagull. [CLS] [SEP] Tropical Storm Kalmaegi (2002) (T0210, 14W) Typhoon Kalmaegi (2008) (T0807, 08W, Helen) – struck Taiwan and China. [CLS] [SEP] Typhoon Kalmaegi (2014) (T1415, 15W, Luis) – a storm which brought flooding in southeast Asia during mid-September. [CLS] [SEP] Typhoon Kalmaegi (2019) (T1926, 27W, Ramon) – impacted northern Philippines during mid-November. [CLS] [SEP]
