Petra Maria Thorén (born 8 August 1969) is a Finnish former tennis player. [CLS] [SEP] Thorén has won 5 singles and 1 doubles titles on the ITF Women's Circuit. [CLS] [SEP] On 23 March 1992, she reached her best singles ranking of world No. [CLS] [SEP] 73. [CLS] [SEP] On 25 April 1994, she peaked at No. [CLS] [SEP] 112 in the WTA doubles rankings. [CLS] [SEP] Playing for Finland at the Fed Cup, Thorén has accumulated a win–loss record of 18–17. [CLS] [SEP]
