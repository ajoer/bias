Cyclone fergus was a tropical cyclone, later becoming an extratropical cyclone, that affected islands in the south-west Pacific Ocean. [CLS] [SEP] It lasted from December 23, 1996, to January 1, 1997, and reached speeds of up to 165 kilometers per hour, or 90 miles per hour. [CLS] [SEP] In additions to its winds, the storm caused heavy rains and severe flooding, causing significant damage to property in some areas. [CLS] [SEP]
