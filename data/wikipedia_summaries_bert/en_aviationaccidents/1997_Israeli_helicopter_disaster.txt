[CLS] The 1997 Israeli helicopter disaster (Hebrew: אסון המסוקים‎: Ason HaMasokim, lit. [SEP] [CLS] Disaster of the Helicopters) occurred on 4 February 1997, when two Israeli Air Force transport helicopters ferrying Israeli soldiers into Israel's security zone in southern Lebanon collided in mid-air, killing all 73 Israeli military personnel on board. [SEP] [CLS] The crash brought about widespread national mourning and is considered a leading factor in Israel's decision to withdraw from southern Lebanon in 2000. [SEP] [CLS]
