[CLS] The 2008 Sulawesi earthquake struck Sulawesi, Indonesia, on 16 November at 17:02:31 UTC. [SEP] [CLS] A 7.3 Mw earthquake, it was followed by seven aftershocks higher than 5.0 Mw . [SEP] [CLS] Tsunami warnings were issued for the region, but later cancelled. [SEP] [CLS] Four people were killed in the quake and 59 injured. [SEP] [CLS]
