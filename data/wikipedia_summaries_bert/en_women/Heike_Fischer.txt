[CLS] Heike Fischer (born 7 September 1982 in Demmin) is a German diver. [SEP] [CLS] She and Ditte Kotzian won bronze for Diving at the 2008 Summer Olympics – Women's synchronized 3 metre springboard. [SEP] [CLS] She and Kotzian had previously won a silver medal for diving at the 2007 World Aquatics Championships.Fischer competed in the Diving at the 2004 Summer Olympics – Women's 3 metre springboard. [SEP] [CLS] She lives in Leipzig, and is a sports soldier in the Bundeswehr. [SEP] [CLS] She is studying business studies at the AKAD-Fachhochschule Leipzig. [SEP] [CLS] Her trainer is Margit Fischer. [SEP] [CLS]
