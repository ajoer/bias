[CLS] Severe Tropical Cyclone Monty was a powerful category 4 tropical cyclone that formed during late February 2004. [SEP] [CLS] Monty was the 6th tropical cyclone and the 3rd Severe tropical cyclone of the 2003-04 Australian region cyclone season. [SEP] [CLS] Monty made landfall in a large, sparsely populated region of the Pilbara, Western Australia. [SEP] [CLS] Due to the low population, damages inland were not high, although there was major flooding and many roads were impassable because they were flooded or washed away by water. [SEP] [CLS] Monty set record high amounts of flooding in some parts of Western Australia. [SEP] [CLS]
