[CLS] This page documents the tornadoes and tornado outbreaks of 2012. [SEP] [CLS] Extremely destructive tornadoes form most frequently in the United States, Bangladesh, Brazil and Eastern India, but they can occur almost anywhere under the right conditions. [SEP] [CLS] Tornadoes also appear regularly in neighboring southern Canada during the Northern Hemisphere's summer season, and somewhat regularly in Europe, Asia, Argentina, and Australia. [SEP] [CLS] There were 939 tornadoes confirmed in the U.S. [SEP] [CLS] in 2012. [SEP] [CLS] Worldwide, 91 fatalities have been confirmed: 69 in the United States, six in Turkey, five each in China and Indonesia, three in New Zealand and one each in Italy, Japan and Poland.
