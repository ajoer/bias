Minami Takahashi (高橋 みなみ, Takahashi Minami, born 8 April 1991) is a Japanese singer, tarento and a former member of the Japanese girl idol group AKB48. [CLS] [SEP] She is in Team A of AKB48. [CLS] [SEP] She became the first General Director (総監督, sōkantoku) of AKB48 and its sister groups. [CLS] [SEP] In an interview with Nippon Television in June 2010, AKB48 producer Yasushi Akimoto says she is the "eldest daughter of the 48 sisters". [CLS] [SEP] She is also regarded as "soul of AKB", and her motto "Making efforts will be paid" has inspired many members of AKB48. [CLS] [SEP] She is commonly part of the main lineup of AKB48 singles. [CLS] [SEP] She is a member of the AKB48 sub-group no3b, along with AKB48 member Minami Minegishi and ex-member Haruna Kojima.
