[CLS] Kessai Hesa Note (born August 7, 1950 in Ailinglaplap) was President of the Marshall Islands from 2000 to 2008. [SEP] [CLS] Having served as Speaker of the Nitijeļā since 1988, he was elected President in 2000. [SEP] [CLS] Note is the first commoner, rather than an Iroijlaplap or traditional chief, to be elected to the Presidency. [SEP] [CLS] He is a member of the United Democratic Party, and was reelected by parliament in January 2004, receiving 20 votes, while Justin deBrum received 9. [SEP] [CLS] On January 7, 2008, following the November 2007 general election, he was defeated in his bid for re-election in a vote of Parliament, receiving 15 votes against 18 for Litokwa Tomeing. [SEP] [CLS] Note is of Japanese-Marshallese descent, having inherited his Japanese heritage from his paternal grandfather. [SEP] [CLS] Note's grandfather was a native of Niigata Prefecture, and settled in the Marshall Islands during the Japanese colonial era and married a Marshallese wife.Since January 2020, he has been the minister of justice.
