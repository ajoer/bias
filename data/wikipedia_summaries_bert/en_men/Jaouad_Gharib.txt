Jaouad Gharib (Arabic: جواد غريب‎, born 22 May 1972 in Khenifra) is a Moroccan long-distance runner who competes in the marathon. [CLS] [SEP] Winner of the 2003 World Championships and 2005 World Championships over the distance, he is also notable because he only began running seriously at the age of twenty-two. [CLS] [SEP] His 2005 retention made him one of only two successive world championship marathon champions, after Abel Antón. [CLS] [SEP] He was the silver medallist in the marathon at the 2008 Summer Olympics. [CLS] [SEP]
