José Manuel Pinto Colorado (born 8 November 1975) is a Spanish retired professional footballer who played as a goalkeeper. [CLS] [SEP] After making a name for himself at Celta, with which he made his La Liga debut, he signed for Barcelona in 2008, going on to act as backup for Víctor Valdés the vast majority of his spell and being part of the squads that won 16 major titles, including four national championships and two Champions Leagues. [CLS] [SEP] Pinto appeared in 160 top-flight games over 16 seasons, also representing in the competition Betis. [CLS] [SEP]
