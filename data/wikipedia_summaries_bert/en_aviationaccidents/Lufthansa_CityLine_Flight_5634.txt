On 6 January 1993, Lufthansa Cityline Flight 5634 departed Bremen Airport for Paris-Charles de Gaulle Airport at 17:30. [CLS] [SEP] Operating the flight was a Dash 8-311, registered D-BEAT with 23 passengers and crew. [CLS] [SEP] When Flight 5634 was nearing Paris, a Korean Air Boeing 747 burst a tire on landing, causing controllers to close the runway briefly. [CLS] [SEP] The pilots were notified to change course to another runway. [CLS] [SEP] While in clouds and heavy fog on short final the airliner entered a high sink rate and crashed into the ground tail-first. [CLS] [SEP] The aircraft broke into two parts, but there was no fire. [CLS] [SEP] The crash killed 4 passengers, and 19 further were injured in the accident. [CLS] [SEP]
