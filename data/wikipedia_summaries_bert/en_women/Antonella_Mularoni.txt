[CLS] Antonella Mularoni (born 27 September 1961) is a Sammarinese politician who was Captain Regent of San Marino from April 2013 until October 2013 (alongside Denis Amici) and Secretary for Foreign Affairs. [SEP] [CLS] She was the Sammarinese judge for the European Court of Human Rights. [SEP] [CLS] She graduated in law from Bologna University. [SEP] [CLS]
