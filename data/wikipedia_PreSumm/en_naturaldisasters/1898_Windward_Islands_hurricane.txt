[CLS] The Windward Islands Hurricane was a strong, destructive hurricane that raged through the eastern Caribbean islands in the early part of September during the 1898 Atlantic hurricane season. [SEP] [CLS] Between 300 and 400 people are believed to have died in the storm. [SEP] [CLS] Damage on Barbados and St. [SEP] [CLS] Vincent was catastrophic. [SEP] [CLS]
