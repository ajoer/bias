[CLS] The 2010 Kaohsiung earthquake, measuring 6.3 Mw, occurred on March 4 at 8:20 a.m. [SEP] [CLS] local time. [SEP] [CLS] The epicenter was located in the mountainous area of Kaohsiung County (now part of Kaohsiung City) of the southwestern Taiwan. [SEP] [CLS] It was the most powerful earthquake in Kaohsiung since 1900. [SEP] [CLS] The earthquake did not cause any deaths, but 96 people were injured. [SEP] [CLS]
