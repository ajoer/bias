[CLS] The 2008 Illinois earthquake was one of the largest earthquakes ever recorded in the Midwest state of Illinois. [SEP] [CLS] This moderate strike-slip shock measured 5.2 on the moment magnitude scale and had a maximum Mercalli intensity of VII (very strong). [SEP] [CLS] It occurred at 04:36:59 local time on April 18 near West Salem and Mount Carmel, Illinois, within the Wabash Valley Seismic Zone. [SEP] [CLS] Earthquakes in this part of the country are often felt at great distances. [SEP] [CLS]
