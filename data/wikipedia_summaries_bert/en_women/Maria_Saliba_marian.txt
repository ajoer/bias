[CLS] Mario Buhagiar Ph.D. [SEP] [CLS] (Lond.) (born 1945) is an author, a Fellow of the Society of Antiquaries of London (FSA) and member of the National Order of Merit (MOM). [SEP] [CLS] He is professor in history of art at the University of Malta and was for 25 years head of the History of Art Department, which he founded. [SEP] [CLS]
