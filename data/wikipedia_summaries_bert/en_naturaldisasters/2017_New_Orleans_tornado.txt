[CLS] The 2017 New Orleans tornado was a large and powerful EF3 tornado that was the strongest to strike New Orleans, Louisiana in recorded history. [SEP] [CLS] The tornado, which touched down on Tuesday, February 7, 2017, caused considerable damage along its path and left approximately 10,000 homes without electricity. [SEP] [CLS] This was one of several strong tornadoes that touched down across Louisiana and Mississippi that day.In response to the disaster, Governor John Bel Edwards declared a state of emergency. [SEP] [CLS] 33 injuries occurred in the area after the tornado hit near Chef Menteur Highway with hundreds of structures sustaining moderate to significant damage along the ten-mile path.
