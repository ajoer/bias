All Nippon Airways (ANA) Flight 58 was a Japanese domestic flight from Sapporo to Tokyo, operated by All Nippon Airways (ANA). [CLS] [SEP] On 30 July 1971, at 2:04 local time, a Japan Air Self-Defense Force (JASDF) F-86F Sabre jet fighter collided with the Boeing 727 airliner operating the flight, causing both aircraft to crash. [CLS] [SEP] All 162 occupants on the airliner were killed, while the Sabre pilot, a trainee with the JASDF, freed himself from the airplane after the collision and survived. [CLS] [SEP] This incident led to the resignation of both the head of Japan's Defense Agency and the JASDF chief of staff. [CLS] [SEP]
