Hordron Edge stone circle, also known as 'The Seven Stones of Hordron' is a Bronze Age stone circle (grid reference SK2152486851) in Derbyshire, England. [CLS] [SEP] It is on the edge of Moscar Moor. [CLS] [SEP] Ladybower reservoir is to the west, and Moscar Cross is to the northeast. [CLS] [SEP] Seven stones are presently (2017) visible with a further three stones, now recumbent and hidden discovered in 1992. [CLS] [SEP] Some authorities believe that the circle might have once comprised 26 stones.The stone circle is approximately 15 to 16 m (49 to 52 ft) in diameter, with eleven stones between 45 cm and 95 cm high extant upright.
