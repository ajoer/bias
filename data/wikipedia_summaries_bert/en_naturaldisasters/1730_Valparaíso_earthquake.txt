[CLS] The 1730 Valparaíso earthquake occurred at 04:45 local time (08:45 UTC) on July 8. [SEP] [CLS] It had an estimated magnitude of 9.1–9.3 and triggered a major tsunami with an estimated magnitude of Mt =8.75, that inundated the lower parts of Valparaíso. [SEP] [CLS] The earthquake caused severe damage from La Serena to Chillan, while the tsunami affected more than 1,000 km (620 mi) of Chile's coastline. [SEP] [CLS]
