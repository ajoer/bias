[CLS] Piedmont Airlines, Inc. [SEP] [CLS] is an American regional airline operating for American Eagle, formerly US Airways Express. [SEP] [CLS] It is a wholly owned subsidiary of the American Airlines Group, headquartered in unincorporated Wicomico County, Maryland, near the city of Salisbury. [SEP] [CLS] It conducts flight operations using Embraer ERJ 145 aircraft. [SEP] [CLS] Piedmont Airlines, Inc. [SEP] [CLS] also provides ground handling and customer service for airports in the northeastern and western United States. [SEP] [CLS] Its main base is Philadelphia International Airport with an additional hub at Charlotte Douglas International Airport. [SEP] [CLS]
