[CLS] The 1979 Easter flood was among the most costly and devastating floods ever to occur in Mississippi, with $500-700 million in damages. [SEP] [CLS] This is equivalent to $1.76 billion in present-day terms. [SEP] [CLS] It was the result of the Pearl River being overwhelmed by severe rain upstream. [SEP] [CLS] Floodwaters sent the Pearl 15 feet above flood stage. [SEP] [CLS] More than 17,000 residents of Jackson, Flowood, Pearl, Richland and other Mississippi communities were forced from their homes. [SEP] [CLS] The flooding of the Pearl River placed most of Jackson, the state's capital city, under water. [SEP] [CLS]
