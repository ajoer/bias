[CLS] The Voice of Germany is a German reality talent show created by John de Mol, based on the concept The Voice of Holland and it is part of an international series. [SEP] [CLS] It began airing on ProSieben and Sat.1 on November 24, 2011.There are five different stages to the show: producers' auditions, blind auditions, battle rounds, sign offs, and live shows. [SEP] [CLS] There have been nine winners to date: Ivy Quainoo, Nick Howard, Andreas Kümmert, Charley Ann Schmutzler, Jamie-Lee Kriewitz, Tay Schmedtmann, Natia Todua, Samuel Rösch, and the latest Claudia Emmanuela Santoso. [SEP] [CLS] The show was originally presented by Stefan Gödde. [SEP] [CLS] However, from 2012 onwards Thore Schölermann has presented the show. [SEP] [CLS] From 2015 until 2019, Schölermann together with Lena Gercke hosted the show. [SEP] [CLS] In 2020, Annemarie Carpendale will replace Gercke as the new host. [SEP] [CLS] The coaches for the tenth season will feature Mark Forster, Nico Santos, Stefanie Kloss & Yvonne Catterfeld and Samu Haber & Rea Garvey. [SEP] [CLS] Other coaches from previous seasons include Nena, The BossHoss, Xavier Naidoo, Max Herre, Michi & Smudo, Andreas Bourani, Michael Patrick Kelly, Alice Merton and Sido.
