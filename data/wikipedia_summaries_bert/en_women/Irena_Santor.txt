Irena Wiśniewska-Santor (Polish pronunciation: [iˈrɛna sanˈtɔr]; born 9 December 1934), known professionally as Irena Santor, is a Polish singer, musical performer and actress. [CLS] [SEP] Her career started in 1950 and continues to this day. [CLS] [SEP] For her clear mezzo-soprano, she enjoys an iconic status in Poland and is referred to as an influence for younger singers. [CLS] [SEP] From 1951 to 1959 Irena Santor (then as Irena Wiśniewska) was a member and the main soloist of the famous Polish folk group Mazowsze, with whom she toured on all continents. [CLS] [SEP] After leaving the group she decided to start solo performance. [CLS] [SEP] She was rewarded many prizes in her country and abroad on multiple occasions. [CLS] [SEP] Irena Santor made her acting debut in Stanisław Bareja's film Przygoda z piosenką (1968). [CLS] [SEP] She appeared in many TV and radio shows, was part of jury some talent shows and different song competitions. [CLS] [SEP] The legendary singer also recorded soundtracks to Polish and Soviet films. [CLS] [SEP] In 2017, as first singer in Poland, she was awarded an honorary doctorate for her activities.
