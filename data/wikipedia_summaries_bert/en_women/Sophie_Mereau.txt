Sophie Friederike Mereau (née Schubart) (27 March 1770 – 31 October 1806) was a writer associated with German Romanticism. [CLS] [SEP] Her maiden name was Schubart, but she did most of her work under the married name of Mereau. [CLS] [SEP] She also later married a second time, to writer Clemens Brentano, and took her husband's surname. [CLS] [SEP] She wrote novels, stories and poems, and she translated and published journals. [CLS] [SEP]
