[CLS] Dagmar Havlová (born Dagmar Veškrnová on 22 March 1953 in Brno, Czechoslovakia) is a Czech actress. [SEP] [CLS] She married Václav Havel, then Czech President, on 4 January 1997. [SEP] [CLS] She has one daughter, Nina Veškrnová (born 1976), from a previous marriage to Radvít Novák (1975–1980). [SEP] [CLS]
