TANS Peru Flight 222 was a domestic passenger flight from Jorge Chávez International Airport to Chachapoyas Airport with a stopover at FAP Captain José Abelardo Quiñones González International Airport in Peru, which crashed on 9 January 2003. [CLS] [SEP] The flight was operated by a Fokker F28-1000 Fellowship short range airliner. [CLS] [SEP] The aircraft crashed into the side of a hill while on approach to Chachapoyas Airport. [CLS] [SEP] All of the 46 passengers and crew aboard were killed. [CLS] [SEP] An investigation led by Peru's air accident investigation body, the Ministry of Transport and Communications determined the primary cause of the crash was multiple crew errors including poor crew communication and over confidence.
