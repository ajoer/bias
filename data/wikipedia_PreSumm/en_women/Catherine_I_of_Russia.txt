[CLS] Catherine I (Russian: Екатери́на I Алексе́евна, tr. [SEP] [CLS] Yekaterina I Alekseyevna; born Polish: Marta Helena Skowrońska, later known as Marta Samuilovna Skavronskaya; 15 April [O.S.
