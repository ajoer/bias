Severe Tropical Cyclone Oscar was one of the worst tropical cyclones to affect Fiji. [CLS] [SEP] The system was first noted as a shallow depression on February 23, while it was located to the north of Suva, Fiji. [CLS] [SEP] The system subsequently developed further as it moved westwards and was named as Tropical Cyclone Oscar during the next day. [CLS] [SEP] Over the next few days Oscar subsequently intensified as it moved westwards and gradually developed further and equivalent to a Category 3 Severe Tropical Cyclone, on the modern day Australian tropical cyclone intensity scale during February 27. [CLS] [SEP] The system subsequently turned and started to move south-eastwards towards Fiji. [CLS] [SEP]
