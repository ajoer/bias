Ijon Tichy (Polish pronunciation: [ˈijɔn ˈtixɨ]) is a fictional character who appears in several works of Stanisław Lem: initially in The Star Diaries, later in The Futurological Congress, Peace on Earth, Observation on the Spot, and Memoirs of a Space Traveller (more stories from The Star Diaries, issued in English translation as a separate volume). [CLS] [SEP] There also is a 1973 novel Ze wspomnień Ijona Tichego - Profesor A. [CLS] [SEP] Dońda. [CLS] [SEP]
