Jeehiun Katherine Lee is an organic chemist and a Professor in the Department of Chemistry at Rutgers University. [CLS] [SEP] She currently runs a research lab on the New Brunswick campus. [CLS] [SEP] Although she is an organic chemist by training, she has expanded her research field to biological chemistry, using mass spectrometry, computer modeling and other methods to characterize reactivity and catalysis.
