Heike Fischer (born 7 September 1982 in Demmin) is a German diver. [CLS] [SEP] She and Ditte Kotzian won bronze for Diving at the 2008 Summer Olympics – Women's synchronized 3 metre springboard. [CLS] [SEP] She and Kotzian had previously won a silver medal for diving at the 2007 World Aquatics Championships.Fischer competed in the Diving at the 2004 Summer Olympics – Women's 3 metre springboard. [CLS] [SEP] She lives in Leipzig, and is a sports soldier in the Bundeswehr. [CLS] [SEP] She is studying business studies at the AKAD-Fachhochschule Leipzig. [CLS] [SEP] Her trainer is Margit Fischer. [CLS] [SEP]
