[CLS] Gün Temür (Mongolian: Гүнтөмөр хаан; Chinese: 坤帖木兒; 1377–1402) was a Mongolian Khagan of the Northern Yuan dynasty based in Mongolia. [SEP] [CLS] Gün Temür was the eldest son of Elbeg Nigülesügchi Khan. [SEP] [CLS] He ruled from 1400 to 1402. [SEP] [CLS] His name, Gün Temür, means "Deep (intellectually) Iron" in the Mongolian language. [SEP] [CLS]
