The 1881 Chios earthquake occurred at 13:40 local time (11:30 UTC) on 3 April. [CLS] [SEP] It caused severe damage on the island of Chios and also affected Çeşme and Alaçatı on the coast of Turkey. [CLS] [SEP] The earthquake had an estimated magnitude of 7.3 and there were an estimated 7,866 casualties. [CLS] [SEP] The devastation from the earthquake was the last of the three 'catastrophes' that affected the island of Chios in the 19th century. [CLS] [SEP]
