Angelika Werthmann (born 7 November 1963 in Schwarzach im Pongau, Austria - died October 17, 2019) is a Member of the European Parliament since 2009, representing Hans-Peter Martin's List in Austria. [CLS] [SEP] Between 4 July 2012 and 7 April 2014 she was a member of the Liberal Group in the European Parliament. [CLS] [SEP]
