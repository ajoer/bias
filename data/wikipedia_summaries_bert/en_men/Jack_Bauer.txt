Jack Bauer is a fictional character and the lead protagonist of the Fox television series 24. [CLS] [SEP] His character has worked in various capacities on the show, often as a member of the Counter Terrorist Unit (CTU) based in Los Angeles, and working with the FBI in Washington, D.C. [CLS] [SEP] Bauer's job usually involves helping prevent major terrorist attacks on the United States, saving both civilian lives and government administrations. [CLS] [SEP] On many occasions, Jack does so at great personal expense, as those he thwarts subsequently target him and his loved ones. [CLS] [SEP] He is not a crooked agent; however, Bauer's frequent use of torture to gather information has generated much controversy and discussion.
