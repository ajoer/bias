Henriett Koósz (born 14 February 1980, Kőszeg) is an Austrian wheelchair tennis player. [CLS] [SEP] As a member of the Austrian Paralympic wheelchair tennis team, she competed at the 2012 Paralympics in London. [CLS] [SEP] In 2016, she represented Austria in para-badminton at the Austrian Open. [CLS] [SEP] In 2013, she was awarded the MiA award by the Austrian Federal Sports Organisation. [CLS] [SEP]
