[CLS] On 4 December 2003, a Polish Mi-8 helicopter operated by the 36th Special Aviation Regiment carrying Poland's Prime Minister Leszek Miller crashed near Piaseczno, just outside Warsaw. [SEP] [CLS] The pilot performed an autorotation landing in a forest following the failure of both engines. [SEP] [CLS] The helicopter suffered extensive damage and was written off as a total loss, but despite the severity of the crash there were no fatalities. [SEP] [CLS] Fourteen of the 15 people on board were injured, including Leszek Miller, who had two of his thoracic vertebrae broken. [SEP] [CLS]
