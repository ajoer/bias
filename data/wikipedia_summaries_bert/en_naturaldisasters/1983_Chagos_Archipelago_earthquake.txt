The 1983 Chagos Archipelago earthquake occurred on November 30 at 17:46 UTC in the region of the Chagos Archipelago, British Indian Ocean Territory. [CLS] [SEP] This earthquake had a magnitude of Ms 7.7. [CLS] [SEP] This earthquake occurred in the Indian Plate near the Central Indian Ridge. [CLS] [SEP] The Central Indian Ridge is unusually active in near-ridge earthquakes. [CLS] [SEP] The intense seismic activity in this region may indicate an early stage of converging plate boundary, which is responsible for the N-S extension near Chagos.
