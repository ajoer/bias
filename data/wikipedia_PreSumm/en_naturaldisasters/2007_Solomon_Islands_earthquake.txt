[CLS] The 2007 Solomon Islands earthquake took place on 2 April 2007, near the provincial capital of Gizo on Ghizo Island, in Solomon Islands. [SEP] [CLS] Its magnitude was estimated at 8.1 on the Mw scale, and 7.8 on the Ms scale. [SEP] [CLS] The tsunami that followed the earthquake killed 52 people. [SEP] [CLS] According to the USGS, the earthquake was recorded around 7:39:56 a.m. [SEP] [CLS] local time (UTC+11). [SEP] [CLS] The focus was 10 km (6 mi) deep and 40 km (25 mi) South South-East of Gizo township on New Georgia Islands in Western Province. [SEP] [CLS] There were numerous aftershocks, the largest of which had a magnitude of 6.2. [SEP] [CLS]
