[CLS] On 10 August 1999, a Breguet Atlantic maritime patrol aircraft of the Pakistan Naval Air Arm was shot down by a MiG 21 fighter of the Indian Air Force over the Rann of Kutch, on the border between India and Pakistan. [SEP] [CLS] The episode took place just a month after the Kargil War, aggravating already tense relations between the two countries. [SEP] [CLS] Foreign diplomats based in Pakistan escorted to the site by the Pakistan Army noted that the plane may have crossed the border. [SEP] [CLS] They also believed that India's reaction was unjustified. [SEP] [CLS] Pakistan later lodged a compensation claim at the International Court of Justice, blaming India for the incident, but the court dismissed the case, ruling that the Court had no jurisdiction in the matter.
