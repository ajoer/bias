[CLS] Ismael Serrano (born 9 March 1974) is a singer/songwriter and guitarist from Spain, popular in Spain and Latin America, known for his often political lyrics and eclectic musical influences. [SEP] [CLS] During his creative career he has been influenced by other Spanish singer-songwriters such as Joaquín Sabina, Joan Manuel Serrat and the Cuban Silvio Rodríguez amongst others. [SEP] [CLS] His music also shows influences from renowned poets such as Luis García Montero and Mario Benedetti. [SEP] [CLS]
