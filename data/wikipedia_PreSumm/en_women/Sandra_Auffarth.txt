[CLS] Sandra Auffarth (born 27 December 1986) is a German equestrian.At the 2012 Summer Olympics she competed in the Individual eventing with Opgun Louvo. [SEP] [CLS] Auffarth and Louvo had won team gold and individual bronze at the 2012 Summer Olympics. [SEP] [CLS] One year before, she had won team gold and individual silver with Opgun Louvo at her first senior rider championship - the 2011 European Eventing Championship. [SEP] [CLS] She lives in Ganderkesee, where she and her parents have a horse farm. [SEP] [CLS]
