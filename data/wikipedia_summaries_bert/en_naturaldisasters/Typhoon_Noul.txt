The name Noul has been used to name two tropical cyclones in the northwestern Pacific Ocean. [CLS] [SEP] The name was contributed by North Korea and it means glowing or sunset. [CLS] [SEP] This name replaced Pongsona after 2002 Pacific typhoon season and it means for garden balsam.
