[CLS] The 1692 Jamaica earthquake struck Port Royal, Jamaica on 7 June. [SEP] [CLS] A stopped pocket watch found in the harbour in 1959 indicated that it occurred around 11:43 a.m.Known as the "storehouse and treasury of the West Indies", and as "one of the wickedest places on Earth", it was, at the time, the unofficial capital of Jamaica, one of the busiest and wealthiest ports in the West Indies, and a common home port for many of the privateers and pirates operating within the Caribbean Sea. [SEP] [CLS] The earthquake caused most of the city to sink below sea level. [SEP] [CLS] About 2,000 people died as a result of the earthquake and the following tsunami; and, about another 3,000 people died in the days following the earthquakes, due to injuries and disease.
