[CLS] On June 22, 1951, Pan Am Flight 151, flown by the Lockheed L-049 Constellation propliner Clipper Great Republic (registration N88846) crashed into a West African hill at an elevation of 1,050 ft (320 m) near the village of Sanoyie in Bong County, Liberia. [SEP] [CLS] All 31 passengers and nine crew on board were killed. [SEP] [CLS]
