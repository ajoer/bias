Emil Rilke (born 19 November 1983) is a Czech footballer who currently plays as a midfielder for Ústí nad Labem, after leaving Romanian Universitatea Cluj. [CLS] [SEP] Previously Rilke has played for Czech clubs Teplice, Jablonec 97, SIAD Most and Slovan Liberec and Hansa Rostock. [CLS] [SEP] Rilke was a member of the Czech Republic Under-20 national team which competed in the 2003 U20 FIFA World Cup in the United Arab Emirates. [CLS] [SEP] He won the Corgoň liga in the 2009–2010 season with Žilina. [CLS] [SEP] On 21 November 2012 he signed for German side Hansa Rostock in the 3. [CLS] [SEP] Liga. [CLS] [SEP]
