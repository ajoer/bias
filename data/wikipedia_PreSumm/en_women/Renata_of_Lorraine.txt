[CLS] Renata of Lorraine (20 April 1544 – 22 May 1602) was by birth a member of the House of Lorraine and by marriage Duchess of Bavaria. [SEP] [CLS] Born in Nancy, France, she was the second child and eldest daughter of Francis I, Duke of Lorraine and Christina of Denmark. [SEP] [CLS] Her paternal grandparents were Antoine, Duke of Lorraine and Renée of Bourbon-Montpensier and her maternal grandparents were Christian II of Denmark and Isabella of Austria. [SEP] [CLS]
