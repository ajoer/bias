Laoag International Airlines Flight 585 was a scheduled flight operated by Laoag International Airlines from Manila to Laoag, Philippines. [CLS] [SEP] On November 11, 2002, the Fokker F-27 Friendship crashed into Manila Bay shortly after takeoff from Ninoy Aquino International Airport. [CLS] [SEP] Of the 34 passengers and crew on board, 15 survived. [CLS] [SEP]
