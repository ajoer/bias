[CLS] Joshua Mark Sussman (born December 30, 1983) is an American actor, best known for his role as Hugh Normous in Wizards of Waverly Place and his role as Jacob Ben Israel in Glee, for which he won a Screen Actors Guild Award. [SEP] [CLS] He also had a minor role on the Nickelodeon TV series Drake & Josh as Clayton. [SEP] [CLS]
