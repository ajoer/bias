[CLS] Laocoön (; Ancient Greek: Λαοκόων, IPA: [laokóɔːn]), the son of Acoetes, is a figure in Greek and Roman mythology and the Epic Cycle. [SEP] [CLS] He was a Trojan priest who was attacked, with his two sons, by giant serpents sent by the gods. [SEP] [CLS] The story of Laocoön has been the subject of numerous artists, both in ancient and in more contemporary times. [SEP] [CLS]
