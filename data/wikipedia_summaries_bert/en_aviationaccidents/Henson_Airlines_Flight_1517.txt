Piedmont Airlines, Inc. [CLS] [SEP] is an American regional airline operating for American Eagle, formerly US Airways Express. [CLS] [SEP] It is a wholly owned subsidiary of the American Airlines Group, headquartered in unincorporated Wicomico County, Maryland, near the city of Salisbury. [CLS] [SEP] It conducts flight operations using Embraer ERJ 145 aircraft. [CLS] [SEP] Piedmont Airlines, Inc.
