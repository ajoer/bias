[CLS] The name Jangmi has been used to name four tropical cyclones in the northwestern Pacific Ocean. [SEP] [CLS] In addition, the variant Changmi was used in 2002 before the spelling was corrected by the WMO Typhoon Committee, The name was contributed by Korea; it means a rose. [SEP] [CLS] Tropical Storm Changmi (2002) (T0219) – wasn't recognized by the JTWC. [SEP] [CLS] Typhoon Jangmi (2008) (T0815, 19W, Ofel) – strongest storm of 2008. [SEP] [CLS] Tropical Storm Jangmi (2014) (T1423, 23W, Seniang) – a rare storm that impacted the Philippines. [SEP] [CLS] Tropical Storm Jangmi (2020) (T2005, 05W, Enteng) – affected South Korea.
