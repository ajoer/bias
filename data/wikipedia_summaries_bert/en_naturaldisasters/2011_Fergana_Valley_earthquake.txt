The 2011 Fergana Valley earthquake affected Uzbekistan, Kyrgyzstan and Tajikistan at 01:35 local time on 20 July. [CLS] [SEP] The dip-slip shock had a moment magnitude of 6.2 and a maximum Mercalli intensity of VIII (Severe). [CLS] [SEP] Its epicenter was located just inside Kyrgyzstan's border in the Fergana Valley region. [CLS] [SEP] Fourteen were killed and eighty-six were injured. [CLS] [SEP]
