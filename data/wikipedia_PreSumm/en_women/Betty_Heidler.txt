[CLS] Betty Heidler (born 14 October 1983) is a retired German track and field athlete who competed in the hammer throw. [SEP] [CLS] She held the world record from 2011 until 2014 with her personal best throw of 79.42 m (260 ft 6 in). [SEP] [CLS] She is the 2012 Olympic silver medallist, the 2007 World champion and the 2009 and 2011 World Championship silver medallist. [SEP] [CLS] She also finished fourth in the Olympic finals in 2004 and 2016. [SEP] [CLS]
