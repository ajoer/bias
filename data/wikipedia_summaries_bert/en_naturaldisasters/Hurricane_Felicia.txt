The name Felicia has been used for four tropical cyclones in the Eastern Pacific Ocean. [CLS] [SEP] Felicia replaced Fefa. [CLS] [SEP] Hurricane Felicia (1997) – formed in the open ocean, causing no known damage or deaths. [CLS] [SEP] Tropical Storm Felicia (2003) – remained at sea, crossing into the Central Pacific, but dissipating well east of Hawaii. [CLS] [SEP] Hurricane Felicia (2009) – remained at sea, dissipating before hitting Hawaii. [CLS] [SEP] Tropical Storm Felicia (2015) – remained at sea as a weak tropical storm. [CLS] [SEP]
