Shanta Janardan Shelke (Marathi: शांता शेळके) (12 October 1922 – 6 June 2002) was a Marathi poet and writer in the Marathi language. [CLS] [SEP] She was also a noted journalist, and academic. [CLS] [SEP] Her work included song compositions, stories, translations, and children's literature. [CLS] [SEP] She presided over many literary gatherings. [CLS] [SEP] Some of her compositions were noted either as stand-alone poetic works or as songs sung by Marathi greats like Lata Mangeshkar, Asha Bhosale and Kishori Amonkar, to name a few. [CLS] [SEP] Her pen name was Vasant Avsare. [CLS] [SEP]
