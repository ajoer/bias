The name Toraji has been used to name four tropical cyclones in the northwestern Pacific Ocean. [CLS] [SEP] The name was submitted by North Korea and refers to a species of flower, the bellflower (Platycodon grandiflorus). [CLS] [SEP] Typhoon Toraji (2001) (T0108, 11W, Isang) – impacted Taiwan and China, killing at least 72 Tropical Storm Toraji (2007) (T0703, 03W) – struck Vietnam Tropical Storm Toraji (2013) (T1317, 15W) – struck Japan.
