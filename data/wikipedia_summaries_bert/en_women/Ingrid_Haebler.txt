[CLS] Ingrid Haebler (born 20 June 1929 in Vienna, Austria) is an Austrian pianist. [SEP] [CLS] Her complete set of Mozart's piano sonatas for the Denon label is still regarded as among the finest sets. [SEP] [CLS] Haebler also recorded all of Mozart's piano concertos (most of them twice), often with her own cadenzas - and all of Schubert's sonatas. [SEP] [CLS] She was one of several Austrian musicians to experiment early with period instruments, having recorded the music of Johann Christian Bach on a fortepiano. [SEP] [CLS] Her recordings of Mozart and Beethoven with the violinist Henryk Szeryng are particularly prized. [SEP] [CLS]
