[CLS] Nogometni klub Inter Zaprešić is a professional Croatian football club based in Zaprešić, a town northwest of the capital Zagreb. [SEP] [CLS] They play in the Druga HNL after they were relegated from the Prva HNL at the end of the 2019–20 season. [SEP] [CLS] The team are nicknamed Keramičari (The ceramics makers), because they were sponsored by a ceramics factory through much of their history) or Div iz predgrađa (The giant from the suburb). [SEP] [CLS] The team's colours are yellow and blue. [SEP] [CLS] Home games at Stadion ŠRC Zaprešić. [SEP] [CLS]
