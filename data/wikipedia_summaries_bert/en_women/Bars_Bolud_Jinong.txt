Barsbolad Jinong Khan (ᠪᠠᠷᠰᠪᠣᠯᠤᠳᠵᠢᠨᠦᠩᠬᠠᠭᠠᠨ Mongolian: Барсболд жонон хаан, transliteration : Barsbold Jonon Khaan, aka : Steel-Tiger Jinong Khagan) or Barsbolad Khan (Mongolian: Барсболд хаан, translit. [CLS] [SEP] : Barsbold Khaan); Chinese: 巴爾斯博羅特; 1490–1531) was a Mongol Khagan of the Northern Yuan dynasty based in Mongolia from 1517 to 1519, and Jinong, from 1519 to 1531. [CLS] [SEP]
