[CLS] Storm David (also called Cyclone Friederike in Germany) was a compact but deadly European windstorm that heavily affected the British Isles, France, Benelux, Central Europe, Northern Italy, Poland and parts of Eastern Europe in early 2018 with widespread hurricane-force gusts and severe snowfall, creating blizzard conditions in some areas. [SEP] [CLS] The storm caused extensive damage and traffic disruption. [SEP] [CLS] It was given the name David by Météo France while the FUB named it Friederike. [SEP] [CLS]
