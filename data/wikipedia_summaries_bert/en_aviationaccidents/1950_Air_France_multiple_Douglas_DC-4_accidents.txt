[CLS] Two Air France Douglas DC-4 aircraft crashed two days apart in June 1950 within a few miles of each other and under similar circumstances. [SEP] [CLS] These two accidents, on 12 and 14 June, occurred while the aircraft were operating the same route from Saigon to Paris. [SEP] [CLS] Both aircraft had stopped at Karachi Airport and crashed into the sea on approach to Bahrain. [SEP] [CLS] A total of 86 passengers and crew were killed, 46 on 12 June and 40 on 14 June.
