Sibel Kekilli (born 16 June 1980) is a German actress. [CLS] [SEP] She gained public attention after starring in the 2004 film Head-On. [CLS] [SEP] She won two Lolas, the most prestigious German film awards, for her performances in Head-On and When We Leave (2010). [CLS] [SEP] Beginning in 2011, she became more widely known for her role as Shae in the HBO series Game of Thrones. [CLS] [SEP]
