The 2015 Caribbean drought is an ongoing drought affecting the Caribbean islands, from Cuba to Trinidad and Tobago. [CLS] [SEP] The situation is further aggravated by the presence of an abnormal amount of dust and dry air over the southern Atlantic. [CLS] [SEP] Besides the especially hard hit U.S. [CLS] [SEP] Commonwealth of Puerto Rico, other Caribbean islands were affected by drought conditions to varying degrees. [CLS] [SEP] These include Cuba, the Dominican Republic, Jamaica, St. [CLS] [SEP] Lucia, and Trinidad and Tobago. [CLS] [SEP] The drought region also extends to Guyana in the northeastern region of South America. [CLS] [SEP]
