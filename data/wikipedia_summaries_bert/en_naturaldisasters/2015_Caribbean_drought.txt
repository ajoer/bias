[CLS] The 2015 Caribbean drought is an ongoing drought affecting the Caribbean islands, from Cuba to Trinidad and Tobago. [SEP] [CLS] The situation is further aggravated by the presence of an abnormal amount of dust and dry air over the southern Atlantic. [SEP] [CLS] Besides the especially hard hit U.S. [SEP] [CLS] Commonwealth of Puerto Rico, other Caribbean islands were affected by drought conditions to varying degrees. [SEP] [CLS] These include Cuba, the Dominican Republic, Jamaica, St. [SEP] [CLS] Lucia, and Trinidad and Tobago. [SEP] [CLS] The drought region also extends to Guyana in the northeastern region of South America. [SEP] [CLS]
