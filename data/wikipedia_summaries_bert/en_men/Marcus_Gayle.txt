[CLS] Marcus Anthony Gayle (born 27 September 1970) is an English-born Jamaican former professional footballer, who was most recently manager of Staines Town. [SEP] [CLS] Gayle's primary position was as a striker but also played as a winger and central defender towards the end of his career. [SEP] [CLS] His most notable clubs included Wimbledon and Watford before he finished his playing career at AFC Wimbledon. [SEP] [CLS] He is a member of the Brentford Hall of Fame and made 230 appearances in two spells with the club. [SEP] [CLS] He won 14 caps for Jamaica and was a key player for his country at the 1998 World Cup tournament. [SEP] [CLS] After retiring as a player, he became manager of the AFC Wimbledon reserve team for three years before taking charge of Staines Town in 2012. [SEP] [CLS]
