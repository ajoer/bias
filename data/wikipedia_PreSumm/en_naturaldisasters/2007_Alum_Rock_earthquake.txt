[CLS] The 2007 Alum Rock earthquake occurred on October 30 at 8:04 p.m. [SEP] [CLS] Pacific Daylight Time in Alum Rock Park in San Jose, in the U.S. [SEP] [CLS] state of California. [SEP] [CLS] It measured 5.6 on the moment magnitude scale and had a maximum Mercalli intensity of VI (Strong). [SEP] [CLS] The event was then the largest in the San Francisco Bay Area since the 1989 Loma Prieta earthquake, which measured 6.9 on the moment magnitude scale, but was later surpassed by the 2014 South Napa earthquake. [SEP] [CLS] Ground shaking from the Alum Rock quake reached San Francisco and Oakland and other points further north. [SEP] [CLS] Sixty thousand felt reports existed far beyond Santa Rosa, as far north as Eugene, Oregon. [SEP] [CLS]
