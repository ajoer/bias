[CLS] The Young Radicals of the Left (French: Jeunes Radicaux de Gauche abbreviated as JRG), are the youth organisation of the French social-liberal Radical Party of the Left. [SEP] [CLS] In opposition to its common understanding of its name, the YRL are a moderate centre-left political party. [SEP] [CLS] The party gathers members under 30 years of age all throughout France. [SEP] [CLS]
