[CLS] Daniel "Dani" Parejo Muñoz (Spanish pronunciation: [daˈnjel ˈdani paˈɾexo]; born 16 April 1989) is a Spanish professional footballer who plays as a central midfielder for Villarreal CF and the Spain national team. [SEP] [CLS] After starting out at Real Madrid, he first made a name in La Liga with Getafe. [SEP] [CLS] Transferring to Valencia in 2011, he went on to appear in 383 official matches for the latter club and win the 2019 Copa del Rey; he also spent four months in England, with Queens Park Rangers. [SEP] [CLS] Across all youth levels, Parejo won 43 caps for Spain and scored nine goals. [SEP] [CLS] He made his full debut in 2018, at the age of 28. [SEP] [CLS]
