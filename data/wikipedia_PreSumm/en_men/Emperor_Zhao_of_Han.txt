[SEP] [CLS] Emperor Zhao, under the tutelage of Huo, took the initiative and lowered taxes as well as reducing government spending. [SEP] [CLS] As a result, citizens prospered and the Han Dynasty enjoyed an era of peace. [SEP] [CLS] Emperor Zhao died after reigning for 13 years, at the age of 20. [SEP] [CLS] He was succeeded by He, Prince of Changyi. [SEP] [CLS]
