[CLS] Verena von Strenge (born 27 July 1975, Munich, Germany) is a German singer and dancer known for being the lead singer for the German happy hardcore dance group Dune from 1996–1997 and 1999–2000. [SEP] [CLS] Von Strenge loved dance since she was very young. [SEP] [CLS] At the age of five, she was taught classical dance by her grandmother who was a former ballerina. [SEP] [CLS] However, von Strenge had to give up her plans of being a professional dancer due to knee problems. [SEP] [CLS]
