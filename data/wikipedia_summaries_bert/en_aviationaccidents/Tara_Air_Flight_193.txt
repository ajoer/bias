Tara Air Flight 193 was a scheduled domestic passenger flight from Pokhara to Jomsom, Nepal. [CLS] [SEP] On 24 February 2016, eight minutes after take-off, the aircraft serving the flight, a Viking Air DHC-6-400 Twin Otter went missing with 23 people on board. [CLS] [SEP] Hours later, the wreckage was found near the village of Dana, Myagdi district. [CLS] [SEP] There were no survivors. [CLS] [SEP] It was Tara Air's deadliest accident. [CLS] [SEP]
