[CLS] Amanda Elizabeth Chessell is a computer scientist and a Distinguished Engineer at IBM. [SEP] [CLS] She has been awarded the title of IBM Master Inventor. [SEP] [CLS] She is also a Member of the IBM Academy of Technology. [SEP] [CLS] Outside IBM, Chessell is the first woman to be awarded the Silver Medal of the Royal Academy of Engineering. [SEP] [CLS] In 2002 she was elected a Fellow of the Royal Academy of Engineering.Chessell is a visiting professor at the University of Sheffield and at the Surrey Centre for the Digital Economy (CoDE) at the University of Surrey.
