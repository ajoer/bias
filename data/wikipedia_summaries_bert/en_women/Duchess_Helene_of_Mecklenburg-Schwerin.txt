Duchess Helene of Mecklenburg-Schwerin (Helene Luise Elisabeth; 24 January 1814 – 17 May 1858) was a French Crown Princess after her marriage in 1837 to the eldest son of Louis Philippe I, Ferdinand Philippe of Orléans. [CLS] [SEP] She was the mother of the future Count of Paris and Duke of Chartres. [CLS] [SEP] Her descendants include present-day pretenders to the throne of France and Italy and the kings of Spain and Belgium. [CLS] [SEP]
