Maria Margaret Klawe ( KLAH-vay; born 1951) is a computer scientist and the fifth president of Harvey Mudd College (since July 1, 2006). [CLS] [SEP] Born in Toronto in 1951, she became a naturalized U.S. [CLS] [SEP] citizen in 2009. [CLS] [SEP] She was previously Dean of the School of Engineering and Applied Science at Princeton University. [CLS] [SEP]
