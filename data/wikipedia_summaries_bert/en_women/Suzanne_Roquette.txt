Suzanne Roquette (born 31 August 1942 - died 28 May 2020) was a German actress. [CLS] [SEP] She is best known for her role as Main Mission Operative Tanya Alexander in Year One of the science fiction television series Space: 1999. [CLS] [SEP] Space:1999 costar Prentis Hancock noted her passing on Facebook on May 29, 2020, and Nick Tate memorialized Suzanne on his Facebook page on May 30. [CLS] [SEP]
