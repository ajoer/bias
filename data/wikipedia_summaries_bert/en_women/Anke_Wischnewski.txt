Anke Wischnewski (born 5 January 1978 in Annaberg-Buchholz, Saxony) is a German former luger who has competed since 2001 to 2014. [CLS] [SEP] She won two medals in the women's singles at the FIL World Luge Championships with a silver in 2007 and a bronze in 2005. [CLS] [SEP] Wischnewski's best finish at the FIL European Luge Championships in the women's singles event was third at Oberhof in 2013. [CLS] [SEP] Her best finish in the overall Luge World Cup title was third in 2006-7. [CLS] [SEP] Wishnewski qualified for the 2010 Winter Olympics where she finished fifth. [CLS] [SEP]
