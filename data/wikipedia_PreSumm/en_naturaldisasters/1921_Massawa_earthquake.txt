[CLS] The 1921 Massawa earthquake took place off the coast of Massawa, Eritrea, on August 14 with a moment magnitude of 6.1 and a Mercalli intensity of VIII (Severe). [SEP] [CLS] The first aftershock after the initial earthquake was of similar magnitude. [SEP] [CLS] Significant damage was caused to the harbour at Massawa with a number of deaths reported. [SEP] [CLS] Aftershocks were felt as far away as Asmara and Dekemhare. [SEP] [CLS]
