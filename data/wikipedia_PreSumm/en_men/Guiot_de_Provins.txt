[CLS] Guiot de Provins, also spelled Guyot (died after 1208), was a French poet and trouvère from the town of Provins in the Champagne area. [SEP] [CLS] A declining number of scholars identify him with Kyot the Provençal, the alleged writer of the source material used by Wolfram von Eschenbach for his romance Parzival, but most others consider such a source to be a literary device made up by Wolfram. [SEP] [CLS] At any rate, Guiot was a popular writer in his day. [SEP] [CLS]
