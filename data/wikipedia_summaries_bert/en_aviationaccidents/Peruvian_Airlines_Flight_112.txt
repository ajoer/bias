[CLS] Peruvian Airlines Flight 112 was a domestic scheduled passenger flight from Lima to Jauja in Peru. [SEP] [CLS] On 28 March 2017, the aircraft operating the flight suffered undercarriage collapse after landing, caught fire and was burnt out. [SEP] [CLS] While there were no fatalities in this accident, thirty-nine of the 150 people on board were injured. [SEP] [CLS]
