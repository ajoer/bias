Michela Figini (born 7 April 1966) is a former World Cup alpine ski racer from Switzerland. [CLS] [SEP] She is an Olympic, World Cup, and world champion. [CLS] [SEP] Figini made her World Cup debut at age 16 in January 1983 and won the downhill at the 1984 Winter Olympics in Sarajevo at age 17. [CLS] [SEP] Through 2014, she remains the youngest Olympic champion in alpine skiing. [CLS] [SEP] She won the downhill the following year at the 1985 World Championships. [CLS] [SEP] She also came second in the Downhill at the 1987 World Championships, and won a silver medal in the Super-G at the 1988 Winter Olympics in Calgary. [CLS] [SEP] Figini won 26 World Cup races and overall titles in 1985 and 1988, as well as four season titles in downhill, one in Super-G. [CLS] [SEP]
