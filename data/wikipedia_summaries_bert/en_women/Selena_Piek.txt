Selena Piek (born 30 September 1991) is a Dutch badminton player who specializes in doubles. [CLS] [SEP] Her current partner is Cheryl Seinen for women's doubles and Robin Tabeling for mixed doubles. [CLS] [SEP] She won the 2014 and 2015 editions of Dutch National Badminton Championships with Eefje Muskens. [CLS] [SEP] She has also won the mixed doubles national championships title since 2012 with three different partners. [CLS] [SEP] Piek was the gold medalist at the 2019 European Games in the women's doubles event with Cheryl Seinen. [CLS] [SEP]
