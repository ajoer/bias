The 1913 Asmara earthquake took place outside Asmara, Eritrea on 27 February. [CLS] [SEP] The data as to the magnitude of the earthquake is imprecise due to the frequency and magnitude of aftershocks, but a maximum felt intensity of VI (Strong) on the Mercalli intensity scale was recorded at Asmara. [CLS] [SEP] The "felt" area of the earthquake extended into Northern Ethiopia as well as Kassala in Sudan. [CLS] [SEP] The earthquake caused significant damage in Asmara (VI), Keren (IV), Massawa (V) and Adi Ugri (V). [CLS] [SEP]
