[CLS] Emperor Shizong of Liao (29 January 919 – 7 October 951), personal name Wuyu, sinicised name Yelü Ruan, was the third emperor of the Khitan-led Liao dynasty. [SEP] [CLS] He was the son of Yelü Bei, the eldest son of Abaoji (Emperor Taizu), the founder of the Liao dynasty. [SEP] [CLS] He came to power in 947 after the death of his uncle, Emperor Taizong, who raised him in his father's absence. [SEP] [CLS]
