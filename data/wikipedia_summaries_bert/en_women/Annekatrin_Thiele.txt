[CLS] Annekatrin Thiele (born 18 October 1984 in Sangerhausen) is a German rower. [SEP] [CLS] She competed at the 2008 Summer Olympics, where she won a silver medal in double sculls with Christiane Huth. [SEP] [CLS] At the 2012 Summer Olympics, she again won a silver medal, this time in the women's quadruple sculls. [SEP] [CLS] She has also twice won World Championship gold, both times in the women's quadruple sculls. [SEP] [CLS] At the 2016 Summer Olympics in Rio de Janeiro she competed in women's quadruple sculls competition in which the German team won the gold medal. [SEP] [CLS]
