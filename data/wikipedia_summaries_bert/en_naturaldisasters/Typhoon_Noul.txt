[CLS] The name Noul has been used to name two tropical cyclones in the northwestern Pacific Ocean. [SEP] [CLS] The name was contributed by North Korea and it means glowing or sunset. [SEP] [CLS] This name replaced Pongsona after 2002 Pacific typhoon season and it means for garden balsam.
