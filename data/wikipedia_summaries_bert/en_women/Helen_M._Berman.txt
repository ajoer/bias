[CLS] Helen Miriam Berman is a Board of Governors Professor of Chemistry and Chemical Biology at Rutgers University and a former director of the RCSB Protein Data Bank (one of the member organizations of the Worldwide Protein Data Bank). [SEP] [CLS] A structural biologist, her work includes structural analysis of protein-nucleic acid complexes, and the role of water in molecular interactions. [SEP] [CLS] She is also the founder and director of the Nucleic Acid Database, and leads the Protein Structure Initiative Structural Genomics Knowledgebase. [SEP] [CLS]
