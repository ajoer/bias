[CLS] Darren Tulett (born 2 June 1965) is an English journalist who presents football on French television. [SEP] [CLS] He was a presenter for Canal+ for a decade, and now works for beIN Sport. [SEP] [CLS] His presenting style is lighthearted and flamboyant. [SEP] [CLS] Although well known in France as the face of English football, he is almost unknown in Britain. [SEP] [CLS]
