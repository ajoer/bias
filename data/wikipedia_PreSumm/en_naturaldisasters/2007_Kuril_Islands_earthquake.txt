[CLS] The 2007 Kuril Islands earthquake occurred east of the Kuril Islands on 13 January at 1:23 p.m. [SEP] [CLS] (JST). [SEP] [CLS] The shock had a moment magnitude of 8.1 and a maximum Mercalli intensity of VI (Strong). [SEP] [CLS] A non-destructive tsunami was generated, with maximum wave amplitudes of 0.32 meters (1 ft 1 in). [SEP] [CLS] The earthquake is considered a doublet of the 8.3 magnitude 2006 Kuril Islands earthquake which occurred the previous November approximately 95 km to the southeast. [SEP] [CLS]
