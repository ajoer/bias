[CLS] Mount Tai earthquake (Chinese: 泰山震) is the first recorded earthquake in history. [SEP] [CLS] It occurred at Mount Tai during the 7th year of the reign of King Fa from the Xia dynasty between circa 2205 and 1600 BCE. [SEP] [CLS] It was mentioned briefly in the Bamboo Annals. [SEP] [CLS] The event has been dated to 1831 or 1731 BCE. [SEP] [CLS]
