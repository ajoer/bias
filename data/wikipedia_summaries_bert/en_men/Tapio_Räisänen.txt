Tapio Räisänen (born May 10, 1949) is a Finnish former ski jumper who competed in the late 1970s. [CLS] [SEP] He was born in Taivalkoski. [CLS] [SEP] Räisänen won a gold medal in the individual large hill competition at the 1978 FIS Nordic World Ski Championships in Lahti and finished 6th in the normal hill at those same championships. [CLS] [SEP] Ski flying-WM 1977 in Vikersund 7th. [CLS] [SEP] Räisänen's best non-world championship career finish was 9th in a normal hill event at Bischofshofen, Austria in 1979, and 1st in Japan, Sapporo large hill 1980. [CLS] [SEP] In 2012, the Finnish Minister for Education and the Ministry of Culture created the Tapio Räisänen Pro Sports Recognition Award worth 20,000 euros. [CLS] [SEP]
