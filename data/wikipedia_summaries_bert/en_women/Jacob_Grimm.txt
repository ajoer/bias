Jacob Ludwig Karl Grimm (4 January 1785 – 20 September 1863), also known as Ludwig Karl, was a German philologist, jurist, and mythologist. [CLS] [SEP] He is known as the discoverer of Grimm's law of linguistics, the co-author of the monumental Deutsches Wörterbuch, the author of Deutsche Mythologie, and the editor of Grimm's Fairy Tales. [CLS] [SEP] He was the older brother of Wilhelm Grimm, of the literary duo the Brothers Grimm. [CLS] [SEP]
