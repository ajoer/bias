[CLS] The 2002 Kalehe earthquake occurred on October 24 at 06:08 UTC. [SEP] [CLS] This earthquake had a magnitude Mw 6.2, and the epicenter was located in Democratic Republic of the Congo, near Lake Kivu. [SEP] [CLS] Eight people were reported dead. [SEP] [CLS] Building damage was reported in Goma, Lwiro, Kalehe, and Mugeri. [SEP] [CLS] The seismicity, volcanism, and uplift in the basin of Lake Kivu delimits the rift of a tectonic plate. [SEP] [CLS] Lake Kivu belongs to the western branch of the East African Rift System (EARS). [SEP] [CLS] The western branch is usually divided into several segments, and Lake Kivu belongs to the northern segment. [SEP] [CLS]
