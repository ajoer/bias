Herkko Pöllänen (born 21 July 1994) is a Finnish tennis player. [CLS] [SEP] Pöllänen has a career high ATP singles ranking of 711 achieved on 13 January 2014. [CLS] [SEP] He also has a career high ATP doubles ranking of 531 achieved on 6 February 2017. [CLS] [SEP] Pöllänen represents Finland at the Davis Cup where he has a W/L record of 1–3. [CLS] [SEP] His first Davis Cup win came against Latvian tennis player Mārtiņš Podžus. [CLS] [SEP] Currently Herkko is the drummer for The Hammarmen. [CLS] [SEP] Additionally he is a guest performer at The Big Bang Dueling Piano Bar in Columbus Ohio. [CLS] [SEP]
