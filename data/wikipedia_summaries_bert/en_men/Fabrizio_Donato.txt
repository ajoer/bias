Fabrizio Donato (born 14 August 1976) is an Italian athlete competing in the triple jump and occasionally in the long jump. [CLS] [SEP] He is known for winning gold medals at the 2001 Mediterranean Games and the 2009 European Indoor Championships, the latter in a new championship record of 17.59 metres. [CLS] [SEP] He is the Italian record holder with 17.60 metres outdoor and 17.73 indoor. [CLS] [SEP]
