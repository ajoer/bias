[CLS] Florent Johan Malouda (French pronunciation: ​[flɔ.ʁɑ̃ ma.lu.da] ; born 13 June 1980) is a French football coach and former professional player. [SEP] [CLS] A left winger, he spent most of his career at Lyon and Chelsea, winning four consecutive Ligue 1 titles with the former and the UEFA Champions League in 2012 with the latter, among other honours. [SEP] [CLS] A France international from 2004 to 2012, Malouda represented Les Bleus on 80 occasions, including in two FIFA World Cups and two European Championships. [SEP] [CLS] He was part of the French team which reached the 2006 World Cup final. [SEP] [CLS] In 2017, he played for his home nation French Guiana. [SEP] [CLS]
