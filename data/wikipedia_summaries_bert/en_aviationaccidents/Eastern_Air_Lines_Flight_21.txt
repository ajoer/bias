Eastern Air Lines Flight 21, registration NC28394, was a Douglas DC-3 aircraft that crashed while preparing to land at Candler Field (now Hartsfield-Jackson Atlanta International Airport) in Atlanta, Georgia, on February 26, 1941. [CLS] [SEP] Eight of the 16 on board were killed including Maryland Congressman William D. [CLS] [SEP] Byron. [CLS] [SEP] Among the injured was Eastern Air Lines president and World War I hero Eddie Rickenbacker. [CLS] [SEP]
