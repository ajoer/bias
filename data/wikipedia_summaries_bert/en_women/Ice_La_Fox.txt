Snoop Dogg's Hustlaz: Diary of a Pimp is a mixed hardcore pornography and hip hop music video featuring the music of rapper Snoop Dogg, produced by Hustler Video. [CLS] [SEP] The video was also directed, co-produced and presented by Snoop, although he does not feature in any sex scenes. [CLS] [SEP] In the film's credits, Snoop is listed under the moniker "Snoop Scorsese". [CLS] [SEP] The film was released in 2002, a year after Snoop Dogg set the trend of mixed hip hop porn films with Snoop Dogg's Doggystyle.The film features Snoop hosting a party with more than 40 porn stars. [CLS] [SEP] Snoop plays the role of a pimp, who dresses in outlandish outfits, and persuades a prudish journalist to become one of his girls.
