Elbeg Nigülesügchi Khan (Mongolian: Элбэг нигүүлсэгч хаан; Chinese: 額勒伯克; 1361–1399) was a Mongol Khan of the Northern Yuan dynasty based in Mongolia. [CLS] [SEP] Elbeg was the younger brother of Jorightu Khan and ruled for seven years. [CLS] [SEP] His reigning title Nigülesügchi Khagan means "Merciful Emperor" in Mongolian language. [CLS] [SEP] Border skirmishes with Ming China and the Oirat rebellion plagued his reign. [CLS] [SEP]
