The Late November 1984 Nor'easter occurred on the United States' Thanksgiving Day, November 22, 1984. [CLS] [SEP] A deep extratropical cyclone located just off the shore of Florida led to significant beach erosion and the grounding of the freighter Mercedes I for several months along the coastal town of Palm Beach. [CLS] [SEP] It formed unusually early, and its effects were seen unusually far south into Florida. [CLS] [SEP]
