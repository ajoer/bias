On 10 November 1971, a Merpati Nusantara Airlines Vickers Viscount, registration PK-MVS, crashed in the Indian Ocean off the coast of Padang, Sumatra, Indonesia, after telling air traffic controllers they could not make their destination due to bad weather. [CLS] [SEP] All 69 people aboard the aircraft were killed in the crash. [CLS] [SEP] It remains the third worst Vickers Viscount accident. [CLS] [SEP]
