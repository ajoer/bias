On the morning of June 27, 2015, heavy rain occurred in a great portion of the Scouting reserve Philmont Scout Ranch, which is near Cimarron, New Mexico, United States, causing a flash flood. [CLS] [SEP] The flood also affected some other nearby areas in Colfax County that morning, including highways and small towns around Philmont. [CLS] [SEP] One youth Scout, Alden Brock, who was situated in a campsite within the staff camp Indian Writings, drowned while being swept away by the flood and died. [CLS] [SEP] The flood also had a significant impact on many individual crews and treks and significantly damaged some campsites. [CLS] [SEP] The 2015 flash flood is the largest documented flood in the history of Philmont, and is the only flood at Philmont that has ever caused the death of a person. [CLS] [SEP]
