[CLS] Samuel Stewart Witwer (born October 20, 1977) is an American actor, voice actor, and musician. [SEP] [CLS] He is known for playing Crashdown in Battlestar Galactica, Davis Bloome in Smallville, Aidan Waite in Being Human, Mr. [SEP] [CLS] Hyde in Once Upon a Time, and Ben Lockwood in Supergirl. [SEP] [CLS] He has voiced Starkiller in Star Wars: The Force Unleashed, the Son in Star Wars: The Clone Wars and Emperor Palpatine in Star Wars Rebels. [SEP] [CLS] He has voiced Darth Maul in The Clone Wars, Rebels and Solo: A Star Wars Story. [SEP] [CLS] In 2019, he joined the cast of Riverdale as Jughead Jones' school teacher Mr. [SEP] [CLS] Rupert Chipping. [SEP] [CLS] Witwer voiced protagonist Deacon St. [SEP] [CLS] John in the PlayStation 4 game Days Gone released in 2019 by SIE Bend Studio. [SEP] [CLS]
