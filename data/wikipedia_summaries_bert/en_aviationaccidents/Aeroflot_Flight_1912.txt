Aeroflot Flight 1912 (Russian: Рейс 1912 Аэрофлота Reys 1912 Aeroflota) was a scheduled domestic Aeroflot passenger flight on the Odessa-Kiev-Chelyabinsk-Novosibirsk-Irkutsk-Karbarovsk-Vladivostok route that crashed on 25 July 1971, making a hard landing at Irkutsk Airport. [CLS] [SEP] It touched down 150 m (490 ft) short of the runway, breaking the left wing and catching fire. [CLS] [SEP] Of the 126 people on board the aircraft, 29 survived. [CLS] [SEP]
