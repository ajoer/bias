[CLS] Lyubov Ivanovna Sadchikova (Russian: Любовь Ивановна Садчикова; 22 September 1951 – 22 November 2012) was a Soviet speed skater who won the all-round world championship in 1978. [SEP] [CLS] In 1975, she set a world record in 500 m. [SEP] [CLS] Next year, she finished sixth in the same event at the 1976 Winter Olympics.She won five national titles in 500 m (1975, 1978) and 1000 m (1974, 1976, 1977) and finished second four times, in all-round (1976, 1974, 1977) and 500 m (1973). [SEP] [CLS] In 1974 and 1976 she was third in 500 m. [SEP] [CLS] She graduated from an Institute of Physical Education in Samara.Personal bests: 500 m – 40.84 (1978) 1000 m – 1:23.80 (1976) 1500 m – 2:13.76 (1976) 3000 m – 5:02.50 (1976)
