[CLS] Maria Gładkowska (born September 16, 1957) is a Polish film, stage, and television actress. [SEP] [CLS] She gained critical acclaim in the 1980s that culminated in her Zbigniew Cybulski Award (1988). [SEP] [CLS] Gładkowska portrayed Daisy, Princess of Pless, her breakthrough performance in Magnat (1987), which was included in the list of 100 Best Polish Films of all time. [SEP] [CLS]
