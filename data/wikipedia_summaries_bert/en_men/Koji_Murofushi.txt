Koji Alexander Murofushi (広治アレクサンダー室伏, Kōji Arekusandā Murofushi, born October 8, 1974, in Numazu, Shizuoka Prefecture) is a former Japanese-Romanian hammer thrower and sports scientist. [CLS] [SEP] He has been among the world elite since the 2001 World Championships, where he won the silver medal. [CLS] [SEP] He was the 2004 Olympic champion. [CLS] [SEP] In 2011, he was crowned world champion. [CLS] [SEP]
