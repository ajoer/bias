Tracy Caldwell Dyson (born Tracy Ellen Caldwell; August 14, 1969) is an American chemist and NASA astronaut. [CLS] [SEP] Caldwell Dyson was a Mission Specialist on Space Shuttle Endeavour flight STS-118 in August 2007. [CLS] [SEP] She was part of the Expedition 23 and Expedition 24 crew on the International Space Station between April 4, 2010 and September 25, 2010. [CLS] [SEP] She has completed three spacewalks, logging more than 22 hrs of EVA including work to replace a malfunctioning coolant pump. [CLS] [SEP]
