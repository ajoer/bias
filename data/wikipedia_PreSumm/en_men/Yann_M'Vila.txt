[CLS] Yann Gérard M'Vila (French pronunciation: ​[jan əm.vi.la]; born 29 June 1990) is a French professional footballer who currently plays for French club Saint-Étienne. [SEP] [CLS] He operates primarily as a defensive midfielder, and is described by his former club as a player who possesses "excellent defensive abilities" and "impressive physical strength", but can also play as a box-to-box midfielder, due to his impressive work rates and stamina. [SEP] [CLS] He is the younger brother of Yohan M'Vila. [SEP] [CLS] M'Vila is a French international, having played at all levels for which he was eligible. [SEP] [CLS] He has captained several of his age groups including the under-19 team that reached the semi-finals of the 2009 UEFA European Under-19 Football Championship. [SEP] [CLS] M'Vila also played on France teams that participated in the 2007 UEFA European Under-17 Football Championship and the 2007 FIFA U-17 World Cup. [SEP] [CLS] He is currently playing for the senior team. [SEP] [CLS] M'Vila made his senior debut on 11 August 2010 in a friendly match against Norway, and was part of the French squad which reached the quarter-finals of UEFA Euro 2012. [SEP] [CLS]
