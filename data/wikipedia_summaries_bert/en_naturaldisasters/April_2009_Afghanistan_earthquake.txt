[CLS] The 2009 Afghanistan earthquake was a dip-slip doublet earthquake occurred in eastern Afghanistan, with an initial shock of magnitude of 5.2 Mw at 01:57:51 April 17 local time, with a second shock of 5.1 Mw occurring several hours later. [SEP] [CLS] The maximum Mercalli intensity was V (Moderate). [SEP] [CLS]
