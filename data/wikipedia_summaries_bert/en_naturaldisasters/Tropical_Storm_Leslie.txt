[CLS] The name Leslie has been used for three tropical cyclones in the Atlantic Ocean. [SEP] [CLS] Tropical Storm Leslie (2000), weak tropical storm that impacted Bermuda, Florida, Cuba, and Newfoundland. [SEP] [CLS] Hurricane Leslie (2012), long-lived Category 1 hurricane that caused minor damage in Bermuda and Newfoundland. [SEP] [CLS] Hurricane Leslie (2018), a long-lived tropical cyclone that constantly fluctuated between tropical storm and Category 1 hurricane intensity, made landfall in the Iberian Peninsula as an extratropical cyclone.The name Leslie has also been used in the Southwest Pacific Ocean: Cyclone Leslie (1979)
