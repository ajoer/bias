USMC R4Q Packet BuNo 131663 was one of twenty aircraft airlifting 1,600 Naval Reserve Officer Training Corps (NROTC) second-class (2/c) midshipmen between summer aviation training in Texas and amphibious warfare training in Virginia in July 1953. [CLS] [SEP] Shortly after midnight, the plane crashed and burned following a refueling stop in Florida. [CLS] [SEP]
