[CLS] Hebe Maria Monteiro de Camargo Ravagnani (Portuguese pronunciation: [ˈɛbi mɐˈɾi.ɐ mõˈtejɾu dʒi kɐˈmaɾɡu ˌʁavɐˈɲɐ̃ni]; March 8, 1929 – September 29, 2012) was a Brazilian television host, singer and actress. [SEP] [CLS] She is considered the "Queen of Brazilian Television" (Portuguese: Rainha da Televisão Brasileira). [SEP] [CLS] She died at her home on September 29, 2012. [SEP] [CLS] Her net worth was over US$360 million. [SEP] [CLS]
