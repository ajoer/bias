Edelgard Bulmahn (born 4 March 1951) is a German politician from the Social Democratic Party of Germany (SPD). [CLS] [SEP] She served as Member of the German Bundestag between 1987 and 2017. [CLS] [SEP] She was Federal Minister of Education and Research from 1998 to 2005. [CLS] [SEP] From 2013 until 2017 she was elected as one of the Vice Presidents of the Bundestag. [CLS] [SEP]
