[CLS] At 14:52 local time on 31 March 2002, an earthquake of magnitude 7.1 on the moment magnitude scale hit Taiwan. [SEP] [CLS] The epicenter was offshore from Hualien, which was the most severely affected area with a maximum felt intensity of VII (very strong) on the Mercalli intensity scale. [SEP] [CLS] At least 5 deaths have been reported, with a further 213 injured. [SEP] [CLS]
