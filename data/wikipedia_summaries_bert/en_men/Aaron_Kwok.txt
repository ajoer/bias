[CLS] Aaron Kwok Fu-shing (born 26 October 1965) is a Hong Kong singer, dancer and actor. [SEP] [CLS] Active since the 1980s, Kwok is one of the "Four Heavenly Kings" of Hong Kong. [SEP] [CLS] Kwok's onstage dancing is influenced by Michael Jackson. [SEP] [CLS] While most of his songs are in the dance-pop genre, he has experimented with rock and roll, ballad, rock, R&B, soul, electronica and traditional Chinese music.He received the "Ten Most Outstanding Young Persons Award" in 2003.
