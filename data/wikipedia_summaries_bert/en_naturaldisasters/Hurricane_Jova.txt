The name Jova has been used for six tropical cyclones in the Eastern Pacific Ocean: Hurricane Jova (1981) Hurricane Jova (1987) Hurricane Jova (1993) Hurricane Jova (2005), passed near Hawaii but did not affect land Hurricane Jova (2011), a Category 3 hurricane, made landfall in Mexico as a Category 2 hurricane Tropical Storm Jova (2017), did not affect land
