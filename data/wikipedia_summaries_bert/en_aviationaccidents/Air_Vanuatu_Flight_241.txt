On 28 July 2018, Air Vanuatu Flight 241, operated by ATR-72 registration YJ-AV71 suffered an in-flight engine fire while operating a domestic scheduled passenger flight from Whitegrass Airport, Tanna to Bauerfield International Airport, Port Vila, Vanuatu. [CLS] [SEP] On landing at Port Vila, a runway excursion occurred. [CLS] [SEP] The aircraft collided with two Britten-Norman Islanders, writing one off and severely damaging the other. [CLS] [SEP] Thirteen of the 43 people on board sustained minor injuries. [CLS] [SEP]
