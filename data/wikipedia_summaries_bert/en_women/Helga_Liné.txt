Helga Liné (pronounced [liˈne]; born Helga Lina Stern on 14 July 1932) is a German-born Portuguese-Spanish film actress and circus acrobat best known for her work in the horror genre of film. [CLS] [SEP] She made 132 appearances mostly in film between 1941 and 2006, but most of her work has been in Spanish cinema. [CLS] [SEP]
