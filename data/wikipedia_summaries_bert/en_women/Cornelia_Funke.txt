[CLS] Cornelia Maria Funke (German: [kɔʁˈneːli̯a ˈfʊŋ.kə] (listen)) is a German author of children's fiction. [SEP] [CLS] She was born on 10 December 1958 in Dorsten, North Rhine-Westphalia. [SEP] [CLS] Funke is best known for her Inkheart trilogy (originally in German: Tintenwelt-Trilogie), published in the United Kingdom between 2004–2008. [SEP] [CLS] Many of her books have now been translated into English. [SEP] [CLS] Her work fits mainly into the fantasy and adventure genres. [SEP] [CLS] She lives in Beverly Hills, California. [SEP] [CLS] Funke has sold over 20 million copies of her books worldwide. [SEP] [CLS]
