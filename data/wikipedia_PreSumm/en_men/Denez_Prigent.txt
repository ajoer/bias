[CLS] Denez Prigent (Breton pronunciation: [ˈdẽːnes ˈpriːʒẽn(t)]; born 17 February 1966 in Santec, Finistère) is a Breton folk singer-songwriter of the gwerz and kan ha diskan styles of Breton music. [SEP] [CLS] From his debut at the age of 16, he was known for singing traditional songs a cappella, and has moved on to singing his own songs with techno music accompaniments. [SEP] [CLS] He has performed in France as well as internationally and has recorded seven studio and two live albums. [SEP] [CLS]
