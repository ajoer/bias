On 31 October 2016, a modified DHC-4 Caribou transport aircraft operated by Alfa Indonesia crashed in the Papuan jungle while en route to Ilaga Airport in Ilaga, Indonesia. [CLS] [SEP] The flight was operated by Alfa Indonesia as a chartered cargo flight with four people on board. [CLS] [SEP] There were no survivors among the four crew members on board. [CLS] [SEP]
