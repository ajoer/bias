[CLS] Taisun Khan (or Toghtoa Bukha, Toγtoγa Buqa; Modern Mongolian: Taisun haan; Chinese: 脫脫不花) (1416–1453) was a Khagan of the Northern Yuan dynasty based in Mongolia. [SEP] [CLS] He was enthroned as Khagan of the Great Yuan. [SEP] [CLS] Under his nominal rule, the Oirats successfully reunited the Mongol tribes and threatened the Ming dynasty to their south for the first time since the Battle of Kherlen in 1409.
