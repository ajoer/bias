[CLS] [SEP] In Illinois he lived in Springfield and Peoria and eventually migrated to the lead-mining region of southern Wisconsin and established Hamilton's Diggings at present-day Wiota. [CLS] [SEP] Hamilton served in various political offices and as a commander in two Midwest Indian Wars. [CLS] [SEP] In 1849 he moved to California on the heels of the California Gold Rush. [CLS] [SEP] He died in Sacramento, most likely of cholera, in October 1850. [CLS] [SEP]
