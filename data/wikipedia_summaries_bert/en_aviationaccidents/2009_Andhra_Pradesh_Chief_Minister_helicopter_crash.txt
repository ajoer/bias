The 2009 Andhra Pradesh helicopter crash occurred on 2 September 2009 near Rudrakonda Hill, 40 nautical miles (74 km) from Kurnool, Andhra Pradesh, India. [CLS] [SEP] The helicopter was a Bell 430 helicopter owned by the Andhra Pradesh Government, and registered VT-APG. [CLS] [SEP] Fatalities included Y. [CLS] [SEP] S.
