[CLS] Yaroslav Vladimirovich Rybakov (Russian: Ярослав Владимирович Рыбаков, born November 22, 1980 in Mogilyev, Belarusian SSR) is a retired Russian high jumper. [SEP] [CLS] He is the 2002 European Champion high jumper, and at the 2005 World Championships he shared the silver medal with Victor Moya of Cuba. [SEP] [CLS] In 2006 he won the World Indoor Championships, and finished fifth in the high jump final at the 2006 European Athletics Championships in Gothenburg. [SEP] [CLS] The next year he won his third World Championships silver medal in a new personal best jump of 2.35 metres. [SEP] [CLS] In 2009, he finally won gold at the World Championships in Berlin. [SEP] [CLS] His indoor personal best is 2.38 metres, set in February 2005 in Stockholm. [SEP] [CLS] Since then he has equalled the mark three times, which has included a meet record for the Hochsprung mit Musik. [SEP] [CLS]
