The name Irving has been used for six tropical cyclones in the northwest Pacific Ocean. [CLS] [SEP] Typhoon Irving (1979) (T7910, 12W) – struck South Korea. [CLS] [SEP] Typhoon Irving (1982) (T8217, 18W, Ruping) – struck the southern tip of Luzon and China. [CLS] [SEP] Tropical Storm Irving (1985) (T8527, 26W) – approached southern Vietnam. [CLS] [SEP] Tropical Storm Irving (1989) (T8910, 10W) – struck northern Vietnam. [CLS] [SEP] Tropical Storm Irving (1992) (T9209, 09W) – struck Japan. [CLS] [SEP] Tropical Storm Irving (1995) (T9506, 09W, Diding) – struck China.The name Irving has also been used once in the Australian region. [CLS] [SEP] Cyclone Irving (2018)
