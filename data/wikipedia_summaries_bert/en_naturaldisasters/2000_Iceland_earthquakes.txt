The 2000 Iceland earthquakes struck southern Iceland on June 17 and 21. [CLS] [SEP] There were no fatalities but one person was injured and there was considerable damage to infrastructure. [CLS] [SEP] The two earthquakes were the first major seismic activity in Iceland for 88 years. [CLS] [SEP] The recorded magnitude of both of the main quakes was 6.5 Mwc. [CLS] [SEP]
