[CLS] The June 16, 2009 storm was an unusually strong derecho containing vivid lightning and spawning several tornadoes. [SEP] [CLS] The worst of the damage was primarily in South Central Kentucky. [SEP] [CLS] This large storm system would later be classified as a derecho by the National Weather Service. [SEP] [CLS] It was a fast moving storm system moving across Kentucky, west to east, damaging several counties with 90 mph straight line winds in some areas, with heavy rain. [SEP] [CLS] There were many warnings issued during the derecho including tornado warnings. [SEP] [CLS]
