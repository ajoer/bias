The 2015 Accra floods resulted from heavy continuous rainfall in Accra, the largest city in Ghana. [CLS] [SEP] The rain started on 1 June 2015. [CLS] [SEP] Other causes of this flood is as a result of the improper planning of settlement in Accra, choked gutters which block the drainage system and a few other human factors. [CLS] [SEP] The floods have resulted in heavy traffic on the roads in the city and also a halt in commercial activities as markets were flooded and workers trapped. [CLS] [SEP] Mayor of Accra Metropolitan Assembly, Alfred Oko Vanderpuije described the flooding as critical. [CLS] [SEP] At least 25 people have died from the flooding directly, while a petrol station explosion caused by the flooding killed at least 200 more people. [CLS] [SEP]
