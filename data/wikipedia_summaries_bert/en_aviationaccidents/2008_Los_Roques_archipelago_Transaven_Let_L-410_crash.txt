On 4 January 2008, a scheduled domestic Transaven flight from Simón Bolívar International Airport to Los Roques Airport, 118 kilometres (64 nmi) north of the departure airport and over water, radioed that both engines had failed and that they were descending through 3,000 feet. [CLS] [SEP] The crew was going to attempt a ditching as close as possible to the Los Roques archipelago. [CLS] [SEP] Shortly thereafter, radio contact was lost and the plane disappeared from radar.
