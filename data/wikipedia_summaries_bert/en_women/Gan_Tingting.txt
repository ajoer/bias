[CLS] Gan Tingting (Chinese: 甘婷婷; born 5 February 1986), also known as Bobo Gan, is a Chinese actress. [SEP] [CLS] Gan is noted for her performance in the Vietnam-China movie Hanoi, Hanoi, for which she won Best Actress award at the Golden Kite Awards. [SEP] [CLS] She is also known for the television series All Men Are Brothers (2011), New Treasure Raider (2016) and General and I (2017). [SEP] [CLS]
