[CLS] The flood of Doubs river was the biggest flooding in the history of the Doubs river (France); the water rose up to nearly 10 metres above the usual level, January 21, 1910. [SEP] [CLS] The principal departements affected were the département of Doubs, the département of Jura and also the département of Saône et Loire. [SEP] [CLS] At least one person was killed, and many others may have been killed because of this flood. [SEP] [CLS]
