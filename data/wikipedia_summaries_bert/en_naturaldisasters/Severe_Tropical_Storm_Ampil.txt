Severe Tropical Storm Ampil, known in the Philippines as Severe Tropical Storm Inday, was a tropical cyclone that caused moderate damage in the Ryukyu Islands and East China in late July 2018. [CLS] [SEP] The tenth named storm of the annual typhoon season, Ampil developed into a tropical depression east of Luzon on July 17. [CLS] [SEP] The storm gradually strengthened in the following days, and achieved its peak intensity late on July 19. [CLS] [SEP] Maintaining peak strength, Ampil passed through the Okinawa Island late on July 20. [CLS] [SEP] After that, Ampil began to slowly weaken, as environmental conditions became less favourable. [CLS] [SEP] Ampil made landfall in Shanghai on July 22, before becoming extratropical two days later. [CLS] [SEP]
