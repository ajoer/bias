Margareta Kozuch (Polish: Małgorzata Kożuch, born 30 October 1986) is a German female volleyball player who plays for Atom Trefl Sopot, and previously played as a wing-spiker for TuS Berne Hamburg, CVMJ Hamburg, TV Fischbek (who changed their name to NA Hamburg), and Unicom Starker Kerakoll Sassuolo. [CLS] [SEP] She was Hamburg's sportswoman of the year in 2005. [CLS] [SEP] She represented the German women's national volleyball team in the FIVB World Grand Prix 2009. [CLS] [SEP] She was German sportswoman of the year in 2010. [CLS] [SEP] She is of Polish origin. [CLS] [SEP]
