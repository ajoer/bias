On July 23, 1982, a Bell UH-1 Iroquois helicopter crashed at Indian Dunes in Valencia, Santa Clarita, California, during the making of Twilight Zone: The Movie. [CLS] [SEP] The crash killed three people on the ground (actor Vic Morrow and child actors Myca Dinh Le and Renee Shin-Yi Chen) and injured the six helicopter passengers. [CLS] [SEP] The incident led to years of civil and criminal action and was responsible for the introduction of new procedures and safety standards in the filmmaking industry. [CLS] [SEP]
