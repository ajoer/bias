[CLS] Avia Traffic Company Flight 768 was a scheduled passenger flight from Bishkek to Osh, Kyrgyzstan. [SEP] [CLS] On 22 November 2015, the Boeing 737-300 operating the flight was on final approach to Osh it touched down hard enough to shear off the left and right main landing gear. [SEP] [CLS] The aircraft skidded off the runway with the left engine being torn from its mount. [SEP] [CLS] There were no fatalities in the accident, but 14 people were injured. [SEP] [CLS]
