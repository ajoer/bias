[SEP] [CLS] She was born as Susanne Emilie Luise Adele Habersack in Munich, but spent most of her childhood with her parents in Amsterdam. [SEP] [CLS] Back in Munich, she made her stage debut at age 13. [SEP] [CLS] Two years later she became a ballerina. [SEP] [CLS] In the early 1930s she turned to cabaret. [SEP] [CLS] In 1939, she was offered her first film role. [SEP] [CLS] In 1940 she moved to Vienna, where she became a member of the Burgtheater. [SEP] [CLS] After her retirement in 1992 she continued her stage career at the Theater in der Josefstadt. [SEP] [CLS] For decades, Nicoletti taught acting and dance at the prestigious Max Reinhardt Seminar in Vienna. [SEP] [CLS] Her husband, Ernst Haeussermann, was a theatre director. [SEP] [CLS]
