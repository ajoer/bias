The 1974 Lima earthquake occurred on October 3 at 14:21 UTC (09:21 local time). [CLS] [SEP] It was located at about 80 km southwest of Lima, Peru. [CLS] [SEP] The magnitude of the earthquake was put at 8.1 Mw, or Ms 7.8. [CLS] [SEP] The earthquake caused 78 deaths and about 2400 injured. [CLS] [SEP] The intensity around Lima was generally about MM V to VII, but the maximum reached IX. [CLS] [SEP] Two buildings of reinforced concrete collapsed in La Molina, where the intensity was MM VIII to IX. [CLS] [SEP] A four-story reinforced concrete building in Callao collapsed.The largest aftershock occurred on November 9, 1974, at 12:59 UTC (07:59 local time) with a magnitude of Mw 7.1, or Ms 7.1. [CLS] [SEP] The November 9 aftershock was located at about 25 km south of the main shock. [CLS] [SEP]
