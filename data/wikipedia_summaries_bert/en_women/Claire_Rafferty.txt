Claire Lauren Rafferty (born 11 January 1989) is an English pundit and retired professional footballer. [CLS] [SEP] She made over 100 appearances as a left back or left winger for Chelsea in the FA WSL and also spent time at Millwall Lionesses and West Ham United. [CLS] [SEP] Rafferty also represented England internationally and played at the 2012 Olympics for Great Britain. [CLS] [SEP]
