Vanessa Anneliese Petruo (born 23 October 1979) is a German psychotherapist and former singer-songwriter, actress and television personality. [CLS] [SEP] Born and raised in Berlin within a family of actors, she had minor roles in television and as a voice actress before she rose to fame as one of the members of the girl group No Angels, which were formed in 2000 on the German television talent show Popstars, becoming one of the best-selling girl groups of European origin of all time. [CLS] [SEP] Throughout her musical career, Petruo scored four number-one hits, including "Something About Us", which she co-wrote, and sold over five million albums and singles worldwide.Following her departure, Petruo scored a top twenty hit with her solo debut single "Drama Queen" and released her only solo album, Mama Lilla Would (2005) to major critical but minor commercial success. [CLS] [SEP] In addition, she lent her voice to several animation films such as Dr. [CLS] [SEP] Dolittle 2 (2001), Lilo & Stitch (2002), and Boo, Zino & the Snurks (2004) and starred in the RTL action series Wilde Engel as well as the comedy film Where Is Fred? [CLS] [SEP] (2006). [CLS] [SEP] While Petruo continued working as a songwriter throughout the late 2000s, further musical projects failed to materialize in 2010, she retired from the recording business in favor of an academic career.
