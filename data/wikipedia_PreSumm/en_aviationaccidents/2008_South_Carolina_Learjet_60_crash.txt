[CLS] On the night of September 19, 2008, a Learjet 60 business jet (registration N999LJ) crashed during take-off from Columbia Metropolitan Airport in South Carolina. [SEP] [CLS] The aircraft overran the runway end, crashed through the airport boundary fence, crossed South Carolina Highway 302, and came to rest onto an embankment, bursting into flames. [SEP] [CLS] Four of the six people on board died in the crash. [SEP] [CLS] The survivors, musician Travis Barker and disc jockey Adam "DJ AM" Goldstein, were critically injured. [SEP] [CLS] The jet had been due to fly Barker, Goldstein, and their entourage to Van Nuys, California, after their TRV$DJAM band's performance at a concert in Five Points. [SEP] [CLS]
