Martha Greenblatt is a chemist, researcher, and faculty member at Rutgers University, New Brunswick, New Jersey. [CLS] [SEP] As of January 2008 she was the only female chair of a science department in the School of Arts and Science. [CLS] [SEP] Greenblatt took the position of Chair of the Chemistry Department at Rutgers while pursuing research interests in solid state inorganic chemistry. [CLS] [SEP] She was also the recipient of the 2003 American Chemical Society’s Garvan-Olin Medal – a national award given yearly to an outstanding woman chemist. [CLS] [SEP] In 2004, she became Board of Governors Professor of Chemistry at Rutgers. [CLS] [SEP]
