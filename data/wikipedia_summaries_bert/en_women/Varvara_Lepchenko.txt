[CLS] Varvara Petrivna Lepchenko (Ukrainian: Варвара Петрiвна Лепченко; born May 21, 1986) is an American professional tennis player. [SEP] [CLS] She formerly played for Uzbekistan, and she is of Ukrainian descent. [SEP] [CLS] She has won 12 ITF singles titles and one ITF doubles title. [SEP] [CLS]
