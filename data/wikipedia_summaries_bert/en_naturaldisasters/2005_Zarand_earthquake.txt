The 2005 Zarand earthquake affected several villages in the Kerman province of Iran on February 22 at 05:55:23 local time. [CLS] [SEP] The shock measured 6.4 on the moment magnitude scale and had a maximum Mercalli intensity of VIII (Severe). [CLS] [SEP] Zarand is located 740 km southeast of Tehran. [CLS] [SEP] The maximum recorded peak ground acceleration was 0.51 g at Shirinrud dam. [CLS] [SEP] The United States' National Earthquake Information Center and the Belgian' Centre for Research on the Epidemiology of Disasters both show that 612 died and 1,411 were injured in the event.
