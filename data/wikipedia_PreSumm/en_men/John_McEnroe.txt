[CLS] John Patrick McEnroe Jr. [SEP] [CLS] (born February 16, 1959) is an American tennis player. [SEP] [CLS] He was known for his shot-making artistry and volleying skills, and for confrontational on-court behavior that frequently landed him in trouble with umpires and tennis authorities.
