The Chatsbury/Bungonia bushfires of 1965 was a series of bushfires that burned from 5 to 14 March 1965 in the Southern Highlands region of New South Wales, Australia. [CLS] [SEP] The fires destroyed the villages of Tallong, Wingello, and most of the surrounding orchards.The fire is thought to be ignited by a spark from a Chatsbury Station angle grinder. [CLS] [SEP] It quickly spread and covered 250,000 hectares (620,000 acres) and destroyed the areas' livestock. [CLS] [SEP] It is thought to be the greatest disaster in the area.Three people were killed. [CLS] [SEP] 28 homes were destroyed in Tallong, 31 in Wingello. [CLS] [SEP] The fire was eventually stopped (or burnt out) near Nowra on the NSW South Coast. [CLS] [SEP]
