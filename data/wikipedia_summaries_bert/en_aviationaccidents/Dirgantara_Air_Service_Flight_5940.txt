[CLS] Dirgantara Air Service Flight 5940 was a domestic scheduled passenger flight, operated by military-owned airline Dirgantara Air Service, from South Kalimantan's provincial capital of Banjarmasin to Sampit, the capital of the East Kotawaringin Regency. [SEP] [CLS] On 7 December 1996, a CASA C-212 Aviocar registered as PK-VSO crashed onto a gas factory shortly after take off from Syamsudin Noor International Airport. [SEP] [CLS] 17 people, including one on the ground, died at the scene, 12-year-old child died on the way to the hospital while a worker died at the hospital. [SEP] [CLS] One passenger, identified as 40-year-old Irianto, was the only one who survived the crash. [SEP] [CLS] Official investigation determined that the crew lost control of the plane after its right engine malfunctioned. [SEP] [CLS]
