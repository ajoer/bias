[SEP] [CLS] Hamilton was born in New York, where he attended the United States Military Academy before he resigned and moved to Illinois in 1817. [SEP] [CLS] In Illinois he lived in Springfield and Peoria and eventually migrated to the lead-mining region of southern Wisconsin and established Hamilton's Diggings at present-day Wiota. [SEP] [CLS] Hamilton served in various political offices and as a commander in two Midwest Indian Wars. [SEP] [CLS] In 1849 he moved to California on the heels of the California Gold Rush. [SEP] [CLS] He died in Sacramento, most likely of cholera, in October 1850. [SEP] [CLS]
