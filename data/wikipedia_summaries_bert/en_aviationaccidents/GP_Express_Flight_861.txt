GP Express Flight 861, from Hartsfield-Jackson International Airport in Atlanta, Georgia to Anniston Metropolitan Airport in Anniston, Alabama, crashed while attempting to land at approximately 8:04 a.m. [CLS] [SEP] CDT on June 8, 1992. [CLS] [SEP] The Beechcraft Model 99 had four passengers and a crew of two on board. [CLS] [SEP] Two passengers and the captain received fatal injuries. [CLS] [SEP] All three survivors were seriously injured. [CLS] [SEP]
