[CLS] Cubana de Aviación Flight 9046 was a chartered Ilyushin Il-62M airliner (registered CU-T1281) operated by Cubana, which crashed on 3 September 1989, shortly after takeoff from José Martí International Airport.Flight 9046 was due to operate a non-scheduled international Havana–Milan–Cologne passenger service. [SEP] [CLS] The crash resulted in the deaths of all 126 occupants of the aircraft plus 24 people on the ground. [SEP] [CLS]
