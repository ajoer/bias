[CLS] China Airlines Flight 206 was a flight operated by a China Airlines NAMC YS-11, registration B-156, that crashed on approach to Taipei Songshan Airport on 12 August 1970. [SEP] [CLS] While preparing to land, the aircraft entered thick fog and a severe thunderstorm. [SEP] [CLS] Whilst on final approach the plane crashed into a bamboo grove near the top of Yuan Mountain, killing 14 of the 31 people aboard. [SEP] [CLS]
