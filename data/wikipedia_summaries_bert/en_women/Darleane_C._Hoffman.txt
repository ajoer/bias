Darleane Christian Hoffman (born November 8, 1926) is an American nuclear chemist who was among the researchers who confirmed the existence of Seaborgium, element 106. [CLS] [SEP] She is a faculty senior scientist in the Nuclear Science Division of Lawrence Berkeley National Laboratory and a professor in the graduate school at UC Berkeley. [CLS] [SEP] In acknowledgment of her many achievements, Discover Magazine recognized her in 2002 as one of the 50 most important women in science. [CLS] [SEP]
