Iberia Airlines Flight 610 was a scheduled domestic passenger flight from Madrid to Bilbao, Spain. [CLS] [SEP] On 19 February 1985, a Boeing 727-200 operating the flight crashed into a television antenna on the summit of Mount Oiz in Biscay near Bilbao. [CLS] [SEP] All 141 passengers and 7 crew on board died. [CLS] [SEP] The crash is the deadliest aviation disaster in both Basque Country and Iberia history. [CLS] [SEP]
