On 23 August 2013, a Eurocopter AS332 Super Puma helicopter belonging to CHC Helicopters crashed into the sea 2 nautical miles (3.7 km; 2.3 mi) from Sumburgh in the Shetland Islands, Scotland, while en route from the Borgsten Dolphin oil platform. [CLS] [SEP] The accident killed four passengers; twelve other passengers and two crew were rescued with injuries. [CLS] [SEP] An investigation by the UK's Air Accident Investigation Branch is ongoing. [CLS] [SEP]
