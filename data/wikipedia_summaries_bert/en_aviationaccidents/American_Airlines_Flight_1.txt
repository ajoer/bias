American Airlines Flight 1 was a domestic, scheduled passenger flight from New York International (Idlewild) Airport (now John F. [CLS] [SEP] Kennedy International Airport) to Los Angeles International Airport. [CLS] [SEP] On March 1, 1962, the Boeing 707 rolled over and crashed into Jamaica Bay two minutes after takeoff, killing all 87 passengers and eight crew members aboard. [CLS] [SEP] A Civil Aeronautics Board (CAB) investigation determined that a manufacturing defect in the autopilot system led to an uncommanded rudder control system input, causing the accident. [CLS] [SEP] A number of notable people died in the crash. [CLS] [SEP] It was the fifth fatal Boeing 707 accident and, at the time, the deadliest. [CLS] [SEP]
