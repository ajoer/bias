[CLS] British European Airways Flight 142 (callsign Beeline 142) was a scheduled service between London Heathrow Airport and Naples Airport. [SEP] [CLS] On 22 October 1958 it was flown by a Vickers Viscount 701, registered G-ANHC, and named "Sir Leopold McClintock" During its flight, the Viscount collided with an Italian Air Force North American F-86E Sabre over Anzio, Italy.
