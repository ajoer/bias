The Florida Commuter Airlines crash occurred on September 12, 1980, when Flight 65 operating from West Palm Beach, Florida to Freeport, Bahamas, crashed into the Atlantic Ocean near West End Settlement on Grand Bahama Island. [CLS] [SEP] The Douglas DC-3A used on the flight was not recovered and all 34 persons on board were killed.Investigators were unable to determine the cause of the crash, but determined that factors likely contributing to the crash included flight into a thunderstorm, unreliable instruments due to discrepancies in the pitot-static system, and lack of proper operational oversight by airline management.
