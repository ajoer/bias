[CLS] Yeti Airlines Pvt. [SEP] [CLS] Ltd. [SEP] [CLS] (Nepali: यती एअरलाइन्स) is an airline based in Kathmandu, Nepal. [SEP] [CLS] The airline was established in May 1998 and received its Air Operators Certificate on 17 August 1998. [SEP] [CLS] Since 2019, Yeti Airlines is the first carbon neutral airline in Nepal and South Asia. [SEP] [CLS] It is the parent company of Tara Air. [SEP] [CLS] As of 2018, Yeti Airlines was the second largest domestic carrier in Nepal, after Buddha Air, with a market share of 23.5%. [SEP] [CLS]
