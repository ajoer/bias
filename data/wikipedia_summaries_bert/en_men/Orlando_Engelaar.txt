Orlando Wensley Engelaar (Dutch pronunciation: [ɔr.ˈlɑn.doː ˈɛŋə.ˌlaːr]) (born 24 August 1979 in Rotterdam) is a Dutch retired footballer. [CLS] [SEP] Engelaar usually played as a midfielder, and is well known for his height. [CLS] [SEP] At 1.96 m tall, he is a towering presence on the pitch. [CLS] [SEP]
