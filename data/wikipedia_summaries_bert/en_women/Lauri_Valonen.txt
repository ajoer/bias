Lauri Valonen (November 28, 1909 – October 2, 1982) was a Finnish nordic combined skier who competed in the 1930s. [CLS] [SEP] He won a silver medal in the individual event at the 1935 FIS Nordic World Ski Championships in Vysoké Tatry. [CLS] [SEP] Valonen also finished 4th in the individual event at the 1936 Winter Olympics in Garmisch-Partenkirchen. [CLS] [SEP]
