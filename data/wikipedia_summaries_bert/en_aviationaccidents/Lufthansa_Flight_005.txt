[CLS] Lufthansa Flight 005 was a scheduled flight en route from Frankfurt to Hamburg with a stopover in Bremen. [SEP] [CLS] The aircraft crashed just beyond the runway in Bremen just before 19:00 on January 28, 1966, in a go-around after an aborted landing. [SEP] [CLS] All occupants — 42 passengers and 4 crew members — died in the accident. [SEP] [CLS]
