[CLS] Pennsylvania Central Airlines Flight 410 was a scheduled flight from Chicago, Illinois, to Norfolk, Virginia, with intermediate stops in Cleveland, Pittsburgh, and Washington, D.C. [SEP] [CLS] On June 13, 1947, the aircraft serving the flight, a Douglas DC-4, crashed into Lookout Rock in the West Virginia Blue Ridge Mountains, while en route to Washington. [SEP] [CLS] All 50 passengers and crew on board were killed in what was at the time the second-worst airplane accident in the history of U.S. [SEP] [CLS] domestic air travel. [SEP] [CLS]
