Austregésilo de Athayde (September 25, 1898 – September 13, 1993, age 94) was a writer and journalist born in Caruaru, Pernambuco, Brazil. [CLS] [SEP] His career includes being invited by Assis Chateaubriand to work at a top position at the Diários Associados. [CLS] [SEP] Later he became an emblematic figure for the Academia Brasileira de Letras as he served as President of the organization for 34 years. [CLS] [SEP] He was active in human rights causes in Brazil. [CLS] [SEP] Austregésilo de Athayde died on September 13, 1993 in Rio de Janeiro at age 94. [CLS] [SEP]
