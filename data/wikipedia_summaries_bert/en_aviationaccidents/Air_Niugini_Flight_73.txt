Air Niugini Flight 73 was a scheduled service from Pohnpei, Federated States of Micronesia (FSM) to Port Moresby, Papua New Guinea, via Chuuk, FSM. [CLS] [SEP] On September 28, 2018, the flight, operated by a Boeing 737, landed short of the runway at Chuuk International Airport in Weno (FSM) and came to rest in Chuuk Lagoon. [CLS] [SEP] Locals in small boats rescued most passengers and all crew members. [CLS] [SEP] One passenger was initially declared missing. [CLS] [SEP] He was later found dead by rescue divers. [CLS] [SEP] Forty-six people survived but six of them were injured. [CLS] [SEP]
