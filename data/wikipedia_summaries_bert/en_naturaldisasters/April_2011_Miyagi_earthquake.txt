The April 2011 Miyagi earthquake (Japanese: "2011 Miyagi Prefecture earthquake" (2011年 宮城県沖地震, 2011-Nen Miyagi-ken-oki jishin)) occurred off the coast of Miyagi Prefecture, approximately 66 kilometres (41 mi) east of Sendai, Japan. [CLS] [SEP] The Mw7.1 thrust earthquake was classified as an aftershock of the March 11 Tōhoku earthquake, and occurred at 23:32 JST (14:32 UTC) on Thursday, 7 April 2011. [CLS] [SEP] Several tsunami warnings were issued for the northeastern coast of Honshu; however, they were all canceled 90 minutes later. [CLS] [SEP] Over 3 million households in the area were left without power, and several nuclear plants suffered minor inconveniences. [CLS] [SEP] There was no major structural damage, but the quake killed at least 4 people and injured 141. [CLS] [SEP]
