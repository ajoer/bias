Stefaan Maria Joris Yolanda De Clerck (born 12 December 1951) is a Belgian politician and former Minister of Justice of Belgium. [CLS] [SEP] He was Minister of Justice from 1995 until 1998 as well, when he resigned following the escape from prison of Marc Dutroux. [CLS] [SEP] He has served as chairman of Christian Democratic and Flemish party and held a seat in the Belgian Chamber of Representatives. [CLS] [SEP] De Clerck was mayor of Kortrijk. [CLS] [SEP] Regarding re-instituting the death penalty in Belgium after the "Joker" murders of 2009, GlobalPost quotes De Clerck as saying: "That is not something for our times. [CLS] [SEP] It's not by killing somebody that we solve society's problems, just look at the United States."
