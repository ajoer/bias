Mutaz Essa Barshim (Arabic: معتز عيسى برشم‎; born 24 June 1991) is a Qatari athlete who is specialized in high jump. [CLS] [SEP] He is the national record and Asian record holder with a best mark of 2.43 m (7 ft 11 1⁄2 in). [CLS] [SEP] He won a gold medal at the 2017 World Championships in Athletics in London and at the 2019 World Championships in Athletics in Doha. [CLS] [SEP] Barshim also won bronze at the London 2012 Summer Olympics, and silver at the 2016 Summer Olympics in Rio. [CLS] [SEP] He was earlier the Asian Indoor and World Junior champion in 2010, and won the high jump gold medals at the 2011 Asian Athletics Championships and 2011 Military World Games. [CLS] [SEP] Mutaz jumps off his left foot, using the Fosbury Flop technique, with a pronounced backwards arch over the bar. [CLS] [SEP] One of his brothers, Muamer, is also a high jumper.
