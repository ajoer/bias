Katja Woywood (born 10 May 1971 in West Berlin, West Germany) is a German actress and former child star in her native land. [CLS] [SEP] She married Marco Girnth in August 1998 and they have one son. [CLS] [SEP] She started playing in movies and series as a 15 year old girl. [CLS] [SEP] Her hobbies are drawing and roller skating. [CLS] [SEP] Since episode 189 she plays the new chief of the highway police in the German action-series Alarm für Cobra 11 – Die Autobahnpolizei. [CLS] [SEP]
