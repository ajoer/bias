Antonina Makarova (née Parfenova, Ginsburg by marriage, Russian: Антонина Макарова, 1920 – 11 August 1979) was a Soviet war criminal and executioner who collaborated with Nazi Germany during World War II. [CLS] [SEP] From 1942 to 1943 she executed some 1,500 Soviet partisans and their family members by machine gun. [CLS] [SEP] Makarova was caught by the Soviet KGB in 1976 and executed three years later. [CLS] [SEP]
