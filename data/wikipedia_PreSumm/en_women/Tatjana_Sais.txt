[CLS] Tatjana Sais (28 January 1910 – 26 February 1981) was a German film actress. [SEP] [CLS] She appeared in 20 films between 1937 and 1967 and was a member of the jury at the 1st Berlin International Film Festival. [SEP] [CLS] She was married to Günter Neumann and later to Hugh Greene. [SEP] [CLS]
