Fereydun Robert Armisen (born December 4, 1966) is an American actor, comedian, writer, producer and musician best known as a cast member on Saturday Night Live from 2002 until 2013. [CLS] [SEP] Armisen has portrayed characters in comedy films, including EuroTrip, Anchorman and Cop Out. [CLS] [SEP] With his comedy partner Carrie Brownstein, Armisen was the co-creator and co-star of the IFC sketch comedy series Portlandia. [CLS] [SEP] Armisen founded ThunderAnt.com, a website that features the comedy sketches created with Brownstein, and is the bandleader and frequent drummer for the Late Night with Seth Meyers house band, The 8G Band. [CLS] [SEP] For his work on Portlandia, Armisen was nominated for an Emmy Award for Outstanding Writing for a Variety Series in 2012, 2013 and 2014 and for Outstanding Supporting Actor in a Comedy Series in 2014. [CLS] [SEP] He has also won two Peabody Awards, one in 2008 as part of the Saturday Night Live political satire cast and one in 2011 for Portlandia. [CLS] [SEP] Since 2019, he co-stars and acts as writer and executive producer on the Spanish-language series Los Espookys, which he co-created. [CLS] [SEP]
