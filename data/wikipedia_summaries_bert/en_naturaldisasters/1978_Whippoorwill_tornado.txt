The 1978 Whippoorwill tornado also known as the Whippoorwill Disaster was a tornado that struck on June 17, 1978. [CLS] [SEP] The tornado, which was on the ground for 8 miles (13 km), struck a tourist boat called the Whippoorwill, causing it to capsize and drowning 16 out of the 58 passengers and crew. [CLS] [SEP] In the United States, the 1978 Whippoorwill tornado is the deadliest F1 rated tornado on the Fujita Scale. [CLS] [SEP]
