[CLS] Avianca Flight 203 was a Colombian domestic passenger flight from El Dorado International Airport in Bogotá to Alfonso Bonilla Aragón International Airport in Cali, Colombia. [SEP] [CLS] It was destroyed by a bomb over the municipality of Soacha on November 27, 1989. [SEP] [CLS] All 107 people on board as well as 3 people on the ground were killed. [SEP] [CLS] The bombing had been ordered by the Medellín drug cartel. [SEP] [CLS]
