[CLS] Todd Russell Platts (born March 5, 1962) is an American attorney and Republican Party politician who serves as a Judge on the York County Court of Common Pleas and is a former U.S. [SEP] [CLS] Representative for Pennsylvania's 19th congressional district, serving from 2001 to 2013. [SEP] [CLS] The district, since redrawn and renumbered, encompassed south-central Pennsylvania, including all of York and Adams Counties, and a large portion of Cumberland County. [SEP] [CLS] York, Hanover, Gettysburg and Carlisle were some of the prominent cities and towns included. [SEP] [CLS] In January 2012, Platts announced his intention to retire from Congress.
