Louise Ebert (23 December 1873 in Melchiorshausen/Weyhe as Louise Rump, – 18 January 1955 in Heidelberg) on 9 May 1894 in Bremen married Friedrich Ebert, who from his election in 1919 until his death on 28 February 1925 served as the first Reichspräsident of the Weimar Republic. [CLS] [SEP] The couple had four recorded sons and one recorded daughter. [CLS] [SEP] The eldest son, named Friedrich like his father, served as mayor of East Berlin between 1948 and 1967.
