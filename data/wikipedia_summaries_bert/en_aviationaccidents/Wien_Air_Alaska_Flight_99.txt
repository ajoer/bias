Wien Air Alaska Flight 99 was a scheduled domestic passenger flight in Alaska to St. [CLS] [SEP] Lawrence Island in the Bering Sea. [CLS] [SEP] On approach to Gambell on 30 August 1975, it crashed into Sevuokuk Mountain, east of the airport's runway. [CLS] [SEP] Of the 32 on board, ten were killed, including the pilot and co-pilot. [CLS] [SEP] The Fairchild F-27B aircraft was operated by Wien Air Alaska. [CLS] [SEP]
