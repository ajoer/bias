Sahra Wagenknecht (German pronunciation: [ˌzaːʁaː ˈvaːɡn̩ˌknɛçt]; born 16 July 1969) is a German left-wing politician, economist, author and publicist. [CLS] [SEP] Along with Dietmar Bartsch, she was the parliamentary chairperson of Die Linke from 2010 to 2019. [CLS] [SEP] Since 2009, she has been a member of the Bundestag. [CLS] [SEP]
