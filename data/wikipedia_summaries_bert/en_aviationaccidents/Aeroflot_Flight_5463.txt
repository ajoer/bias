Aeroflot Flight 5463 was a Soviet domestic passenger flight from Chelyabinsk to Almaty which crashed on 30 August 1983 while approaching Almaty. [CLS] [SEP] The Tupolev Tu-134A collided with the western slope of Dolan Mountain at an altitude of 690 m (2,260 ft). [CLS] [SEP] As a result of the accident, all ninety people on board were killed. [CLS] [SEP] Crew error was cited as the cause of the accident. [CLS] [SEP]
