Rodger Bumpass (born November 20, 1951) is an American actor and voice actor. [CLS] [SEP] He is known for his long-running role as Squidward Tentacles on the American animated television series SpongeBob SquarePants. [CLS] [SEP] He voices several other characters on the show as well, including the Purple Doctor Fish and various anchovies. [CLS] [SEP] He also voiced The Chief in the animated series Where on Earth Is Carmen Sandiego?, and Mr. [CLS] [SEP] Besser, the school principal in the animated series The Kids from Room 402. [CLS] [SEP] Bumpass has many other credits in animated films, animated television series, and video games. [CLS] [SEP]
