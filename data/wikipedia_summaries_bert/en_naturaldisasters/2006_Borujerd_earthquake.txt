The 2006 Borujerd earthquake occurred in the early morning of 31 March in the South of Borujerd with destruction in Borujerd, Silakhor and Dorood areas of the Loristan Province in western Iran. [CLS] [SEP] The centre of the earthquake was in Darb-e Astaneh village south of the Borujerd City. [CLS] [SEP] The earthquake measured 6.1 on the moment magnitude scale. [CLS] [SEP]
