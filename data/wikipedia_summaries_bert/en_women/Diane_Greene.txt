Diane B. [CLS] [SEP] Greene (born June 9, 1955) is an American technology entrepreneur and executive. [CLS] [SEP] Greene started her career as a naval architect before transitioning to the tech industry, where she was a founder and the CEO of VMware from 1998 until 2008. [CLS] [SEP] She later served on the board of Alphabet and as the CEO of Google Cloud from 2015 until early 2019. [CLS] [SEP] She also co-founded and was CEO of two startups, Bebop and VXtreme, which were acquired by Google and Microsoft, respectively. [CLS] [SEP]
