[CLS] China Airlines Flight 120 was a regularly scheduled flight from Taiwan Taoyuan International Airport in Taoyuan County (now Taoyuan City), Taiwan to Naha Airport in Okinawa, Japan. [SEP] [CLS] On August 20, 2007, the Boeing 737-800 aircraft operating the flight caught fire and exploded after landing and taxiing to the gate area at Naha Airport. [SEP] [CLS] Four people—three from the aircraft and one ground crew—sustained injuries in the accident. [SEP] [CLS]
