[CLS] "JMM" is the seventh episode of the fifth season of the AMC television series Better Call Saul, the spinoff series of Breaking Bad. [SEP] [CLS] The episode aired on March 30, 2020 on AMC in the United States. [SEP] [CLS] Outside of the United States, the episode premiered on streaming service Netflix in several countries.
