Asbel Kiprop (born 30 June 1989) is a Kenyan middle-distance runner, who specialises in the 1500 metres. [CLS] [SEP] He was awarded the 1500 m gold medal at the 2008 Summer Olympics after the original winner, Rashid Ramzi, tested positive for doping. [CLS] [SEP] Kiprop has won three World Championship titles in the event, in 2011, 2013 and 2015. [CLS] [SEP] Kiprop failed his own doping test in November 2017 and received a four-year doping ban.He won his first major title at the 2007 All-Africa Games, taking the 1500 m gold medal, and also won the event at the 2010 African Championships in Athletics, improving upon a bronze medal performance from 2008. [CLS] [SEP] His personal best for the distance is 3:26.69. [CLS] [SEP]
