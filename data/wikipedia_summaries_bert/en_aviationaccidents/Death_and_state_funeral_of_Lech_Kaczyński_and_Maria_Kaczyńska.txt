Lech Kaczyński, the fourth President of the Republic of Poland, died on 10 April 2010, after a Polish Air Force Tu-154 crashed outside of Smolensk, Russia, killing all 96 aboard. [CLS] [SEP] His wife, economist and First Lady Maria Kaczyńska, was also among those killed. [CLS] [SEP] After the death of Kaczyński was announced, a week of mourning was declared by the acting President of Poland, Bronisław Komorowski, spanning 11 to 18 April with a state funeral for the couple held on 18 April. [CLS] [SEP] Several countries observed a day of national mourning on the date of the funeral. [CLS] [SEP] The couple was buried together in a crypt in the Wawel Cathedral, Kraków, afterwards.
