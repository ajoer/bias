[CLS] On 27 April 1974, an Aeroflot Il-18 airliner crashed while operating a charter flight from Leningrad (now St. [SEP] [CLS] Petersburg) to Zaporizhia, continuing to Krasnodar, Russia. [SEP] [CLS] The plane crashed shortly after takeoff from Pulkovo Airport in Leningrad. [SEP] [CLS] None of the 109 people on board survived. [SEP] [CLS]
