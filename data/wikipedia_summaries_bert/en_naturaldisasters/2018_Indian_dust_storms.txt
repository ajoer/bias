From 2 to 3 May 2018, high-velocity dust storms swept across parts of North India and more than 125 people died and over 200 were injured. [CLS] [SEP] In Uttar Pradesh, 43 died in the city of Agra and about 30 died elsewhere in the state. [CLS] [SEP] In neighboring Rajasthan, about 35 people died and over 200 were injured after winds downed more than 8,000 electricity poles and uprooted hundreds of trees. [CLS] [SEP] Storms are not uncommon in the region; however, because these storms occurred at night and with greater wind speeds than average, the death toll was higher than usual. [CLS] [SEP]
