[CLS] Aeroflot Flight 99 was a Tupolev Tu-124 operating a scheduled domestic passenger flight from Leningrad to Murmansk, both in the Soviet Union, which crashed while attempting to land on 11 November 1965. [SEP] [CLS] Of the 64 passengers and crew on board, 32 were killed in the accident, and many of the survivors sustained injuries. [SEP] [CLS]
