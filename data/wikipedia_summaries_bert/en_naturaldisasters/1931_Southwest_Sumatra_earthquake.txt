The 1931 Southwest Sumatra earthquake occurred on 25 September at 05:59 UTC. [CLS] [SEP] It was located between the Enggano Island and Sumatra, Indonesia, then under the rule of Dutch East Indies. [CLS] [SEP] It had a magnitude of Mw 7.3, or Ms 7.5.The earthquake occurred off southwest Sumatra. [CLS] [SEP] Some huts were shifted by about 0.5 metre. [CLS] [SEP] The earthquake could be felt in Padang, more than 500 km away and in Bandung, about 600 km away. [CLS] [SEP] It was reported that the earthquake was even felt in Pamekasan, Madura Island, about 1250 km away. [CLS] [SEP] A tsunami of 1 metre high was reported. [CLS] [SEP]
