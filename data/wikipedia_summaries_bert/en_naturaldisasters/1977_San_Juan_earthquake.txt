The 1977 San Juan earthquake, also known as Caucete earthquake, took place in the province of San Juan, Argentina, on 23 November at 09:26:26 AM. [CLS] [SEP] It measured 7.4 on the surface wave magnitude scale, and had a maximum perceived intensity of IX (Violent) on the Mercalli intensity scale. [CLS] [SEP] The earthquake caused fatalities and severe damage to buildings throughout the province, especially in the city of Caucete, where at least 65 people died. [CLS] [SEP] It also caused slight damage in the north of the Greater Mendoza metropolitan area. [CLS] [SEP] The effects of the earthquake were felt as far away as Buenos Aires, where people were awakened that Wednesday by the tremor. [CLS] [SEP] People left their houses at dawn in panic at the Argentinian capital, located at 1,000 km to the East South East. [CLS] [SEP]
