Jana Šenková Napolitano (born (1982-11-07)7 November 1982) is a retired Czech female volleyball player. [CLS] [SEP] She was part of the Czech Republic women's national volleyball team. [CLS] [SEP] She participated at the 2002 FIVB Volleyball Women's World Championship in Germany. [CLS] [SEP] On club level she played with SK UP Olomouc. [CLS] [SEP]
