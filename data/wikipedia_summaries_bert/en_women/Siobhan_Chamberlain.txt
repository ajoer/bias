Siobhan Rebecca Chamberlain (born 15 August 1983) is an English professional footballer who has most recently played as a goalkeeper for FA WSL club Manchester United and the England national team. [CLS] [SEP] She joined Manchester United in 2018 after playing for Liverpool. [CLS] [SEP] Before joining Liverpool in December 2015, she had been at Notts County on loan from Arsenal. [CLS] [SEP] Chamberlain previously played at the top club level for Chelsea, Fulham, Birmingham City and Bristol Academy, as well as for North American W-League club Vancouver Whitecaps. [CLS] [SEP] She has represented the England women's national football team on 50 occasions since her debut in 2004. [CLS] [SEP] This included being part of the squad at three FIFA Women's World Cups and three UEFA Women's Championships. [CLS] [SEP]
