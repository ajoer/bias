[CLS] Tatjana Mittermayer (born July 26, 1964 in Rosenheim, Bavaria) is a German freestyle skier and Olympic medalist. [SEP] [CLS] She won the silver medal at the 1998 Winter Olympics in Nagano, in moguls.She participated at the 1988 Winter Olympics moguls (demonstration event), where she finished 1st. [SEP] [CLS] She participated at the 1992 Winter Olympics, finishing 4th, and at the 1994 Winter Olympics, finishing 6th. [SEP] [CLS]
