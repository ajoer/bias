Timo Nieminen (born October 6, 1981 in Helsinki) is a retired professional Finnish tennis player. [CLS] [SEP] He reached his highest ATP Tour singles ranking of World No. [CLS] [SEP] 254 in July 2003. [CLS] [SEP] Nieminen primarily played on the Futures circuit and the Challenger circuit. [CLS] [SEP] He has been a member of the Finland Davis Cup team since 2002, posting a 2–12 record in singles and 0–1 record in doubles in 12 ties.He has no relation to Jarkko Nieminen. [CLS] [SEP]
