[CLS] Amy Joy Williams, (born 29 September 1982) is a British former skeleton racer and Olympic gold medallist. [SEP] [CLS] Originally a runner, she began training in skeleton in 2002 after trying the sport on a push-start track at the University of Bath. [SEP] [CLS] Although unable to qualify for the 2006 Winter Olympics, she was a member of the Great Britain team four years later at the 2010 Games. [SEP] [CLS] She won a gold medal, becoming the first British individual gold medallist at a Winter Olympics for 30 years and the only British medallist in those Olympics. [SEP] [CLS]
