[CLS] Harivansh Rai Bachchan (27 November 1907 - 18 January 2003) was an Indian poet of the Nayi Kavita literary movement (romantic upsurge) of early 20th century Hindi literature. [SEP] [CLS] Born in the village of Babupatti, in the district of Pratapgarh, in the United Provinces of Agra and Oudh in British India, he was also a poet of the Hindi Kavi Sammelan. [SEP] [CLS] He is best known for his early work Madhushala (मधुशाला). [SEP] [CLS] He is also the husband of social activist, Teji Bachchan, father of Amitabh Bachchan and grandfather of Abhishek Bachchan. [SEP] [CLS] In 1976, he received the Padma Bhushan for his service to Hindi literature. [SEP] [CLS]
