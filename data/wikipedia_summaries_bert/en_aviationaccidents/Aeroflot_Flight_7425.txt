Aeroflot Flight 7425 was a domestic scheduled Karshi–Ufa–Leningrad passenger flight that crashed near Uchkuduk, Uzbek SSR, Soviet Union on 10 July 1985. [CLS] [SEP] The crash killed all 200 occupants (148 adults, including 9 crewmembers, and 52 children) on board. [CLS] [SEP] Investigators determined that crew fatigue was a factor in the accident.
