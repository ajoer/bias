[CLS] Christoph Lehmann (born 28 December 1968) is a Swiss former ski jumper who competed from 1988 to 1993. [SEP] [CLS] He finished eighth in the team large hill event at the 1988 Winter Olympics in Calgary. [SEP] [CLS] At the 1989 FIS Nordic World Ski Championships in Lahti, Lehmann earned his best finish of 44th in the individual large hill event. [SEP] [CLS] He finished 38th at the 1990 Ski-flying World Championships in Vikersund. [SEP] [CLS] Lehmann's best World Cup career finish was ninth in a normal hill event in West Germany in 1988.
