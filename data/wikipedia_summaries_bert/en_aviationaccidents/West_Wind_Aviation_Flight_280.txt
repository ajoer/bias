West Wind Aviation Flight 280 was a domestic passenger flight from Fond-du-Lac Airport to Stony Rapids Airport, Canada. [CLS] [SEP] The aircraft was an ATR 42-320 registered C-GWEA. [CLS] [SEP] On 13 December 2017, shortly after taking off from Fond-du-Lac, the ATR-42 lost altitude and hit the ground. [CLS] [SEP] All 25 passengers and crews initially survived the crash, but one passenger later died of his injuries in hospital. [CLS] [SEP] Investigation on the cause of the crash is still ongoing.
