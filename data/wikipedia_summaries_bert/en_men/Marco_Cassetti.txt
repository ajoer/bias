Marco Cassetti (Italian pronunciation: [ˈmarko kasˈsetti]; born 29 May 1977) is an Italian former footballer who played as a defender. [CLS] [SEP] Born in Brescia, he has previously played in Serie A for Verona, Lecce and Roma and in England with Watford. [CLS] [SEP] Cassetti won five caps for Italy at full international level. [CLS] [SEP] Principally a right-back, Cassetti started his career as a right midfielder. [CLS] [SEP] He has also played as a central defender. [CLS] [SEP]
