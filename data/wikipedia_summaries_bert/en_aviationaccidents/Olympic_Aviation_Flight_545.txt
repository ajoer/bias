On August 3rd 1989, Olympic Aviation Flight 545, operated by a Shorts 330-200, crashed into a cloud-shrouded Mount Kerkis on Samos due to the pilots using Visual Flight Rules in Instrumental Flight Rules conditions. [CLS] [SEP] All on board perished. [CLS] [SEP] This accident is the worst involving a Shorts 330. [CLS] [SEP]
