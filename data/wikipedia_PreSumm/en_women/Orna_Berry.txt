[CLS] Orna Berry (Hebrew: ארנה ברי‎; born December 19, 1949), is an Israeli Computer Scientist, high-tech entrepreneur, businesswoman, senior executive among the Israeli science and technology industries and laureate of the Ben-Gurion University of the Negev Award. [SEP] [CLS] Orna Berry is recognized for her work in promoting excellence, entrepreneurship, and innovation in Israeli society. [SEP] [CLS] She was the first woman to serve as chief scientist and head of the industrial R&D operation of the Israeli Ministry of Industry, Trade and Labour (1996–2000). [SEP] [CLS]
