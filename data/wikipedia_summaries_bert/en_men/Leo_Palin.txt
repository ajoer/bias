Leo Palin (born 20 October 1956) is a retired professional tennis player from Finland. [CLS] [SEP] During his career on the ATP Tour, Palin won one doubles title, and reached one singles and one other doubles final. [CLS] [SEP] He reached career-high rankings of No. [CLS] [SEP] 92 in singles (in 1982) and No. [CLS] [SEP] 111 in doubles (in 1986). [CLS] [SEP]
