Margo Ilene Seltzer is a professor and researcher in computer systems. [CLS] [SEP] She is currently the Canada 150 Research Chair in Computer Systems and the Cheriton Family Chair in Computer Science at the University of British Columbia. [CLS] [SEP] Previously, she was the Herchel Smith Professor of Computer Science at Harvard University's John A. [CLS] [SEP] Paulson School of Engineering and Applied Sciences and director at the Center for Research on Computation and Society. [CLS] [SEP]
