On 7 April 2012, an avalanche hit a Pakistan Army base in Gayari Sector, near the Siachen Glacier region, trapping 140 soldiers and civilian contractors under deep snow. [CLS] [SEP] The incident occurred at an altitude of about 4,000 meters and 300 km northeast of Skardu (capital of Baltistan). [CLS] [SEP] It was the worst avalanche that the Pakistani military has experienced in the area.On 29 May 2012, Pakistan declared that the 129 soldiers and 11 civilians were killed. [CLS] [SEP]
