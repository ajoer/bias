Alicia Suskin Ostriker (born November 11, 1937) is an American poet and scholar who writes Jewish feminist poetry. [CLS] [SEP] She was called "America's most fiercely honest poet" by Progressive. [CLS] [SEP] Additionally, she was one of the first women poets in America to write and publish poems discussing the topic of motherhood. [CLS] [SEP] In 2015, she was elected a Chancellor of the Academy of American Poets. [CLS] [SEP] In 2018, she was named the New York State Poet Laureate. [CLS] [SEP]
