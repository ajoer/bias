The Near East earthquakes of 1759 were a series of devastating earthquakes that shook a large portion of the Levant in October and November of that year. [CLS] [SEP] This geographical crossroads in the Eastern Mediterranean includes portions of Syria, Lebanon, Turkey, and Palestine.
