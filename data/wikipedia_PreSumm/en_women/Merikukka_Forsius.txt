[CLS] Merikukka Forsius (born August 12, 1972, in Helsinki) is a Finnish politician and former member of the Finnish Parliament, representing the centre-right National Coalition Party (Kokoomus). [SEP] [CLS] She was first elected to parliament in 1999 from the Finnish Green League, but defected to National Coalition Party in 2008. [SEP] [CLS] She has also been a member of the city council of Vihti from 1997 to 2003. [SEP] [CLS] Forsius has a master's degree in education from the University of Turku. [SEP] [CLS] She has been married twice, and has two sons: Dan (born 2004) and Jim (born 2008). [SEP] [CLS] Her second husband was Harry Harkimo, and he is the father of her first son, Dan. [SEP] [CLS] Forsius and Harkimo divorced after Dan's birth. [SEP] [CLS] Forsius was involved with prime minister Matti Vanhanen during spring and summer 2007. [SEP] [CLS] In September 2007 she got engaged to Vihti municipal manager Petri Härkönen, but they split up in November. [SEP] [CLS] Their son, Jim, was born in spring 2008. [SEP] [CLS] Forsius currently lives in Vihti with her sons. [SEP] [CLS] In 2014 it was announced that Forsius got engaged to a female teacher. [SEP] [CLS]
