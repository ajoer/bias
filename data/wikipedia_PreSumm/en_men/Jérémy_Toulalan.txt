[CLS] Jérémy Toulalan (French pronunciation: ​[ʒeʁemi tulalɑ̃]; born 10 September 1983) is a French professional footballer who most recently played for Bordeaux in Ligue 1. [SEP] [CLS] His usual position is as a defensive midfielder, but he can also be utilized as a central defender. [SEP] [CLS] He is best known for his humble demeanor, simple distribution, good technique and effective tackling.Toulalan started his career at hometown club Nantes and made his debut in March 2002. [SEP] [CLS] His performances during the 2004–05 season earned him the UNFP Ligue 1 Player of the Year award and also an appearance in the Team of the Year. [SEP] [CLS] In May 2006, Toulalan joined Lyon on a four-year contract and helped the team win back-to-back league titles in 2007 and 2008. [SEP] [CLS] In June 2011, he joined Málaga. [SEP] [CLS] Toulalan is also a French international, having previously represented his country at youth level. [SEP] [CLS] He made his senior international debut in October 2006 and has represented his nation at two international tournaments: UEFA Euro 2008 and the 2010 FIFA World Cup. [SEP] [CLS]
