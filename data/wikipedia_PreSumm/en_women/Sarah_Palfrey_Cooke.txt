[CLS] Sarah Hammond Palfrey Danzig (née Palfrey; September 18, 1912 – February 27, 1996) was an American tennis player whose career spanned two decades from the late 1920s until the late 1940s. [SEP] [CLS] She won two singles, nine women's doubles, and four mixed doubles titles at the U. [SEP] [CLS] S. [SEP] [CLS] National Championships. [SEP] [CLS]
