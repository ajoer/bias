[SEP] [CLS] Fresh east winds were forecast for the afternoon and evening of November 27, with storm warnings in effect by the morning of November 28. [SEP] [CLS] Storm-force winds and heavy snows accompanied the cyclone's passage. [SEP] [CLS] The storm, named after the steamship Mataafa, ended up destroying or damaging about 29 vessels, killing 36 seamen, and causing shipping losses of US$$3.567 million (1905 dollars) on Lake Superior.
