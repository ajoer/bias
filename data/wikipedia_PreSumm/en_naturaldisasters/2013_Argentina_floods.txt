[CLS] Between 1 and 3 April 2013, the northeastern section of Buenos Aires Province, Argentina, experienced several flash floods that claimed the lives of 101 people. [SEP] [CLS] Greater La Plata was hardest hit with 91 reported deaths, and Greater Buenos Aires reported 10 deaths. [SEP] [CLS] The flooding was the result of extremely heavy rainfall and is said to be the worst flooding in La Plata's history. [SEP] [CLS] Three days of national mourning were declared on 3 April. [SEP] [CLS] Two days later, government benefits for flood victims were announced. [SEP] [CLS]
