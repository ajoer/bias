[CLS] René François Ghislain Magritte (French: [ʁəne fʁɑ̃swa ɡilɛ̃ maɡʁit]; 21 November 1898 – 15 August 1967) was a Belgian Surrealist artist. [SEP] [CLS] He became well known for creating a number of witty and thought-provoking images. [SEP] [CLS] Often depicting ordinary objects in an unusual context, his work is known for challenging observers' preconditioned perceptions of reality. [SEP] [CLS] His imagery has influenced pop art, minimalist art and conceptual art. [SEP] [CLS]
