[CLS] James Michael Bennett (born February 9, 1996) is an American actor and musician. [SEP] [CLS] He is known for his roles as a child actor in Daddy Day Care, Hostage, The Amityville Horror, Poseidon, Evan Almighty, Orphan, Shorts, and as young James T. [SEP] [CLS] Kirk in the 2009 film Star Trek. [SEP] [CLS] He also starred on the ABC series No Ordinary Family as JJ Powell, a teenager gifted with vast intelligence after a plane crash. [SEP] [CLS]
