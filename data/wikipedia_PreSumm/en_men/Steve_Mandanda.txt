[CLS] Steve Mandanda Mpidi (French pronunciation: ​[stɛv mɑ̃dɑ̃da]; born 28 March 1985) is a French professional footballer who plays as a goalkeeper for Marseille, where he is captain, and the France national team. [SEP] [CLS] He spent most of his professional career with Marseille, making over 500 appearances across all competitions, and was voted Ligue 1 Goalkeeper of the Year five times. [SEP] [CLS] Since his debut in 2008, Mandanda has represented France at five international tournaments: three UEFA European Championships (2008, 2012, and 2016) and two FIFA World Cups (2010 and 2018), winning the last of those.
