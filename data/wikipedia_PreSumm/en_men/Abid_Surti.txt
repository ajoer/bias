[CLS] Abid Surti or Aabid Surti (born 5 May 1935) is a painter, author, cartoonist, journalist, environmentalist, playwright and screenwriter from India. [SEP] [CLS] He was given a National Award by the government of India in 1993 for writing a series of short stories called the "Teesri Aankh". [SEP] [CLS]
