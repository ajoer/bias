The Manila tornado occurred on 4:30 pm Philippine Time on August 14, 2016. [CLS] [SEP] This was only the second time in recorded history that a tornado struck Manila, the capital of the Philippines. [CLS] [SEP] The tornado affected in the cities of Manila and Quezon City. [CLS] [SEP] The most destructive impact of the tornado was in the southern part of Metro Manila. [CLS] [SEP]
