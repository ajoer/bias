[CLS] Rodger Bumpass (born November 20, 1951) is an American actor and voice actor. [SEP] [CLS] He is known for his long-running role as Squidward Tentacles on the American animated television series SpongeBob SquarePants. [SEP] [CLS] He voices several other characters on the show as well, including the Purple Doctor Fish and various anchovies. [SEP] [CLS] He also voiced The Chief in the animated series Where on Earth Is Carmen Sandiego?, and Mr. [SEP] [CLS] Besser, the school principal in the animated series The Kids from Room 402. [SEP] [CLS] Bumpass has many other credits in animated films, animated television series, and video games. [SEP] [CLS]
