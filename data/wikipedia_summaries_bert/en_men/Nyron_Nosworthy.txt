Nyron Paul Henry Nosworthy (born 11 October 1980) is an English-born Jamaican former footballer. [CLS] [SEP] Beginning his career with Gillingham he moved to Sunderland in 2005 with whom he played in the Premier League. [CLS] [SEP] After two lengthy loan spells with Sheffield United he made a permanent switch to Watford in 2012 but was released in the summer of 2014 after a loan spell with Bristol City. [CLS] [SEP] Born in Brixton, South London, Nosworthy has represented Jamaica at international level, playing ten games and scoring once. [CLS] [SEP]
