[SEP] [CLS] The 11 surviving passengers were seriously injured. [SEP] [CLS] The cause of the accident was judged to be pilot error, the captain having incorrectly determined the position of the plane. [SEP] [CLS] As of 2020, Avianca Flight 011 remains the second-deadliest aviation accident in Spanish territory behind the Tenerife airport disaster, and the worst accident in the history of Avianca.
