[CLS] Daniel Christian Manuel Boemle, Dänu Boemle, "Sleepy Dan", "Öml" (7 November 1960 in Bern – 21 February 2007 in Les Ponts-de-Martel) was a well-known Swiss radio DJ. [SEP] [CLS] He presented various shows on DRS3 between 1988 and 1994. [SEP] [CLS] He was also a singer, author, culture critic, and painter. [SEP] [CLS]
