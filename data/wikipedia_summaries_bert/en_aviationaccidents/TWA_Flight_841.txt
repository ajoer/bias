[CLS] TWA Flight 841 was a domestic, scheduled passenger flight from John F. [SEP] [CLS] Kennedy International Airport in New York City, en route to Minneapolis-Saint Paul International Airport in Minneapolis, Minnesota. [SEP] [CLS] On April 4, 1979, at or around 9:48 p.m. [SEP] [CLS] Eastern Standard Time (01:48 UTC), while flying over Saginaw, Michigan, the Boeing 727-31 airliner began a sharp, uncommanded roll to the right, and subsequently went into a spiral dive. [SEP] [CLS] The pilots were able to regain control of the aircraft and made a successful emergency landing at Detroit Metropolitan Airport. [SEP] [CLS]
