Verena von Strenge (born 27 July 1975, Munich, Germany) is a German singer and dancer known for being the lead singer for the German happy hardcore dance group Dune from 1996–1997 and 1999–2000. [CLS] [SEP] Von Strenge loved dance since she was very young. [CLS] [SEP] At the age of five, she was taught classical dance by her grandmother who was a former ballerina. [CLS] [SEP] However, von Strenge had to give up her plans of being a professional dancer due to knee problems. [CLS] [SEP]
