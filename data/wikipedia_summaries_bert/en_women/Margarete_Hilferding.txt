[CLS] Margarete Hilferding, born Hönigsberg (June 20, 1871 in Hernals (Vienna)– September 23, 1942 in Maly Trostenets) was an Austrian physician and psychoanalyst. [SEP] [CLS] Hilferding was the first woman admitted into the Vienna Psychoanalytic Society. [SEP] [CLS] Her husband was the Austro-Marxist economist Rudolf Hilferding. [SEP] [CLS] She died in transit from Theresienstadt to Maly Trostenets. [SEP] [CLS]
