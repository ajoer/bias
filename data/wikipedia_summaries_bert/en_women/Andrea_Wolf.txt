Andrea Wolf (Kurdish nickname: Ronahî, born January 15, 1965 in Munich, died October 23, 1998 in Çatak, Van province, Turkey) was a radical leftist activist. [CLS] [SEP] She was a PKK militant. [CLS] [SEP] She was killed while fighting with the PKK in 1998. [CLS] [SEP] Before her death, Wolf had been implicated in some revolutionary activities, and was accused of being part of the Red Army Faction from Germany. [CLS] [SEP] The Andrea Wolf case is the focus of a short film by Hito Steyerl called November. [CLS] [SEP]
