[CLS] The name Andres has been used for seven tropical cyclones in the Eastern Pacific Ocean: Hurricane Andres (1979), made landfall in Mexico as a tropical depression. [SEP] [CLS] Tropical Storm Andres (1985), formed near the Mexican coast but moved out to sea. [SEP] [CLS] Tropical Storm Andres (1991), stayed well clear of land. [SEP] [CLS] Tropical Storm Andres (1997), formed near Central America and made landfall in San Salvador Tropical Storm Andres (2003), remained in mid-ocean. [SEP] [CLS] Hurricane Andres (2009), tracked parallel to the southwestern coast of Mexico. [SEP] [CLS] Hurricane Andres (2015), remained in the open ocean and never affected land. [SEP] [CLS]
