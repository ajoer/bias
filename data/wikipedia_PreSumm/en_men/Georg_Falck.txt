[CLS] Rothenburg ob der Tauber (German pronunciation: [ˈʁoːtn̩bʊʁk ʔɔp deːɐ̯ ˈtaʊbɐ] (listen)) is a town in the district of Ansbach of Mittelfranken (Middle Franconia), the Franconia region of Bavaria, Germany. [SEP] [CLS] It is well known for its well-preserved medieval old town, a destination for tourists from around the world. [SEP] [CLS] It is part of the popular Romantic Road through southern Germany. [SEP] [CLS] Rothenburg was a Free imperial city from the late Middle Ages to 1803. [SEP] [CLS] In 1884 Johann Friedrich (von) Hessing (1838–1918) built Wildbad Rothenburg o.d.T.
