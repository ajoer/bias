[CLS] Samantha Crawford (born February 18, 1995) is an inactive American tennis player. [SEP] [CLS] Crawford has won one singles and five doubles titles on the ITF circuit. [SEP] [CLS] In July 2016, she reached her best singles ranking of world number 98. [SEP] [CLS] On July 13, 2015, she peaked at number 216 in the WTA doubles rankings. [SEP] [CLS] As a junior, Atlanta-born Crawford won the girls' singles title at the 2012 US Open, defeating Anett Kontaveit in the final. [SEP] [CLS]
