[CLS] Andrea Glass (born 17 July 1976) is a former professional German tennis player. [SEP] [CLS] Her highest WTA singles ranking is 53rd, which she reached on 1 February 1999, as a result of reaching the third round of the Australian Open, where Anna Kournikova beat her 4–6, 6–2, 6–3. [SEP] [CLS] Her career high in doubles was at 85 set on 20 November 2000. [SEP] [CLS] Glass won the German Tennis Championship in both singles and doubles, partnering Barbara Rittner, in 1997. [SEP] [CLS] She played for Germany in the Fed Cup from 1998 to 2001. [SEP] [CLS]
