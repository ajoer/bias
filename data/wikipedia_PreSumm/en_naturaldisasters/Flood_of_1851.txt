[CLS] The Great Flood of 1851 occurred after record-setting rainfalls across the U.S. [SEP] [CLS] Midwest and Plains from May to August, 1851. [SEP] [CLS] Hardest hit was the State of Iowa, with significant flooding extending to the Lower Mississippi River basin. [SEP] [CLS] Historical evidence suggest flooding occurred in the eastern Plains, from Nebraska to the Red River basin, but these areas were sparsely settled in 1851. [SEP] [CLS] Heavy rainfall also occurred in the Ohio River basin. [SEP] [CLS]
