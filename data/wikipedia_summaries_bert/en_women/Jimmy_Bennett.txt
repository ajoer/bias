James Michael Bennett (born February 9, 1996) is an American actor and musician. [CLS] [SEP] He is known for his roles as a child actor in Daddy Day Care, Hostage, The Amityville Horror, Poseidon, Evan Almighty, Orphan, Shorts, and as young James T. [CLS] [SEP] Kirk in the 2009 film Star Trek. [CLS] [SEP] He also starred on the ABC series No Ordinary Family as JJ Powell, a teenager gifted with vast intelligence after a plane crash. [CLS] [SEP]
