Severe Tropical Cyclone Marcia was a powerful tropical cyclone that made landfall at its peak strength over central Queensland, near Shoalwater Bay on 20 February 2015. [CLS] [SEP] The cyclone went on to affect various areas including Yeppoon and Rockhampton. [CLS] [SEP] It passed just to the west of Yeppoon as a Category 4 system, then traversed over the regional city of Rockhampton as a Category 2 system on the same day. [CLS] [SEP] Eventually, the cyclone weakened, moved southeast out to sea, before dissipating. [CLS] [SEP] Marcia caused at least A$750 million (US$587 million) worth of damage. [CLS] [SEP]
