Susan Tex "Sue" Green (born August 13, 1953) is an American professional wrestler. [CLS] [SEP] She began wrestling at the age of 15 and was trained by The Fabulous Moolah. [CLS] [SEP] She was the frequent tag team partner of Sandy Parker, with whom she won the National Wrestling Alliance (NWA)'s NWA Women's World Tag Team Championship. [CLS] [SEP] The title reign, however, is unrecognized. [CLS] [SEP] In 1975, Green defeated Moolah for the NWA World Women's Championship, which is unrecognized by the NWA. [CLS] [SEP] The following year, Green was recognized by Pro Wrestling Illustrated as the Girl Wrestler of the Year. [CLS] [SEP] In 1992, she joined the Professional Girl Wrestling Association, holding the promotion's championship and later acting as commissioner. [CLS] [SEP]
