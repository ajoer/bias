[CLS] Aeroflot Flight 1668 was a scheduled flight from Yakutsk to Novosibirsk with stopovers at Olekminsk, Lensk, Ust-Kut and Krasnoyarsk that crashed shortly after take-off from Olekminsk on 6 January 1968. [SEP] [CLS] All 45 people onboard died. [SEP] [CLS] The subsequent investigation was unable to determine the root cause of the accident. [SEP] [CLS]
