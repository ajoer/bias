[CLS] The 1987 Ecuador earthquakes occurred over a six-hour period on March 6. [SEP] [CLS] The sequence of shocks measured 6.7, 7.1, and 6.0 on the moment magnitude scale. [SEP] [CLS] The main shock had a maximum Mercalli intensity of IX (Violent). [SEP] [CLS] The earthquakes were centered in Napo Province in northeast Ecuador; the epicenters were on the eastern slopes of the Andes, about 75 km ENE of Quito and 25 km north of Reventador Volcano. [SEP] [CLS] The earthquakes caused about 1,000 deaths. [SEP] [CLS] Four-thousand were missing and an estimated US$1 billion in damage was caused. [SEP] [CLS]
