The Europe Zone was one of the two regional zones of the 1935 International Lawn Tennis Challenge. [CLS] [SEP] Due to increased political tensions in Europe, entries for the Europe Zone declined sharply, causing the Qualifying Round system to be scrapped. [CLS] [SEP] 11 teams entered the Europe Zone, with the winner going on to compete in the Inter-Zonal Final against the winner of the America Zone; 17 teams entering the qualifying rounds. [CLS] [SEP] Germany defeated Czechoslovakia in the final, and went on to face the United States in the Inter-Zonal Final. [CLS] [SEP]
