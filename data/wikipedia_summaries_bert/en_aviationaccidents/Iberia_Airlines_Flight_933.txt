Iberia Airlines Flight 933 was an international flight from Madrid Barajas International Airport bound for its destination, Boston-Logan International Airport in Boston, that suffered a runway incident on December 17, 1973. [CLS] [SEP] As the McDonnell Douglas DC-10, the aircraft operating the flight was approaching the airport, it collided with the ALS system 500 feet short from the runway threshold. [CLS] [SEP] This critically damaged the frontal gear, collapsing it. [CLS] [SEP] The aircraft came to a rest 300 feet before the runway. [CLS] [SEP] Although all 168 onboard survived, the plane was written off. [CLS] [SEP] This accident was the first hull loss of the DC-10. [CLS] [SEP]
