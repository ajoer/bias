[CLS] Lao Airlines Flight 301 was a scheduled domestic passenger flight from Vientiane to Pakse, Laos. [SEP] [CLS] On 16 October 2013, the ATR 72-600 aircraft operating the flight crashed into the Mekong River near Pakse, killing all 49 people on board. [SEP] [CLS] The accident was the first involving an ATR 72-600 and the deadliest ever to occur on Laotian soil.The investigation report concluded that the probable cause of the accident was the flight crew's failure to execute properly the published missed approach procedure following the aborted landing at Pakse airport.
