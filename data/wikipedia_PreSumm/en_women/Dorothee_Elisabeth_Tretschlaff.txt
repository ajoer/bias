[SEP] [CLS] She was the last person to have been executed for witchcraft in the state of Brandenburg. [SEP] [CLS] Her case attracted much attention, and was the subject of a great deal of contemporary debate. [SEP] [CLS] Tretschlaff was employed as a maid. [SEP] [CLS] She confessed that she had made a pact with the Devil, and that he visited her regularly for intercourse in her bed in the shape of a fly. [SEP] [CLS] The Devil supposedly paid Dorothee for her favors. [SEP] [CLS] The case against her was based on this alleged intercourse. [SEP] [CLS] Tretschlaff was found guilty of witchcraft and executed by decapitation. [SEP] [CLS] The sentence was criticized both before and after it was made. [SEP] [CLS] Later the same year, the monarch ordered an investigation to inquire into whether the process had been correct. [SEP] [CLS] Tretschlaff was found to be depressed and suicidal.
