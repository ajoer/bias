Shere Hite (born November 2, 1942) is an American-born German sex educator and feminist. [CLS] [SEP] Her sexological work has focused primarily on female sexuality. [CLS] [SEP] Hite builds upon biological studies of sex by Masters and Johnson and by Alfred Kinsey. [CLS] [SEP] She also references theoretical, political and psychological works associated with the feminist movement of the 1970s, such as Anne Koedt's The Myth of the Vaginal Orgasm. [CLS] [SEP] She renounced her United States citizenship in 1995 to become German. [CLS] [SEP]
