Sandra Klösel (born 22 June 1979) is a former tennis player from Germany. [CLS] [SEP] Kösel turned professional in July 1995, and won nine singles and six doubles titles on the ITF Women's Circuit. [CLS] [SEP] In March 2007, she reached a career-high singles ranking of world No. [CLS] [SEP] 87. [CLS] [SEP] On 1 October 2007, she peaked at No. [CLS] [SEP] 128 in the doubles rankings.
