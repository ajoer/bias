[CLS] The 2014–2015 eruption of Bárðarbunga was a Hawaiian eruption in the Bárðarbunga volcanic system in Iceland, that began on August 29, 2014 and ended on February 27, 2015. [SEP] [CLS] The eruption emitted large volumes of sulphur dioxide and impacted air quality in Iceland. [SEP] [CLS] There was no effect on flights outside of the immediate vicinity due to a lack of a significant emission of volcanic ash. [SEP] [CLS] The eruption took place in the lava field of Holuhraun northeast of the Bárðarbunga caldera proper. [SEP] [CLS]
