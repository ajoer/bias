[CLS] Cyclone fergus was a tropical cyclone, later becoming an extratropical cyclone, that affected islands in the south-west Pacific Ocean. [SEP] [CLS] It lasted from December 23, 1996, to January 1, 1997, and reached speeds of up to 165 kilometers per hour, or 90 miles per hour. [SEP] [CLS] In additions to its winds, the storm caused heavy rains and severe flooding, causing significant damage to property in some areas. [SEP] [CLS]
