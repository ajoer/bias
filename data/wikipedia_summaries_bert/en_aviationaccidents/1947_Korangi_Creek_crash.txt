The Korangi Creek crash took place when an Air India flight from Karachi to Bombay crashed shortly after takeoff. [CLS] [SEP] All 19 passengers and 4 crew members were killed. [CLS] [SEP] Poor visibility and malfunctioning instruments in the cockpit led the pilots to lose control. [CLS] [SEP] The Douglas DC-3 aircraft was damaged beyond repair. [CLS] [SEP] This was the first airline fatality in Pakistan after its independence in 1947. [CLS] [SEP]
