Waris Dirie (Somali: Waris Diiriye) (born 1965) is a Somali model, author, actress and human rights activist in the fight against Female Genital Mutilation (FGM). [CLS] [SEP] From 1997 to 2003, she served as a UN special ambassador against female genital cutting. [CLS] [SEP] In 2002 she founded her own organization in Vienna (Austria), the Desert Flower Foundation. [CLS] [SEP]
