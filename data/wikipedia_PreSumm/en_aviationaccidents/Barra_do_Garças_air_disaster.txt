[CLS] The Barra do Garças air disaster was a suicide attack that occurred in Barra do Garças, Mato Grosso in Brazil on June 1, 1980, when pilot Mauro Milhomem attempted to crash a single-engine aircraft into the Hotel Presidente owned by his mother-in-law, killing six persons and himself and wounding four others. [SEP] [CLS] This incident is possibly one of the first cases of suicide by pilot in Brazil. [SEP] [CLS]
