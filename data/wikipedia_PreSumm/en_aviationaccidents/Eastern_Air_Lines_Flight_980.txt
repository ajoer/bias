[SEP] [CLS] The wreckage was scattered over a large area of a glacier covered with snow. [SEP] [CLS] Over the decades, several search expeditions were only able to recover a small amount of debris, and searches for the flight recorders were unsuccessful. [SEP] [CLS] The accident remains the highest-altitude controlled flight into terrain in commercial aviation history. [SEP] [CLS]
