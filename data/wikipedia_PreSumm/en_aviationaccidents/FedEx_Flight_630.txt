[CLS] FedEx Flight 630 was a regular scheduled cargo flight from Seattle-Tacoma International Airport to Memphis International Airport, Memphis, Tennessee. [SEP] [CLS] On July 28, 2006, the McDonnell Douglas MD-10-10F operating the flight, crashed upon landing due to a landing gear failure. [SEP] [CLS] The main left undercarriage collapsed 7 seconds after touchdown causing the MD-10 to roll off the runway out of control. [SEP] [CLS] The aircraft finally came to a stop near taxiway M4 and caught fire. [SEP] [CLS] The fire consumed the wing and port engine and the crash and evacuation left the 2 crew-members and the sole passenger, an off-duty crew-member, injured. [SEP] [CLS]
