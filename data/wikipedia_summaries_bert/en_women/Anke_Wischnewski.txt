[CLS] Anke Wischnewski (born 5 January 1978 in Annaberg-Buchholz, Saxony) is a German former luger who has competed since 2001 to 2014. [SEP] [CLS] She won two medals in the women's singles at the FIL World Luge Championships with a silver in 2007 and a bronze in 2005. [SEP] [CLS] Wischnewski's best finish at the FIL European Luge Championships in the women's singles event was third at Oberhof in 2013. [SEP] [CLS] Her best finish in the overall Luge World Cup title was third in 2006-7. [SEP] [CLS] Wishnewski qualified for the 2010 Winter Olympics where she finished fifth. [SEP] [CLS]
