The name Prapiroon (Thai: พระพิรุณ, RTGS: Phra Phirun, pronounced [pʰráʔ pʰí(ʔ).rūn]) has been used to name four tropical cyclones in the northwestern Pacific Ocean. [CLS] [SEP] The name was submitted by the Kingdom of Thailand and is the name of a Thai rain god. [CLS] [SEP] Typhoon Prapiroon (2000) (T0012, 20W, Lusing) – affected the Ryukyu Islands and Korean Peninsula Typhoon Prapiroon (2006) (T0606, 07W, Henry) – affected China Typhoon Prapiroon (2012) (T1221, 22W, Nina) Typhoon Prapiroon (2018) (T1807, 09W, Florita)
