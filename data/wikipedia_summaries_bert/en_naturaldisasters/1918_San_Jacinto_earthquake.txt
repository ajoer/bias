[CLS] The 1918 San Jacinto earthquake occurred in extreme eastern San Diego County in Southern California on April 21 at 14:32:29 local time. [SEP] [CLS] The shock had a moment magnitude of 6.7 and a maximum Mercalli intensity of IX (Violent). [SEP] [CLS] Several injuries and one death occurred with total losses estimated to be $200,000.
