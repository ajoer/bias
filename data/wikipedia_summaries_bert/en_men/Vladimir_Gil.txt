Vladimir Vladimirovich Gil (Russian: Владимир Владимирович Гиль; born 11 June 1906, Vileyka – died 14 May 1944, Nakol), also known by the pseudonyms I.G. [CLS] [SEP] Rodionov or Radionov (German: Radjanoff), was a colonel of the Red Army and the founder and leader of the German-backed Union of Russian Nationalists and the 1st Russian Waffen-SS "Druzhina". [CLS] [SEP] This unit later went over to the Soviet partisans, still under Gil's command. [CLS] [SEP]
