Kerstin Thiele (born August 26, 1986 in Riesa, East Germany) is a German judoka and a silver medallist at the Olympic Games 2012 in London.Kerstin Thiele is a German champion in judo in the Middleweight (-70 kg) division. [CLS] [SEP] She competes for the German club JC Leipzig, and was part of the German national team.
