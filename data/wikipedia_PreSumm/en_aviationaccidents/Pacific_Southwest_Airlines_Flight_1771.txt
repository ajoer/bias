[CLS] Pacific Southwest Airlines Flight 1771 was a scheduled flight along the West Coast of the United States, from Los Angeles, California, to San Francisco. [SEP] [CLS] On 7 December 1987, the British Aerospace 146-200A, registration N350PS, crashed in San Luis Obispo County near Cayucos, after being hijacked by a passenger. [SEP] [CLS] All 43 passengers and crew aboard the plane died, five of whom, including the two pilots, were presumably shot dead before the plane crashed. [SEP] [CLS] The man who caused the crash, David Burke, was a disgruntled former employee of USAir, the parent company of Pacific Southwest Airlines. [SEP] [CLS]
