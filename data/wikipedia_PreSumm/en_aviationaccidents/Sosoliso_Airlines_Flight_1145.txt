[CLS] Sosoliso Airlines Flight 1145 (SO1145/OSL1145) was a scheduled domestic passenger flight between the Nigerian cities of Abuja (ABV) and Port Harcourt (PHC). [SEP] [CLS] At about 14:08 local time (13:08 UTC) on 10 December 2005, Flight 1145 from Abuja crash-landed at Port Harcourt International Airport. [SEP] [CLS] The plane, a McDonnell Douglas DC-9-32 with 110 people on board, slammed into the ground and burst into flames. [SEP] [CLS] Immediately after the crash, seven survivors were recovered and taken to hospitals, but only two people survived.It was the second air disaster to occur in Nigeria in less than three months, after Bellview Airlines Flight 210, which crashed on 22 October 2005 for reasons unknown, killing all 117 people on board. [SEP] [CLS] It was the company's first and only fatal accident. [SEP] [CLS]
