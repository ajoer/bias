Air France Flight 66 was a scheduled international passenger flight from Paris Charles de Gaulle Airport to Los Angeles International Airport, operated by Air France and using an Airbus A380-861. [CLS] [SEP] On 30 September 2017, the aircraft suffered an uncontained engine failure and made an emergency landing at Goose Bay Airport, Canada. [CLS] [SEP] The outboard right-side Engine Alliance GP7000 engine failed and its fan hub and intake separated 150 kilometres (93 mi) southeast of Paamiut, Greenland, while the aircraft was in cruise. [CLS] [SEP] This was the second uncontained engine failure suffered by an Airbus A380, following that of a Rolls-Royce Trent 900 engine on Qantas Flight 32 in 2010. [CLS] [SEP]
