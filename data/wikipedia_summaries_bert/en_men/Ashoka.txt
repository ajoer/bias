[CLS] [SEP] It covered the entire Indian subcontinent except for parts of present-day Tamil Nadu, Karnataka and Kerala. [CLS] [SEP] Ashoka waged a destructive war against the state of Kalinga (modern Odisha), which he conquered in about 260 BCE. [CLS] [SEP] He converted to Buddhism after witnessing the mass deaths of the Kalinga War, which he had waged out of a desire for conquest and which reportedly directly resulted in more than 100,000 deaths and 150,000 deportations.
