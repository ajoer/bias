[CLS] The 1957 crash of a Douglas C-47 plane named Mt. [SEP] [CLS] Pinatubo on the slopes of Mount Manunggal, Cebu, Philippines, killed the seventh President of the Philippines, Ramon Magsaysay, and 24 other passengers. [SEP] [CLS] The crash is estimated to have occurred at 1:40 a.m., March 17, 1957, Philippine Standard Time (17:40 p.m., March 16, 1957, GMT). [SEP] [CLS] Several high-ranking Philippine government and military officials, as well as journalists, were also among the dead. [SEP] [CLS] A reporter for the Philippine Herald, Nestor Mata, was the sole survivor of the accident. [SEP] [CLS] At the time of his death, President Magsaysay, a Nacionalista, was widely popular and was expected to easily win re-election in the November presidential elections. [SEP] [CLS]
