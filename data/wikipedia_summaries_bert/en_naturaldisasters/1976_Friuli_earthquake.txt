[CLS] The 1976 Friuli earthquake, also known in Italy as Terremoto del Friuli (Friulian earthquake), took place on May 6 with a moment magnitude of 6.5 and a maximum Mercalli intensity of X (Extreme). [SEP] [CLS] The shock occurred in the Friuli region in northeast Italy near the town of Gemona del Friuli. [SEP] [CLS] 990 people were killed, up to about 3,000 were injured, and more than 157,000 were left homeless. [SEP] [CLS]
