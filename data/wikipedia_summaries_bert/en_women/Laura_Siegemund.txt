[CLS] Laura Natalie Siegemund (born 4 March 1988 in Filderstadt) is a German professional tennis player. [SEP] [CLS] She has won two singles and five doubles titles on the WTA Tour, as well as 12 singles and 20 doubles titles on the ITF Circuit. [SEP] [CLS] On 29 August 2016, she reached her best singles ranking of world No. [SEP] [CLS] 27, and on 25 April 2016, she peaked at No. [SEP] [CLS] 40 in the doubles rankings. [SEP] [CLS]
