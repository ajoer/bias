Mathieu Debuchy (French pronunciation: ​[ma.tjø də.by.ʃi] ; born 28 July 1985) is a French professional footballer who plays for Ligue 1 club Saint-Étienne. [CLS] [SEP] He is often deployed as a right back, but began his career playing as a midfielder usually acting as a deep-lying playmaker.Debuchy started his senior career at Lille, where he won a Ligue 1 and Coupe de France double in 2010–11. [CLS] [SEP] He played in Ligue 1 for 10 seasons for Lille. [CLS] [SEP] He joined Newcastle United in January 2013 and stayed there for 18 months. [CLS] [SEP] He played for Arsenal from July 2014 until January 2018.Debuchy made his debut for the France national team on 7 October 2011. [CLS] [SEP] He has since earned 27 caps, playing in the final tournaments of the Euro 2012 and the 2014 World Cup. [CLS] [SEP]
