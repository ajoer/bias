Ida Noddack (25 February 1896 – 24 September 1978), née Tacke, was a German chemist and physicist. [CLS] [SEP] In 1934 she was the first to mention the idea later named nuclear fission. [CLS] [SEP] With her husband Walter Noddack and Otto Berg she discovered element 75, rhenium. [CLS] [SEP] She was nominated three times for the Nobel Prize in Chemistry. [CLS] [SEP]
