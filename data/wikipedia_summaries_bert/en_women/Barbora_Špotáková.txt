Barbora Špotáková (Czech pronunciation: [ˈbarbora ˈʃpotaːkovaː]; born 30 June 1981) is a Czech track and field athlete who competes in the javelin throw. [CLS] [SEP] She is a two-time Olympic Champion and three-time World Champion, as well as the current world record holder with a throw of 72.28 m. [CLS] [SEP]
