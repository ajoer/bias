[CLS] Leonard Clemence "Leo" Tindemans (Dutch: [ˈleːjoː ˈtɪndəmɑns] (listen); 16 April 1922 – 26 December 2014) was a Belgian politician. [SEP] [CLS] He served as the 43rd Prime Minister of Belgium serving from 25 April 1974 until he resigned as minister on 20 October 1978. [SEP] [CLS] He was a member of the Christian Democratic and Flemish party. [SEP] [CLS]
