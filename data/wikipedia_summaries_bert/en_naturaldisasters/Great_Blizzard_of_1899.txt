The Great Blizzard of 1899 also known as the Great Arctic Outbreak of 1899 and the St. [CLS] [SEP] Valentine's Day Blizzard, was a very severe winter weather event that affected most of the United States, especially east of the Rocky Mountains. [CLS] [SEP] On February 11, Swift Current in present-day Saskatchewan reported a record-high barometric pressure of 31.42 inches of mercury (1,064 mb). [CLS] [SEP]
