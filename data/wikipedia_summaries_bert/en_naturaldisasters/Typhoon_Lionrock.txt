Typhoon Lionrock, known in the Philippines as Typhoon Dindo, was a large, powerful, long-lived and erratic tropical cyclone which caused significant flooding and casualties in North Korea and Japan in late August 2016. [CLS] [SEP] It was the tenth named storm and was the third typhoon of the annual Pacific typhoon season. [CLS] [SEP] Damages recorded after the season were recorded about US$3.93 billion.
