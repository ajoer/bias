[CLS] The 1919 Fergus Falls tornado was a large and destructive tornado that struck Fergus Falls, Minnesota on Sunday, June 22, 1919. [SEP] [CLS] It killed 57 people and is the second deadliest tornado in Minnesota's history (1st was the 1886 Sauk Rapids tornado). [SEP] [CLS] This tornado occurred just 10 months after a tornado in Tyler, Minnesota killed 36 people. [SEP] [CLS] That twister was Minnesota's fourth deadliest on record. [SEP] [CLS]
