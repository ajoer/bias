[CLS] Linea Aeropostal Venezolana Flight 253 was a regularly scheduled passenger flight from Idlewild International Airport, New York to Caracas International Airport in Caracas, Venezuela. [SEP] [CLS] On 20 June 1956, approximately one hour and twenty minutes after departure, the flight crew reported trouble with one of the engines and turned back to New York. [SEP] [CLS] While dumping fuel in preparation for landing, the airplane, a Lockheed L-1049 Super Constellation, caught fire and plummeted into the Atlantic Ocean off Asbury Park, New Jersey. [SEP] [CLS] All 74 aboard were killed; at the time, it was the world's deadliest disaster involving a scheduled commercial flight. [SEP] [CLS] However, its death toll would be surpassed only ten days later. [SEP] [CLS]
