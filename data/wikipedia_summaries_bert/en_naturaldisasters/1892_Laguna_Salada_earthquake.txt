[CLS] The 1892 Laguna Salada earthquake occurred at 23:20 Pacific Standard Time on February 23. [SEP] [CLS] It had an estimated moment magnitude of 7.1–7.2 and a maximum perceived intensity of VIII (Severe). [SEP] [CLS] The shock was centered near the Mexico–United States border and takes its name from a large dry lake bed in Baja California, Mexico. [SEP] [CLS] There were no reported casualties, but the event affected the then largely-uninhabited areas of northern Mexico and Southern California. [SEP] [CLS]
