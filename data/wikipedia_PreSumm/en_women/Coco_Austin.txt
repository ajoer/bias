[CLS] Nicole Natalie Marrow (née Austin) (born March 17, 1979), commonly known as Coco Austin, Coco, Coco Marie Austin, Coco Marie, Coco-T, is an American actress, dancer, glamour model, and web personality. [SEP] [CLS] She has been married to rapper-actor Ice-T (né Tracy Marrow) since January 2002. [SEP] [CLS]
