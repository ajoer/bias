Gertrude Wirschinger, better known as Penny McLean (born 4 October 1948), is an Austrian vocalist who initially gained acclaim with the disco music act Silver Convention, but also had exposure as a single recording artist. [CLS] [SEP] As a solo singer, she is most remembered for her million seller "Lady Bump". [CLS] [SEP] She is also an author. [CLS] [SEP]
