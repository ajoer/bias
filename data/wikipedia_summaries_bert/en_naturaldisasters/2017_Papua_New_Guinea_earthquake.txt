[CLS] The 2017 Papua New Guinea earthquake was an Mww 7.9 earthquake that struck at 04:30 UTC on 22 January 2017. [SEP] [CLS] Three people were killed during the quake and one was injured. [SEP] [CLS] Damage occurred in Arawa and parts of central Bougainville, while a power outage occurred in Buka. [SEP] [CLS]
