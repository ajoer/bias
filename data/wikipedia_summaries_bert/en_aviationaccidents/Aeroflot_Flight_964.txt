Aeroflot Flight 964 was a flight operated by Aeroflot from Kutaisi Airport, Georgia to Domodedovo Airport, Moscow, Russian SFSR. [CLS] [SEP] On 13 October 1973 the Tupolev Tu-104 operating on the route crashed during its approach to Moscow, killing all 122 passengers and crew on board. [CLS] [SEP] It remains the deadliest accident involving a Tupolev Tu-104. [CLS] [SEP]
