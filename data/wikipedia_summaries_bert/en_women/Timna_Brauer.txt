Timna Brauer (Hebrew: תמנע בראואר‎; born May 1, 1961) is an Austrian-Israeli singer-songwriter, and the daughter of painter, writer and singer Arik Brauer. [CLS] [SEP] She collaborates with the Israeli pianist Elias Meiri. [CLS] [SEP] Both have Israeli roots. [CLS] [SEP] They are the parents of daughter Jasmin and son Jonathan. [CLS] [SEP] She represented Austria at the Eurovision Song Contest 1986, singing "Die Zeit ist einsam" (English: Time is Lonely). [CLS] [SEP] Brauer participated in the third season of the Austrian television dance competition Dancing Stars in 2007, coming in tenth place. [CLS] [SEP]
