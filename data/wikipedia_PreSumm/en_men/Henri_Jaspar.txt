[CLS] Henri Jaspar (28 July 1870 – 15 February 1939) was a Belgian Catholic Party politician. [SEP] [CLS] Jaspar was born in Schaerbeek and trained as a lawyer. [SEP] [CLS] He represented Liège as a Catholic in the Belgian Chamber of Representatives from 1919 until 1936. [SEP] [CLS] He helped create the Belgium-Luxembourg Economic Union in 1921, and served as the 27th Prime Minister of Belgium from 1926 to 1931. [SEP] [CLS] In 1924 he was made an honorary Minister of State. [SEP] [CLS] He held several ministerial positions including; Minister of Economic Affairs (1918–1920) Foreign Minister (1920–1924 and 1934) Minister of Finance (1932–1934)
