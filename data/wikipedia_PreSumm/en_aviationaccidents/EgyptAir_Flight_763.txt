[CLS] EgyptAir Flight 763 was an international non-scheduled passenger flight from Cairo, Egypt, to Aden, South Yemen. [SEP] [CLS] On 19 March 1972, the flight was a McDonnell Douglas DC-9-32 registered in Yugoslavia as YU-AHR and operated by the newly renamed EgyptAir. [SEP] [CLS] It crashed into the Shamsan Mountains on approach to Aden, killing all 30 people on board. [SEP] [CLS]
