Ensio Hyytiä (24 March 1938 – 24 March 2019) was a Finnish ski jumper who competed in the late 1950s and early 1960s. [CLS] [SEP] He won the ski jumping silver medal at the 1958 FIS Nordic World Ski Championships in Lahti. [CLS] [SEP] He was born in Rovaniemi. [CLS] [SEP] He also competed at the 1960 Winter Olympics and the 1964 Winter Olympics. [CLS] [SEP]
