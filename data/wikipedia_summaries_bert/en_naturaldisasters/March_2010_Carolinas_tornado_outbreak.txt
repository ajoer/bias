[CLS] The March 2010 Carolinas tornado outbreak was a tornado outbreak that affected the Southeast United States and The Bahamas on March 28, 2010. [SEP] [CLS] A low pressure system pushed northward through the Ohio Valley on March 28. [SEP] [CLS] Several tornadoes were reported in the warm sector stretching from Virginia south to Florida. [SEP] [CLS] The Piedmont Triad was hardest hit. [SEP] [CLS] A tornado emergency was declared for parts of Forsyth, Randolph, Davidson, and Guilford counties. [SEP] [CLS] Damage was also reported near Charlotte. [SEP] [CLS] An EF3 tornado struck High Point, NC. [SEP] [CLS] An EF2 tornado hit Linwood in Davidson County, NC, and 2 more EF2 tornadoes struck south-central South Carolina. [SEP] [CLS]
