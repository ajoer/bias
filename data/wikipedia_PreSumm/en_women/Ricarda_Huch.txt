[CLS] Ricarda Huch (German: [huχ]; 18 July 1864 – 17 November 1947) was a pioneering German intellectual. [SEP] [CLS] Trained as an historian, and the author of many works of European history, she also wrote novels, poems, and a play. [SEP] [CLS] Asteroid 879 Ricarda is named in her honour. [SEP] [CLS] She was nominated for the Nobel Prize in Literature seven times. [SEP] [CLS]
