[CLS] Antonietta Di Martino (born 1 June 1978 in Cava de' Tirreni) is a retired Italian high jumper. [SEP] [CLS] She currently holds the Italian national women's high jump record at 2.03 metres for outdoor events and 2.04 metres for indoor events. [SEP] [CLS] She also currently holds the women's all-time highest jump-differential, meaning she has jumped the highest (0.35 metres) more than her own height. [SEP] [CLS] Her first significant international achievement was winning silver medal at the 2007 European Indoor Championships in Birmingham. [SEP] [CLS] In the same year, she won the silver medal at the 2007 World Championships in Osaka. [SEP] [CLS] After a disappointing Olympics in Beijing, she won her first gold medal at the European Indoor Championships in 2011 in Paris and the bronze medal at the 2011 World Championships in Daegu. [SEP] [CLS] In 2012, she won another silver medal at the World Indoor Championships. [SEP] [CLS]
