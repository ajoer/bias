[CLS] Steffi Nerius (born 1 July 1972) is a retired German track and field athlete who competed in the javelin throw. [SEP] [CLS] During her career, she was a European and World Champion. [SEP] [CLS] She also won silver at the 2004 Summer Olympics. [SEP] [CLS] Her personal best throw was 68.34 m, set in 2008.
