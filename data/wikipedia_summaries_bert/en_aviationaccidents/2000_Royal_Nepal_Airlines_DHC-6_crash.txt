Nepal Airlines Corporation (Nepali: नेपाल वायुसेवा निगम), formerly known as Royal Nepal Airlines (Nepali: शाही नेपाल वायुसेवा), is the flag carrier of Nepal. [CLS] [SEP] Founded in 1958, it is the oldest airline of the country. [CLS] [SEP] Out of its main base at Tribhuvan International Airport, Kathmandu, the airline operates domestic services within Nepal and medium-haul services in Asia. [CLS] [SEP] The airline's first aircraft was a Douglas DC-3, used to serve domestic routes and a handful of destinations in India. [CLS] [SEP] The airline acquired its first jet aircraft, Boeing 727s, in 1972. [CLS] [SEP] As of 13 July 2020, the airline operates a fleet of seven aircraft. [CLS] [SEP] Since 2013, the airline has been on the list of air carriers banned in the European Union. [CLS] [SEP]
