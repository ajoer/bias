Mats Leven is a singer, songwriter and producer. [CLS] [SEP] Some of his most notable collaborations have been with guitar legend Yngwie Malmsteen, symphonic metallers Therion and doom metallers Candlemass. [CLS] [SEP] Mats also started touring with Trans-Siberian Orchestra in 2016. [CLS] [SEP] In 2019 Mats releases his first solo album under the name Skyblood. [CLS] [SEP]
