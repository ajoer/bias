[CLS] Harriet Margaret Louisa Bolus née Kensit (31 July 1877, Burgersdorp – 5 April 1970, Cape Town) was a South African botanist and taxonomist, and the longtime curator of the Bolus Herbarium, from 1903. [SEP] [CLS] Bolus also has the legacy of authoring more land plant species than any other female scientist, in total naming 1,494 species. [SEP] [CLS]
