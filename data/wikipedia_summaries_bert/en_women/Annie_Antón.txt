[CLS] Annie Antón is a Professor in the School of Interactive Computing at Georgia Tech, within its College of Computing. [SEP] [CLS] Antón served as chair of the School of Interactive Computing from 2012 to 2017. [SEP] [CLS] From 1998 to 2012, Antón served as a professor of software engineering at North Carolina State University. [SEP] [CLS] She is the founder and director of ThePrivacyPlace.org, a research center devoted to issues of privacy protection in information systems. [SEP] [CLS] She is known for her research, and for her service in several significant advisory positions in industry and government. [SEP] [CLS]
