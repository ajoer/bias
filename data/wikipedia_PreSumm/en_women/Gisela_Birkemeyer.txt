[CLS] Gisela Birkemeyer (née Köhler on 22 December 1931) is a retired German sprint runner who won two medals in the 80 m hurdles at the 1956 and 1960 Olympics. [SEP] [CLS] During her career she set nine world records in the 80 m hurdles and in the 4×100 m, 4×110 yd and 4×200 m relays. [SEP] [CLS] She won 40 East German championships, mostly in the 80 m hurdles (1953–1961) and 200 m sprint (1956–1960). [SEP] [CLS] At the European Championships in Stockholm in 1958, she was third in the 80 m hurdles. [SEP] [CLS] In 1959, she was voted GDR Sportswoman of the Year.Earlier in 1957 she married Heinz Birkemeyer and since 1960 competed under his name. [SEP] [CLS] After retiring from competitions she worked as a coach. [SEP] [CLS] She now lives as a pensioner in Berlin Marzahn. [SEP] [CLS]
