[CLS] The 1983 Borah Peak earthquake occurred on October 28, at 8:06:09 a.m. [SEP] [CLS] MDT in the western United States, in the Lost River Range at Borah Peak in Central Idaho.The shock measured 6.9 on the moment magnitude scale and had a maximum Mercalli intensity of IX (Violent). [SEP] [CLS] It was the most violent earthquake in the lower 48 states in over 24 years, since the 1959 Hebgen Lake earthquake in nearby southwestern Montana. [SEP] [CLS]
