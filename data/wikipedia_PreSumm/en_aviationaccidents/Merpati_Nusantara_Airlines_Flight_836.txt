[CLS] Merpati Nusantara Airlines Flight 836 was a scheduled domestic flight between Sorong and Manokwari, Indonesia. [SEP] [CLS] On 13 April 2010, the flight, operated by Boeing 737-300 PK-MDE, overran the runway on landing. [SEP] [CLS] The aircraft broke into three pieces. [SEP] [CLS] All people on board survived, although 44 suffered injuries. [SEP] [CLS]
