[CLS] Jean-Claude Killy (born 30 August 1943) is a former French World Cup alpine ski racer. [SEP] [CLS] Born in Saint-Cloud, Hauts-de-Seine, he dominated the sport in the late 1960s. [SEP] [CLS] He was a triple Olympic champion, winning the three alpine events at the 1968 Winter Olympics, becoming the most successful athlete there. [SEP] [CLS] He also won the first two World Cup titles, in 1967 and 1968. [SEP] [CLS]
