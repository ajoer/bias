[CLS] Dana Air Flight 992 was a scheduled domestic commercial passenger flight from Abuja to Lagos, Nigeria. [SEP] [CLS] On 3 June 2012, the McDonnell Douglas MD-83 aircraft serving the route crashed into buildings in Lagos while attempting an emergency landing, killing all 153 people on board and 6 on the ground.The subsequent investigation concluded that both engines had suffered loss of power, and that the associated procedures had not been followed by the crew.
