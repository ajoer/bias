Petra Roth (born 9 May 1944 in Bremen) is a German politician of the Christian Democratic Union (CDU). [CLS] [SEP] She was the mayor of Frankfurt am Main from 1995 to 2012. [CLS] [SEP] In addition she twice served as president of the Deutscher Städtetag, resuming her previous post there in 2009.
