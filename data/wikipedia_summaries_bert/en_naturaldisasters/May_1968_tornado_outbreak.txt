The Tornado outbreak of May 1968 was a significant and deadly tornado outbreak that struck most of the central and southern United States from May 15 to May 16, 1968. [CLS] [SEP] Producing 46 tornadoes, the outbreak killed at least 72 people, including 45 in Arkansas alone. [CLS] [SEP] The outbreak also produced two F5 tornadoes in Iowa. [CLS] [SEP] It was one of the deadliest tornado outbreaks in the United States since the 1960s and is one of the deadliest outbreaks in Iowa history. [CLS] [SEP]
