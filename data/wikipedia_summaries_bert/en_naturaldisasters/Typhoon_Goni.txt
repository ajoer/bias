The name Goni has been used to name three tropical cyclones in the northwestern Pacific Ocean. [CLS] [SEP] In addition, the variant Koni was used in 2003 before the spelling was corrected by the WMO Typhoon Committee. [CLS] [SEP] The name was contributed by South Korea and is a Korean word for swan. [CLS] [SEP] Severe Tropical Storm Koni (2003) (T0308, 08W, Gilas) Tropical Storm Goni (2009) (T0907, 08W, Jolina) Typhoon Goni (2015) (T1515, 16W, Ineng)
