[CLS] On 11 February 1990, an active service unit of the Provisional IRA East Tyrone Brigade shot down a British Army Gazelle helicopter (serial number ZB687) along the border between Northern Ireland and the Republic of Ireland. [SEP] [CLS] It took place between Augher in County Tyrone and Derrygorry in County Monaghan. [SEP] [CLS] The helicopter was hit several times by heavy machine-gun fire and crash-landed on an open field, injuring three members of its crew of four. [SEP] [CLS]
