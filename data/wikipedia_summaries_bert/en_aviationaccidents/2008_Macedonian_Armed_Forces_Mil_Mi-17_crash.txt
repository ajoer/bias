The Mil Mi-17 (NATO reporting name: Hip) is a Soviet/Russian helicopter in production at two factories in Kazan and Ulan-Ude. [CLS] [SEP] It is known as the Mi-8M series in Russian service. [CLS] [SEP] It is a medium twin-turbine transport helicopter. [CLS] [SEP] There are also armed gunship versions. [CLS] [SEP]
