Southeast Airlines Flight 308 was an American domestic flight operated by Southeast Airlines between Knoxville and Bristol, Tennessee, in the United States. [CLS] [SEP] On 8 January 1959 it was flown by a Douglas DC-3A registered N18941. [CLS] [SEP] On approach to Tri-Cities Regional Airport at Bristol, the aircraft crashed into the Holston Mountain range with the loss of all on board. [CLS] [SEP]
