The name Matmo has been used to name three tropical cyclones in the Western North Pacific Ocean. [CLS] [SEP] It means "heavy rain" in the Chamorro language. [CLS] [SEP] Matmo replaced Chataan after the damaging 2002 typhoon. [CLS] [SEP] Tropical Storm Matmo (2008) (T0803, 04W, Dindo) Typhoon Matmo (2014) (T1410, 10W, Henry) Severe Tropical Storm Matmo (2019) (T1422, 23W)
