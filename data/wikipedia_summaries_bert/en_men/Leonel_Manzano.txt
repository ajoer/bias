Leonel "Leo" Manzano (born September 12, 1984) is a retired Mexican-American middle-distance track and field athlete specializing in the 1500 m and mile. [CLS] [SEP] He was a silver medalist at the 2012 Summer Olympics. [CLS] [SEP] Manzano is known for finishing his races with a strong finishing kick. [CLS] [SEP]
