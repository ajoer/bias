[CLS] Hurricane Cleo was the strongest tropical cyclone of the 1964 Atlantic hurricane season. [SEP] [CLS] It was the third named storm, first hurricane, and first major hurricane of the season. [SEP] [CLS] Cleo was one of the longest-lived storms of the season. [SEP] [CLS] This compact yet powerful hurricane travelled through the Caribbean Sea and later hit Florida before moving offshore Georgia into the Carolinas, killing 156 people and causing roughly $187 million in damage. [SEP] [CLS] Major damage was seen as far north as east-central Florida, with the heaviest rains falling along the immediate coast of the Southeast United States into southeast Virginia. [SEP] [CLS]
