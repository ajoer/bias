[CLS] On 4 July 2002 a Boeing 707-123B operated by Prestige Airlines and owned by New Gomair, crashed during an emergency landing at Bangui Airport. [SEP] [CLS] 28 people on board were killed and two survived. [SEP] [CLS] The flight was bound to Brazzaville, but the crew decided to divert to Bangui when the landing gear had not retracted.
