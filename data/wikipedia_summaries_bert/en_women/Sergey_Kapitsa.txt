Sergey Petrovich Kapitsa (Russian: Сергей Петрович Капица; 14 February 1928 – 14 August 2012) was a Russian physicist and demographer. [CLS] [SEP] He was best known as host of the popular and long-running Russian scientific TV show, Evident, but Incredible. [CLS] [SEP] His father was the Nobel laureate Soviet-era physicist Pyotr Kapitsa, and his brother was the geographer and Antarctic explorer Andrey Kapitsa. [CLS] [SEP]
