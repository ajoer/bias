Ikechi Anya (born 3 January 1988) is a Scottish professional footballer who plays as a midfielder. [CLS] [SEP] A versatile player, he has been fielded in a number of other positions, including winger, wing-back and full-back. [CLS] [SEP] Anya has previously played for Wycombe Wanderers and Northampton Town and Watford, as well as Spanish sides Celta de Vigo, Sevilla Atlético and Granada. [CLS] [SEP] Eligible to also represent Romania, Nigeria or England internationally, he chose to play for the country of his birth, Scotland. [CLS] [SEP] He received his first cap for the Scottish national team on 6 September 2013, coming off the bench in a 2–0 defeat to Belgium and scored his first international goal four days later in Scotland's following match away to Macedonia.
