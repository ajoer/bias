[CLS] On January 15, 1987, at 12:52 local time, SkyWest Airlines Flight 1834 a Swearingen SA-226TC (METRO II), and a Mooney M20 were involved in a midair collision near Kearns, Utah. [SEP] [CLS] The two pilots and six passengers aboard the METRO II and the two pilots aboard the Mooney were killed. [SEP] [CLS] NTSB investigators primarily blamed the small plane pilots for wandering into restricted airspace, but a judge later assigned 51% responsibility to FAA air traffic controllers.
