[SEP] [CLS] He was the second son and the only surviving child of his predecessor Guangzong and like his father, Ningzong was weak-minded; easily dominated by women. [SEP] [CLS] During Ningzong's reign, he had built 75 commemorative shrines and steles, the most in Song history. [SEP] [CLS] He was a great patron of art, promoting artists such as Liang Kai and Ma Yuan to painter-in-waiting and writing poems about their paintings. [SEP] [CLS] Upon Ningzong's death, a minor official and a relative of Ningzong became Emperor Lizong. [SEP] [CLS]
