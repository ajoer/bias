Miss World 2018, the 68th edition of the Miss World pageant, was held on 8 December 2018 at the Sanya City Arena in Sanya, China. [CLS] [SEP] Manushi Chhillar of India crowned her successor Vanessa Ponce of Mexico at the end of the event. [CLS] [SEP] She is the first Mexican woman to win Miss World. [CLS] [SEP]
