[CLS] American Eagle Flight 4184 was a scheduled domestic passenger flight from Indianapolis, Indiana to Chicago, Illinois, United States. [SEP] [CLS] On October 31, 1994, the ATR 72 performing this route flew into severe icing conditions, lost control and crashed into a field. [SEP] [CLS] All 68 people aboard were killed in the high-speed impact. [SEP] [CLS]
