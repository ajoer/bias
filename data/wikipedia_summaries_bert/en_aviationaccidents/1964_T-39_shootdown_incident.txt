On 28 January 1964, an unarmed T-39 Sabreliner aircraft of the United States Air Force was shot down while on a training mission over Erfurt, East Germany, by a MiG-19 jet fighter of the Soviet Air Force. [CLS] [SEP] The occupants of the aircraft were Lieutenant Colonel Gerald K. [CLS] [SEP] Hannaford, Captain Donald Grant Millard and Captain John F. [CLS] [SEP] Lorraine. [CLS] [SEP] All three died, becoming direct casualties of the Cold War in Europe. [CLS] [SEP]
