[CLS] Haankhes (ḥ3-ˁnḫ=s, "may she live") was an ancient Egyptian queen consort of the 17th Dynasty, likely a wife of Pharaoh Sekhemre-Heruhirmaat Intef.She is only known from a stela of her son Prince Ameni. [SEP] [CLS] The stela was found in Koptos and it may be originally from Dendera; one half of it is in the Petrie Museum of Egyptian Archaeology, the other is in the Pushkin Museum. [SEP] [CLS] Ameni married Princess Sobekemheb, a daughter of Sobekemsaf I and Nubemhat.Her only known title is "King's wife" (ḥmt-nỉswt). [SEP] [CLS]
