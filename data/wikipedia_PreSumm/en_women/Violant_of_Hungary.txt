[CLS] Violant of Hungary (Hungarian: Jolán; Catalan: Iolanda or Violant d'Hongria; Spanish: Yolanda or Violante de Hungría; c. [SEP] [CLS] 1215 – c. [SEP] [CLS] 1251) was the queen of Aragon from 1235 until 1251 as the second wife of King James I of Aragon. [SEP] [CLS] A member of the Hungarian House of Árpád, Queen Violant was a valuable and influential advisor of her husband. [SEP] [CLS] She remains in folk memory in Catalonia and Valencia.
