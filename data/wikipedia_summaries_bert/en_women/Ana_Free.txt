Ana Gomes Ferreira, known professionally as Ana Free, Lisboa, June 29, 1987), is a singer, musician, songwriter and performer who has had a series of top-five hit singles, including 4 number one hits, in Portugal. [CLS] [SEP] She has a growing musical presence on the popular video sharing website YouTube, gaining over 40.1 million views to date. [CLS] [SEP] Ana Free's single "Electrical Storm" from her debut album "Together" occupied the number 1 spot on the MTV Portugal Top 50 National Music Chart. [CLS] [SEP]
