On 27 October 2018, an AgustaWestland AW169 helicopter crashed shortly after take-off from the King Power Stadium, the home ground of Leicester City Football Club in Leicester, England, United Kingdom. [CLS] [SEP] All five people on board – the pilot and four passengers, including club owner Vichai Srivaddhanaprabha – were killed. [CLS] [SEP] The Air Accidents Investigation Branch attributed the crash to a loss of yaw control owing to a failure of the tail rotor control linkage. [CLS] [SEP]
