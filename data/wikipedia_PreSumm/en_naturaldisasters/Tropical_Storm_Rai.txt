[CLS] Tropical Storm Rai was a weak and short-lived tropical cyclone which affected Indochina in September 2016. [SEP] [CLS] Formed from a tropical disturbance on September 11, the system developed into a tropical storm and reached its peak intensity on September 12, before making landfall in Vietnam and affecting Laos, Thailand and Cambodia. [SEP] [CLS] In Vietnam total damage reached US$37 million. [SEP] [CLS]
