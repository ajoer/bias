[CLS] Judith "Judy" L. [SEP] [CLS] Estrin (born 1954/1955) is an American entrepreneur, business executive, and philanthropist. [SEP] [CLS] Estrin worked with Vinton Cerf on the Transmission Control Protocol project at Stanford University in the 1970s. [SEP] [CLS] Estrin is an entrepreneur who co-founded eight technology companies. [SEP] [CLS] She was the chief technology officer of Cisco Systems from 1998 to 2000. [SEP] [CLS] She is currently CEO of JLABS, LLC, a privately held company focused on furthering innovation in business, government, and nonprofit organizations. [SEP] [CLS]
