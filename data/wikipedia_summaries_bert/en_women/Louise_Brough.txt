Althea Louise Brough Clapp (née Brough; March 11, 1923 – February 3, 2014) was an American tennis player. [CLS] [SEP] In her career between 1939 and 1959, she won six Grand Slam singles titles as well as numerous doubles and mixed-doubles titles. [CLS] [SEP] At the end of the 1955 tennis season, Lance Tingay of the London Daily Telegraph ranked her world No. [CLS] [SEP] 1 for the year. [CLS] [SEP]
