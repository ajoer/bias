[CLS] The 1995 Wuding earthquake occurred on October 23, 1995, at 22:46 UTC (October 24, 1995, at 06:46 local time). [SEP] [CLS] The epicenter was located near Fenduo Village (芬多村), Fawo Township (发窝乡) of the Wuding County, Yunnan, China. [SEP] [CLS] The magnitude of the earthquake was put at Mw 6.2, or Ms 6.5. [SEP] [CLS] 53 people were reported dead and 13,903 injured. [SEP] [CLS] Many houses and public buildings were damaged, including the Fawo Middle School (发窝中学) and the Fawo Township Office. [SEP] [CLS] This earthquake could be felt in southwestern Sichuan. [SEP] [CLS]
