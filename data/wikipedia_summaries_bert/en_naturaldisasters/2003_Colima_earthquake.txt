The 2003 Colima earthquake occurred on 21 January with a moment magnitude of 7.5 and a maximum Mercalli intensity of VIII (Severe). [CLS] [SEP] The epicenter was located on the Pacific coast in the Mexican state of Colima. [CLS] [SEP] The earthquake was felt as far away as Mexico City and in southern parts of the United States.
