Anna Sundström, born as Anna Christina Persdotter, (26 February 1785 in Kymlinge, Spånga – 1871), was a Swedish chemist. [CLS] [SEP] She was the assistant of the chemist Jöns Jacob Berzelius from 1808 to 1836. [CLS] [SEP] Anna Sundström has been referred to as the first female chemist in Sweden. [CLS] [SEP]
