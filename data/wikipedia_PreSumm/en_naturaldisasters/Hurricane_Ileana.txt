[CLS] The name Ileana has been used for five tropical cyclones in the Eastern Pacific Ocean. [SEP] [CLS] The name replaced Iva, which was retired after the 1988 season. [SEP] [CLS] Hurricane Ileana (1994) – a Category 1 hurricane that affected the Baja California Peninsula. [SEP] [CLS] Tropical Storm Ileana (2000) – affected the coast of Mexico. [SEP] [CLS] Hurricane Ileana (2006) – a Category 3 hurricane that briefly threatened Mexico but turned away. [SEP] [CLS] Hurricane Ileana (2012) – a Category 1 hurricane that did not affect land. [SEP] [CLS] Tropical Storm Ileana (2018) – briefly affected the coast of Mexico, before being absorbed by Hurricane John. [SEP] [CLS]
