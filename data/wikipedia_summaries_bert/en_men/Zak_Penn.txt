Zak Penn (born March 23, 1968) is an American screenwriter. [CLS] [SEP] Penn wrote and directed Incident at Loch Ness and The Grand, and co-wrote the scripts for X2, X-Men: The Last Stand, and the story for The Avengers. [CLS] [SEP] With Michael Karnow, Penn is the co-creator of the TV series Alphas on the Syfy network. [CLS] [SEP]
