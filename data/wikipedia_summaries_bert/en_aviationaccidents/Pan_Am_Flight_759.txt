Pan Am Flight 759 was a regularly scheduled domestic passenger flight from Miami to San Diego, with en route stops in New Orleans and Las Vegas. [CLS] [SEP] On July 9, 1982, the Boeing 727 flying this route crashed in the New Orleans suburb of Kenner after being forced down by a microburst shortly after takeoff. [CLS] [SEP] All 145 on board and 8 people on the ground were killed. [CLS] [SEP] The crash had the highest number of aviation fatalities in 1982. [CLS] [SEP]
