The 2009 India floods affected various states of India in July 2009, killing at least 36 people in Orissa and 13 in Kerala. [CLS] [SEP] The most affected states were Karnataka, Orissa, Kerala, Gujarat and North-East Indian states. [CLS] [SEP] Floods triggered by heavy monsoon rains killed at least 36 people in the eastern Indian state of Orissa alone and inundated half a million homes. [CLS] [SEP] On 13 July, seven people were killed and many others missing when a bus fell into a rivulet after being swept away by flood waters in Nayagarh district in Orissa. [CLS] [SEP] Nayagarh is 87 km from the Orissa state capital, Bhubaneswar. [CLS] [SEP] The world-famous Sun Temple at Konark is also water-logged, causing hardship for tourists.
