[CLS] The 1688 Sannio earthquake occurred in the late afternoon of June 5 in the province of Benevento of southern Italy. [SEP] [CLS] The moment magnitude is estimated at 7, with a Mercalli intensity of XI. [SEP] [CLS] It severely damaged numerous towns in a vast area, completely destroying Cerreto Sannita and Guardia Sanframondi. [SEP] [CLS] The exact number of victims is unknown, and is estimated at about 10,000. [SEP] [CLS] It is among the most destructive earthquakes in the history of Italy. [SEP] [CLS]
