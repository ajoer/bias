[CLS] Öljei Temür Khan (Mongolian: Өлзийтөмөр хаан; Chinese: 完者帖木兒汗), Bunyashir Khan (full name: Bunyashiri; Chinese: 本雅失里; died 1412) was the Mongol khan of the Northern Yuan dynasty based in Mongolia. [SEP] [CLS] He was a son of Elbeg Nigülesügchi Khan and younger brother of Gün Temür Khan. [SEP] [CLS] He was one of the Borjigin princes, such as Tokhtamysh and Temür Qutlugh, backed by Timur to seize the throne. [SEP] [CLS]
