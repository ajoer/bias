The 2003 Bachu earthquake occurred on 24 February at 10:03 local time in the Xinjiang Autonomous Region in northwest China. [CLS] [SEP] The epicentre was located near to the town of Jiashi and Bachu County, approximately 105 km east of Kashgar and 310 km west of Aksu. [CLS] [SEP] At least 261 people died as a result of the earthquake, and more than 4,000 were injured. [CLS] [SEP] Most of the casualties occurred in Bachu county. [CLS] [SEP] Almost 10,000 homes were levelled by the earthquake. [CLS] [SEP] A further 5 people were killed in powerful aftershocks which occurred on 26 February 2003. [CLS] [SEP]
