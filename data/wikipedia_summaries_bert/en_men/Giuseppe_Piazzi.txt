[CLS] Giuseppe Piazzi (US: PYAHT-see, Italian: [dʒuˈzɛppe ˈpjattsi]; 16 July 1746 – 22 July 1826) was an Italian Catholic priest of the Theatine order, mathematician, and astronomer. [SEP] [CLS] He established an observatory at Palermo, now the Osservatorio Astronomico di Palermo – Giuseppe S. [SEP] [CLS] Vaiana. [SEP] [CLS] Perhaps his most famous discovery was the first dwarf planet, Ceres. [SEP] [CLS]
