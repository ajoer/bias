X-Perience is a eurodance band from Berlin, Germany. [CLS] [SEP] Their music style varies between synthpop, trance and ethnopop. [CLS] [SEP] The band members are Claudia Uhle (vocals, lyrics), Matthias Uhle (composition, keyboards) and Alexander Kaiser (lyrics, keyboards). [CLS] [SEP] Singer Claudia Uhle, Matthias' sister, left the band in 2007 and was replaced by Manja Kaletka, but returned in 2019. [CLS] [SEP]
