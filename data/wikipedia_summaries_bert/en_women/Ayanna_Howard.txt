Ayanna MacCalla Howard (born January 24, 1972) is an American roboticist and the School Chair for Interactive Computing, Georgia Institute of Technology. [CLS] [SEP] She is also the Linda J. [CLS] [SEP] and Mark C. [CLS] [SEP] Smith Endowed Chair in Bioengineering in the School of Electrical and Computer Engineering, and the director of the Human-Automation Systems (HumAnS) Lab. [CLS] [SEP] Currently, she is the Chair of the School of Interactive Computing in the Georgia Tech College of Computing. [CLS] [SEP]
