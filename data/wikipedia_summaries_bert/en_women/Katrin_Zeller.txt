[CLS] Katrin Zeller (born March 1, 1979 in Oberstdorf, Bavaria, West Germany) is a German cross-country skier who competed between 1997 and 2014. [SEP] [CLS] She won a silver medal in the 4 × 5 km relay at the 2010 Winter Olympics in Vancouver, Canada. [SEP] [CLS] Zeller also won a silver medal in the 4 × 5 km relay at the FIS Nordic World Ski Championships 2009 in Liberec. [SEP] [CLS] Her best individual finish at the championships was 17th in the 7.5 km + 7.5 km double pursuit at those same championships. [SEP] [CLS] Her best individual World Cup finish was third in a 10 km event at Lahti in 2008. [SEP] [CLS] She has seven total victories at various levels in her career from 2003 to 2008.
