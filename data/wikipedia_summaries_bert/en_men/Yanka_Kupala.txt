[CLS] Yanka Kupala (also spelled Janka Kupała, Belarusian: Я́нка Купа́ла; July 7 [O.S. [SEP] [CLS] June 25] 1882 – June 28, 1942) – was the pen name of Iván Daminíkavich Lutsévich (Ivan Daminikavič Łucevič, Belarusian: Іва́н Даміні́кавіч Луцэ́віч), a Belarusian poet and writer. [SEP] [CLS] He is considered one of the greatest Belarusian-language writers of the 20th century. [SEP] [CLS]
