Kālidāsa was a Classical Sanskrit author. [CLS] [SEP] His plays and poetry are primarily based on the Vedas, the Ramayana, the Mahabharata and the Puranas. [CLS] [SEP] His surviving works consist of three plays, two epic poems and two shorter poems. [CLS] [SEP] Much about his life is unknown, only what can be inferred from his poetry and plays. [CLS] [SEP] His works cannot be dated with precision, but they were most likely authored before 5th century CE. [CLS] [SEP]
