[CLS] The United Airlines Flight 663 incident was a "minor international incident" in 2010 involving Qatari diplomat Mohammed Yacoub Al Madadi on the leg of a United Airlines flight from Ronald Reagan Washington National Airport (Washington, DC) to Denver International Airport (Denver, Colorado). [SEP] [CLS] The diplomat prompted a mid-air terrorism alert after smoking in the aircraft lavatory, which led the Qatari government to recall him two days later. [SEP] [CLS] United no longer uses Flight 663 as a DCA-DEN-LAS route. [SEP] [CLS] UA663 is now used on the Houston-Edmonton route, flown by an Airbus A320. [SEP] [CLS]
