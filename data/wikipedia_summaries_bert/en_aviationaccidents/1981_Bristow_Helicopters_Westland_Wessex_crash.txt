G-ASWI was a Westland Wessex 60, operated by Bristow Helicopters operating between Bacton Gas Terminal, in Norfolk, and Amoco gas platforms in the North Sea. [CLS] [SEP] On 13 August 1981 the helicopter lost power to the main rotor gearbox, going out of control during the ensuing autorotation. [CLS] [SEP] The flight was carrying 11 gas workers from the Leman gas field to Bacton. [CLS] [SEP] All people on board were lost. [CLS] [SEP]
