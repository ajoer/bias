[CLS] Elena Alexandra Apostoleanu (born 16 October 1986), known professionally as Inna ( E-n-ə; stylized in all caps), is a Romanian singer and songwriter. [SEP] [CLS] Born in Mangalia and raised in Neptun, she studied political science at Ovidius University before meeting the Romanian trio Play & Win and pursuing a music career. [SEP] [CLS] She adopted the stage name "Alessandra" in 2008 and adopted a pop-rock style; later that year, she changed her stage name to "Inna" and began releasing house music. [SEP] [CLS] "Hot" (2008), her debut single, was a commercial success worldwide and topped the Romanian and Billboard's Hot Dance Airplay chart, among others. [SEP] [CLS] Inna's debut studio album of the same name followed in August 2009 and was certified Gold and Platinum. [SEP] [CLS] It featured several other successful singles in Europe, including "Amazing" (2009), her second number one single in Romania. [SEP] [CLS] Inna's second album, I Am the Club Rocker (2011), yielded global success for the single "Sun Is Up" (2010). [SEP] [CLS] The track won the Eurodanceweb Award, making Inna the first and only Romanian artist to win the award. [SEP] [CLS] In 2011, it was announced Inna was the highest-paid Romanian and Eastern European artist. [SEP] [CLS] Her follow-up studio album, Party Never Ends (2013), was nominated for two consecutive years for Best Album at the Romanian Music Awards and reached the top ten in Mexico. [SEP] [CLS] It featured "More than Friends", a moderate European hit in collaboration with Daddy Yankee. [SEP] [CLS] In 2014, Inna signed with Atlantic Records and released the commercially successful "Cola Song" with J Balvin, which was used to promote that year's FIFA World Cup. [SEP] [CLS] Inna's fourth and eponymous studio album was released in October 2015 and included "Diggy Down", the singer's third number-one single in Romania. [SEP] [CLS] Beginning in 2017, Inna has been a coach on the talent show Vocea României Junior alongside Andra and Marius Moga. [SEP] [CLS] In the same year, she also released her fifth record Nirvana, whose singles found chart success in European countries such as Romania and Turkey. [SEP] [CLS] She signed a record deal with Roc Nation in 2018 to release her sixth studio album Yo in May 2019. [SEP] [CLS] Entirely envisioned by Inna, the Spanish-language effort marks a change in direction for her, as she approaches experimental and gypsy-influenced music styles. [SEP] [CLS] Her first single outside the Yo era, "Bebe", reached number one in Romania in March 2020. [SEP] [CLS] With global album sales of four million copies from her first three studio albums, Inna is the best-selling Romanian artist. [SEP] [CLS] She has received several awards and nominations, including the Balkan Music Awards, European Border Breakers Award, MTV Europe Music Awards and the Romanian Music Awards. [SEP] [CLS] Inna is a human rights activist, participating in campaigns against domestic violence and in support of children's and LGBT rights. [SEP] [CLS]
