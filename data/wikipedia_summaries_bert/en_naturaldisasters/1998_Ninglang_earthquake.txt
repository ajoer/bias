The 1998 Ninglang earthquake (1998年宁蒗地震) occurred on November 19 at 19:38 local time. [CLS] [SEP] The epicenter was near the border between the provinces of Sichuan and Yunnan, China. [CLS] [SEP] The United States Geological Survey stated that the epicenter was located in the Sichuan Province, while the China Earthquake Data Center provided a different location in Lanniqing Township (烂泥箐乡), Ninglang, Yunnan Province. [CLS] [SEP] The magnitude of the earthquake was put at Ms 6.2 and it caused five deaths, with 208 seriously injured in Yunnan and 20 people seriously injured in Sichuan. [CLS] [SEP] Building damage was reported in Ninglang, Yunnan and Yanyuan, Sichuan. [CLS] [SEP] A dammed lake was formed by a landslide in the Lanniqing Township.The earthquake occurred around the time of New Year Festival of the Yi people. [CLS] [SEP] The lowest temperature in the mountainous area of Ninglang had dropped to about 0 °C at night. [CLS] [SEP] Around 30,000 people lived outside. [CLS] [SEP] The local government distributed tents and blankets in the earthquake stricken areas. [CLS] [SEP] The Japanese government provided 200,000 US dollars.The focal mechanism was of right-lateral strike-slip faulting in the NNW direction. [CLS] [SEP] The earthquake was preceded by prominent foreshocks, the largest of which occurred on October 2 at 12:49 UTC (20:49 local time) with a magnitude of Ms 5.3. [CLS] [SEP] The aftershocks were distributed in a belt in the NW direction. [CLS] [SEP] Underground water anomalies were recorded before the earthquake. [CLS] [SEP]
