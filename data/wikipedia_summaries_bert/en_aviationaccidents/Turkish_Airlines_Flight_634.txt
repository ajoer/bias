[CLS] Turkish Airlines Flight 634 was a scheduled domestic passenger flight from Turkish Airlines' hub at Istanbul Atatürk Airport to Diyarbakır Airport in southeastern Turkey. [SEP] [CLS] On 8 January 2003 at 20:19 EET (18:19 UTC), the aircraft operating the flight, a British Aerospace Avro RJ100, struck the ground on final approach approximately 900 metres (3,000 ft) short of the runway threshold during inclement weather conditions. [SEP] [CLS] In the following collision with a slope, a post-crash fire broke out, killing 75 of the 80 occupants, including both pilots. [SEP] [CLS]
