[CLS] Vladivostok Air Flight 352 was a scheduled passenger flight from Yekaterinburg, Russia to Vladivostok via Irkutsk. [SEP] [CLS] On 4 July 2001, the plane operating the flight, a Tupolev Tu-154M with tail number RA-85845, lost control, stalled, and crashed while approaching Irkutsk Airport. [SEP] [CLS] All 136 passengers and 9 flight crew members aboard perished, making it the third deadliest aircraft crash over Russian territory to date after Aeroflot Flight 3352 and Aeroflot Flight 217. [SEP] [CLS] At the time, it was the 5th deadliest accident involving a Tupolev Tu-154; it is currently the 7th deadliest. [SEP] [CLS]
