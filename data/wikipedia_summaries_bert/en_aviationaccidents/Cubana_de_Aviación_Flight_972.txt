Cubana de Aviación Flight 972 was a scheduled domestic flight from José Martí International Airport, Havana, Cuba, to Frank País Airport in Holguín, Cuba. [CLS] [SEP] On May 18, 2018, the 39-year-old Boeing 737-201 Adv. [CLS] [SEP] operating the route crashed shortly after takeoff, near Santiago de las Vegas, 19 kilometres (12 mi; 10 nmi) from Havana city centre. [CLS] [SEP] Of those on board, 112 died and one passenger survived with critical injuries. [CLS] [SEP] There were initially four survivors, but three of them later died at a local hospital. [CLS] [SEP] Most passengers on board were Cuban nationals, and the whole crew was Mexican. [CLS] [SEP] An investigation into the incident involving the United States and Mexico determined in September 2019 that the aircraft was outside centre of gravity limits.
