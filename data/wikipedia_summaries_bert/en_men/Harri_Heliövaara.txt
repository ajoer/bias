Harri Heliövaara (born 4 June 1989) is a Finnish tennis player. [CLS] [SEP] His highest ranking in singles is 194 which was achieved on 19 December 2011, and in doubles 128 which was achieved on 16 July 2012. [CLS] [SEP] He won the 2007 Australian Open boys' doubles title with Graeme Dyce. [CLS] [SEP] In singles he has won eight ITF Futures tournaments. [CLS] [SEP] In doubles he has won two ATP Challenger tournaments, first in Tashkent, Uzbekistan in 2011 and second in Båstad, Sweden in 2018. [CLS] [SEP] He has also won 19 ITF Futures tournaments in doubles. [CLS] [SEP]
