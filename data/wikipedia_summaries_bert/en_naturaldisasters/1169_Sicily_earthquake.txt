[CLS] The 1169 Sicily earthquake occurred on 4 February 1169 at 08:00 local time on the eve of the feast of St. [SEP] [CLS] Agatha of Sicily (in southern Italy). [SEP] [CLS] It had an estimated magnitude of between 6.4 and 7.3 and an estimated maximum perceived intensity of X (Extreme) on the Mercalli intensity scale. [SEP] [CLS] Catania, Lentini and Modica were severely damaged. [SEP] [CLS] It triggered a tsunami. [SEP] [CLS] Overall, the earthquake is estimated to have caused the deaths of at least 15,000 people. [SEP] [CLS]
