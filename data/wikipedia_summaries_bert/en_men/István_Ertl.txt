[CLS] Libera Folio (English: Free Folio) is an online Esperanto periodical about the Esperanto movement independent from the Universal Esperanto Association, launched on 12 April 2003. [SEP] [CLS] It aims to shed light on current developments in the movement soberly and critically. [SEP] [CLS] István Ertl and Kalle Kniivilä founded it, with Kniivilä as editor. [SEP] [CLS] Jan-Ulrich Hasecke was technical director from 2004 to 2016. [SEP] [CLS] In September 2018 Jukka Pietiläinen replaced Kniivilä as editor. [SEP] [CLS]
