Pedro Elías Pablo Montt Montt (American Spanish: [ˈpeðɾo mõnd]; 29 June 1849, Santiago, Chile – 16 August 1910, Bremen, Germany) was a Chilean political figure. [CLS] [SEP] He served as the president of Chile from 1906 to his death from a probable stroke in 1910. [CLS] [SEP] His government furthered railroad and manufacturing activities but ignored pressing social and labour problems. [CLS] [SEP]
