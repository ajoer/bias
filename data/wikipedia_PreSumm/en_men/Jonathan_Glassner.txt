[CLS] Jonathan Glassner is a television writer, director, and producer. [SEP] [CLS] He is known for his involvement with Stargate SG-1 (where he was executive producer for the first three seasons) and The Outer Limits. [SEP] [CLS] Glassner was initially noticed as a writer for his work on Alfred Hitchcock Presents. [SEP] [CLS] After writing for several television series, including 21 Jump Street, Glassner moved on to The Outer Limits, which naturally segued into his involvement with Stargate SG-1 as a staff writer and executive producer. [SEP] [CLS] His most recent work is as a writer and director for CSI: Miami, CSI: NY, and other shows. [SEP] [CLS] He was also a Co-Executive Producer on the NBC show Heist and the FOX show Standoff, as well as for Sci-Fi Channel's The Invisible Man. [SEP] [CLS] While at Northwestern University, Glassner also played a leading role in the development and production of Rising Stars, which became the model upon which Star Search was developed.
