[CLS] Stacy Lewis (born February 16, 1985) is an American professional golfer on the U.S.-based LPGA Tour. [SEP] [CLS] She has won two major championships: the Kraft Nabisco Championship in 2011 and the Women's British Open in 2013. [SEP] [CLS] She was ranked number one in the Women's World Golf Rankings for four weeks in 2013, and reclaimed the position in June 2014 with a victory at the ShopRite LPGA Classic for another 21 weeks.
