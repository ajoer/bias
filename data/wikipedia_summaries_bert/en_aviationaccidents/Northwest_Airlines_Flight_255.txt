Northwest Airlines Flight 255, a McDonnell Douglas MD-82, crashed shortly after takeoff from Detroit Metropolitan Airport on August 16, 1987, at about 8:46 p.m. [CLS] [SEP] EDT (00:46 UTC August 17), killing all six crew members and 148 of its 149 passengers, along with two people on the ground. [CLS] [SEP] The sole survivor was a 4-year-old girl who sustained serious injuries. [CLS] [SEP] It was the second-deadliest aviation accident at the time in the United States. [CLS] [SEP]
