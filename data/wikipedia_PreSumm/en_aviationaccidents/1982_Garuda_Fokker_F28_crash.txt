[CLS] The 1982 Garuda Fokker F28 crash (flight number unknown) occurred on 20 March 1982, when a Fokker F28, operated by Garuda Indonesia, overran the runway at Tanjung Karang-Branti Airport (Lampung) in the province of Lampung in Indonesia during very heavy rain. [SEP] [CLS] The plane was scheduled to fly from Jakarta to Lampung. [SEP] [CLS] The aircraft came to rest just 700 meters from the runway in a field, with the aircraft catching fire. [SEP] [CLS] All passengers and crew died. [SEP] [CLS]
