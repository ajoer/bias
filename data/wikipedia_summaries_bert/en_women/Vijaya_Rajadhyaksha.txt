Vijaya Rajadhyaksha (Devanagari: विजया राजाध्यक्ष) (born 1933) is a Marathi writer from Maharashtra, India. [CLS] [SEP] She studied at Rajaram College in Kolhapur, and obtained her doctorate from Mumbai University. [CLS] [SEP] Rajadhyaksha taught Marathi literature at Elphinstone College affiliated with Mumbai University, and then served as the head of Marathi department in SNDT Women's University, also in Mumbai.
