[CLS] Sandy Mölling (born 27 April 1981) is a German singer-songwriter, actress and television personality. [SEP] [CLS] Born in Wuppertal and raised in Koblenz, she performed in various singing competitions and became a studio singer in her teenage years. [SEP] [CLS] Mölling later rose to fame as one of the members of the girl group No Angels, which were formed in 2000 on the German television talent show Popstars, becoming one of the best-selling girl groups of European origin of all time. [SEP] [CLS] During their hiatus, Mölling released a solo album, Unexpected (2004), which produced the top ten singles "Unnatural Blonde" and "Tell Me", and became a judge on the fourth season of Popstars. [SEP] [CLS] In 2006, she released her second studio album Frame of Mind to moderate success and finished third on the debut season of Let's Dance. [SEP] [CLS] The following year, she became a presenter and actress in several short-living television shows on Sat.1, Das Vierte, and VIVA Germany before serving as a judge on Die singende Firma. [SEP] [CLS] In 2010, Mölling made her stage debut in the musical Vom Geist der Weihnacht, which led her to parts in productions such as Joseph and the Amazing Technicolor Dreamcoat and Cabaret. [SEP] [CLS] For starring as Eliza Doolittle in the 2016 Bad Hersfeld revival of the musical My Fair Lady, she won the Audience Award for Favorite Actress. [SEP] [CLS] Throughout her career, Mölling has scored four number-one hits and sold over five million albums and singles worldwide. [SEP] [CLS] Outside her work in the entertainment industry, she is involved in many charitable activities, promoting various causes, including education, children's care and animal welfare. [SEP] [CLS] In 2015, she co-authored the children's book Pumpel, der Baumzwerg along with her mother. [SEP] [CLS] The same year, Mölling relocated to Los Angeles, California, where she gave birth to her second son, her first child with Canadian music producer and Magic! [SEP] [CLS] lead singer Nasri Atweh. [SEP] [CLS]
