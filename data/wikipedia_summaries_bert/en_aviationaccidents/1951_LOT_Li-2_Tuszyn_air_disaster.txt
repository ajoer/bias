The 1951 LOT Li-2 Tuszyn air disaster occurred on 15 November 1951 when a LOT Polish Airlines Lisunov Li-2 flew into power lines near Tuszyn, crashed and burst into flames. [CLS] [SEP] All 15 passengers and 3 crew died. [CLS] [SEP] It was the first LOT aircraft disaster since the end of World War II. [CLS] [SEP]
