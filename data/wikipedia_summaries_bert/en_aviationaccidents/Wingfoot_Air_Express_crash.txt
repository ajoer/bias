The Wingfoot Air Express was a dirigible that crashed into the Illinois Trust and Savings Building in Chicago on Monday July 21, 1919. [CLS] [SEP] The Type FD dirigible, owned by the Goodyear Tire and Rubber Company, was transporting people from Grant Park to the White City amusement park. [CLS] [SEP] One crew member, two passengers and ten bank employees were killed in what was, up to that point, the worst dirigible disaster in United States history. [CLS] [SEP]
