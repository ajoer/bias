[CLS] Pintoricchio or Pinturicchio (US: , Italian: [pintuËˆrikkjo]) whose birth name was Bernardino di Betto, also known as Benetto di Biagio or Sordicchio, was an Italian painter during the Renaissance. [SEP] [CLS] Born in Perugia in 1454 and dying in Siena in 1513, Pintoricchio acquired his nickname, meaning ("little painter"), because of his small stature. [SEP] [CLS] He also used it to sign some of his 15th and 16th century artworks. [SEP] [CLS]
