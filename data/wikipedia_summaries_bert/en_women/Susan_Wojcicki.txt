Susan Diane Wojcicki ( wuu-CHITS-kee; born July 5, 1968) is the CEO of YouTube.Wojcicki was involved in the founding of Google, and became Google's first marketing manager in 1999. [CLS] [SEP] She later led the company's online advertising business and was put in charge of Google's original video service. [CLS] [SEP] After observing the success of YouTube, Wojcicki proposed the acquisition of YouTube by Google in 2006, and has served as CEO of YouTube since 2014. [CLS] [SEP] Wojcicki has an estimated net worth of nearly $500 million. [CLS] [SEP]
