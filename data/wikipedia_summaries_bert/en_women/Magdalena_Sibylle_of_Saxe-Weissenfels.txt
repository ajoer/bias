[CLS] Magdalena Sibylle of Saxe-Weissenfels (2 September 1648 – 7 January 1681) was a German noblewoman. [SEP] [CLS] She was a daughter of August, duke of Saxe-Weissenfels, and his wife Anna Maria of Mecklenburg-Schwerin. [SEP] [CLS] Her paternal grandparents were John George I, Elector of Saxony, and Magdalene Sibylle of Prussia. [SEP] [CLS] On 14 November 1669, she married Duke Friedrich I of Saxe-Gotha-Altenburg.
