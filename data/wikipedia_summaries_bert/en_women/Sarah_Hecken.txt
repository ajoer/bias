[CLS] Sarah Stefanie Hecken (born 27 August 1993) is a German figure skater. [SEP] [CLS] She is a four-time German national champion (2008, 2010, 2011 and 2013) and has won twelve senior international medals, including six gold. [SEP] [CLS] She has placed as high as 11th at the World Championships. [SEP] [CLS] Her first international victory was at the 2007 Junior Grand Prix event in Germany. [SEP] [CLS]
