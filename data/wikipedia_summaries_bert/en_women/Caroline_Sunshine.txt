Caroline Mohr Sunshine (born September 5, 1995) is an American actress, singer, and civil servant currently working for U.S. [CLS] [SEP] President Donald Trump as a White House press assistant. [CLS] [SEP] As an actress, she is best known for her co-starring role as flashy, rude European exchange student Tinka Hessenheffer on the Disney Channel series Shake It Up. [CLS] [SEP] Sunshine has also appeared in the feature film Marmaduke (2010), the documentary Behind The Scenes (2012), and the direct-to-video film The Outfield (2015). [CLS] [SEP]
