The 1967 Caracas earthquake occurred in Caracas, Venezuela, and La Guaira, Vargas on 29 July at 8:00 p.m (UTC−04:00 at that time). [CLS] [SEP] Its epicenter took place in the litoral central (20 km from Caracas) and lasted 35 seconds. [CLS] [SEP] It heavily affected areas such as Altamira, Los Palos Grandes, and Litoral Central. [CLS] [SEP] After the earthquake, there were several aftershocks of lower intensity. [CLS] [SEP] The earthquake left a toll of 1,536 injured, 225–300 dead, and cost $50–140 million United States Dollars in property damage. [CLS] [SEP]
