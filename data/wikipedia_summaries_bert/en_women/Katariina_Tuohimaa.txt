Katariina Tuohimaa (born 28 April 1988 in Helsinki) is a Finnish former tennis player. [CLS] [SEP] In her career, Tuohimaa won five doubles titles on the ITF Women's Circuit. [CLS] [SEP] On 13 August 2007, she reached her best singles ranking of world No. [CLS] [SEP] 730. [CLS] [SEP] On the same date, she peaked at No. [CLS] [SEP] 481 in the doubles rankings. [CLS] [SEP] Tuohimaa played six rubbers for Finland at the 2006 Fed Cup. [CLS] [SEP]
