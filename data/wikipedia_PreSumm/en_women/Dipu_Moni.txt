[CLS] Dipu Moni (born 8 December 1965) is a Bangladeshi politician, and since 2019 has been the Minister of Education of Bangladesh since January 2019. [SEP] [CLS] She was Foreign Minister of Bangladesh from 2009 to 2013. [SEP] [CLS] She was appointed the first female Foreign Minister on 6 January 2009 after victory for the Awami League-led Grand Alliance on 29 December 2008. [SEP] [CLS]
