[CLS] Jean Rosemary Shrimpton (born 7 November 1942) is an English model and actress. [SEP] [CLS] She was an icon of Swinging London and is considered to be one of the world's first supermodels. [SEP] [CLS] She appeared on numerous magazine covers including Vogue, Harper's Bazaar, Vanity Fair, Glamour, Elle, Ladies' Home Journal, Newsweek, and Time. [SEP] [CLS] In 2009, Harper's Bazaar named Shrimpton one of the 26 best models of all time, and in 2012, Time named her one of the 100 most influential fashion icons of all time. [SEP] [CLS] She starred alongside Paul Jones in the film Privilege (1967). [SEP] [CLS]
