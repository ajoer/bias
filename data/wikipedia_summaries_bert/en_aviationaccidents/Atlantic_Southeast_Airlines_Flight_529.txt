[CLS] Atlantic Southeast Airlines Flight 529 was an Embraer EMB 120 Brasilia aircraft that crashed near Carrollton, Georgia on August 21, 1995. [SEP] [CLS] Nine of the 29 passengers and crew on board were killed as a result of the accident. [SEP] [CLS] The accident bore similarities to Atlantic Southeast Airlines Flight 2311, which had occurred four years earlier, and resulted in the deaths of everyone on board. [SEP] [CLS] The inquiries of both crashes concluded that design flaws in the aircraft's propellers were to blame. [SEP] [CLS]
