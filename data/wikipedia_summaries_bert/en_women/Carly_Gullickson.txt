[CLS] Carly Gullickson (born November 26, 1986) is an American former professional tennis player. [SEP] [CLS] Her career-high WTA singles ranking was No. [SEP] [CLS] 123, which she reached on July 20, 2009. [SEP] [CLS] Her career-high doubles ranking was No. [SEP] [CLS] 52, set on April 3, 2006 at age 19. [SEP] [CLS] She is the daughter of former major league baseball player Bill Gullickson and the older sister of tennis player Chelsey Gullickson. [SEP] [CLS] She won the 2009 U.S. [SEP] [CLS] Open mixed doubles event, partnering with Travis Parrott. [SEP] [CLS] She married Australian tennis coach Cameron Eagle. [SEP] [CLS] They reside in Palm Beach Gardens, Florida. [SEP] [CLS] Gullickson retired from tennis in 2013. [SEP] [CLS]
