[CLS] Severe Tropical Cyclone Rosita was a tropical cyclone that affected northern Australia from 15 April through 21 April 2000. [SEP] [CLS] Rosita was one of the most intense tropical cyclones to hit the west Kimberley coast in the last century. [SEP] [CLS] Crossing the coast as a Category 5 about 40 kilometres (25 mi) south of Broome on 20 April, Rosita caused severe damage in the Eco Beach resort and the vegetation around Broome. [SEP] [CLS] Its region of very destructive winds (gusts exceeding 170 km/h) passed south of Broome by only 15 kilometres (9.3 mi). [SEP] [CLS] Cyclone Rosita was the first cyclone to directly hit Broome since Cyclone Lindsay in March 1985. [SEP] [CLS]
