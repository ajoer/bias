[CLS] The Yankee hurricane of 1935 was a rare Category 2 hurricane that affected the Bahamas and South Florida in November. [SEP] [CLS] It remains the only tropical cyclone to affect Miami from the northeast in November. [SEP] [CLS] The sixth tropical cyclone and fifth hurricane of the 1935 Atlantic hurricane season, it developed 227 miles (365 km) east of Bermuda. [SEP] [CLS] It strengthened to a hurricane on November 1, initially posing a threat to the Carolinas. [SEP] [CLS] Subsequently, the cyclone turned southwest. [SEP] [CLS] It attained its peak intensity on November 3, and it made landfall near Miami Beach on November 4. [SEP] [CLS] The hurricane's unusual approach toward Florida and late arrival earned it the nickname of the Yankee Hurricane. [SEP] [CLS]
