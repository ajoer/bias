[CLS] On April 25, 2014, a localized tornado outbreak struck North Carolina, resulting in the first tornado-related fatality during that year in the United States. [SEP] [CLS] The event marked the latest formation of the first EF3 or stronger tornado during a calendar year and latest date for the first tornadic death. [SEP] [CLS] Throughout North Carolina, the tornadoes killed 1 person and injured 27 others. [SEP] [CLS] A total of 327 homes were damaged or destroyed across four counties, with 60% of these being in Beaufort County. [SEP] [CLS]
