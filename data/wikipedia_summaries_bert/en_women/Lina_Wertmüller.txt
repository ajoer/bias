Lina Wertmüller (Italian: [ˈliːna vertˈmyul:er, -mul-]; born 14 August 1928) is an Italian screenwriter and film director. [CLS] [SEP] She was the first woman nominated for an Academy Award for Best Director for Seven Beauties in 1977. [CLS] [SEP] She is also known for her films The Seduction of Mimi, Love and Anarchy and Swept Away. [CLS] [SEP] In 2019, Wertmüller was announced as one of the four recipients of the Academy Honorary Award for her career. [CLS] [SEP]
