Mary Theresa Ledóchowska (Polish: Maria Teresa Ledóchowska) (29 April 1863 – 6 July 1922), was a Polish Roman Catholic Religious Sister and missionary, who founded the Missionary Sisters of St. [CLS] [SEP] Peter Claver, dedicated to service in Africa. [CLS] [SEP] She has been beatified by the Catholic Church. [CLS] [SEP]
