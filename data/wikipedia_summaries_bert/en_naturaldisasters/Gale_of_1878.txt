The Gale of 1878, was an intense Category 2 hurricane that was active between October 18 and October 25. [CLS] [SEP] It caused extensive damage from Cuba to New England. [CLS] [SEP] Believed to be the strongest storm to hit the Washington - Baltimore region since hurricane records began in 1851, A complete record exists of this hurricane throughout its lifecycle from formation to dissipation.
