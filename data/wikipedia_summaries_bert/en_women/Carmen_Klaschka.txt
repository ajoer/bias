Carmen Klaschka (born 8 January 1987) is a retired German tennis player. [CLS] [SEP] Klaschka won three singles titles and ten doubles titles on the ITF circuit in her career. [CLS] [SEP] On 27 July 2009, she reached her best singles ranking of world No. [CLS] [SEP] 167. [CLS] [SEP] On 3 August 2009, she peaked at No. [CLS] [SEP] 125 in the doubles rankings. [CLS] [SEP] Carmen's sister, Sabine, is also a professional tennis player. [CLS] [SEP]
