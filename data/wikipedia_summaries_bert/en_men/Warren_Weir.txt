[CLS] Warren Weir (born October 1989) is a Jamaican sprinter, who specialized in the 200 metres. [SEP] [CLS] He was the bronze medallist in the event at the 2012 London Olympics, helping Jamaica sweep the medals. [SEP] [CLS] In 2013 at the Moscow World Championships, Warren Weir won the silver medal equalling his personal best. [SEP] [CLS] He finished behind Usain Bolt who set a World Leading time. [SEP] [CLS] His personal best is 19.79 seconds set at the National Stadium in his home country Kingston, Jamaica. [SEP] [CLS] He has since equalled his personal best in Moscow, in the World Championship final. [SEP] [CLS] He trained with the Glen Mills-coached Racers Track Club, alongside Usain Bolt and Yohan Blake. [SEP] [CLS]
