[CLS] The 1942 Ruislip Wellington accident occurred on 18 October 1942 when a Vickers Wellington 1C medium bomber of No. [SEP] [CLS] 311 Squadron RAF crashed near South Ruislip station, Middlesex, on approach to RAF Northolt. [SEP] [CLS] The crash killed all 15 people aboard the aircraft, and six civilians on the ground including four children. [SEP] [CLS]
