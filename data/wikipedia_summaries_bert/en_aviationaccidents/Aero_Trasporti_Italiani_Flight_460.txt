Aero Trasporti Italiani Flight 460 was a flight that crashed on 15 October 1987 on Mount Crezzo, near Conca di Crezzo, Italy, that took the lives of 34 passengers and 3 crew. [CLS] [SEP] The aircraft took off from Milan-Linate (LIN) for a flight to Cologne (CGN). [CLS] [SEP] Icing conditions existed. [CLS] [SEP] Fifteen minutes after takeoff, while climbing through FL147 in IAS hold mode (constant speed set at 133 kts) the aircraft rolled left and right. [CLS] [SEP] The plane rolled 41deg right, 100deg left, 105deg right and 135deg left. [CLS] [SEP] During pull up three anomalous pitch trim movements set the elevator to pitch down, preventing recovery. [CLS] [SEP] The ATR crashed nose down into a 700 m high mountain following an uncontrolled descent. [CLS] [SEP]
