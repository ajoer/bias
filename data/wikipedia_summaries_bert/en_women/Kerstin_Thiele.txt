[CLS] Kerstin Thiele (born August 26, 1986 in Riesa, East Germany) is a German judoka and a silver medallist at the Olympic Games 2012 in London.Kerstin Thiele is a German champion in judo in the Middleweight (-70 kg) division. [SEP] [CLS] She competes for the German club JC Leipzig, and was part of the German national team. [SEP] [CLS] At the 2012 Olympics she reached the women's middleweight judo final vs. [SEP] [CLS] Lucie Decosse, who won the gold medal. [SEP] [CLS] On the way Thiele beat Moira de Villiers, Anett Meszaros, Edith Bosch and Chen Fei. [SEP] [CLS]
