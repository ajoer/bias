Tariku Bekele (Amharic: ታሪኩ በቀለ; born 28 February 1987) is an Ethiopian long-distance runner, who specializes in the 5000 metres and has moved up to 10000 metres as well. [CLS] [SEP] He is the younger brother of Kenenisa Bekele. [CLS] [SEP] He is the fourth fastest Ethiopian ever over 5000 m and 3000 metres. [CLS] [SEP] His indoor 3000 m best of 7:31.09 ranks him as the ninth fastest of all-time in the event. [CLS] [SEP] He was the 10,000 m bronze medallist at the 2012 Summer Olympics. [CLS] [SEP] His first major victory came at the 2008 IAAF World Indoor Championships, where he won the 3000 m gold medal. [CLS] [SEP] He was the 2006 World Junior Champion over the distance and also won a cross country junior bronze medal that year. [CLS] [SEP] He took a continental silver medal at the 2007 All-Africa Games. [CLS] [SEP] Tariku has finished in the top eight of 5000 m finals at the 2008 Summer Olympics and at the World Championships in Athletics in 2005 and 2007. [CLS] [SEP]
