[CLS] Gabon Express Flight 221 was a scheduled domestic passenger flight which crashed into the Atlantic Ocean on 8 June 2004. [SEP] [CLS] The Hawker Siddeley HS 748 was carrying 26 passengers and 4 crew and was flying from Gabon's capital Libreville to Franceville via Port-Gentil when an engine failed. [SEP] [CLS] The crew tried to return to Libreville International Airport; however they overflew it and nosedived into the sea. [SEP] [CLS] At least 19 people were killed in the crash. [SEP] [CLS] It was the second deadliest plane crash in Gabon. [SEP] [CLS] Gabon President Omar Bongo declared three days of national mourning in response to the disaster. [SEP] [CLS]
