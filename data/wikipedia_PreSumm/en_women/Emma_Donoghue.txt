[CLS] Emma Donoghue (born 24 October 1969) is an Irish-Canadian playwright, literary historian, novelist, and screenwriter. [SEP] [CLS] Her 2010 novel Room was a finalist for the Man Booker Prize and an international best-seller. [SEP] [CLS] Donoghue's 1995 novel Hood won the Stonewall Book Award. [SEP] [CLS] and Slammerkin (2000) won the Ferro-Grumley Award for Lesbian Fiction. [SEP] [CLS] She is a 2011 recipient of the Alex Awards. [SEP] [CLS] Room was adapted by Donoghue into a film of the same name. [SEP] [CLS] For this, she was nominated for the Academy Award for Best Adapted Screenplay. [SEP] [CLS]
