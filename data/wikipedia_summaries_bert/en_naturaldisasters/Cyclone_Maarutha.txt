Cyclone Maarutha was the first tropical cyclone of the 2017 North Indian Ocean cyclone season. [CLS] [SEP] It was a relatively short-lived and weak system, but it was the first to make landfall in Myanmar in April. [CLS] [SEP] Although a weak system, it caused notable damage in Myanmar. [CLS] [SEP] Maarutha formed from an area of low pressure over the southern Bay of Bengal on April 15. [CLS] [SEP] The next morning, RSMC New Delhi upgraded the low pressure area to a Depression and designated it as BOB 01. [CLS] [SEP]
