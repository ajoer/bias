The 1997 Jabalpur earthquake occurred on May 22, at 04:21:31 AM in Jabalpur District in the Indian state of Madhya Pradesh. [CLS] [SEP] The epicenter of the earthquake was located near Koshamghat village. [CLS] [SEP] According to geologist Dr V. [CLS] [SEP] Subramanyan, former professor at the Indian Institute of Technology Bombay (IIT Bombay), the earthquake was caused by movement on the Narmada Fault. [CLS] [SEP]
