[CLS] Kalle Niilo Ponteva Halonen (born 25 December 1940 in Kouvola) is a Finnish former ski jumper who competed between 1960 and 1967. [SEP] [CLS] His biggest success was the silver medal in the individual large hill at the 1960 Winter Olympics in Squaw Valley. [SEP] [CLS] Halonen also won the bronze medal in the individual large hill at the 1962 FIS Nordic World Ski Championships in Zakopane. [SEP] [CLS] He also finished second as well in a normal hill competition in Germany in 1964. [SEP] [CLS]
