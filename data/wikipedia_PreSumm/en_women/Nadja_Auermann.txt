[CLS] Nadja Auermann (born 19 March 1971) is a German supermodel and actress. [SEP] [CLS] Fashion designer Valentino once commented on her physical resemblance to Marlene Dietrich. [SEP] [CLS] A New York Times fashion columnist, Guy Trebay, wrote of her "ice maiden visage and pole vaulter's legs". [SEP] [CLS] She once held the record for being the model with the longest legs in the world in the Guinness Book of Records. [SEP] [CLS]
