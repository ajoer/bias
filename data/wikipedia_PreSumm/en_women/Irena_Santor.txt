[CLS] Irena Wiśniewska-Santor (Polish pronunciation: [iˈrɛna sanˈtɔr]; born 9 December 1934), known professionally as Irena Santor, is a Polish singer, musical performer and actress. [SEP] [CLS] Her career started in 1950 and continues to this day. [SEP] [CLS] For her clear mezzo-soprano, she enjoys an iconic status in Poland and is referred to as an influence for younger singers. [SEP] [CLS] From 1951 to 1959 Irena Santor (then as Irena Wiśniewska) was a member and the main soloist of the famous Polish folk group Mazowsze, with whom she toured on all continents. [SEP] [CLS] After leaving the group she decided to start solo performance. [SEP] [CLS] She was rewarded many prizes in her country and abroad on multiple occasions. [SEP] [CLS] Irena Santor made her acting debut in Stanisław Bareja's film Przygoda z piosenką (1968). [SEP] [CLS] She appeared in many TV and radio shows, was part of jury some talent shows and different song competitions. [SEP] [CLS] The legendary singer also recorded soundtracks to Polish and Soviet films. [SEP] [CLS] In 2017, as first singer in Poland, she was awarded an honorary doctorate for her activities.
