The 2012 Haida Gwaii earthquake occurred just after 8:04 p.m. [CLS] [SEP] PDT on October 27. [CLS] [SEP] The shock had a moment magnitude of 7.8 and a maximum Mercalli Intensity of VIII (Severe). [CLS] [SEP] The earthquake's epicentre was on Moresby Island of the Haida Gwaii archipelago (formerly known as the Queen Charlotte Islands). [CLS] [SEP] This was the second largest Canadian earthquake ever recorded by a seismometer, after the 1949 Queen Charlotte Islands earthquake, about 135 kilometres (84 mi) away. [CLS] [SEP]
