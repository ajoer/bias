Princess Marie Auguste Anna of Thurn and Taxis (11 August 1706 – 1 February 1756) was a Regent of Württemberg. [CLS] [SEP] She was a member of the Princely House of Thurn and Taxis as a daughter of Anselm Franz, 2nd Prince of Thurn and Taxis and his wife Maria Ludovika Anna Franziska, Princess of Lobkowicz. [CLS] [SEP] Through her marriage to Karl Alexander, Duke of Württemberg, she became Duchess consort of Württemberg. [CLS] [SEP]
