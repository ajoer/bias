[CLS] Tropical Storm Ewiniar was a tropical cyclone in early June 2018 that brought prolonged heavy rains to Vietnam and South China, causing damaging floods and landslides. [SEP] [CLS] The fourth named storm of the 2018 Pacific typhoon season, Ewiniar developed as a tropical depression just east of Vietnam on June 2. [SEP] [CLS] The system moved generally northwards over the South China Sea, before intensifying into a tropical storm near the Qiongzhou Strait on June 5. [SEP] [CLS] Ewiniar proceeded to stall over the region as steering currents collapsed, making landfall over the Leizhou Peninsula then over northern Hainan. [SEP] [CLS] Ewiniar accelerated to the northeast on June 7 and moved back over open sea, allowing it to strengthen slightly and reach peak intensity. [SEP] [CLS] The storm made landfall in eastern Guangdong shortly thereafter and weakened into a tropical depression on June 8. [SEP] [CLS] The system ultimately dissipated east of Taiwan on June 11. [SEP] [CLS] In combination with the southwest monsoon, Ewiniar dumped heavy rain over Vietnam and South China for over a week. [SEP] [CLS] In Vietnam, a few houses were damaged and one person was killed. [SEP] [CLS] Roads in Ho Chi Minh City were inundated. [SEP] [CLS] In Hainan and Guangdong, over a hundred flights were cancelled and ships were forced to take shelter. [SEP] [CLS] Daily rainfall records for June were set in eight cities. [SEP] [CLS] Twenty-nine houses were destroyed and over 27,000 hectares (67,000 acres) of crops were damaged. [SEP] [CLS] Landslides killed five people in Yunfu and left one missing in Jiangmen. [SEP] [CLS] Strong winds blew down a construction site in Guangzhou, killing one and injuring eight, and caused a building collapse in Shenzhen that killed four and injured four others. [SEP] [CLS] Another two people were electrocuted by faulty wiring. [SEP] [CLS] A tornado occurred in Foshan, damaging a market and three vehicles. [SEP] [CLS] Travel disruptions also posed difficulties for students taking the National College Entrance Examination.
