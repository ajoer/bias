[SEP] [CLS] Terry appeared in 52 films between 1914 and 1928, sometimes as a leading lady but often in supporting roles. [SEP] [CLS] She married Danish actor Carl Gerard in 1910, who she remained with until her death. [SEP] [CLS] Terry died on January 6, 1931 in Los Angeles and her ashes were interred in her mother's crypt at Hollywood Forever Cemetery. [SEP] [CLS]
