[CLS] The 2001 southern Peru earthquake occurred at 20:33:15 UTC (15:33:15 local time) on June 23 with a moment magnitude of 8.4 and a maximum Mercalli intensity of VIII (Severe). [SEP] [CLS] The quake affected the Peruvian regions of Arequipa, Moquegua and Tacna. [SEP] [CLS] It was the most devastating earthquake in Peru since the catastrophic 1970 Ancash earthquake and globally the largest earthquake since the 1965 Rat Islands earthquake. [SEP] [CLS]
