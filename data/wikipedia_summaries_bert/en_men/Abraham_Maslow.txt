[SEP] [CLS] Maslow was a psychology professor at Alliant International University, Brandeis University, Brooklyn College, New School for Social Research, and Columbia University. [SEP] [CLS] He stressed the importance of focusing on the positive qualities in people, as opposed to treating them as a "bag of symptoms". [SEP] [CLS] A Review of General Psychology survey, published in 2002, ranked Maslow as the tenth most cited psychologist of the 20th century. [SEP] [CLS]
