1998 Mionica earthquake was a 5.5 magnitude earthquake in western Central Serbia, near the town of Mionica. [CLS] [SEP] The epicentral area was in the region of Brežđe village, where it had a maximum intensity of VIII on the Mercalli intensity scale. [CLS] [SEP] It was felt in Central Serbia, Vidin (Bulgaria), Sarajevo (Bosnia) and parts of Croatia, Hungary and Greece. [CLS] [SEP] One person died from a heart attack and 17 were injured. [CLS] [SEP] Some 60 schools (mostly in villages) were damaged. [CLS] [SEP]
