The St. [CLS] [SEP] Felix's flood (in Dutch Sint-Felixvloed) happened on Saturday, 5 November 1530, the name day of St. [CLS] [SEP] Felix. [CLS] [SEP] This day was later known as Evil Saturday (kwade zaterdag). [CLS] [SEP] Large parts of Flanders and Zeeland were washed away, including the Verdronken Land van Reimerswaal. [CLS] [SEP] According to Audrey M. [CLS] [SEP] Lambert, "all the Oost Wetering of Zuid-Beveland was lost, save only the town of Reimerswaal."More than 100,000 were killed in Netherlands by the St. [CLS] [SEP] Felix's flood.
