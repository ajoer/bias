[CLS] On 25 August 2010, a Let L-410 Turbolet passenger aircraft of Filair crashed on approach to Bandundu Airport in the Democratic Republic of the Congo, killing all but one of the 21 people on board.The accident was reportedly the result of the occupants rushing to the front of the aircraft to escape from a crocodile smuggled on board by one of the passengers. [SEP] [CLS] The move compromised the aircraft's balance to the point that control of the aircraft was lost. [SEP] [CLS]
