[CLS] The January 1999 North American ice storm was a severe ice storm that struck the Washington D.C. [SEP] [CLS] metropolitan area on January 14 and 15, 1999. [SEP] [CLS] Heavy ice accumulation bringing down power lines resulted in around 745,000 people in the area losing power. [SEP] [CLS] Many of the major power companies supplying DC, Maryland, and Virginia had significant portions of their customer bases impacted. [SEP] [CLS] At the height of the storm, around one third of PEPCO's customers were without power, with some waiting up to two weeks for power to be restored. [SEP] [CLS]
