Friedl Czepa (1898–1973) was an Austrian stage, film and television actress. [CLS] [SEP] Czepa made her film debut in 1935, and went on to appear in roughly thirty cinema and television films during her career. [CLS] [SEP] Along with Oskar Sima, Fred Hennings and Leni Riefenstahl she was identified as being an active supporter of the Nazi Party. [CLS] [SEP] She was the director of the Vienna Stadttheater from 1940 to 1945. [CLS] [SEP] Because of her Nazi links, she received a professional ban following the Second World War but slowly rebuilt her career. [CLS] [SEP] She was married three her times; her husbands included Hans Schott-Schöbinger and Rolf Wanka. [CLS] [SEP]
