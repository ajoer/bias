The 1979 Tumaco earthquake occurred at 02:59 local time on 12 December with a moment magnitude of 8.2 and a maximum Mercalli intensity of IX (Violent). [CLS] [SEP] The epicenter was just offshore from the border between Ecuador and Colombia, near the port city of Tumaco. [CLS] [SEP] It triggered a major tsunami, which was responsible for most of the estimated 300–600 deaths. [CLS] [SEP] The hardest hit area was Colombia's Nariño Department. [CLS] [SEP]
