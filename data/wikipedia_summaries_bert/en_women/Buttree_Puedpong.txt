Buttree Puedpong (Thai: บุตรี เผือดผ่อง; RTGS: Buttri Phueatphong; born October 16, 1990; nicknamed Song which means "two") is a female Thai Taekwondo practitioner who competes at the 2008 Summer Olympics in the -49 kg class.
