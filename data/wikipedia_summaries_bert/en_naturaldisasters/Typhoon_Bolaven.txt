The name Bolaven has been used for four tropical cyclones in the western Pacific Ocean. [CLS] [SEP] The name, contributed by Laos, refers to the Bolaven Plateau, located in the southern part of the country. [CLS] [SEP] Severe Tropical Storm Bolaven (2000) (T0006, 11W, Huaning) – crossed the Ryūkyū Islands and brushed southern Japan. [CLS] [SEP] Severe Tropical Storm Bolaven (2005) (T0523, 24W, Pepeng) – hit the Philippines as a tropical storm. [CLS] [SEP] Typhoon Bolaven (2012) (T1215, 16W, Julian) – hit Korea and Okinawa. [CLS] [SEP] Tropical Storm Bolaven (2018) (T1801, 01W, Agaton) – formed in two seasons. [CLS] [SEP]
