[CLS] Christine Lieberknecht (born 7 May 1958 in Weimar) is a German politician of the Christian Democratic Union (CDU). [SEP] [CLS] From 2009 to 2014 she served as the Minister President of Thuringia. [SEP] [CLS] Lieberknecht was the first woman to become head of government in Thuringia and only the second woman to govern a German state. [SEP] [CLS]
