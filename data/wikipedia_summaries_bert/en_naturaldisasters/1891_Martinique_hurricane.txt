[CLS] The 1891 Martinique hurricane, also known as Hurricane San Magín, was an intense major hurricane that struck the island of Martinique and caused massive damage. [SEP] [CLS] It was the third hurricane of the 1891 Atlantic hurricane season and the only major hurricane of the season. [SEP] [CLS] It was first sighted east of the Lesser Antilles on August 18 as a Category 2 hurricane. [SEP] [CLS] The storm made landfall on the island of Martinique, where it caused severe damage, over 700 deaths and at least 1,000 injuries. [SEP] [CLS] It crossed eastern Dominican Republic while tracking on a northwestward direction on August 19–20, passed the Mona Passage on August 20 and the Bahamas on August 22–23. [SEP] [CLS] It crossed the U.S. [SEP] [CLS] State of Florida and dissipated in the Gulf of Mexico after August 25. [SEP] [CLS] Total damage is estimated at $10 million (1891 USD). [SEP] [CLS] The storm is considered to be the worst on Martinique since 1817. [SEP] [CLS]
