[CLS] Jean II Carondelet (1469 in Dôle – 7 February 1545 in Mechelen), was a Burgundian cleric, politician, jurist and one of the most important advisors to Charles V, Holy Roman Emperor. [SEP] [CLS] He was a patron of the Dutch philosopher Erasmus and a brother of the Habsburg diplomat Ferry Carondelet. [SEP] [CLS]
