[CLS] Antje Buschschulte (born 27 December 1978 in Berlin) is a former German swimmer. [SEP] [CLS] Her best disciplines were the short distance backstroke and freestyle races. [SEP] [CLS] Buschschulte swam for the sporting club SC Magdeburg. [SEP] [CLS] She participated in Olympic Games 1996, 2000, 2004, 2008 and won 5 olympic Bronze Medals. [SEP] [CLS] She was World Champion in 100m backstroke 2003 in Barcelona. [SEP] [CLS] In total Antje won 57 international medals (Olympic Games, World Championships, European Championships).
