Greta Schröder (27 June 1892 – 8 June 1980) was a German actress. [CLS] [SEP] She is best known for the role of Thomas Hutter's wife and Count Orlok's victim in the 1922 silent film Nosferatu. [CLS] [SEP] In the fictionalized 2000 film Shadow of the Vampire, she is portrayed as having been a famous actress during the making of Nosferatu, but in fact she was little known. [CLS] [SEP] The bulk of her career was during the 1920s, and she continued to act well into the 1950s, but by the 1930s her roles had diminished to only occasional appearances. [CLS] [SEP] Following a failed marriage with struggling actor Ernst Matray, she was married to actor and film director Paul Wegener. [CLS] [SEP] According to the Austrian writer Kay Weniger, Greta Schröder died in 1980, though some sources mention 1967. [CLS] [SEP]
