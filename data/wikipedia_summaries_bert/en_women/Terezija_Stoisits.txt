Terezija Stoisits (pronounced [ˈtɛrɛzija ˈstɔjʃitɕ]; born November 14, 1958 in Stinatz, Austria) is an Austrian politician of the Green Party. [CLS] [SEP] Since July 2007 she serves as an ombudswoman of the Republic of Austria. [CLS] [SEP] Before that she was the longest serving member of parliament of her party. [CLS] [SEP]
