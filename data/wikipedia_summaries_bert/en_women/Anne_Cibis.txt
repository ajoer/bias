Anne Cibis (née Möllinger; born 27 September 1985 in Worms) is a track and field sprint athlete who competes internationally for Germany.Cibis represented Germany at the 2008 Summer Olympics in Beijing. [CLS] [SEP] She competed at the 4 × 100 metres relay together with Verena Sailer, Cathleen Tschirch and Marion Wagner. [CLS] [SEP] In their first round heat they placed third behind Jamaica and Russia and in front of China. [CLS] [SEP] Their time of 43.59 seconds was the eighth time overall out of sixteen participating nations. [CLS] [SEP] With this result they qualified for the final in which they sprinted to a time of 43.28 seconds for fifth place.She again appeared in the German 4 × 100 m team at the 2012 Summer Olympics. [CLS] [SEP] She and teammates Leena Günther, Tatjana Pinto and Verena Sailer reached the final and again finished fifth, with a time of 42.67. [CLS] [SEP]
