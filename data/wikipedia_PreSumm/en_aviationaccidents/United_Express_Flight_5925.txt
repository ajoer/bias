[CLS] United Express Flight 5925, operated by Great Lakes Airlines with a Beechcraft 1900 twin turboprop, was a regularly scheduled flight from Chicago O'Hare International Airport to Quincy, Illinois, with an intermediate stop in Burlington, Iowa. [SEP] [CLS] On November 19, 1996, the aircraft collided on landing at Quincy with another Beechcraft, a private King Air, that was taking off from an intersecting runway. [SEP] [CLS] The crash was known as Quincy runway disaster. [SEP] [CLS] Fourteen people, twelve on board the 1900 and two on board the King Air, were killed as a result. [SEP] [CLS]
