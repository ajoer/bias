[CLS] Grand Duchess Alexandra Iosifovna of Russia (8 July 1830 – 6 July 1911), born Princess Alexandra Friederike Henriette of Saxe-Altenburg, was the fifth daughter of Joseph, Duke of Saxe-Altenburg and Amelie Theresa Luise, Duchess of Württemberg. [SEP] [CLS] She is an ancestor of the British, Greek, Romanian, Yugoslavian and Spanish Royal Families through her elder daughter Olga. [SEP] [CLS]
