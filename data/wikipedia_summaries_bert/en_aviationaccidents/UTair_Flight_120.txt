[CLS] UTair Flight 120 was a scheduled domestic passenger flight from Tyumen to Surgut, Russia. [SEP] [CLS] On 2 April 2012, the ATR-72 turboprop aircraft operating the flight crashed shortly after take-off from Roschino International Airport, killing 33 of the 43 people on board. [SEP] [CLS] Investigation carried out by the Russian MAK revealed that the aircraft had not been de-iced prior to its take-off, even though it had been parked for hours in snowy condition. [SEP] [CLS] The crew of the flight were aware that ice and snow had accumulated on the aircraft, but decided not to de-ice it. [SEP] [CLS]
