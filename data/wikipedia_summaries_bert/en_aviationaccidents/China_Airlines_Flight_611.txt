[CLS] China Airlines Flight 611 was a regularly scheduled passenger flight from Chiang Kai-shek International Airport (now Taoyuan International Airport) in Taiwan to Hong Kong International Airport in Hong Kong. [SEP] [CLS] On 25 May 2002, the Boeing 747-209B operating the route disintegrated in mid-air and crashed into the Taiwan Strait, 23 nautical miles (26 mi; 43 km) northeast of the Penghu Islands, 20 minutes after takeoff, killing all 225 people on board. [SEP] [CLS] The in-flight break-up was caused by improper repairs to the aircraft 22 years earlier. [SEP] [CLS] As of 2020, the crash remains the deadliest in Taiwanese history. [SEP] [CLS]
