[CLS] Heide Simonis (German pronunciation: [ˈhaɪdə ziˈmoːnɪs]; born 4 July 1943 in Bonn as Heide Steinhardt; nickname: Simone Heidis) is a German politician. [SEP] [CLS] She is a member of the SPD. [SEP] [CLS] From 1993 to 2005 she served as the Minister President of Schleswig-Holstein. [SEP] [CLS] She was the first woman to serve as head of a state government in German history and the only woman to do so in the 20th century. [SEP] [CLS]
