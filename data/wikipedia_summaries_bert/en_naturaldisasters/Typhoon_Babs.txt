The name Babs has been used for four tropical cyclones in the northwest Pacific Ocean. [CLS] [SEP] Typhoon Babs (1951) (T5121) Typhoon Babs (1956) (T5609), struck Japan Tropical Storm Babs (1959) (T5917, 41W), struck Taiwan Typhoon Babs (1998) (T9811, 20W, Loleng), Category 4 super typhoon; struck the Philippines, resulting in 156 deaths and heavy flooding
