Lee Ha-yi (Korean: 이하이; born September 23, 1996), known by her stage name Lee Hi, is a South Korean singer and songwriter, who is best known as the runner-up of SBS' K-pop Star Season 1. [CLS] [SEP] She debuted with single "1, 2, 3, 4" on October 28, 2012 and reached number one with first week sales of 667,549 downloads. [CLS] [SEP] Her first full-length album First Love was released in two parts, part 1 on March 7, 2013 and part 2 on March 28, 2013. [CLS] [SEP]
