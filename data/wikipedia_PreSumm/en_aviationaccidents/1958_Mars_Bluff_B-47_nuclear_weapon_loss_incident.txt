[CLS] The 1958 Mars Bluff B-47 nuclear weapon loss incident was an inadvertent nuclear weapon release over Mars Bluff, South Carolina, during 1958. [SEP] [CLS] The bomb, which lacked the fissile nuclear core, fell over the area, causing damage to buildings below. [SEP] [CLS] Six people were injured by the explosion of the bomb's conventional explosive load. [SEP] [CLS] The United States Air Force (USAF) was sued by the family of the victims, who received US$54,000, equivalent to $478,526 in 2019. [SEP] [CLS]
