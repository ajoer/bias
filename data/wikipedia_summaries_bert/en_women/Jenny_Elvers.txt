Jenny Elvers (pronounced [ˈd͡ʒe.ni ˈɛl.vɐs]] (listen), during her marriage from 2003 to 2013 Jenny Elvers-Elbertzhagen), (born 11 May 1972) is a German actress and hostess. [CLS] [SEP] She studied acting in Berlin, Hamburg and Chicago, and has worked as an actress and hostess since the early 1990s. [CLS] [SEP] She was married to her agent. [CLS] [SEP]
