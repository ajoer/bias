[CLS] On 14 March 1972, Sterling Airways Flight 296 crashed into a mountain ridge on approach to Dubai near Kalba, United Arab Emirates. [SEP] [CLS] Flight 296 was a charter flight from Colombo to Copenhagen with stops in Bombay, Dubai, and Ankara. [SEP] [CLS] All 112 passengers and crew on board died in the crash which was attributed to pilot error. [SEP] [CLS] The flight was operated by a Sud Aviation Caravelle, registration OY-STL. [SEP] [CLS] To date, it is the deadliest air disaster to involve a Caravelle and the deadliest air disaster in the history of the United Arab Emirates. [SEP] [CLS]
