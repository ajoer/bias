Molly My Marianne Sandén (born 3 July 1992) is a Swedish pop singer and voice actress. [CLS] [SEP] As a teenager, she represented Sweden in the Junior Eurovision Song Contest in 2006 and participated in Melodifestivalen in 2009 and as a young adult in 2012 and 2016. [CLS] [SEP] Her sisters Frida and Mimmi are also singers. [CLS] [SEP] In 2018 Sandén released her third studio album, Större. [CLS] [SEP] Her fourth studio album was released in early 2019, called Det bästa kanske inte hänt än. [CLS] [SEP]
