[CLS] Aeroflot Flight 19 was a scheduled passenger flight from Bykovo Airport, Moscow, to Bryansk Airport, Bryansk. [SEP] [CLS] On 2 November 1973, a Yak-40 aircraft operating the flight was hijacked by 4 people 10 minutes before landing. [SEP] [CLS] The aircraft was then diverted to Moscow's Vnukovo Airport, where the hijackers demanded a buyout and provision of a flight to Sweden. [SEP] [CLS] The hostages inside the aircraft were subsequently liberated after the authorities stormed the aeroplane. [SEP] [CLS] This is one of the first well-known cases of storming a hijacked aircraft on the territory of the USSR. [SEP] [CLS]
