Donna Auguste (born 1958) is an American businesswoman, entrepreneur, and philanthropist. [CLS] [SEP] She was the founder and chief executive officer (CEO) of Freshwater Software from 1996 to 2001. [CLS] [SEP] Prior to Freshwater Software, she was a senior engineering manager at Apple Computer who helped to coordinate the development of the Newton personal digital assistant (PDA).In 2001, she sold Freshwater Software to Mercury Interactive Corp. [CLS] [SEP] for $147 million. [CLS] [SEP] In the same year, she was recognized as one of the "25 Women Who Are Making It Big in Small Business" by Fortune Magazine. [CLS] [SEP] She also won the 2001 Golden Torch Award for Outstanding Woman in Technology. [CLS] [SEP]
