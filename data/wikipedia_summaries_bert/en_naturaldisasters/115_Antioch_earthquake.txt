[CLS] The 115 Antioch earthquake occurred on 13 December 115 AD. [SEP] [CLS] It had an estimated magnitude of 7.5 on the surface wave magnitude scale and an estimated maximum intensity of XI (Extreme) on the Mercalli intensity scale. [SEP] [CLS] Antioch and surrounding areas were devastated with a great loss of life and property. [SEP] [CLS] It triggered a local tsunami that badly damaged the harbour at Caesarea Maritima. [SEP] [CLS] The Roman Emperor Trajan was caught in the earthquake, as was his successor Hadrian. [SEP] [CLS] Although the consul Marcus Pedo Vergilianus was killed, they escaped with only slight injuries and later began a program to rebuild the city. [SEP] [CLS]
