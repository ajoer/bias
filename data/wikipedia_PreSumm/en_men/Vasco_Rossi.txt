[CLS] Vasco Rossi (born 7 February 1952), also known mononymously as Vasco or with the nickname Il Blasco, is an Italian singer-songwriter. [SEP] [CLS] During his career, he has published 30 albums (not including unofficial releases) and has written over 250 songs, as well as lyrics for other artists. [SEP] [CLS] Rossi calls himself a provocautore (an Italian portmanteau for "provoking author") as throughout his career he has been regularly criticized over his choice of lifestyle and the lyrics in his songs. [SEP] [CLS] With more than 35 millions of copies sold, he is one of the best-selling Italian singers. [SEP] [CLS] His Modena Park 2017 concert is the current highest-attended ticketed concert of all time. [SEP] [CLS]
