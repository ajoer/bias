[CLS] Yassine Benzia (Arabic: ياسين بنزية‎; born 8 September 1994) is an Algerian footballer who plays for French club Dijon mainly as an attacking or a central midfielder but can also play as a winger or striker. [SEP] [CLS] He began his professional career at Olympique Lyonnais, where he played 53 total games and scored 6 goals, winning the 2012 Trophée des Champions. [SEP] [CLS] In August 2015, he was signed by Lille. [SEP] [CLS] Benzia was a France youth international, having represented his nation at under-16, under-17, under-18 and under-21 level. [SEP] [CLS] He played with the under-17 team at the 2011 FIFA U-17 World Cup. [SEP] [CLS] In March 2016, FIFA approved his request to change his international allegiance to Algeria. [SEP] [CLS]
