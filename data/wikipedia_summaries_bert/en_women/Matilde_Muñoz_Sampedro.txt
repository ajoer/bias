[CLS] Matilde Muñoz Sampedro (2 March 1900 – 14 April 1969) was a Spanish film actress whose career stretched from the 1940s through the 1970s. [SEP] [CLS] Born in Madrid, she was the grandmother of Carlos Bardem, Mónica Bardem and Javier Bardem. [SEP] [CLS] She was the mother of Juan Antonio Bardem and actress Pilar Bardem. [SEP] [CLS] Her husband was actor Rafael Bardem. [SEP] [CLS] Matilde Muñoz Sampedro died in Madrid in 1969, aged 69. [SEP] [CLS] Her sisters Guadalupe Muñoz Sampedro and Mercedes Muñoz Sampedro were both actresses. [SEP] [CLS]
