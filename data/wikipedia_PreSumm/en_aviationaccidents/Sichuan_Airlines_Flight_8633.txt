[CLS] Sichuan Airlines Flight 8633 was a flight from Chongqing Jiangbei International Airport to Lhasa Gonggar Airport on 14 May 2018, which was forced to make an emergency landing at Chengdu Shuangliu International Airport after the cockpit windshield failed. [SEP] [CLS] The aircraft involved was an Airbus A319-100. [SEP] [CLS] The incident has been adapted into the 2019 film The Captain. [SEP] [CLS]
