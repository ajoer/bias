[SEP] [CLS] Her pioneering work in sanitary engineering, and experimental research in domestic science, laid a foundation for the new science of home economics. [SEP] [CLS] She was the founder of the home economics movement characterized by the application of science to the home, and the first to apply chemistry to the study of nutrition.Richards graduated from Westford Academy (second oldest secondary school in Massachusetts) in 1862. [SEP] [CLS] She was the first woman admitted to the Massachusetts Institute of Technology. [SEP] [CLS] She graduated in 1873 and later became its first female instructor.
