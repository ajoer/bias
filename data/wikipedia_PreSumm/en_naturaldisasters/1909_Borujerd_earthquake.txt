[CLS] The 1909 Borujerd earthquake also known as Silakhor earthquake occurred in Silakhor plain (in the south of today's Borujerd County), Persia (modern day Iran) on January 23. [SEP] [CLS] Around 8,000 fatalities were caused directly from the magnitude 7.3 earthquake. [SEP] [CLS] An indefinite number of aftershocks continued for six months after the main shock. [SEP] [CLS] The section on this fault ruptured was the same as the main rupture zone of the 2006 Borujerd earthquake. [SEP] [CLS]
