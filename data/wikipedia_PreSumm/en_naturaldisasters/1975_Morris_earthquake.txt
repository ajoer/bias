[CLS] The 1975 Morris earthquake occurred in western Minnesota on July 9 at 14:54:15 UTC, or 9:54 a.m. [SEP] [CLS] The strongest instrumentally recorded rupture in the history of the state, it registered at magnitude 4.6 Mn and had a maximum Mercalli intensity of VI (Strong). [SEP] [CLS] It was the first earthquake to be recorded on any seismic instrument in the state since 1917. [SEP] [CLS] Tremors were felt over much of Minnesota, northern Iowa, and the eastern Dakotas.
