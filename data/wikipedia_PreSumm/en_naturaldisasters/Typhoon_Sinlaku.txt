[CLS] Sinlaku may refer to tropical cyclones in the Pacific Ocean. [SEP] [CLS] The name was contributed by the Federated States of Micronesia and means Kosrae, a legendary goddess. [SEP] [CLS] Typhoon Sinlaku (2002) (T0216, 22W)-struck China Typhoon Sinlaku (2008) (T0813, 15W, Marce)- struck Taiwan and approached Japan Tropical Storm Sinlaku (2014) (T1421, 22W, Queenie)- struck Philippines and Vietnam Tropical Storm Sinlaku (2020) (T2003, 04W)- A weak tropical storm that affected southern China and northern Vietnam
