[CLS] The 1918 Tyler tornado was a large and destructive tornado that devastated the town of Tyler, Minnesota on Wednesday, August 21, 1918. [SEP] [CLS] The F4-estimated tornado hit the town at approximately 9:20 pm, killing 36 people and injuring over 100 others. [SEP] [CLS] Debris from Tyler was found up to 23 miles (37 km) away. [SEP] [CLS] It is the fourth-deadliest tornado in Minnesota's history. [SEP] [CLS]
