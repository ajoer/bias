[CLS] The 1953 Concepción earthquake occurred on May 6 at 17:16 UTC (13:16 local time). [SEP] [CLS] The epicenter was located offshore Biobío Region, Chile. [SEP] [CLS] It had a magnitude of Ms 7.6, or ML 7.6. [SEP] [CLS] Twelve people were reported dead in this earthquake.In Concepción, 15% of the buildings were damaged. [SEP] [CLS] Electricity and gas were not affected. [SEP] [CLS] However, the electricity service was suspended as a precaution.In Chillán, the intensity reached MM X. [SEP] [CLS] Many houses were destroyed. [SEP] [CLS] The electricity, telegraph, and telephone services were interrupted.In Tomé, several houses were damaged, and many families stayed in the open.This earthquake was located close to the 1939 Chillán earthquake. [SEP] [CLS] However, the focal mechanism of the 1953 Concepción earthquake was reverse faulting, while that of the 1939 Chillán earthquake was normal faulting. [SEP] [CLS] It was not clear whether the 1953 Concepción earthquake was an interplate or intraplate earthquake. [SEP] [CLS]
