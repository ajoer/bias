[CLS] The name Neoguri has been used to name four tropical cyclone in the western north Pacific Ocean. [SEP] [CLS] In addition, the variant Noguri was used in 2002 before the spelling was corrected by the WMO Typhoon Committee. [SEP] [CLS] The name was contributed by South Korea and is a Korean word for raccoon dog. [SEP] [CLS] Severe Tropical Storm Noguri (2002) (T0204, 07W, Espada) – approached Japan. [SEP] [CLS] Typhoon Neoguri (2008) (T0801, 02W, Ambo) – struck China. [SEP] [CLS] Typhoon Neoguri (2014) (T1408, 08W, Florita)- A Category 5 storm that eventually made landfall in Japan and Korea.
