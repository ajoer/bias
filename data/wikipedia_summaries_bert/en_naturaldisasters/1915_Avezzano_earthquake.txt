The 1915 Avezzano earthquake occurred on 13 January in central Italy at 07:52:42 local time. [CLS] [SEP] The shock had a moment magnitude of 6.7 and a maximum Mercalli intensity of XI (Extreme). [CLS] [SEP] The epicenter was located in the town of Avezzano (which was destroyed) in the Province of L'Aquila. [CLS] [SEP] Around 30,000 direct fatalities and $60 million in damage resulted from the earthquake. [CLS] [SEP]
