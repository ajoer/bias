The Hongxi Emperor (洪熙 [xʊ̌ŋɕí]; 16 August 1378 – 29 May 1425), personal name Zhu Gaochi (朱高熾), was the fourth Emperor of the Ming dynasty, reigned from 1424 to 1425. [CLS] [SEP] He succeeded his father, the Yongle Emperor, in 1424. [CLS] [SEP] His era name "Hongxi" means "vastly bright". [CLS] [SEP]
