[CLS] The CH-53 Sea Stallion (Sikorsky S-65) is a family of heavy-lift transport helicopters designed and built by Sikorsky Aircraft. [SEP] [CLS] Originally developed for use by the United States Marine Corps, it is currently in service with Germany, Iran, Israel, and Mexico. [SEP] [CLS] The United States Air Force operated the HH-53 "Super Jolly Green Giant" during the late– and post–Vietnam-War era, updating most of them as the MH-53 Pave Low. [SEP] [CLS] The dimensionally-similar CH-53E Super Stallion is a heavier-lifting, improved version designated S-80E by Sikorsky. [SEP] [CLS] Its third engine makes it more powerful than the Sea Stallion, which it has replaced in the heavy-lift mission. [SEP] [CLS]
