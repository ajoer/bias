The 1909 Borujerd earthquake also known as Silakhor earthquake occurred in Silakhor plain (in the south of today's Borujerd County), Persia (modern day Iran) on January 23. [CLS] [SEP] Around 8,000 fatalities were caused directly from the magnitude 7.3 earthquake. [CLS] [SEP] An indefinite number of aftershocks continued for six months after the main shock.
