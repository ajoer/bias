[CLS] On the evening of 27 April 1993, a DHC-5 Buffalo transport aircraft of the Zambian Air Force crashed into the Atlantic Ocean shortly after taking off from Libreville, Gabon. [SEP] [CLS] The flight was carrying most of the Zambian national football team to a FIFA World Cup Qualifier against Senegal in Dakar. [SEP] [CLS] All 25 passengers and five crew members were killed. [SEP] [CLS] The official investigation concluded that the pilot had shut down the wrong engine following an engine fire. [SEP] [CLS] It also found that pilot fatigue and a faulty instrument had contributed to the accident. [SEP] [CLS]
