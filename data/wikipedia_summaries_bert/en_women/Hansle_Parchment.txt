Hansle Parchment (born 17 June 1990) is a Jamaican track and field athlete, competing in the 110 metres hurdles.Hansle is a student of Psychology at the University of the West Indies, Kingston and won a gold medal at the 2011 Summer Universiade, running a personal best 13.24 to win the title for Jamaica. [CLS] [SEP] In June 2012, Parchment won the Jamaica Olympic trials in a new personal best of 13.18 to qualify for the Jamaican team for the 2012 Summer Olympics. [CLS] [SEP] On 8 August 2012, Parchment won the bronze medal in the final of the 110 m hurdles. [CLS] [SEP] He ran a new Jamaican record of 13.12 to clinch third place, behind Aries Merritt and Jason Richardson. [CLS] [SEP] At the 2015 World Championships in Beijing, Parchment took the silver medal in the final of the 110m hurdles. [CLS] [SEP] In a high-quality field consisting of world record holder and Olympic Champion Aries Merritt, defending champion David Oliver and European champion Sergey Shubenkov, Parchment was beaten only by the Shubenkov, running a season's best time of 13.03.
