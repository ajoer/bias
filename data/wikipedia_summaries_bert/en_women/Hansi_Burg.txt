[SEP] [CLS] She took up professional acting in 1917, appearing mostly on the stage but also in several silent films. [SEP] [CLS] Burg became known for her long-term relationship with the film star Hans Albers who she met while they were acting together. [SEP] [CLS] The Nazi Propaganda Minister Joseph Goebbels forced them to split up because she was Jewish, although they remained in secret contact. [SEP] [CLS] During the Second World War she lived in exile in Britain. [SEP] [CLS] She returned in 1945 and settled down to live with Albers again. [SEP] [CLS] Her father died in Theresienstadt concentration camp. [SEP] [CLS]
