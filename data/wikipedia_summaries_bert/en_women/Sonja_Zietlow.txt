Sonja Zietlow (born 13 May 1968 in Bonn) is a German television presenter. [CLS] [SEP] She began her career as a host of Hugo & Hexana and is most famous for her ongoing hosting, since 2004 of Ich bin ein Star – Holt mich hier raus!. [CLS] [SEP] More recently, she has hosted several RTL shows, most notably Der Schwächste fliegt!, a German adaptation of the hit BBC quiz show The Weakest Link. [CLS] [SEP] Since 2004, she hosts Ich bin ein Star – Holt mich hier raus!, the German adaption of I'm a Celebrity...Get Me Out of Here!. [CLS] [SEP] In 2004, she was awarded the title "Woman of the year (moderation)" by Maxim Deutschland. [CLS] [SEP] After she graduated from school and before her media career began in 1993, she worked as first officer for the German airline Lufthansa. [CLS] [SEP] When Zietlow was a candidate in a TV show, she was offered a job as host. [CLS] [SEP]
