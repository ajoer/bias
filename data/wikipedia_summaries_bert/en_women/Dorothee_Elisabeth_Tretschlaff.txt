[CLS] [SEP] She was the last person to have been executed for witchcraft in the state of Brandenburg. [CLS] [SEP] Her case attracted much attention, and was the subject of a great deal of contemporary debate. [CLS] [SEP] Tretschlaff was employed as a maid. [CLS] [SEP] She confessed that she had made a pact with the Devil, and that he visited her regularly for intercourse in her bed in the shape of a fly. [CLS] [SEP] The Devil supposedly paid Dorothee for her favors. [CLS] [SEP] The case against her was based on this alleged intercourse. [CLS] [SEP] Tretschlaff was found guilty of witchcraft and executed by decapitation. [CLS] [SEP] The sentence was criticized both before and after it was made. [CLS] [SEP] Later the same year, the monarch ordered an investigation to inquire into whether the process had been correct. [CLS] [SEP] Tretschlaff was found to be depressed and suicidal.
