Samuel Stewart Witwer (born October 20, 1977) is an American actor, voice actor, and musician. [CLS] [SEP] He is known for playing Crashdown in Battlestar Galactica, Davis Bloome in Smallville, Aidan Waite in Being Human, Mr. [CLS] [SEP] Hyde in Once Upon a Time, and Ben Lockwood in Supergirl. [CLS] [SEP] He has voiced Starkiller in Star Wars: The Force Unleashed, the Son in Star Wars: The Clone Wars and Emperor Palpatine in Star Wars Rebels. [CLS] [SEP] He has voiced Darth Maul in The Clone Wars, Rebels and Solo: A Star Wars Story. [CLS] [SEP] In 2019, he joined the cast of Riverdale as Jughead Jones' school teacher Mr. [CLS] [SEP] Rupert Chipping.
