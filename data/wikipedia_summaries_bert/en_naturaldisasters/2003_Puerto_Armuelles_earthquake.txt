The 2003 Puerto Armuelles earthquake occurred on December 25 at 02:11 local time (07:11 UTC). [CLS] [SEP] The epicenter was located in Panama, at about 7 km east of Puerto Armuelles, near the Panama-Costa Rica border. [CLS] [SEP] The earthquake had a magnitude of Mw 6.5. [CLS] [SEP] Two people were reported dead in Puerto Armuelles. [CLS] [SEP] There was building damage in Panama and Costa Rica. [CLS] [SEP] Power outage lasted for about four hours in Puerto Armuelles. [CLS] [SEP] The maximal intensity was MM VIII in Finca Naranjo, Costa Rica. [CLS] [SEP] The intensity was MM VII in Armuelles, Panama, and MM IV in Limón and the Central Valley, Costa Rica, including San José. [CLS] [SEP] This earthquake could also be felt in Panama City.
