[CLS] Valerie Anita Aurora is a software engineer and feminist activist. [SEP] [CLS] She was the co-founder of the Ada Initiative, a non-profit organization that sought to increase women's participation in the free culture movement, open source technology, and open source culture. [SEP] [CLS] Aurora is also known within the Linux community for advocating new developments in filesystems in Linux, including ChunkFS and the Union file system. [SEP] [CLS] Her birth name was Val Henson, but she changed it shortly before 2009, choosing her middle name after the computer scientist Anita Borg. [SEP] [CLS] In 2012, Aurora, and Ada Initiative co-founder Mary Gardiner, were named two of the most influential people in computer security by SC Magazine. [SEP] [CLS] In 2013, she won the O'Reilly Open Source Award. [SEP] [CLS]
