[CLS] Famke Beumer Janssen (Dutch: [ˈfɑmkə ˈjɑnsə(n)]; born c. [SEP] [CLS] 1964) is a Dutch actress, director, screenwriter, and former fashion model. [SEP] [CLS] She played Xenia Onatopp in GoldenEye (1995), Jean Grey / Phoenix in the X-Men film series (2000–2014), Ava Moore on Nip/Tuck, and Lenore Mills in the Taken film trilogy (2008–2014). [SEP] [CLS] In 2008, she was appointed a Goodwill Ambassador for Integrity by the United Nations. [SEP] [CLS] She made her directorial debut with Bringing Up Bobby in 2011. [SEP] [CLS] She is also known for her role in the Netflix original series Hemlock Grove and for her role in ABC's How to Get Away with Murder. [SEP] [CLS] Janssen starred in the 2017 NBC crime thriller The Blacklist: Redemption. [SEP] [CLS]
