Gün Temür (Mongolian: Гүнтөмөр хаан; Chinese: 坤帖木兒; 1377–1402) was a Mongolian Khagan of the Northern Yuan dynasty based in Mongolia. [CLS] [SEP] Gün Temür was the eldest son of Elbeg Nigülesügchi Khan. [CLS] [SEP] He ruled from 1400 to 1402. [CLS] [SEP] His name, Gün Temür, means "Deep (intellectually) Iron" in the Mongolian language. [CLS] [SEP]
