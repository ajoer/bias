[CLS] Pasherienptah (III) (p3-šrỉ-n-ptḥ, 'Son of Ptah'; November 4, 90 BCE – July 13 or 14, 41 BCE) was an ancient Egyptian high Priest of Ptah in Memphis from 76 BCE until his death. [SEP] [CLS] Two of his stelas are known, the one with a hieroglyphic inscription is in the Ashmolean Museum (Ash. [SEP] [CLS] M. [SEP] [CLS] 1971/18), the other, Demotic stela, of which only seven fragments have been found, is in the British Museum (BM 886). [SEP] [CLS]
