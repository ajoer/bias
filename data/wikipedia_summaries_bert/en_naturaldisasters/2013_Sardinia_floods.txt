The 2013 Sardinia floods took place in and around the Sardinian town of Olbia between 17 and 19 November 2013. [CLS] [SEP] They also affected other parts of northeastern Sardinia and killed at least 18 people. [CLS] [SEP] Thousands of people were left homeless. [CLS] [SEP] The places which suffered the most damage were Olbia, and in general the whole region of Gallura. [CLS] [SEP] The flood caused damages of over 1 billion euro. [CLS] [SEP]
