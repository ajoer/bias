Juju or ju-ju (French: joujou, lit. [CLS] [SEP] 'plaything') is a spiritual belief system incorporating objects, such as amulets, and spells used in religious practice, as part of witchcraft in West Africa especially the people of Nigeria and Cameroon. [CLS] [SEP] The term has been applied to traditional African religions.In a general sense the term "juju" can be used to refer to magical properties dealing with good luck. [CLS] [SEP]
