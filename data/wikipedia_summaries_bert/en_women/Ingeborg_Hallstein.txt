Ingeborg Hallstein (born 23 May 1936) is a German coloratura soprano, known for the purity and range of her voice. [CLS] [SEP] She had an international career as a guest singer on the opera houses of Europe and was a member of the Bavarian State Opera from 1961 to 1973. [CLS] [SEP] Her signature roles were the Queen of the Night in Mozart's Die Zauberflöte and Zerbinetta in Ariadne auf Naxos by Richard Strauss. [CLS] [SEP] She created roles including Scolatella in Henze's König Hirsch. [CLS] [SEP]
