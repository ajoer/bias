[CLS] On 3 July 1968 Airspeed Ambassador registration G-AMAD of BKS Air Transport crashed at London Heathrow Airport, damaging two parked Trident airliners as it cartwheeled into the incomplete London Heathrow Terminal 1, then under construction. [SEP] [CLS] Six of the eight people on board the Ambassador were killed, along with the eight racehorses being transported on it. [SEP] [CLS] The crash was blamed on the failure of a flap-operating rod due to metal fatigue, resulting in asymmetrical lift. [SEP] [CLS]
