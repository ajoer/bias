Juliana Rotich is a Kenyan information technology professional, who has developed web tools for crowdsourcing crisis information and coverage of topics related to the environment. [CLS] [SEP] She is the co-founder of iHub, a collective tech space in Nairobi, Kenya, and of Ushahidi, open-source software for collecting and mapping information. [CLS] [SEP] She is a TED Senior Fellow.She is a trustee of Bankinter Foundation for Entrepreneurship and Innovation in Spain, as well as a board member of Standard Media Group and the Kenya Vision 2030 Delivery Board. [CLS] [SEP] She was the head of East Africa Country Cluster for BASF from May to December 2018. [CLS] [SEP]
