[CLS] Brigitte Margret Ida Mohnhaupt (born 24 June 1949) is a German convicted former terrorist associated with the second generation of the Red Army Faction (RAF) members. [SEP] [CLS] She was also part of the Socialist Patients' Collective (SPK). [SEP] [CLS] From 1971 until 1982 she was active within the RAF. [SEP] [CLS]
