[CLS] The 1983 Biga earthquake hit northwestern Turkey on 5 July 1983. [SEP] [CLS] Responsible for five deaths and approximately twenty-six casualties throughout Biga and Erdek and damage in Istanbul, the earthquake measured 6.1 on the surface wave magnitude scale. [SEP] [CLS] It shook places as far away as eastern Greece. [SEP] [CLS] The United States Geological Survey listed the earthquake among the "Significant Earthquakes of the World" for 1983.
