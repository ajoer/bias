Emperor Muzong of Liao (19 September 931 – 12 March 969), personal name Yelü Jing, infant name Shulü, was the fourth emperor of the Khitan-led Liao dynasty. [CLS] [SEP] He was the eldest son of the second Liao emperor, Emperor Taizong. [CLS] [SEP] He succeeded his cousin, Emperor Shizong, after the latter was murdered in 951. [CLS] [SEP]
