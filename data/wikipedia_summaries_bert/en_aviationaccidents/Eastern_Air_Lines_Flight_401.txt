Eastern Air Lines Flight 401 was a scheduled flight from New York to Miami. [CLS] [SEP] Shortly before midnight on December 29, 1972, the Lockheed L-1011-1 Tristar crashed into the Florida Everglades, causing 101 fatalities. [CLS] [SEP] The pilots and the flight engineer, two of 10 flight attendants, and 96 of 163 passengers died; 75 passengers and crew survived. [CLS] [SEP] The crash occurred while the entire cockpit crew was preoccupied with a burnt-out landing gear indicator light. [CLS] [SEP] They failed to notice that the autopilot had inadvertently been disconnected and, as a result, the aircraft gradually lost altitude and crashed. [CLS] [SEP] This was the first fatal crash of a wide-body aircraft. [CLS] [SEP]
