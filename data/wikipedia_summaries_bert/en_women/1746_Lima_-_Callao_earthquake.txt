The 1746 Lima–Callao earthquake occurred at 22:30 local time on 28 October with a moment magnitude of 8.6–8.8 and a maximum Mercalli intensity of XI (Extreme). [CLS] [SEP] The epicenter was located about 90 km (56 mi) north-northwest of the capital Lima, which was almost completely destroyed, and the subsequent tsunami devastated the port city of Callao. [CLS] [SEP] It was the deadliest earthquake in Peru’s history prior to the 1970 earthquake. [CLS] [SEP]
