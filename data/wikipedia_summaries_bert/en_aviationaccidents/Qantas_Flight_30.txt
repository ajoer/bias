[CLS] Qantas Flight 30 (QF30, QFA30), on 25 July 2008, operated by Qantas Boeing 747-438, construction number 25067, registration VH-OJK, was a scheduled flight from London Heathrow to Melbourne with a stopover in Hong Kong. [SEP] [CLS] The flight was interrupted on the Hong Kong leg by an exploding oxygen tank that ruptured the fuselage just forward of the starboard wing root. [SEP] [CLS] The plane made an emergency descent to a breathable altitude of about 10,000 feet and diverted to Ninoy Aquino International Airport, Metro Manila, Philippines. [SEP] [CLS] There were no injuries. [SEP] [CLS]
