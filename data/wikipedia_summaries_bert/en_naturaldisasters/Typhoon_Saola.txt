The name Saola has been used to name three tropical cyclones in the northwestern Pacific Ocean. [CLS] [SEP] The name was contributed by Vietnam and refers to the saola, a rarely observed bovine species first found in Vietnam. [CLS] [SEP] Typhoon Saola (2005) (T0517, 18W) Typhoon Saola (2012) (T1209, 10W, Gener) Severe Tropical Storm Saola (2017) (T1722, 27W, Quedan)
