Tímea Nagy (born 22 August 1970) is a Hungarian épée fencer. [CLS] [SEP] She competed at the 1996, 2000 and 2004 Olympics and won individual gold medals in 2000 and 2004; her teams placed fourth-fifth. [CLS] [SEP] She won the individual world title in 2006 and a gold medal with the Hungarian teams in 1992, 1993, 1995, 1997 and 1999. [CLS] [SEP] In 2006, she was voted Hungarian Sportswoman of the Year. [CLS] [SEP]
