[CLS] Pakistan International Airlines Flight 268 was an Airbus A300, registration AP-BCP, which crashed on approach to Kathmandu's Tribhuvan International Airport on 28 September 1992. [SEP] [CLS] All 167 people on board were killed. [SEP] [CLS] It is the deadliest aviation crash to occur on Nepalese soil. [SEP] [CLS]
