[CLS] Japan Airlines Flight 2 was a flight piloted by Captain Kohei Asoh on November 22, 1968. [SEP] [CLS] The plane was a new Douglas DC-8 named "Shiga", flying from Tokyo International Airport (Haneda) to San Francisco International Airport. [SEP] [CLS] Due to heavy fog and other factors, Asoh mistakenly landed the plane near Coyote Point in the shallow waters of San Francisco Bay, two and a half miles short of the runway. [SEP] [CLS] None of the 96 passengers or 11 crew were injured in the landing. [SEP] [CLS]
