Evelyn Schlag (born 1952) is an Austrian poet and novelist. [CLS] [SEP] She was born and raised in Waidhofen an der Ybbs and studied at the University of Vienna. [CLS] [SEP] She lived in Vienna for a few years before moving back to her native Waidhofen, where she continues to live today. [CLS] [SEP] Schlag has published more than a dozen books ranging from prose fiction to acclaimed poetry. [CLS] [SEP] She has also translated the work of the British poet Douglas Dunn into German. [CLS] [SEP] Schlag’s novel Die Kränkung was published in English translation under the title Quotations of a Body. [CLS] [SEP] Karen Leeder's translation of Schlag's selected poetry won the Schlegel-Tieck Prize. [CLS] [SEP]
