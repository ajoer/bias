[CLS] Thiago Alcântara do Nascimento (born 11 April 1991), simply known as Thiago (Spanish: [ˈtjaɣo]; Portuguese: [tʃiˈaɡu]), is a Spanish professional footballer who plays as a central midfielder for Bundesliga club Bayern Munich and the Spain national team. [SEP] [CLS] Born in Italy to Brazilian parents, his father being former footballer and World Cup champion Mazinho who played for Lecce in Serie A at the time of Thiago's birth, he joined Barcelona aged 14, and made his first team debut in 2009. [SEP] [CLS] After winning honours including four La Liga titles, the UEFA Champions League and the FIFA Club World Cup for Barcelona, he was signed by Bayern for €25 million in 2013. [SEP] [CLS] At Bayern, he has won 14 trophies, including the Bundesliga in each of his first seven seasons. [SEP] [CLS] After helping Spain to European Championships at under-19 and under-21 levels, he made his full international debut in 2011. [SEP] [CLS] He was part of the provisional squad for Spain in the 2014 World Cup but withdrew due to a knee injury. [SEP] [CLS] Thiago was selected for UEFA Euro 2016 and the 2018 FIFA World Cup.
