[CLS] On 15 December 2010, a DHC-6 Twin Otter passenger aircraft of Tara Air crashed shortly after take-off on a domestic flight from Lamidanda to Kathmandu, Nepal. [SEP] [CLS] The wreckage of the aircraft was found in Bilandu forest near the village of Shreechaur the morning after the crash. [SEP] [CLS] All 19 passengers and three crew aboard were killed in the crash. [SEP] [CLS] There was initial speculation that bad weather or the overloading of the aircraft might have caused the crash. [SEP] [CLS] An investigation into the crash was launched by Nepalese authorities after the accident site was located. [SEP] [CLS]
