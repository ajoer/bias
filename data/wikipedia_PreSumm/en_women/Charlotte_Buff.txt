[SEP] [CLS] She rejected him and instead married Johann Christian Kestner, vice-archivist and privy councillor to the Hanoverian court. [SEP] [CLS] The character of Lotte, in Goethe's novel The Sorrows of Young Werther, is partly based on her. [SEP] [CLS] Their relationship was characterized by heartiness and lack of constraint. [SEP] [CLS] Goethe bought the wedding rings for her and Kestner, in Frankfurt am Main. [SEP] [CLS] Charlotte and Kestner had four daughters and eight sons, among them August Kestner. [SEP] [CLS]
