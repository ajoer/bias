Leah H. [CLS] [SEP] Jamieson (born August 27, 1949, in Trenton, NJ, USA) is an American engineering educator, currently the Ransburg Distinguished Professor of Electrical and Computer Engineering at Purdue University. [CLS] [SEP] From 2006-2017, she served as the John A. [CLS] [SEP] Edwardson Dean of Engineering at Purdue. [CLS] [SEP] She is a member of the US National Academy of Engineering and served as the 2007 President and CEO of the Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers (IEEE). [CLS] [SEP] Jamieson was a founder of the Engineering Projects in Community Service program (EPICS), a multi-university engineering design program that operates in a service-learning context. [CLS] [SEP] She is a recipient of the Gordon Prize. [CLS] [SEP]
