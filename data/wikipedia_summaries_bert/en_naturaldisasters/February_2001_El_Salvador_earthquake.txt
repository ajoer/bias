The February 2001 El Salvador earthquake occurred with a moment magnitude of 6.6 on 13 February at 14:22:05 UTC. [CLS] [SEP] The epicentre was 15 miles (30 km) E of San Salvador, El Salvador. [CLS] [SEP] At least 315 people were killed, 3,399 were injured, and extensive damage affected the area. [CLS] [SEP] Another 16,752 homes were damaged and 44,759 destroyed. [CLS] [SEP] The most severe damage occurred in the San Juan Tepezontes-San Vicente-Cojutepeque area, though it was felt throughout the country and in neighboring Guatemala and Honduras. [CLS] [SEP] Landslides occurred in many areas of El Salvador. [CLS] [SEP]
