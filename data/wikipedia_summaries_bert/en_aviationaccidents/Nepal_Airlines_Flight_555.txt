[CLS] Nepal Airlines Flight 555 was a short domestic scheduled flight from Pokhara Airport to Jomsom Airport in Nepal of about 20 minutes' flying time, operated by Nepal Airlines. [SEP] [CLS] On 16 May 2013 the de Havilland Canada DHC-6 Twin Otter aircraft operating the flight crashed while landing at Jomsom Airport. [SEP] [CLS] Seven of the twenty-one on board were seriously injured. [SEP] [CLS] There were no fatalities, but the aircraft was damaged beyond economic repair. [SEP] [CLS]
