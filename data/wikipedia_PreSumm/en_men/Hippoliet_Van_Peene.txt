[SEP] [CLS] He studied medicine at the State University of Leuven and became a physician in Kaprijke and later in Ghent. [SEP] [CLS] In 1847 he wrote the lyrics of the Flemish anthem De Vlaamse Leeuw of which the music was composed by Karel Miry. [SEP] [CLS] He was the first modern Flemish playwright and he wrote 35 theatre plays, which in his time were performed by Broedermin en Taelyver in Ghent. [SEP] [CLS] At the inauguration of the Minard Theatre in 1847, his Brigitta of de Twee Vondelingen was performed. [SEP] [CLS]
