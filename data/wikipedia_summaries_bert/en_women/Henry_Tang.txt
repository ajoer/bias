Henry Tang Ying-yen (Chinese: 唐英年; born 6 September 1952) is a Hong Kong politician who served as the Chief Secretary of Hong Kong between 2007 and 2011. [CLS] [SEP] He held the position of Financial Secretary from 2003 to 2007. [CLS] [SEP] In 2012, he lost the Hong Kong Chief Executive Election to Leung Chun-ying. [CLS] [SEP]
