[CLS] Kotono Mitsuishi (三石 琴乃, Mitsuishi Kotono, born December 8, 1967) is a Japanese voice actress, singer, and narrator. [SEP] [CLS] She was affiliated with Arts Vision and Lasley Arrow, but is now freelance. [SEP] [CLS] Mitsuishi lived in Nagareyama, Chiba. [SEP] [CLS] She graduated from high school and entered the Katsuta Voice Actor's Academy in 1986. [SEP] [CLS] She is well known for her roles Usagi Tsukino in Sailor Moon, Misato Katsuragi in Neon Genesis Evangelion, and Sayaka Mine in Yaiba. [SEP] [CLS]
