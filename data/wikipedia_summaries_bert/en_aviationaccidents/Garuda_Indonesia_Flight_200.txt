[CLS] Garuda Indonesia Flight 200 (GA200/GIA 200) was a scheduled domestic passenger flight of a Boeing 737-400 operated by Garuda Indonesia between Jakarta and Yogyakarta, Indonesia. [SEP] [CLS] The aircraft overran the runway, crashed into a rice field and burst into flames while landing at Adisutjipto International Airport on 7 March 2007. [SEP] [CLS] Twenty passengers and one crew member were killed. [SEP] [CLS] Both the captain and the first officer survived, but then were fired shortly after the accident occurred. [SEP] [CLS]
