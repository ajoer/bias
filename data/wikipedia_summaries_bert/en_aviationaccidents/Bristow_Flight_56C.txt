[CLS] Bristow Helicopters Flight 56C was a helicopter flight that flew between Aberdeen and the Brae Alpha oil rig in the North Sea. [SEP] [CLS] On 19 January 1995, the AS 332L Super Puma helicopter operating the route, registered G-TIGK and named Cullen, was struck by lightning. [SEP] [CLS] The flight was carrying 16 oil workers from Aberdeen to an oil platform at the Brae oilfield. [SEP] [CLS] All 18 people on board survived. [SEP] [CLS]
