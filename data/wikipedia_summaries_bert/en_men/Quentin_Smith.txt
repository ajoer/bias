Quentin Persifor Smith (born August 27, 1952, Rhinebeck, New York) is a professor emeritus of philosophy at Western Michigan University in Kalamazoo, Michigan. [CLS] [SEP] He has worked in the philosophy of time, philosophy of language, philosophy of physics and philosophy of religion. [CLS] [SEP] Smith has published over 140 articles. [CLS] [SEP] Of his published books, he has authored three, co-authored two, and co-authored and edited seven. [CLS] [SEP] He was an editor for Prometheus Books and was the chief editor for Philo from 2001 to 2007. [CLS] [SEP] He has debated William Lane Craig over the existence of God. [CLS] [SEP]
