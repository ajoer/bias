[CLS] Joachim Löw (German pronunciation: [ˈjoːaxɪm ˈløːf, joˈʔa-]; born 3 February 1960) is a German football coach and former player. [SEP] [CLS] He is the head coach of the Germany national team, which he led to victory at the 2014 FIFA World Cup in Brazil and the 2017 FIFA Confederations Cup in Russia. [SEP] [CLS]
