[CLS] The 2010 Serbia earthquake (also referred to as the Kraljevo earthquake) occurred on 3 November in central Serbia just several kilometers from Kraljevo. [SEP] [CLS] The earthquake had a magnitude of 5.5 and a maximum Mercalli intensity of VI (Strong). [SEP] [CLS] The shock was felt across the country, including the capital Belgrade, and in neighboring countries. [SEP] [CLS] Two people were killed and over 100 suffered light injuries. [SEP] [CLS] There were 5,967 structures that sustained some damage, 1,551 declared unsafe for use and require repairment, and 138 were damaged beyond repair. [SEP] [CLS] There were more than 350 aftershocks, including a magnitude Mwr 4.3 earthquake on November 4. [SEP] [CLS]
