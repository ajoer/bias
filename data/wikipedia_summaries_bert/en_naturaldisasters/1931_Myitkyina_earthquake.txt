The 1931 Myitkyina earthquake, or also known as the 1931 Kamaing earthquake, occurred on January 28 at 02:35 local time (20:9 January 27 UTC). [CLS] [SEP] It was located in northern Burma, then part of British India. [CLS] [SEP] The magnitude of this earthquake was put at Mw 7.6. [CLS] [SEP] According to some sources the depth was 35 km, and according to a study of Phyo M. [CLS] [SEP] M. [CLS] [SEP] the depth was 5 to 30 km.The shock was very violent and lasted at least 30 seconds. [CLS] [SEP] The intensity reached MMI IX. [CLS] [SEP] There were numerous fissures and cracks. [CLS] [SEP] Sand blows were reported. [CLS] [SEP] The earthquake may have been caused by slip along the Sagaing Fault. [CLS] [SEP] The Sagaing Fault is a continental transform fault between the India Plate and the Sunda Plate. [CLS] [SEP] This earthquake is located along the northern Sagaing Fault. [CLS] [SEP] Sagaing Fault at 22° N is narrow, about 10 km wide. [CLS] [SEP] The part of Sagaing Fault between 25°30' and 26° is wider, with a shear zone about 70 km wide, and has four branches identified. [CLS] [SEP]
