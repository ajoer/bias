[CLS] Patrik Köbele (born 1962 in Weil am Rhein) is a German politician and leader of the German Communist Party (DKP). [SEP] [CLS] Between 1989 and 1994, Köbele was leader of the Sozialistische Deutsche Arbeiterjugend (SDAJ), the DKP's youth organization. [SEP] [CLS] Between 2004 and 2009, he was member of the city council of Essen. [SEP] [CLS] On 2 March 2013 he was elected leader of the DKP. [SEP] [CLS] Köbele won with 91 to 60 votes against former leader Bettina Jürgensen. [SEP] [CLS] Privately, Köbele lives in Essen with his two children and works as IT consultant. [SEP] [CLS]
