Marion Wagner (born 1 February 1978, in Mainz) is a retired German sprinter who specialized in the 100 metres. [CLS] [SEP] Her personal best time is 11.24 seconds, which was achieved in July 2009 at the German Championships in Ulm. [CLS] [SEP] She represents the sports club USC Mainz. [CLS] [SEP] Wagner represented Germany at the 2008 Summer Olympics in Beijing. [CLS] [SEP] She competed in the 4×100 metres relay together with Anne Möllinger, Cathleen Tschirch and Verena Sailer. [CLS] [SEP] In their first round heat they placed third behind Jamaica and Russia and in front of China. [CLS] [SEP] Their time of 43.59 seconds was the eighth time overall out of sixteen participating nations. [CLS] [SEP] With this result they qualified for the final in which they sprinted to a time of 43.28 seconds, which was the fifth place. [CLS] [SEP] She was an unused reserve for the German 4 × 100 m team at the 2012 Summer Olympics. [CLS] [SEP]
