The name Norman has been used for seven tropical cyclones in the eastern Pacific Ocean. [CLS] [SEP] Hurricane Norman (1978) – a Category 4 hurricane that struck California as a tropical depression. [CLS] [SEP] Hurricane Norman (1982) – a Category 2 hurricane that turned toward Baja California. [CLS] [SEP] Tropical Storm Norman (1994) – weak, short-lived tropical storm. [CLS] [SEP] Tropical Storm Norman (2000) – struck Mexico. [CLS] [SEP] Tropical Storm Norman (2006) – near southwestern Mexico. [CLS] [SEP] Tropical Storm Norman (2012) – weak and short lived, it also struck Mexico. [CLS] [SEP] Hurricane Norman (2018) – powerful Category 4 hurricane that moved into the Central Pacific.Norman has been used for one tropical cyclone in the Southern Hemisphere: Cyclone Norman (2000) – no direct impact on Western Australia.
