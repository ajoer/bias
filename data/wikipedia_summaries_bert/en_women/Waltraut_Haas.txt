[CLS] Waltraut Haas (born 9 June 1927) is an Austrian actress and singer.Born in Vienna, Haas grew up at Schloss Schönbrunn, where her mother was a restaurateur. [SEP] [CLS] Haas made her stage debut in Linz but was soon won over to the big screen. [SEP] [CLS] She achieved fame and recognition already through her first movie, the cult classic Der Hofrat Geiger (1947), in which she played Mariandl, the illegitimate daughter of a woman who runs an inn in the picturesque Wachau valley. [SEP] [CLS] In 1961, Haas would play Mariandl's single mother in a remake entitled Mariandl. [SEP] [CLS] Her other famous movie role was that of Josepha Vogelhuber in the 1960 film The White Horse Inn (Im weißen Rößl) opposite Peter Alexander. [SEP] [CLS] After 60 movies, mainly comedies and musicals, she stopped making films around 1970. [SEP] [CLS] Since then, she has also appeared on television. [SEP] [CLS] From 1966 until his death in 2011, Waltraut Haas was married to actor Erwin Strahl, with whom she frequently performed both on the stage and in films. [SEP] [CLS] Their son Marcus Strahl is an actor and director. [SEP] [CLS] Haas lives in Hietzing, Vienna. [SEP] [CLS]
