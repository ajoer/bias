[CLS] Park Won-soon (Korean: 박원순; March 26, 1956 – July 9, 2020) was a South Korean politician, philanthropist, activist, and lawyer who served as the longest-serving mayor of Seoul from 2011 until his death in July 2020. [SEP] [CLS] A member of the Democratic Party, he was first elected in 2011 and won re-election in 2014 and 2018. [SEP] [CLS] Prior to being elected mayor, Park was a community and social justice activist. [SEP] [CLS] He was a member of the Truth and Reconciliation Commission. [SEP] [CLS] A noted political donor in Seoul, Park contributed to political organizations and think tanks that advocated for grassroots solutions towards social, educational, environmental, and political issues.
