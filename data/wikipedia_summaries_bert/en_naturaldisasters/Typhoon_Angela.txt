[CLS] Typhoon Angela, known in the Philippines as Typhoon Rosing, was a catastrophic Category 5 typhoon with 180 mph (290 km/h) sustained winds. [SEP] [CLS] Typhoon Angela was the third storm in a row that struck the Philippines, following Yvette and Zack. [SEP] [CLS] Typhoon Angela was the twenty-ninth tropical cyclone, and the fifth super typhoon of the moderately active 1995 Pacific typhoon season. [SEP] [CLS] Angela caused 9.33 billion Philippine pesos in catastrophic damage across the Philippines, resulting in 882 fatalities. [SEP] [CLS] It was the strongest typhoon to hit the Philippines in 25 years. [SEP] [CLS]
