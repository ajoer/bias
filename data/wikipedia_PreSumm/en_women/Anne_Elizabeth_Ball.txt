[CLS] Anne Elizabeth Ball (1808–1872) was an Irish botanist, amateur algologist, and botanical illustrator. [SEP] [CLS] Born in Cobh 1808, Ball was a sister of naturalist Robert Ball (1802–1857) and zoologist Mary Ball (1812–1898). [SEP] [CLS] The siblings became interested in natural history through the passion of their father, Bob Stawell Ball. [SEP] [CLS]
