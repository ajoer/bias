The eruption of the Trevejo volcano also called the eruption of the Arenas Negras volcano was a volcanic eruption that took place from 5 to 14 May 1706, about 8 kilometers south of the Garachico in the north of the island of Tenerife (Spain). [CLS] [SEP] The eruption destroyed the old port of Garachico (the most important of Tenerife at the time) and ended the golden period of the town. [CLS] [SEP]
