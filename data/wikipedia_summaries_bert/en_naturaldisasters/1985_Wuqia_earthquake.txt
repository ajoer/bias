The 1985 Wuqia earthquake occurred on August 23, 1985, at 20:42 local time (12:42 UTC) near the border of Wuqia County and Shufu County, Xinjiang, China. [CLS] [SEP] It had a magnitude of Ms 7.4 and caused 71 deaths, 162 injuries, and left 15,000 homeless, as well as destroying 85% of buildings and highways. [CLS] [SEP] The source of this earthquake is the Kazkeaerte Fault (卡兹克阿尔特断裂). [CLS] [SEP] The earthquake could be felt throughout much of the Fergana Basin, USSR, as well as in Pakistan.The slipping of the earth during this earthquake caused a deformation zone along the Kezilesu River valley stretching 15 kilometres (9 1⁄3 miles) long and 800 metres (1⁄2 mile) wide. [CLS] [SEP] The zone is made up of smaller faults, fissures, and pressure ridges. [CLS] [SEP]
