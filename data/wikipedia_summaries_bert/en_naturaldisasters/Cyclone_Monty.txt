Severe Tropical Cyclone Monty was a powerful category 4 tropical cyclone that formed during late February 2004. [CLS] [SEP] Monty was the 6th tropical cyclone and the 3rd Severe tropical cyclone of the 2003-04 Australian region cyclone season. [CLS] [SEP] Monty made landfall in a large, sparsely populated region of the Pilbara, Western Australia. [CLS] [SEP] Due to the low population, damages inland were not high, although there was major flooding and many roads were impassable because they were flooded or washed away by water. [CLS] [SEP] Monty set record high amounts of flooding in some parts of Western Australia. [CLS] [SEP]
