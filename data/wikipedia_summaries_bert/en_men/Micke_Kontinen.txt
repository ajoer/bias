[CLS] Micke Kontinen (born 18 December 1992) is a Finnish tennis player. [SEP] [CLS] He is the younger brother of Henri Kontinen who is also a tennis player. [SEP] [CLS] Kontinen has a career high ATP singles ranking of 355 achieved on 15 September 2014. [SEP] [CLS] He also has a career high ATP doubles ranking of 489 achieved on 26 May 2014. [SEP] [CLS] Kontinen made his ATP main draw debut at the 2013 Swedish Open in the doubles event where he partnered Isak Arvidsson as a wildcard entrant but lost in the first round to Martín Alund and João Souza, 4–6, 6–7(5–7)
