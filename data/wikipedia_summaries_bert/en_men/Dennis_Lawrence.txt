Dennis William Lawrence CM (born 1 August 1974) is a Trinidad and Tobago former footballer. [CLS] [SEP] He is currently the manager of the Trinidad and Tobago national team. [CLS] [SEP] Prior to coaching, he had a successful playing career in England, Wales and Trinidad and Tobago. [CLS] [SEP] He lifted the Caribbean Cup with the Soca Warriors and won several cup competitions with Wrexham before winning a league title with Swansea City. [CLS] [SEP] Before moving to Everton, he had coached for three years at Wigan Athletic during which time he became the first Trinidadian to win The FA Cup. [CLS] [SEP]
