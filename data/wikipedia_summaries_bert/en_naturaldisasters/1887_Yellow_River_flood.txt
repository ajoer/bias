The 1887 Yellow River flood was a devastating flood on the Yellow River (Huang He) in China. [CLS] [SEP] This river is prone to flooding due to the elevated nature of the river, running between dikes above the broad plains surrounding it. [CLS] [SEP] The flood, which began in September 1887, killed at least 900,000 people. [CLS] [SEP] The highest estimated death toll is however 2,000,000. [CLS] [SEP] It was one of the deadliest natural disasters ever recorded. [CLS] [SEP]
