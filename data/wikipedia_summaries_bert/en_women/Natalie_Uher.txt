Emmanuelle 6 is a 1988 French softcore erotica movie directed by Bruno Zincone, and starred Austrian actress Natalie Uher. [CLS] [SEP] The screenplay was written by cult erotic horror specialist Jean Rollin, based on the novel Emmanuelle: The Joys of a Woman by Emmanuelle Arsan. [CLS] [SEP] The filming location was Venezuela. [CLS] [SEP] Although hardcore scenes were filmed to make this remake of the original 1974 version more edgy, they were not used in any known available cuts except for the French VHS version, which is 10 minutes longer.
