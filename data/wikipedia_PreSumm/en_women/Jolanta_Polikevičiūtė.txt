[CLS] Jolanta Polikevičiūtė (born 25 September 1970 in Panevėžys) is a retired female road racing cyclist from Lithuania, who competed in three Summer Olympics for her native country: 1996, 2004 and 2008. [SEP] [CLS] She is the twin sister of Rasa Polikevičiūtė, who also had a professional career in women's cycling. [SEP] [CLS]
