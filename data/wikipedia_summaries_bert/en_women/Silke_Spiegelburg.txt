[SEP] [CLS] She represented Germany at the Summer Olympics in 2004, 2008 and 2012, as well as having competed at the World Championships in Athletics. [SEP] [CLS] She is a European silver medallist in the event both indoors and outdoors. [SEP] [CLS] Spiegelburg showed her talent for the event from a young age, winning the World Youth title in 2001 before becoming the European Junior champion two years later. [SEP] [CLS] She competed at the 2004 Athens Olympics at the age of eighteen and set a world junior record for the pole vault in 2005. [SEP] [CLS] She is a two-time national champion in the event and holds the German indoor record with her mark of 4.76 metres. [SEP] [CLS]
