Lufthansa Flight 540 was a scheduled commercial flight for Lufthansa operated with a Boeing 747-130, carrying 157 people (140 passengers and 17 crew members). [CLS] [SEP] The flight was operating the final segment of its Frankfurt–Nairobi–Johannesburg route. [CLS] [SEP] On 20 November 1974 it crashed and caught fire shortly past the runway after taking off from Jomo Kenyatta International Airport in Nairobi. [CLS] [SEP] This was the first fatal accident of a Boeing 747. [CLS] [SEP]
