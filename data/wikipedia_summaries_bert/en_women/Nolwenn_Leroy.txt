[CLS] Nolwenn Le Magueresse (born 28 September 1982 in Saint-Renan, Brittany, France), known by her stage name Nolwenn Leroy (French pronunciation: [nolwɛn ləʁwa]), is a French singer-songwriter, musician and voice actress. [SEP] [CLS] Originally classically trained (violin and opera singing), she rose to fame after winning the second season of the French television music competition Star Academy, in 2002. [SEP] [CLS] She has since recorded seven studio albums and scored two number one singles, "Cassé" and "Nolwenn Ohwo!", on the French charts. [SEP] [CLS] In 2012, her album Bretonne was certified two times diamond for sales exceeding one million copies.Leroy is fluent in English, having spent a year in the US as an exchange student. [SEP] [CLS] She sings in many languages, including French, Breton, English and Irish. [SEP] [CLS] She has also performed in Italian and Spanish. [SEP] [CLS] Leroy has received numerous awards and nominations. [SEP] [CLS] In January 2015, she was ranked 17th on Le Journal du Dimanche's 50 Most Loved Celebrities in France, making her the top female singer on the list since December 2012. [SEP] [CLS]
