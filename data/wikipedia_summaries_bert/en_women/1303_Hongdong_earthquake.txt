The 1303 Hongdong earthquake occurred in China, then part of the Mongol Empire, on September 25. [CLS] [SEP] The shock was estimated to have a magnitude of 8.0 and it had a maximum Mercalli intensity of XI (Extreme). [CLS] [SEP] With catastrophic damage, it was one of the deadliest recorded earthquakes of all time. [CLS] [SEP]
