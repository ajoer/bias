[CLS] The 1966 Peru earthquake occurred on October 17 at 16:41 local time (21:41 UTC). [SEP] [CLS] It had a magnitude of Mw 8.1. [SEP] [CLS] The epicenter was located offcoast near Huacho. [SEP] [CLS] About 100 people were reported dead. [SEP] [CLS] Most of the damage was recorded in low buildings. [SEP] [CLS] Cracks were reported in higher buildings. [SEP] [CLS] The intensity was MM IX in San Nicolás, MM VIII in Huacho, and MM VII in Callao. [SEP] [CLS] A tsunami was generated by this earthquake, which was recorded in Callao, Chimbote, and San Juan. [SEP] [CLS] Landslides and huge ground cracks were reported along the Pan American Highway north of Ancón. [SEP] [CLS]
