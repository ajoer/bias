The March 2008 Midwest floods were a massive flooding event in the Southern Midwest and portions of the Southern Plains. [CLS] [SEP] Cape Girardeau, Missouri officially reported 11.48 inches (29.2 cm) between March 18 and 19. [CLS] [SEP] At least 17 people died as a result of the flooding.
