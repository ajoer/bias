Transbrasil Flight 303 was a flight from Congonhas-São Paulo Airport in São Paulo, Brazil, to Hercílio Luz International Airport in Florianópolis, Brazil, on April 12, 1980. [CLS] [SEP] It crashed on approach to Hercílio Luz International Airport. [CLS] [SEP] Only three of the 58 people on board survived. [CLS] [SEP]
