[CLS] The May 1995 Louisiana flood, also known as the May 1995 Southeast Louisiana and Southern Mississippi Flood, was a heavy rainfall event which occurred across an area stretching from the New Orleans metropolitan area into southern Mississippi. [SEP] [CLS] A storm total rainfall maximum of 27.5 inches (700 mm) was recorded near Necaise, Mississippi. [SEP] [CLS] Considerable flooding was caused by the rainfall including several record flood crests along impacted river systems. [SEP] [CLS] The flooding caused six fatalities and more than $3.1 billion in damage. [SEP] [CLS]
