The 1971 Kuala Lumpur floods was a major flash flood disaster in Malaysia that occurred in January 1971. [CLS] [SEP] The flooding was the result of heavy monsoon rains, which swelled the Klang, Batu, and Gombak rivers. [CLS] [SEP] 32 people were killed and 180,000 people were affected. [CLS] [SEP] The Malaysian Prime Minister Tun Abdul Razak declared a state of national disaster in Western Malaysia.The floods were the worst in the country since 1926. [CLS] [SEP] As a result of the flooding, the Kuala Lumpur Flood Mitigation Programme was set up. [CLS] [SEP]
