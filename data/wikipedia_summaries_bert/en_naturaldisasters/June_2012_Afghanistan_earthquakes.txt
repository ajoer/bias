On 11 June 2012, two moderate earthquakes struck northern Afghanistan, causing a large landslide. [CLS] [SEP] The landslide buried the town of Sayi Hazara, trapping 71 people. [CLS] [SEP] After four days of digging, only five bodies were recovered and the search was called off. [CLS] [SEP] Overall, 75 people were killed and 13 others were injured. [CLS] [SEP]
