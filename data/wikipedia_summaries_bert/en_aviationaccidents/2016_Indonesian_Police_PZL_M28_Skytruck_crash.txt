[CLS] The PZL M28 Skytruck is a Polish STOL light cargo and passenger plane, produced by PZL Mielec, as a development of license-built Antonov An-28s. [SEP] [CLS] Early licence-built planes were designated PZL An-28. [SEP] [CLS] The maritime patrol and reconnaissance variants are named PZL M28B Bryza ("sea breeze"). [SEP] [CLS]
