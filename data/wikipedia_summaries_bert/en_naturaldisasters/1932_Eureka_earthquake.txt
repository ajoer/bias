The 1932 Eureka earthquake occurred on June 6 at 00:44:26 local time along the northern coastal area of California in the United States. [CLS] [SEP] With a moment magnitude of 6.4 and a maximum Mercalli intensity of VIII (Severe), this earthquake left one person dead from a falling chimney and several injured. [CLS] [SEP] The shock was the largest in the area since 1923 and was felt in southern Oregon and northern California. [CLS] [SEP]
