China Northwest Airlines Flight 2303 was a domestic flight from Xi'an to Guangzhou, People's Republic of China. [CLS] [SEP] On June 6, 1994, the aircraft operating the flight, a Tupolev Tu-154M, broke up in-flight and crashed as a result of an autopilot malfunction which caused violent shaking and overstressed the airframe. [CLS] [SEP] All 160 people on board were killed. [CLS] [SEP] As of 2020, it remains the deadliest airplane crash ever in mainland China. [CLS] [SEP]
