[CLS] Edward Leonard O'Neill (born April 12, 1946) is an American actor and comedian. [SEP] [CLS] His roles include Al Bundy on the Fox Network sitcom Married... [SEP] [CLS] with Children, for which he was nominated for two Golden Globes, and Jay Pritchett on the award-winning ABC sitcom Modern Family, a role for which he was nominated for three Primetime Emmy Awards and won four Screen Actors Guild Awards (all four for being part of the best Ensemble in a Comedy Series). [SEP] [CLS] He has also appeared in the Wayne's World film series, Little Giants, Prefontaine, The Bone Collector and Sun Dogs, and has done voice-work for the Wreck It Ralph franchise and Finding Dory.
