[CLS] Daniela Schreiber (born 26 June 1989) is a German swimmer. [SEP] [CLS] She competed at the 2012 Summer Olympics in the 100 m, 4 × 100 m and 4 × 200 m freestyle events and finished in 15th, 9th and 11th place, respectively.Between 2009 and 2012 she won three gold, two silver and five bronze medals at European and world championships, mostly in the 4×50 m and 4 × 100 m freestyle relays.
