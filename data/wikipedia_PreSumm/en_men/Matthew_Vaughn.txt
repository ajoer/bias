[CLS] Matthew Allard Robert Vaughn (born 7 March 1971) is an English film producer, director, and screenwriter. [SEP] [CLS] He has produced films including Lock, Stock and Two Smoking Barrels (1998) and Snatch (2000), and directed Layer Cake (2004), Stardust (2007), Kick-Ass (2010), X-Men: First Class (2011), Kingsman: The Secret Service (2014), and its sequel Kingsman: The Golden Circle (2017), and will write and direct its prequel The King's Man (2020).
