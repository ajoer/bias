[SEP] [CLS] Like Edward Sapir and Otto Jespersen, he collaborated with Alice Vanderbilt Morris to develop the research program of the International Auxiliary Language Association (IALA). [SEP] [CLS] From 1936 to 1939, he was Research Director of IALA. [SEP] [CLS] Under Collinson's guidance, methods of compiling international word material were tested at Liverpool. [SEP] [CLS] In 1939 IALA moved from Liverpool to New York and E. [SEP] [CLS] Clark Stillman succeeded Collinson as Research Director. [SEP] [CLS] Alexander Gode, editor of the first English-Interlingua dictionary published in 1951, remained in contact with Collinson which had collected much of linguistic material in the University of Liverpool.Collinson wrote a vulgarization book in Esperanto about linguistics : La Homa Lingvo ("The Human Language") in 1927. [SEP] [CLS] He also was a collaborator of Centro por Esploro kaj Dokumentado and Internacia Scienca Universitato where he gave 7 lectures. [SEP] [CLS] He was chairman of Internacia Scienca Asocio Esperantista 1959-1965 and a longtime member of the Akademio de Esperanto.He was the joint author of The German Language (with R. [SEP] [CLS] Priebsch) first published in 1934; fifth edition Faber & Faber, 1962. [SEP] [CLS]
