Françoise Sagan (born Françoise Quoirez; 21 June 1935 – 24 September 2004) was a French playwright, novelist, and screenwriter. [CLS] [SEP] Sagan was known for works with strong romantic themes involving wealthy and disillusioned bourgeois characters. [CLS] [SEP] Her best-known novel was her first – Bonjour Tristesse (1954) – which was written when she was a teenager. [CLS] [SEP]
