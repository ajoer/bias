[CLS] G-ASWI was a Westland Wessex 60, operated by Bristow Helicopters operating between Bacton Gas Terminal, in Norfolk, and Amoco gas platforms in the North Sea. [SEP] [CLS] On 13 August 1981 the helicopter lost power to the main rotor gearbox, going out of control during the ensuing autorotation. [SEP] [CLS] The flight was carrying 11 gas workers from the Leman gas field to Bacton. [SEP] [CLS] All people on board were lost. [SEP] [CLS]
