[CLS] Aeroflot Flight 1802 (Russian: Рейс 1802 Аэрофлота Reys 1802 Aeroflota) was a commercial flight from Vinnytsia to Moscow that crashed after the rudder deflected sharply and the propellers feathered on 15 May 1976. [SEP] [CLS] All 52 passengers and crew aboard the aircraft perished in the crash. [SEP] [CLS]
