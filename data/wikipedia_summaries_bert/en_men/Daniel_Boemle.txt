Daniel Christian Manuel Boemle, Dänu Boemle, "Sleepy Dan", "Öml" (7 November 1960 in Bern – 21 February 2007 in Les Ponts-de-Martel) was a well-known Swiss radio DJ. [CLS] [SEP] He presented various shows on DRS3 between 1988 and 1994. [CLS] [SEP] He was also a singer, author, culture critic, and painter. [CLS] [SEP]
