[CLS] Madonna Louise Ciccone (; Italian: [tʃikˈkoːne]; born August 16, 1958) is an American singer-songwriter, actress, author, and record executive. [SEP] [CLS] Having been referred to as the "Queen of Pop" since the 1980s, she is regarded as one of the most impactful figures in popular culture. [SEP] [CLS] Madonna is noted for her continual reinvention and versatility in music production, songwriting, and visual presentation. [SEP] [CLS] She has pushed the boundaries of artistic expression in mainstream music, while remaining completely in charge of every aspect of her career. [SEP] [CLS] Her works, which incorporate social, political, sexual, and religious themes, have generated both critical acclaim and controversy. [SEP] [CLS] Madonna is often cited as an influence by other artists. [SEP] [CLS] Born and raised in Michigan, Madonna moved to New York City in 1978 to pursue a career in modern dance. [SEP] [CLS] After performing as a drummer, guitarist, and vocalist in the rock bands Breakfast Club and Emmy, Madonna signed with Sire Records in 1982 and released her eponymous debut album the next year. [SEP] [CLS] She followed it with a series of successful albums, including all-time bestsellers Like a Virgin (1984) and True Blue (1986) as well as Grammy Award winners Ray of Light (1998) and Confessions on a Dance Floor (2005). [SEP] [CLS] Madonna has attained many number-one singles throughout her career, including "Like a Virgin", "La Isla Bonita", "Like a Prayer", "Vogue", "Take a Bow", "Frozen", "Music", "Hung Up", and "4 Minutes". [SEP] [CLS] Madonna's popularity was further enhanced by roles in films such as Desperately Seeking Susan (1985), Dick Tracy (1990), A League of Their Own (1992), and Evita (1996). [SEP] [CLS] While Evita earned her a Golden Globe Award for Best Actress, many of her other films received poor reviews. [SEP] [CLS] As a businesswoman, Madonna founded an entertainment company called Maverick (including the label Maverick Records) in 1992. [SEP] [CLS] Her other ventures include fashion brands, children's books, health clubs, and filmmaking. [SEP] [CLS] She contributes to various charities, having founded Ray of Light Foundation in 1998 and Raising Malawi in 2006. [SEP] [CLS] Having sold over 300 million records worldwide, Madonna is certified as the best-selling female music artist of all time by Guinness World Records. [SEP] [CLS] According to Billboard, Madonna is the most successful solo artist in its Hot 100 chart history. [SEP] [CLS] She holds the record for the most number-one singles by a female artist in Australia, Canada, Italy, Spain, and the United Kingdom. [SEP] [CLS] She remains the highest-grossing solo touring artist of all time, accumulating U.S. [SEP] [CLS] $1.4 billion from her concert tickets. [SEP] [CLS] Madonna was inducted into the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame in 2008, her first year of eligibility. [SEP] [CLS] VH1 ranked her atop the 100 Greatest Women in Music, while Rolling Stone listed her among the 100 Greatest Artists of All Time and the 100 Greatest Songwriters of All Time. [SEP] [CLS]
