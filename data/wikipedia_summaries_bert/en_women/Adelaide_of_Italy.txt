[CLS] Adelaide of Italy (German: Adelheid; 931 – 16 December 999 AD), also called Adelaide of Burgundy, was Holy Roman Empress by marriage to Emperor Otto the Great; she was crowned with him by Pope John XII in Rome on 2 February 962. [SEP] [CLS] She was regent of the Holy Roman Empire as the guardian of her grandson in 991–995. [SEP] [CLS]
