Cornelia Maria Funke (German: [kɔʁˈneːli̯a ˈfʊŋ.kə] (listen)) is a German author of children's fiction. [CLS] [SEP] She was born on 10 December 1958 in Dorsten, North Rhine-Westphalia. [CLS] [SEP] Funke is best known for her Inkheart trilogy (originally in German: Tintenwelt-Trilogie), published in the United Kingdom between 2004–2008. [CLS] [SEP] Many of her books have now been translated into English. [CLS] [SEP] Her work fits mainly into the fantasy and adventure genres. [CLS] [SEP] She lives in Beverly Hills, California. [CLS] [SEP] Funke has sold over 20 million copies of her books worldwide. [CLS] [SEP]
