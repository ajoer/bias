[CLS] Jutta Speidel (pronounced [ˈjʊ.ta ˈʃpaɪ̯.dl̩] (listen), born 26 March 1954, in Munich) is a German actress. [SEP] [CLS] She had her first television role at the age of 15. [SEP] [CLS] Jutta Speidel appeared in Schulmadchen Report 1 (1970), which was directed by Ernst Hofbauer and produced by Wolf C. [SEP] [CLS] Hartwig. [SEP] [CLS] She acted mainly in German television series and TV films like Rivalen der Rennbahn. [SEP] [CLS] She is remembered for playing several roles in the very popular TV series Derrick. [SEP] [CLS] She has two daughters. [SEP] [CLS] She founded the HORIZONT e.V. [SEP] [CLS] charity in 1997 for homeless children and their mothers. [SEP] [CLS] As of 03.11.2012, she is living in Rome, Italy. [SEP] [CLS]
