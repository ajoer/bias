Barbara Joan "Barbra" Streisand (; born April 24, 1942) is an American singer, actress, and filmmaker. [CLS] [SEP] In a career spanning six decades, she has achieved success in multiple fields of entertainment and has been recognized with two Academy Awards, ten Grammy Awards including the Grammy Lifetime Achievement Award and the Grammy Legend Award, five Emmy Awards, four Peabody Awards, the Presidential Medal of Freedom, and nine Golden Globes. [CLS] [SEP] She is among a small group of entertainers who have been honored with an Emmy, Grammy, Oscar, and Tony Award.After beginning a successful recording career in the 1960s, Streisand ventured into film by the end of that decade. [CLS] [SEP] She starred in the critically acclaimed Funny Girl, for which she won the Academy Award and Golden Globe Award for Best Actress. [CLS] [SEP] Her other films include The Prince of Tides, Hello, Dolly!, The Way We Were, Nuts and A Star Is Born, for which she received her second Academy Award, composing music for the love theme "Evergreen", the first woman to be honored as a composer. [CLS] [SEP] With the release of Yentl in 1983, Streisand became the first woman to write, produce, direct, and star in a major studio film. [CLS] [SEP] The film won an Oscar for Best Score and a Golden Globe for Best Motion Picture Musical. [CLS] [SEP] Streisand also received the Golden Globe Award for Best Director, becoming the first (and to date only) woman to win that award. [CLS] [SEP] Streisand is one of the best-selling recording artists of all time, with more than 68.5 million albums in the U.S. [CLS] [SEP] and with a total of 150 million records sold worldwide making her the best-selling female artist among top-selling artists recognized by the Recording Industry Association of America (RIAA). [CLS] [SEP] The RIAA and Billboard recognize Streisand as holding the record for the most top 10 albums of any female recording artist: a total of 34 since 1963. [CLS] [SEP] According to Billboard, she holds the record for the female with the most number one albums (11). [CLS] [SEP] Billboard also recognizes her as the greatest female of all time on its 200 chart and one of the greatest artists of all time on its Hot 100 chart. [CLS] [SEP] Streisand is the only recording artist to have a number-one album in each of the last six decades, having released 53 gold albums, 31 platinum albums, and 14 multi-platinum albums in the United States.
