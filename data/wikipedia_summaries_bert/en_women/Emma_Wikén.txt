Emma Christina Wikén (born 1 May 1989) is a Swedish cross-country skier who competes in the cross-country World Cup. [CLS] [SEP] During the Swedish championships in skiathlon in Falun 2013, Wikén won gold. [CLS] [SEP] At the 2014 Winter Olympics, Wikén won gold in the 4 × 5 km relay along with Ida Ingemarsdotter, Anna Haag and Charlotte Kalla. [CLS] [SEP]
