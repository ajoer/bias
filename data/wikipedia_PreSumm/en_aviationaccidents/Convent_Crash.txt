[CLS] The Convent Crash, also known as the Orléans air disaster and Villa St. [SEP] [CLS] Louis disaster, occurred on May 15, 1956, after a CF-100 fighter jet crashed into the Villa St. [SEP] [CLS] Louis in the community of Orléans, Ontario. [SEP] [CLS] 15 people were killed in the crash: 11 members of the Grey Nuns, two aviators, a civilian servant at the Villa and the chaplain, a retired naval padre. [SEP] [CLS]
