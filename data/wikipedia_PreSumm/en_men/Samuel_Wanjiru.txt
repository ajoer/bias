[SEP] [CLS] He set the current 10,000m World Junior Record in 2005 and set the half marathon world record 3 times.He moved to the full marathon and won the event at the 2008 Beijing Olympics in an Olympic record time of 2:06:32; becoming the first Kenyan to win the Olympic gold in the marathon. [SEP] [CLS] He became the youngest gold medallist in the marathon since 1932. [SEP] [CLS] The following year, he won both the London Marathon and Chicago Marathon, running the fastest marathons ever recorded in the United Kingdom and United States, respectively. [SEP] [CLS] He retained his Chicago title in 2010 in a season fraught with injury. [SEP] [CLS] In 2011, he died after a fall from a balcony at his home in Nyahururu following a domestic dispute.
