[CLS] The 2000 Turkmenistan earthquake took place at 8:11 p.m. [SEP] [CLS] Moscow Time on December 6 and had a magnitude of 7.0. [SEP] [CLS] The intensity of the earthquake reached VIII at its epicenter, and VI at the Turkmen capital of Ashgabat. [SEP] [CLS] The epicenter was located approximately 25 kilometers north of the city of Balkanabat and 125 kilometers southeast of Türkmenbaşy. [SEP] [CLS] There were unconfirmed reports that the quake killed up to 11 people and injured 5 others. [SEP] [CLS]
