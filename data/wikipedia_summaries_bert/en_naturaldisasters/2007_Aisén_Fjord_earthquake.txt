The 2007 Aysén Fjord earthquakes occurred in Aisén Fjord, Chile from January 22 – April 22. [CLS] [SEP] The biggest occurred at 1:53 p.m. [CLS] [SEP] (local time) on April 21 and reached a felt intensity of VII (Very strong) on the Mercalli intensity scale. [CLS] [SEP] On the moment magnitude scale, the earthquake reached a gradation of 6.2. [CLS] [SEP] Ten people disappeared, according to ONEMI (Chile's National emergency office), but three bodies were found on April 22 by the Chilean Navy. [CLS] [SEP]
