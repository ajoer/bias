[CLS] Peter Abelard (; Latin: Petrus Abaelardus or Abailardus; French: Pierre Abélard, pronounced [a.be.laːʁ]; c. [SEP] [CLS] 1079 – 21 April 1142) was a medieval French scholastic philosopher, theologian, and preeminent logician. [SEP] [CLS] His love for, and affair with, Héloïse d'Argenteuil has become legendary. [SEP] [CLS] The Chambers Biographical Dictionary describes him as "the keenest thinker and boldest theologian of the 12th century". [SEP] [CLS]
