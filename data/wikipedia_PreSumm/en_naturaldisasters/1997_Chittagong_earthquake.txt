[CLS] The 1997 Chittagong earthquake (also known as the Bandarban earthquake) occurred on 21 November at 11:23 UTC in the Bangladesh-India-Myanmar border region. [SEP] [CLS] It had a magnitude of Mw 6.1. [SEP] [CLS] The epicenter was located in southern Mizoram, India. [SEP] [CLS] No fatalities were reported there, but 23 people were killed in Chittagong when a five-story building collapsed in Bangladesh. [SEP] [CLS] The shaking could also be felt in Dhaka.
