[CLS] Leonid Mikhaylovich Razvozzhayev (Russian: Леони́д Миха́йлович Развозжа́ев; born 12 June 1973 in Angarsk) is a member of the political coalition Left Front and an aide to Ilya Ponomarev, a member of the Russian Parliament. [SEP] [CLS] Razvozzhayev was kidnapped from Kiev, Ukraine in October 2012, allegedly by Russian security forces. [SEP] [CLS]
