[CLS] UPS Airlines Flight 6 was a cargo flight operated by UPS Airlines. [SEP] [CLS] On September 3, 2010, the Boeing 747-400F flying the route between Dubai, United Arab Emirates, and Cologne, Germany, developed an in-flight fire which caused the aircraft to crash, killing both crew members, the only people on board. [SEP] [CLS] It was the first fatal air crash for UPS Airlines. [SEP] [CLS] The crash prompted a re-evaluation of safety procedures protecting airliners from cockpit smoke. [SEP] [CLS]
