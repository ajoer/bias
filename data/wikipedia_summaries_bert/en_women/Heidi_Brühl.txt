Heidi Rosemarie Brühl (German: [ˈhaɪ̯.di bʁyːl] (listen); 30 January 1942 in Gräfelfing, Upper Bavaria – 8 June 1991 in Starnberg) was a German singer and actress who came to prominence as a young teenager and had a prolific career in film and television. [CLS] [SEP] She was also a successful recording artist, and is known for her participation in the 1963 Eurovision Song Contest. [CLS] [SEP]
