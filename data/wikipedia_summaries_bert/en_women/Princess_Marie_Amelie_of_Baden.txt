[CLS] Princess Marie Amelie of Baden (Marie Amelie Elisabeth Caroline; 11 October 1817 – 17 October 1888) was the youngest daughter of Charles, Grand Duke of Baden and Stéphanie de Beauharnais,. [SEP] [CLS] In 1843, she married the Scottish nobleman William Hamilton, Marquess of Douglas and Clydesdale. [SEP] [CLS] They became the Duke and Duchess of Hamilton upon the death of William's father in 1852. [SEP] [CLS] Their only daughter Mary married the future Albert I, Prince of Monaco and was the mother of Louis II. [SEP] [CLS] Princess Marie was a cousin of Napoleon III of France, as well as a friend of his and his wife's, Empress Eugénie. [SEP] [CLS] She often accompanied the couple at official events, and provided them lodging when they visited her outside France. [SEP] [CLS]
