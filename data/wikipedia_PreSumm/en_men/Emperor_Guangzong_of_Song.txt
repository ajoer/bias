[CLS] Emperor Guangzong of Song (30 September 1147 – 17 September 1200), personal name Zhao Dun, was the 12th emperor of the Song dynasty in China and the third emperor of the Southern Song dynasty. [SEP] [CLS] He was the third son of his predecessor, Emperor Xiaozong. [SEP] [CLS] His mother was Emperor Xiaozong's first wife, Lady Guo (郭氏; 1126–1156), who was posthumously honoured as "Empress Chengmu" (成穆皇后). [SEP] [CLS] His reign was relatively peaceful, but his lack of filial piety eventually made officials replace him with his son Emperor Ningzong. [SEP] [CLS]
