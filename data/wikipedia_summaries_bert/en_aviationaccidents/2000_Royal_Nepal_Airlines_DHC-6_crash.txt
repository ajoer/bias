[CLS] Nepal Airlines Corporation (Nepali: नेपाल वायुसेवा निगम), formerly known as Royal Nepal Airlines (Nepali: शाही नेपाल वायुसेवा), is the flag carrier of Nepal. [SEP] [CLS] Founded in 1958, it is the oldest airline of the country. [SEP] [CLS] Out of its main base at Tribhuvan International Airport, Kathmandu, the airline operates domestic services within Nepal and medium-haul services in Asia. [SEP] [CLS] The airline's first aircraft was a Douglas DC-3, used to serve domestic routes and a handful of destinations in India. [SEP] [CLS] The airline acquired its first jet aircraft, Boeing 727s, in 1972. [SEP] [CLS] As of 13 July 2020, the airline operates a fleet of seven aircraft. [SEP] [CLS] Since 2013, the airline has been on the list of air carriers banned in the European Union. [SEP] [CLS]
