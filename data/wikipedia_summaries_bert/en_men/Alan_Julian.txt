Alan John Julian (born 11 March 1983) is a semi-professional footballer who plays for National League South club Billericay Town as a goalkeeper. [CLS] [SEP] Julian started his career at Brentford, progressing through the youth side and making his first-team debut in 2002. [CLS] [SEP] After failing to cement a place as Brentford's number one goalkeeper, he left to join Stevenage Borough in February 2005. [CLS] [SEP] Julian was a regular fixture in the side, helping the club win the FA Trophy in May 2007. [CLS] [SEP] He left Stevenage to join Gillingham on a free transfer in May 2008. [CLS] [SEP] Julian was released by Gillingham in May 2011, having played 80 games for the club during his three-year tenure. [CLS] [SEP] He spent a season at his former club, Stevenage, before being released in May 2012, and subsequently joined Newport County a month later. [CLS] [SEP] He was released by the club in May 2013, and subsequently joined Dartford on a free transfer. [CLS] [SEP]
