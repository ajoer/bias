[CLS] The 1821 New England tornado outbreak was a tornado outbreak that affected the New England states during September 1821. [SEP] [CLS] Five destructive tornadoes can be verified from the event. [SEP] [CLS] The first known storm of the outbreak touched down in Washington County, Vermont, and caused tree damage near the town of Berlin. [SEP] [CLS] Shortly thereafter, tornadoes touched down in Grafton County, New Hampshire, and a series of large tornadoes tracked through parts of Rutland County, Vermont, and Sullivan and Merrimack counties in New Hampshire. [SEP] [CLS] Other tornadoes struck in Windham County, Vermont, and Franklin County, Massachusetts. [SEP] [CLS]
