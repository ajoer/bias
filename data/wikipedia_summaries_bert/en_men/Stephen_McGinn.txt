Stephen McGinn (born 2 December 1988) is a Scottish footballer who most recently played as a midfielder for Scottish Premiership club St Mirren. [CLS] [SEP] Born in Glasgow, he began started his career with St Mirren in his native Scotland, before moving south of the border to sign for English Championship side Watford. [CLS] [SEP] He spent three and a half years at Watford but spent over a year of that on the sidelines following a knee injury, before being loaned to Shrewsbury Town on his return to fitness. [CLS] [SEP] He moved to Sheffield United in June 2013, leaving the club in February 2015, when he signed for Dundee. [CLS] [SEP] He has also represented Scotland at under-19 and under-21 level.
