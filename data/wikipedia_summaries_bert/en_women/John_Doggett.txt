FBI Special Agent John Jay Doggett is a fictional character in the Fox science fiction-supernatural television series The X-Files. [CLS] [SEP] With his FBI partners Dana Scully (season 8) and Monica Reyes (season 9), they work on the X-Files together, which is concerned with cases with particularly mysterious or possibly supernatural circumstances that were left unsolved and shelved by the FBI.
