[CLS] On 11 November 2010, an Antonov An-24 passenger aircraft of Tarco Airlines on a domestic service from Khartoum to Zalingei, Sudan, crashed on landing at Zalingei Airport, bursting into flames on the runway. [SEP] [CLS] Two passengers died, although reports on the number of fatalities varied from one to six. [SEP] [CLS]
