Birgit Rockmeier (born 29 November 1973 in Moosburg) is a former German sprinter who specialised in the 200 metres. [CLS] [SEP] Rockmeier's personal best time is 22.90 seconds, achieved in July 2001 in Stuttgart. [CLS] [SEP] She represented the sports club LG Olympia Dortmund and is the twin sister of Gabi Rockmeier. [CLS] [SEP]
