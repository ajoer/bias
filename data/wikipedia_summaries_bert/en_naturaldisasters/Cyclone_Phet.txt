[CLS] Very Severe Cyclonic Storm Phet was a powerful tropical cyclone that made landfall on Oman, Western India, and Pakistan. [SEP] [CLS] The third named cyclone of the 2010 cyclone season, Phet developed in the Arabian Sea on May 31 to the west of India. [SEP] [CLS] With conducive environmental conditions, the storm intensified to reach peak sustained winds of 155 km/h (100 mph) on June 2, based on analysis by the India Meteorological Department (IMD). [SEP] [CLS] On the next day, Phet dropped heavy rainfall while moving across eastern Oman, with a peak of 603 mm (23.7 in) in Qurayyat.
