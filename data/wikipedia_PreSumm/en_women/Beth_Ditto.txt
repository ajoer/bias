[CLS] Mary Beth Patterson (born February 19, 1981), known by her stage name Beth Ditto, is an American singer-songwriter and actress most notable for her work with the indie rock band Gossip. [SEP] [CLS] Her voice has been compared to Etta James, Janis Joplin and Tina Turner. [SEP] [CLS] She disbanded Gossip to pursue a career in fashion, and has since started a solo career. [SEP] [CLS]
