Nigar Aydin qizi Jamal (née Mutallibzadeh Azerbaijani: Nigar Aydın qızı Camal (Mütəllibzadə); born 7 September 1980), sometimes known as Nikki Jamal, is an English-Azerbaijani singer. [CLS] [SEP] Born and raised in Baku, she has resided in London since 2005. [CLS] [SEP] She won the Eurovision Song Contest 2011 with Eldar Gasimov with the song "Running Scared". [CLS] [SEP] She and Gasimov have since performed as a duo on various occasions under the name Ell & Nikki. [CLS] [SEP]
