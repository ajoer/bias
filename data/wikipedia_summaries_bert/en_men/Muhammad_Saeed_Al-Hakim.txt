[CLS] Grand Ayatollah Sayyid Muhammad Saeed al-Tabataba'i al-Hakim (Arabic: آية الله العظمى السيد محمد سعيد الطباطبائي الحكيم‎; born 1 February 1934) is a senior Iraqi Twelver Shi'a marja, one of the Big Four Grand Ayatollahs of the Hawza of Najaf and one of the most senior Shia clerics in Iraq after Ayatollah Ali al-Sistani.
