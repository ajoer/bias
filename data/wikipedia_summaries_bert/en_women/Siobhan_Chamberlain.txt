[CLS] Siobhan Rebecca Chamberlain (born 15 August 1983) is an English professional footballer who has most recently played as a goalkeeper for FA WSL club Manchester United and the England national team. [SEP] [CLS] She joined Manchester United in 2018 after playing for Liverpool. [SEP] [CLS] Before joining Liverpool in December 2015, she had been at Notts County on loan from Arsenal. [SEP] [CLS] Chamberlain previously played at the top club level for Chelsea, Fulham, Birmingham City and Bristol Academy, as well as for North American W-League club Vancouver Whitecaps. [SEP] [CLS] She has represented the England women's national football team on 50 occasions since her debut in 2004. [SEP] [CLS] This included being part of the squad at three FIFA Women's World Cups and three UEFA Women's Championships. [SEP] [CLS]
