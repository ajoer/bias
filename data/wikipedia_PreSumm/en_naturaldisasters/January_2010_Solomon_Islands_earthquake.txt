[CLS] The 2010 Solomon Islands earthquake occurred with a moment magnitude of 7.1 on January 3 at 22:36:28 (UTC). [SEP] [CLS] A tsunami measuring approximately 10 feet affected some parts of the islands. [SEP] [CLS] The earthquake was the largest in a series of quakes to strike the Solomon Islands over the preceding days. [SEP] [CLS] The 7.1 mainshock was preceded by a 6.6 magnitude foreshock some 48 minutes previous. [SEP] [CLS] As many as 1,000 people were left homeless on the island of Rendova after the earthquake and tsunami destroyed approximately 200 homes. [SEP] [CLS]
