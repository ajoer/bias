[CLS] Irmgard Hermann (4 October 1942 – 26 May 2020) was a German actress. [SEP] [CLS] She worked in film, television, and the stage, appearing in over 160 film and television productions. [SEP] [CLS] She was discovered, without formal training, by Rainer Werner Fassbinder who cast her in many of his films. [SEP] [CLS] She was awarded the Deutscher Filmpreis for playing Irmgard Epp in Fassbinder's The Merchant of Four Seasons, and again for appearing as Else Gebel, a woman in prison with Sophie Scholl, in Percy Adlon's Fünf letzte Tage.
