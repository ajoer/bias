Adel Ferdosipour (Persian: عادل فردوسی‌پور; born 3 October 1974), is an Iranian journalist, translator, university professor, football commentator and television show host and producer. [CLS] [SEP] He was the host and producer of the popular TV show Navad. [CLS] [SEP] He is producer of the popular TV show Football 120. [CLS] [SEP]
