Kuusamo is a town and municipality in Finland. [CLS] [SEP] It is located in the Northern Ostrobothnia region. [CLS] [SEP] The municipality has a population of 15,193 (31 January 2019) and covers an area of 5,808.92 square kilometres (2,242.84 sq mi) of which 830.81 km2 (320.78 sq mi) is water. [CLS] [SEP] The population density is 3.05 inhabitants per square kilometre (7.9/sq mi). [CLS] [SEP] The municipality is unilingually Finnish. [CLS] [SEP] Kuusamo is a major center for winter sports and receives approximately a million tourists every year. [CLS] [SEP] One of the largest ski resorts in Finland, Ruka, is situated in Kuusamo. [CLS] [SEP] Ruka is also the host of many international competitions in ski jumping, cross country skiing and Nordic combined. [CLS] [SEP] The 2005 World Championships of Freestyle Skiing was held in Kuusamo. [CLS] [SEP] Kuusamo Airport is located 6 kilometres (4 mi) north-east from Kuusamo town centre. [CLS] [SEP]
