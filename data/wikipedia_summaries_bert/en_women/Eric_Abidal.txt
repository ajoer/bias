Eric Sylvain Abidal (French pronunciation: ​[eʁik abidal]; born 11 September 1979) is a French former professional footballer who played as a left back or centre back, and the director of football at FC Barcelona. [CLS] [SEP] In his career, he played mainly for Lyon and Barcelona, winning 18 titles with both teams combined, including two Champions League trophies with the latter. [CLS] [SEP] His later career was marred by a liver tumour, which resulted in a transplant. [CLS] [SEP] A French international across nine years, Abidal represented the nation in two World Cups – finishing second in 2006 – and Euro 2008. [CLS] [SEP]
