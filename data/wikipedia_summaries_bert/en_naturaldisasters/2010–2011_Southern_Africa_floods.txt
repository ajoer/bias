The 2010–11 Southern Africa floods were a series of floods across three countries in Southern Africa. [CLS] [SEP] Linked to a La Niña event, above-average rains starting in December led to widespread flooding. [CLS] [SEP] Thousands of people were displaced and evacuations of more continued. [CLS] [SEP] As of 24 January 2011, at least 141 people are known to have been killed, including 88 in KwaZulu Natal. [CLS] [SEP] The South African government declared 33 disaster zones. [CLS] [SEP]
