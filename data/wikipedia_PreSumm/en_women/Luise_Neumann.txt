[CLS] Luise Neumann (7 December 1818 – 17 October 1905) was a German actress, the daughter of the actress Amalie Haizinger. [SEP] [CLS] Her younger sister, Adolfine Neumann, was also an esteemed actress of the period. [SEP] [CLS] She made her debut at the age of 16 in a performance of the Deutschen Hausfrau in Karlsruhe. [SEP] [CLS] In 1839, she joined the Burgtheater in Vienna, of which she was a member until 1856. [SEP] [CLS] On 14 January 1857, she married Karl Graf von Schönfeld and ended her career as an actor.
