Monika Hohlmeier (née Strauß, born 2 July 1962) is a German politician who has been serving as a Member of the European Parliament (MEP) since 2009. [CLS] [SEP] She is a member of the Christian Social Union, part of the European People's Party. [CLS] [SEP] Between 1998 and 2005 she served as Bavarian State Minister for Education and Cultural Affairs. [CLS] [SEP]
