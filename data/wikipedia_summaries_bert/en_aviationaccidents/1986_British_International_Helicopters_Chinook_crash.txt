[CLS] On 6 November 1986, a Boeing-Vertol Model 234LR Chinook helicopter returning workers from the Brent oilfield crashed on approach to land at Sumburgh Airport in the Shetland Islands. [SEP] [CLS] At 2.5 mi (4.0 km) from the runway the helicopter had a catastrophic forward transmission failure which caused the tandem rotor blades to collide. [SEP] [CLS] The helicopter crashed into the sea and sank. [SEP] [CLS] Forty-three passengers and two crew members were killed in the crash; one passenger and one crew member survived with wounds.
