[CLS] Brimin Kipruto (born 31 July 1985) is a Kenyan middle distance runner who specialises in the 3000 metres steeplechase. [SEP] [CLS] He was born in Korkitony, Keiyo District. [SEP] [CLS] He holds a personal best of 7:53.64 minutes which is the second fastest time ever run, and the African Record. [SEP] [CLS] His personal best is only 0.01 of a second behind the world record of former county mate Saif Saaeed Shaheen, who switched national affiliation to Qatar after growing up as Stephen Cherono in the Keiyo District. [SEP] [CLS] He won a silver medal at the 2004 Summer Olympics at the age of nineteen and improved to win the gold medal at the 2008 Beijing Olympics four years later. [SEP] [CLS] A World Championships bronze medallist in 2005, he took his first global title in the steeplechase with a win at the 2007 World Championships in Athletics. [SEP] [CLS]
