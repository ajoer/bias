[CLS] Alitalia Flight 112 was a scheduled flight from Leonardo da Vinci Airport, in Rome, Italy, to Palermo International Airport in Palermo, Italy, with 115 on board. [SEP] [CLS] On 5 May 1972, it crashed into Mount Longa, about 3 miles (4.8 km) southwest of Palermo while on approach to the airport. [SEP] [CLS] Investigators believe that the crew had 3 miles visibility and did not adhere to the established vectors issued by air traffic control. [SEP] [CLS] It remains the deadliest single-aircraft disaster in Italy, and the second-deadliest behind the Linate Airport disaster in 2001. [SEP] [CLS] The incident is the worst in Alitalia's history. [SEP] [CLS] A memorial has been erected at the site of the crash. [SEP] [CLS]
