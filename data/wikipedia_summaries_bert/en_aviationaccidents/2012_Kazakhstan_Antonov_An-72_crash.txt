The Antonov An-72 (NATO reporting name: Coaler) is a Soviet/Ukrainian transport aircraft, developed by Antonov. [CLS] [SEP] It was designed as an STOL transport and intended as a replacement for the Antonov An-26, but variants have found success as commercial freighters. [CLS] [SEP] The An-72 and the related An-74 get their nickname, Cheburashka, from the large engine intake ducts, which resemble the oversized ears of the popular Soviet animated character of the same name.
