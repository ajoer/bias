[CLS] Vijaya Rajadhyaksha (Devanagari: विजया राजाध्यक्ष) (born 1933) is a Marathi writer from Maharashtra, India. [SEP] [CLS] She studied at Rajaram College in Kolhapur, and obtained her doctorate from Mumbai University. [SEP] [CLS] Rajadhyaksha taught Marathi literature at Elphinstone College affiliated with Mumbai University, and then served as the head of Marathi department in SNDT Women's University, also in Mumbai. [SEP] [CLS] Her book मर्ढेकरांची कविता : स्वरूप आणि संदर्भ received a Sahitya Akademi Award in 1993. [SEP] [CLS] She presided over Marathi Sahitya Sammelan at Indore in 2001. [SEP] [CLS] Recently, she was awarded first Vinda Karandikar Jeevan Gaurav Puraskar (2010) which carries a prize money of Rs 1,00,000/- and an honorary certificate.Her husband M.V. [SEP] [CLS] Rajadhyaksha (1913-2010) was also a noted Marathi writer. [SEP] [CLS]
