[CLS] The March 2008 Midwest floods were a massive flooding event in the Southern Midwest and portions of the Southern Plains. [SEP] [CLS] Cape Girardeau, Missouri officially reported 11.48 inches (29.2 cm) between March 18 and 19. [SEP] [CLS] At least 17 people died as a result of the flooding. [SEP] [CLS] Levee breaks were observed in several areas, most notably in Southeastern Missouri, where levee breaks occurred through mid-April. [SEP] [CLS]
