[CLS] Emil Rilke (born 19 November 1983) is a Czech footballer who currently plays as a midfielder for Ústí nad Labem, after leaving Romanian Universitatea Cluj. [SEP] [CLS] Previously Rilke has played for Czech clubs Teplice, Jablonec 97, SIAD Most and Slovan Liberec and Hansa Rostock. [SEP] [CLS] Rilke was a member of the Czech Republic Under-20 national team which competed in the 2003 U20 FIFA World Cup in the United Arab Emirates. [SEP] [CLS] He won the Corgoň liga in the 2009–2010 season with Žilina. [SEP] [CLS] On 21 November 2012 he signed for German side Hansa Rostock in the 3. [SEP] [CLS] Liga. [SEP] [CLS]
