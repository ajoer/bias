Los Angeles Airways Flight 417 was a Sikorsky S-61 helicopter that crashed on August 14, 1968 in the city of Compton, California. [CLS] [SEP] All eighteen passengers and three crew members were killed. [CLS] [SEP] The aircraft was destroyed by impact and fire. [CLS] [SEP] According to the National Transportation Safety Board the probable cause of the accident was fatigue failure. [CLS] [SEP] The accident happened when the (arbitrarily designated) yellow blade, one of five main rotor blades, separated at the spindle which attached the blade to the rotor head. [CLS] [SEP] Following failure, the helicopter was uncontrollable and it fell to the ground. [CLS] [SEP] The fatigue crack originated in an area of substandard hardness and inadequate shot peening.
