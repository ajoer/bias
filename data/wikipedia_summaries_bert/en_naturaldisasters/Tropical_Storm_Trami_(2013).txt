Severe Tropical Storm Trami, known in the Philippines as Tropical Storm Maring, was a tropical cyclone that brought heavy rains to Taiwan and East China during mid-August 2013. [CLS] [SEP] Trami also made a fujiwhara interaction with Tropical Depression 13W north of it. [CLS] [SEP] The storm also enhanced the southwest monsoon causing more than 20 casualties in the Philippines. [CLS] [SEP]
