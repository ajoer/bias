The 2007 Peru earthquake, which measured 8.0 on the moment magnitude scale, hit the central coast of Peru on August 15 at 23:40:57 UTC (18:40:57 local time) and lasted two minutes. [CLS] [SEP] The epicenter was located 150 km (93 mi) south-southeast of Lima at a depth of 39 km (24 mi). [CLS] [SEP] The United States Geological Survey National Earthquake Information Center reported that it was a Very strong earthquake. [CLS] [SEP] The Peruvian government stated that 519 people were killed by the quake. [CLS] [SEP]
