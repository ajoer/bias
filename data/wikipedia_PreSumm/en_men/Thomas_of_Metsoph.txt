[CLS] Thomas of Metsoph (Armenian: Թովմա Մեծոփեցի, Thovma Metsobetsi) (1378–1446) was an Armenian cleric and chronicler who left an account of Timur’s invasions of the Caucasus (1386–1403). [SEP] [CLS] What we know of Thomas's life comes from a biography written by his student Kirakos Banaser and from a number of 15th-century colophons. [SEP] [CLS]
