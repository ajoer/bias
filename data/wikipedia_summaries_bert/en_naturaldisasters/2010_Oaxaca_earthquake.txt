The 2010 Oaxaca earthquake struck Oaxaca, Mexico on June 30, 2010, with an Mw magnitude of 6.3. [CLS] [SEP] Many people in different cities left their beds and ran into the street, as the quake struck at 2:22 am. [CLS] [SEP] Helicopters and police vehicles were sent to inspect possible damage in Mexico City, primarily in downtown and central areas, where some buildings were evacuated. [CLS] [SEP] In Mexico City, some cases of power outage in Azcapotzalco, Iztapalapa, and Benito Juárez and cracks in buildings were reported. [CLS] [SEP] 1 person died in San Andrés Huaxpaltepec, Oaxaca. [CLS] [SEP]
