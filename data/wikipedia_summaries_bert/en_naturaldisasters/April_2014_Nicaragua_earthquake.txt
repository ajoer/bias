The April 2014 Nicaragua earthquake occurred on April 10 at 17:27 local time. [CLS] [SEP] The earthquake hit about 50 kilometres (31 mi) north of the town of Managua. [CLS] [SEP] The shock measured 6.1 on the moment magnitude scale and had a maximum Mercalli intensity of VI (Strong).The earthquake resulted in the death of one person and 266 injured. [CLS] [SEP] Over 1500 houses were damaged, and tremors continued for many hours afterwards. [CLS] [SEP] The town of Nagarote and nearby villages in the Leon Region were the most affected by the earthquake. [CLS] [SEP] In less than a day, another earthquake hit Nicaragua, this time near the city of Granada, at 3:29 local time. [CLS] [SEP] The second quake registered at a 6.6 magnitude, however, it did not result in any casualties. [CLS] [SEP]
