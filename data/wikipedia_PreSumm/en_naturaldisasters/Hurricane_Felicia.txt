[CLS] The name Felicia has been used for four tropical cyclones in the Eastern Pacific Ocean. [SEP] [CLS] Felicia replaced Fefa. [SEP] [CLS] Hurricane Felicia (1997) – formed in the open ocean, causing no known damage or deaths. [SEP] [CLS] Tropical Storm Felicia (2003) – remained at sea, crossing into the Central Pacific, but dissipating well east of Hawaii. [SEP] [CLS] Hurricane Felicia (2009) – remained at sea, dissipating before hitting Hawaii. [SEP] [CLS] Tropical Storm Felicia (2015) – remained at sea as a weak tropical storm. [SEP] [CLS]
