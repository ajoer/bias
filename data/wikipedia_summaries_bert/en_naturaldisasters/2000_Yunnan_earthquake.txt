[CLS] The 2000 Yunnan earthquake occurred on January 14 at 23:37 UTC, in Yunnan, China. [SEP] [CLS] The earthquake killed 7 people, and caused much damage in central Yunnan Province. [SEP] [CLS] The quake was moderate, with a magnitude of 5.9; however, it left 2,528 injured, 92,479 homeless and destroyed over 41,000 homes.It was preceded by a magnitude 5.5 foreshock at 22:09 on the same day.
