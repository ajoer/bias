Éléonore Marie Desmier d'Olbreuse (3 or 7 January 1639 – 5 February 1722), was a French noblewoman, who became firstly in mistress and later wife of George William of Brunswick, Duke of Lauenburg and Prince of Celle. [CLS] [SEP] She was the mother of Sophia Dorothea of Celle, who was the wife of George I of Great Britain. [CLS] [SEP] Thus she is the maternal grandmother of George II. [CLS] [SEP]
