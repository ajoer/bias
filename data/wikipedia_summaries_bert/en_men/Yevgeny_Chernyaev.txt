[CLS] Hero of the Russian Federation (Russian: Герой Российской Федерации), also unofficially Hero of Russia (Russian: Герой России), is the highest honorary title of the Russian Federation. [SEP] [CLS] The title comes with a Gold Star medal, an insignia of honour that identifies recipients. [SEP] [CLS] The title is awarded to persons for "service to the Russian state and nation, usually connected with a heroic feat of valour". [SEP] [CLS] The title is bestowed by decree of the president of the Russian Federation. [SEP] [CLS] Russian citizenship or being in the service of the Russian state is not obligatory. [SEP] [CLS] The title was established in 1992 and, as of 2015, was awarded more than 970 times, of which more than 440 were posthumously. [SEP] [CLS]
