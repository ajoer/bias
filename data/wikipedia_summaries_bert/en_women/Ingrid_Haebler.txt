Ingrid Haebler (born 20 June 1929 in Vienna, Austria) is an Austrian pianist. [CLS] [SEP] Haebler also recorded all of Mozart's piano concertos (most of them twice), often with her own cadenzas - and all of Schubert's sonatas. [CLS] [SEP] She was one of several Austrian musicians to experiment early with period instruments, having recorded the music of Johann Christian Bach on a fortepiano. [CLS] [SEP] Her recordings of Mozart and Beethoven with the violinist Henryk Szeryng are particularly prized. [CLS] [SEP]
