Pauline Betz Addie (née Pauline May Betz, August 6, 1919 – May 31, 2011) was an American professional tennis player. [CLS] [SEP] She won five Grand Slam singles titles and was the runner-up on three other occasions. [CLS] [SEP] Jack Kramer has called her the second best female tennis player he ever saw, behind Helen Wills Moody. [CLS] [SEP]
