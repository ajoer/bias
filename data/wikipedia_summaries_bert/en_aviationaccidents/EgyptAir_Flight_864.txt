EgyptAir Flight 864 was a flight from Rome Fiumicino Airport to Tokyo International Airport, via Cairo, Bombay, and Bangkok. [CLS] [SEP] On 25 December 1976, the Boeing 707 on the route crashed into an industrial complex in Bangkok. [CLS] [SEP] All 52 people on board were killed, plus 19 on the ground in the crash.
