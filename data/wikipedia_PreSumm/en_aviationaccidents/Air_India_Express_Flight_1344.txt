[CLS] Air India Express is an Indian low-cost airline headquartered in Kochi, Kerala. [SEP] [CLS] It is operated by Air India Express Limited (AIEL), a wholly owned subsidiary of Indian flag carrier airline Air India. [SEP] [CLS] It operates around 649 flights per week to 33 destinations including the Middle East and Southeast Asia. [SEP] [CLS] The airline carries around 4.3 million passengers every year connecting 140 city pairs. [SEP] [CLS] It has secondary hubs in Trivandrum, Kozhikode and Mangaluru airports
