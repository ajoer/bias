[CLS] Prince Lev Vladimirovich Urusov (Russian: Лев Владимирович Урусов, French: Leon Ouroussoff, (1877-12-31)December 31, 1877, Saint Petersburg — 1933 (aged 55–56), Paris) was a Russian diplomat. [SEP] [CLS] From 1910 until his death, he was a member of International Olympic Committee. [SEP] [CLS]
