Latte and the Magic Waterstone (German: Latte Igel und der magische Wasserstein) is a 2019 animation film based on the book of the same name by Sebastian Lybeck. [CLS] [SEP] Directed by Mimi Maynard, Regina Welker and Nina Wels, it stars Ashley Bornancin, Danny Fehsenfeld and Leslie Miller. [CLS] [SEP] The film premiered at the 2019 Schlingel International Film Festival, and released in German movie theaters on December 25, 2019.On July 31, 2020, the film made its exclusive streaming debut on Netflix.
