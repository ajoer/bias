Gabon Express Flight 221 was a scheduled domestic passenger flight which crashed into the Atlantic Ocean on 8 June 2004. [CLS] [SEP] The Hawker Siddeley HS 748 was carrying 26 passengers and 4 crew and was flying from Gabon's capital Libreville to Franceville via Port-Gentil when an engine failed. [CLS] [SEP] The crew tried to return to Libreville International Airport; however they overflew it and nosedived into the sea. [CLS] [SEP] At least 19 people were killed in the crash. [CLS] [SEP] It was the second deadliest plane crash in Gabon. [CLS] [SEP] Gabon President Omar Bongo declared three days of national mourning in response to the disaster. [CLS] [SEP]
