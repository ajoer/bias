[CLS] Heidi Bertha Auguste Kabel (German: [ˈhaɪ̯.di ˈkaː.bl̩] (listen); 27 August 1914 – 15 June 2010) was a German actress and musician. [SEP] [CLS] Most of her stage roles were performed at the Ohnsorg-Theater in Hamburg, many of them in Low German. [SEP] [CLS] She became famous in Germany as many of the productions of the Ohnsorg Theater were transmitted on German television. [SEP] [CLS] Heidi Kabel was born in Hamburg; the city and its landmarks inspired many of her best-known songs (In Hamburg sagt man Tschüss, Jungfernstiegmarsch). [SEP] [CLS] She was married to stage director and actor Hans Mahler, who died in 1970. [SEP] [CLS] Their daughter, Heidi Mahler, is also an actress. [SEP] [CLS] She died in Hamburg on 15 June 2010 at the age of 95 and was honoured with a funeral service at St. [SEP] [CLS] Michael's Church ten days later. [SEP] [CLS]
