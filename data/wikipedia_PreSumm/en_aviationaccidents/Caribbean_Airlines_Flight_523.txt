[CLS] Caribbean Airlines Flight 523 was a passenger flight which overran the runway at Cheddi Jagan International Airport, Georgetown, Guyana, on 30 July 2011. [SEP] [CLS] Seven of the 163 aboard suffered injuries. [SEP] [CLS] The aircraft involved, a Boeing 737-800, was operating Caribbean Airlines' scheduled international service from John F. [SEP] [CLS] Kennedy Airport, New York, to Georgetown. [SEP] [CLS] The aircraft failed to stop in rainy weather, overrunning the runway at 01:32 local time (05:32 UTC), crashing through the perimeter fence. [SEP] [CLS] The aircraft stopped 100 metres (330 ft) past the end of runway 06 after it went over a road and broke into two sections. [SEP] [CLS]
