On 16 August 2017, severe flooding caused a mountainside to collapse, triggering a landslide which affected a fishing village in the Democratic Republic of Congo's (DR Congo) Ituri province. [CLS] [SEP] The disaster killed at least 200 people. [CLS] [SEP] Much of the damage is attributed to poorly regulated housing in vulnerable mountainous regions and heavy deforestation. [CLS] [SEP]
