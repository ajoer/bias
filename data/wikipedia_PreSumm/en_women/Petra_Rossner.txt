[CLS] Petra Rossner (born 14 November 1966) is a German cyclist, who won the gold medal in 3 km pursuit track cycling at the 1992 Summer Olympics in Barcelona. [SEP] [CLS] In the same event she won the 1991 World Championships and finished second in 1989. [SEP] [CLS] Competing in road bicycle racing, she won the World Cup in 2002.
