[CLS] Hugh Primas of Orléans was a Latin lyric poet of the 12th century, a scholar from Orléans who was jokingly called Primas, "the Primate", by his friends at the University of Paris. [SEP] [CLS] He was probably born in the 1090s and may have died about 1160. [SEP] [CLS] Along with his younger contemporary known as the Archpoet, he marks the opening of a new period in Latin literature. [SEP] [CLS]
