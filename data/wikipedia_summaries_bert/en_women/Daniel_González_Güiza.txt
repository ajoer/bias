Daniel González Güiza (Spanish pronunciation: [daˈnjel ˈɣwiθa]; born 17 August 1980) is a Spanish professional footballer who plays as a striker for Atlético Sanluqueño CF. [CLS] [SEP] A late bloomer, he made a name for himself at the age of 27, being crowned La Liga top scorer with Mallorca. [CLS] [SEP] Subsequently, he helped the Spain national team win Euro 2008, and signed with Fenerbahçe in Turkey shortly afterwards. [CLS] [SEP] Over eight seasons, Güiza amassed Spanish top division totals of 141 matches and 52 goals, mainly with Mallorca and Getafe. [CLS] [SEP]
