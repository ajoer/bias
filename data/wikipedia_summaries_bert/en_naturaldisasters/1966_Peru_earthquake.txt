The 1966 Peru earthquake occurred on October 17 at 16:41 local time (21:41 UTC). [CLS] [SEP] It had a magnitude of Mw 8.1. [CLS] [SEP] The epicenter was located offcoast near Huacho. [CLS] [SEP] About 100 people were reported dead. [CLS] [SEP] Most of the damage was recorded in low buildings. [CLS] [SEP] Cracks were reported in higher buildings. [CLS] [SEP] The intensity was MM IX in San Nicolás, MM VIII in Huacho, and MM VII in Callao. [CLS] [SEP] A tsunami was generated by this earthquake, which was recorded in Callao, Chimbote, and San Juan. [CLS] [SEP] Landslides and huge ground cracks were reported along the Pan American Highway north of Ancón. [CLS] [SEP]
