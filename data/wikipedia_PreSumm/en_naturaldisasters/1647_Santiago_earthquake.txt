[CLS] The 1647 Santiago earthquake struck Santiago, Chile on the night of 13 May (22:30 local time, 02:30 UTC on 14 May) and is said to have brought virtually every building in the city to the ground. [SEP] [CLS] The earthquake was felt throughout the so-called Captaincy General of Chile, an administrative territory of the Spanish Empire. [SEP] [CLS] The maximum felt intensity was XI on the Mercalli intensity scale and there were about a thousand casualties. [SEP] [CLS]
