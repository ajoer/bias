Claudia Nystad (née Künzel, born 1 February 1978) is a German top-level woman cross-country skier. [CLS] [SEP] She was born in Zschopau, East Germany, and represents the sports club WSC Erzgebirge Oberwiesenthal. [CLS] [SEP] Besides her civilian sports career, she serves as a sports soldier with the German Federal Armed Forces. [CLS] [SEP]
