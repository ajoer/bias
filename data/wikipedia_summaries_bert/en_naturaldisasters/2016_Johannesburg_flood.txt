[CLS] The 2016 Johannesburg flood was a natural disaster in South Africa that took place on 9 November 2016. [SEP] [CLS] The flooding, which occurred in the area east of Johannesburg, affected both Johannesburg and Ekurhuleni. [SEP] [CLS] The storm and flash floods caused significant damage to the township of Alexandra, while the suburb of Buccleuch was declared a disaster area. [SEP] [CLS] The flooding was caused by a significant cloud burst from a convective system. [SEP] [CLS]
