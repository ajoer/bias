On 9 March 2019 a Douglas DC-3 operated by Latinoamericana de Servicios Aereo (Laser Aéreo) crashed near San Carlos de Guaroa, Colombia. [CLS] [SEP] The plane was on a flight from Jorge Enrique González Torres Airport, San José del Guaviare to La Vanguardia Airport, Villavicencio. [CLS] [SEP] All 14 people on board were killed, including the mayor of Taraira, Doris Villegas. [CLS] [SEP] The crew were attempting to land at Villavicencio and had just declared an emergency.
