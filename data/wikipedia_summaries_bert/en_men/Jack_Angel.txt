Jack Angel (born October 24, 1930) is an American voice actor and radio personality. [CLS] [SEP] He has provided voice-overs for animation and video games. [CLS] [SEP] Angel has voiced characters in shows by Hasbro and Hanna-Barbera such as Super Friends, The Transformers and G.I. [CLS] [SEP] Joe and has been involved in numerous productions by DreamWorks, Disney and Disney Pixar. [CLS] [SEP] Before becoming involved with voiceover work, Angel was initially a disc jockey for radio stations, namely KMPC and KFI. [CLS] [SEP]
