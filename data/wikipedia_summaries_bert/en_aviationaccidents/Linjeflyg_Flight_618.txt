Linjeflyg Flight 618 was a crash of a Vickers 838 Viscount during approach to Stockholm Bromma Airport at 09:05 on 15 January 1977. [CLS] [SEP] All twenty-two people on board the aircraft perished when it hit ground at Kälvesta in Stockholm, Sweden. [CLS] [SEP] The domestic service from Malmö via Kristianstad, Växjö and Jönköping was operated by Skyline on behalf of Linjeflyg as part of the latter's domestic scheduled services. [CLS] [SEP] The accident was caused by atmospheric icing on the horizontal stabilizer. [CLS] [SEP] Low power on two of the engines had caused reduced function of the ice protection system, causing a buildup of ice. [CLS] [SEP] The icing caused the loss of pitch control and the aircraft entered a steep dive. [CLS] [SEP] Among the deceased was table tennis player Hans Alsér. [CLS] [SEP]
