Esen Taishi (Mongolian: Эсэн тайш; Chinese: 也先; d. [CLS] [SEP] 1455) was a powerful Oirat Taishi and de facto ruler of the Northern Yuan in 15th century Mongolia. [CLS] [SEP] He is best known for capturing Emperor Yingzong of Ming in 1450 after the Battle of Tumu Fortress and briefly reuniting the Mongols. [CLS] [SEP] The Four Oirat reached the peak of their power under his rule. [CLS] [SEP]
