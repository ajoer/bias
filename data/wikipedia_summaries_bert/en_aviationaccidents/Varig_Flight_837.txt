Varig Flight 837 was a flight from Fiumicino Airport, Rome, Italy to Roberts International Airport, Monrovia, Liberia, originating in Beirut, Lebanon. [CLS] [SEP] On 5 March 1967, due to pilot error, the flight crashed during approach to Runway 04 of Roberts International Airport. [CLS] [SEP] Of the 71 passengers and 19 crew on board, 50 passengers and the flight engineer perished. [CLS] [SEP] In addition, 5 people on the ground were also killed. [CLS] [SEP] The aircraft caught fire and was written off. [CLS] [SEP] This is the worst aviation accident in Liberia to this day.Investigators determined the probable cause of the crash to be "The failure of the pilot-in-command to arrest in time the fast descent at a low altitude upon which he had erroneously decided, instead of executing a missed approach when he found himself too high over the locator beacon."
