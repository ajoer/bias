Elisabeth Magdalena "Nina" Schenk Gräfin von Stauffenberg (27 August 1913 – 2 April 2006) was the wife of Colonel Claus Schenk Graf von Stauffenberg, the leader of the failed plot to assassinate Adolf Hitler on 20 July 1944. [CLS] [SEP] Following the plot's failure, she was arrested and imprisoned, during which time she delivered her youngest child. [CLS] [SEP]
