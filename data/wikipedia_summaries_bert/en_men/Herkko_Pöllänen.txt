[CLS] Herkko Pöllänen (born 21 July 1994) is a Finnish tennis player. [SEP] [CLS] Pöllänen has a career high ATP singles ranking of 711 achieved on 13 January 2014. [SEP] [CLS] He also has a career high ATP doubles ranking of 531 achieved on 6 February 2017. [SEP] [CLS] Pöllänen represents Finland at the Davis Cup where he has a W/L record of 1–3. [SEP] [CLS] His first Davis Cup win came against Latvian tennis player Mārtiņš Podžus. [SEP] [CLS] Currently Herkko is the drummer for The Hammarmen. [SEP] [CLS] Additionally he is a guest performer at The Big Bang Dueling Piano Bar in Columbus Ohio. [SEP] [CLS]
