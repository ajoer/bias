Thai Airways Flight 231 was a scheduled passenger flight that crashed on 27 April 1980. [CLS] [SEP] The Hawker Siddeley HS 748 operating the flight, registration HS-THB, stalled and crashed after entering a thunderstorm on approach to Bangkok. [CLS] [SEP] The accident killed 44 out of 53 passengers and crew on board. [CLS] [SEP]
