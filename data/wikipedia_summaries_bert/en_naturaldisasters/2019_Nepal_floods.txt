The 2019 Nepal Floods were a series of flash floods that affected widespread areas of Nepal. [CLS] [SEP] Severe flooding was seen in many regions, especially in the Terai area and Kathmandu. [CLS] [SEP] Heavy rainfall on account of the monsoon season began on the 11th of July and caused disruption in many areas, specially in Province No. [CLS] [SEP] 2 and Province No. [CLS] [SEP] 1.
