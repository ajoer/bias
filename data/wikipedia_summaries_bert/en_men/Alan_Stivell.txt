[CLS] Alan Stivell (born Alan Cochevelou on 6 January 1944) is a Breton and Celtic musician and singer, recording artist, and master of the Celtic harp. [SEP] [CLS] From the early 1970s, he revived global interest in the Celtic (specifically Breton) harp and Celtic music as part of world music. [SEP] [CLS] As a bagpiper and bombard player, he modernized traditional Breton music and singing in the Breton language. [SEP] [CLS] He was the precursor of Celtic rock. [SEP] [CLS] He is inspired by the union of the Celtic cultures and is a staunch defender of the Breton culture. [SEP] [CLS]
