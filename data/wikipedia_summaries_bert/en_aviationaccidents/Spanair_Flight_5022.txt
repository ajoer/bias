Spanair Flight 5022 (JK5022) was a scheduled domestic passenger flight from Barcelona–El Prat Airport to Gran Canaria Airport, Spain, via Madrid–Barajas Airport that crashed just after take-off from runway 36L at Madrid Airport at 14:24 CEST (12:24 UTC) on 20 August 2008. [CLS] [SEP] The aircraft was a McDonnell Douglas MD-82, registration EC-HFP. [CLS] [SEP] Of the 172 passengers and crew on board, 154 died and 18 survived.It was the only fatal accident for Spanair (part of the SAS Group) in the 25-year history of the company, and the 14th fatal accident and 24th hull loss involving McDonnell Douglas MD-80 series aircraft. [CLS] [SEP] It was Spain's deadliest accident since the 1983 crash of Avianca Flight 011.The accident further worsened Spanair's already negative image at the time and exacerbated its financial difficulties. [CLS] [SEP] Spanair subsequently ceased operations on 27 January 2012. [CLS] [SEP]
