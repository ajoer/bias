[CLS] The Interfrisian Council is a geopolitical organization that represents the common interests of the Frisians. [SEP] [CLS] The organization consists of three regional councils or "sections": North Frisia, East Frisia and West Frisia. [SEP] [CLS] Every three years, the presidency of the Interfrisian Council is handed over to another section. [SEP] [CLS] The council was established in 1956. [SEP] [CLS]
