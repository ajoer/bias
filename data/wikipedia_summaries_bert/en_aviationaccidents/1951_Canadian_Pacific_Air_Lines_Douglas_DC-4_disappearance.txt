The 1951 Canadian Pacific Douglas DC-4 disappearance occurred on 21 July when a Douglas DC-4 four-engined piston airliner registered CF-CPC of Canadian Pacific Air Lines disappeared on a scheduled flight for the United Nations from Vancouver, Canada, to Tokyo, Japan. [CLS] [SEP] Neither the aircraft nor the 31 passengers and six crew have been found. [CLS] [SEP] The incident marked the first aircraft loss during the Korean Airlift. [CLS] [SEP]
