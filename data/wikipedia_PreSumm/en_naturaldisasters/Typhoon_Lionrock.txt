[CLS] Typhoon Lionrock, known in the Philippines as Typhoon Dindo, was a large, powerful, long-lived and erratic tropical cyclone which caused significant flooding and casualties in North Korea and Japan in late August 2016. [SEP] [CLS] It was the tenth named storm and was the third typhoon of the annual Pacific typhoon season. [SEP] [CLS] Damages recorded after the season were recorded about US$3.93 billion.
