[CLS] Tsarevich Dmitry or Dmitri Ivanovich (Russian: Дмитрий Иванович, tr. [SEP] [CLS] Dmitrii Ivanovich; 19 October 1582 – 15 May 1591), also known as Dmitry of Uglich (Дмитрий Угличский, Uglichskii) or Dmitry of Moscow (Дмитрий Московский, Moskovskii), was a Russian tsarevich famously impersonated by a series of pretenders after the death of his father Ivan the Terrible.
