[CLS] This page documents notable tornadoes and tornado outbreaks worldwide in 2005. [SEP] [CLS] Strong and destructive tornadoes form most frequently in the United States, Bangladesh, Argentina, Brazil and Eastern India, but they can occur almost anywhere under the right conditions. [SEP] [CLS] Tornadoes also develop occasionally in southern Canada during the Northern Hemisphere's summer and somewhat regularly at other times of the year across Europe, Asia, and Australia. [SEP] [CLS] Tornadic events are often accompanied with other forms of severe weather, including strong thunderstorms, strong winds, and hail. [SEP] [CLS]
